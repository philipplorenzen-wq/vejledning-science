Every year SCIENCE welcomes hundreds of international students from all over the world.

The Buddies of the SCIENCE Buddy Programme provide assistance to incoming exchange students to make them feel welcome and safe when they first arrive in Copenhagen and help them get settled into their new student life at SCIENCE.

All Buddies are volunteer SCIENCE students from across the faculty.

Benefits of being a Buddy

There are many benefits to being part of the SCIENCE Buddy Programme besides helping fellow students, making new friends and having fun.

As a SCIENCE Buddy, you will get the chance to

  • improve your linguistic, organizational and intercultural skills
  • gain insights into different worldviews and the challenges of cultural adjustment
  • add valuable experiences to your resume
  • expand your social and professional network
  • have fun with and meet new people from different departments and around the world.

As a part of the SCIENCE Buddy Programme you will receive training and support so you are prepared for your role as a Buddy.
You will also receive a Buddy t-shirt, a water bottle, a tote-bag and an official Certificate of Recognition that acknowledges your contribution to making SCIENCE an attractive international study destination.
