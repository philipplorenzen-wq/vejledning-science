What do I get out of having a student job?

In addition to providing you with a financial supplement to your state education grant, a student job may give you a number of valuable experiences and competences that can help you enter the labour market after you have completed your study programme.
A student job can help you with:
  • knowledge of how you can use your education
  • an opportunity to try out the theory you have learnt in your study programme in practice
  • insights into everyday life in a workplace
  • experience for your next student job or first job as a graduate
  • a bigger network which you can use when you are to apply for your first 'real' job
  • building your academic, general and personal competences.
