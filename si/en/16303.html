If your registration is rejected when you try to register for a course during the late-registration period, it may mean that

  • your curriculum does not allow you to take the course
  • you do not meet the formal requirements for the course
  • you do not have available ECTS credits for the course. You might have to cancel a course.

If you need help with your registration, please fill out the form 'Get Study Administrative Help'
