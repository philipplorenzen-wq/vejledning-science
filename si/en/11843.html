You have chosen a minor subject during your admission process. To see the structure of your programme please choose your minor subject.

The programme is two years, if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Biotechnology and minor in [titel of your minor subject] / Cand.scient i bioteknologi med sidefag i [titlen på dit sidefag]
