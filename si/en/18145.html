As a non-EU citizen, your student visa allows you to stay in Denmark after you graduate and apply for jobs.

Check your student visa to get to know the specific rules in your situation e.g., 

  • how long you are allowed to stay in Denmark for
  • the number of hours you can work while you apply for full-time jobs etc.


If you get a full-time job in Denmark, you must apply for a work visa.

Read more about work visa on New to Denmark (SIRI's website)
