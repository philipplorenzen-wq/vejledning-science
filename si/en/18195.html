Can I take exams on special conditions due to pregnancy-related discomfort?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-take-exams-on-special-conditions-due-to-pregnancy-related-discomfort-SCI.aspx

You can apply for special exam conditions if you experience so much pregnancy-related discomfort (nausea, pelvic girdle pain etc.) that you have to

  • take many breaks
  • leave the room during the exam.

Read more about how to apply for special exam conditions
