2 steps when applying for special conditions for exams or extended time for your Master's thesis:

  1. Get documentation for illness or decreased functionality, e.g. doctor's certificate, test results.
    See a guide on what your documentation should include
  2. Fill out an application by clicking the button below. In the application you must describe how your illness or decreased functionality affects you at the exam and what you need. This is also where you upload your documentation.

special conditions/extended Master's thesis


If you have a general dispensation

If you already have a general dispensation and need special conditions for courses with ongoing tests, you do not need to apply again but notify SCIENCE for each course.

The deadline to notify about a course with ongoing tests is at least 6 weeks before the block starts.

Nofity about courses with ongoing tests 


Deadlines for applying

The application deadline for special conditions for an exam depends on the type of your exam. In all cases, you should apply as soon as possible.

  • If your course has ongoing tests or part of your exam takes place during the block, you must apply no later than 6 weeks before the block starts. Are you a new student, you must apply as soon as possible.
  • You can apply for extended time for your Master's thesis at the latest when you submit your Master's thesis contract for approval.
  • When your exam is at the end of your course, the deadline is Friday in the 3rd week of the course's block. If your course runs over 2 blocks, the deadline is Friday in the 3rd week of the course's last block.


How to apply after the deadline

Did you not have – or did you not discover – your need for special conditions or extended Master’s thesis time until after the deadline, you still have a chance to get your application processed.


Apply for special conditions for exams or extended Master's thesis time after the deadline

How to apply after the deadline:

  1. Apply for a dispensation immediately following the normal procedure.
  2. Make it clear in your explanation why you have not been able to apply before now.
  3. Write in the application's comment field that it is urgent as you have an upcoming exam.


Apply for extended Master's thesis time after the Master's thesis start date

After the start date of your Master's thesis, you can apply to postpone the submission.
