How do I prepare a good unsolicited application?

You can, to advantage, submit an unsolicited application if e.g. you are interested in a specific workplace that does not have any current job advertisements. Without a job advertisement to help you focus your application, you must do some research and networking to find out more about the workplace and possible tasks.

For example, you need to focus on which
  • tasks the workplace performs
  • challenges the workplace has that you can contribute to meeting
  • values, goals, objectives and organisation the workplace has.

You can find much information
  • on the workplace's website
  • in previous job advertisements on the Internet
  • by asking around in your network
  • by contacting the workplace directly and acquiring more specific knowledge for your application.
