You can use your elective ECTS credits on

  • courses at SCIENCE
  • courses at other faculties or universities in Denmark.
  • projects, e.g. with a company or a research group
  • a period of studying abroad.


Requirements for elective courses

Your elective courses must primarily be at Master level.

You may take Bachelor's programme courses equal to 15 ECTS credits.

You register for courses at SCIENCE via Self Service

Courses outside SCIENCE require a bit more preliminary work


Important when choosing courses/projects

Check whether it is possible for you to take the course/project due to:

  • the scope (number of ECTS credits) and scheduling of the course/project – it must fit into your course of study.
  • requirements and prerequisites (only for courses) – there may be specific formal requirements (e.g. courses you must have passed) for taking the course, and recommended academic qualifications, and you need to consider whether you meet these.

Ask the course coordinator or read about it in the course description
Questions to help you choose:

  • What elements in your study programme have interested and motivated you the most during your studies?
  • Have you come across particularly exciting areas that you would like to know more about?   
  • Are there any methods you would like to learn or academic directions you would like to strengthen?
  • Is there another subject area in which you can cultivate interests outside your own study programme?
