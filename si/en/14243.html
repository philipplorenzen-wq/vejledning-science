Stop/limit the accident, if possible.


Give life-saving first aid if necessary.

Get help, if possible, and alert people as soon as possible.

In the event of a heart attack: If possible, give mouth-to-mouth resuscitation and cardiac massage. If necessary,                                         use a defibrillator. 

                                    Locate the closest defibrillator: You will find the maps in the accordion below.  

                                    Follow the instructions given by the defibrillator.

Call 112 (or 18 13 if it is not an emergency).

Agree whether an ambulance is needed or whether the person should be driven to the doctor, possibly by taxi. The person in question should not drive.

Guide any ambulances to the adress. 


Continue with life-saving first aid or provide ordinary first aid until the ambulance arrives.


If possible, send someone to meet the ambulance.


Call UCPH Emergency service 35 33 88 00, and someone from operations or the emergency service will help you.
