An increasing number of job interviews are being conducted online on, for example, Teams or Zoom.

3 tips for online interviews

#1 Test the technology

Test your audio and camera well in advance of the interview, so that it does not steal time from the interview. Make sure your user name on the platform you use matches your real name. 

#2 Select a location with a presentable background

It should be somewhere that is not too private. I.e. preferably without laundry or a bed in the background. If you do not have the perfect place, some platforms allow you to blur your background.

#3 Be clear

Make sure to be very clear about what you want to communicate. Online meetings carry the risk of subtleties being lost because we cannot read each other's body language and all the little non-verbal signals.


Make sure to look into the camera when you speak. It may feel unnatural for you, but the others will have an experience of eye contact.

Besides the tips mentioned, just prepare as if it were a conventional face-to-face interview.
