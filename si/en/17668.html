Frederiksberg Campus offers many opportunities to socialise with your fellow students, both on weekdays and at parties: 

  • Café Væksthuset is located in the beautiful garden of the Royal Veterinary and Agricultural University. The café serves breakfast, lunch, soft drinks, coffee etc. for students as well as the garden’s many guests. Café Væksthuset is run by students from SCIENCE.
    Read more about Café Væksthuset on Facebook
  • A-vej is the gathering point for students at and around Frederiksberg Campus. The bar is run by students from the study programmes based at Frederiksberg Campus. You must use your UCPH student identity card to enter.
    Keep up-to-date on A-vej’s Facebook page
  • Smediefesten is an annual celebration that is full of traditions for all students linked to Frederiksberg Campus.
    Read more about Smediefesten on Facebook
