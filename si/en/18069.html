Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Am-I-allowed-to-take-my-course-again-to-prepare-for-the-re-exam-SCI.aspx

You may attend lectures without being registered for a course. 

Things you may not participate in without being registered for the course:

  • Laboratory-based teaching activities
  • Exercises
  • Calculation classes, etc.

Get access to the new course room in Absalon by contacting the course coordinator.

See how and when you can re-register for a course under Course registration
