Here are my recommendations regarding projects:

  • Many students enjoy and get new inspiration by doing a Project in Practice.
  • It is a good idea to give your programme more insight and personality by having contact to older students, Ph.D. students and professors.
  • You can do your Master's thesis in cooperation with an external host, e.g. a potential employer.
  • You can do your thesis abroad, either having your supervisor abroad or from KU-science. Read more about the different opportunities for your Master's Thesis.
  • Many research groups post conducted master's theses for your inspiration or even suggest thesis subjects. Here is an example.
  • Besides doing a Project in Practice and your Master's thesis, you can also do a Project outside the course scope. 
  • I offer an introduction to project in practice and thesis work for all Nature Management students in May.
