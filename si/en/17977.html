If you change your registered address from Denmark to the country where you will be studying abroad, UCPH (University of Copenhagen) will need your Tax Identification Number (TIN) to disburse your scholarship.

This means that if you already have your official address abroad, or if you plan to report a change of address to a foreign one during your study abroad, you must provide UCPH with your foreign Tax Identification Number (TIN) and address. UCPH cannot disburse your scholarship if you do not report your official foreign address and Tax Identification Number.

NOTE: UCPH only needs your foreign Tax Identification Number and address if you officially report your move to another country in the Danish civil register.
