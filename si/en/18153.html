What is generative AI?

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that produces content such as text, images, audio or video. The content is generated based on patterns and data influenced by input from many different users.

This means the content you create is not entirely your own product.


A few examples of generative AI tools:

  • Large Language Models like Copilot Enterprise, ChatGPT, Llama, Claude, and Gemini
  • Generative Image Models like DALL·E and MidJourney


Be aware that generative AI is embedded in many different programs/software. It is not only the well-known chatbots – such as Copilot or ChatGPT – that use it. E.g., Grammarly uses it for proofreading and Adobe Illustrator for image editing.
