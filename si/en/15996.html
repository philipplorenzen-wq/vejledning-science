How many and which ECTS credits can I use on a project in practice?

Gælder for målgrupperne: Nanoscience - Kandidat · Human Ernæring - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-many-and-which-ECTS-credits-can-I-use-on-a-project-in-practice-NANO-SCI.aspx

You may do one project in practice for 15 ECTS credits (workload: 412 hours) during your course of study.


If you were admitted in September 2023 or later, a project in practice can only count as elective ECTS credits on your study programme.


If you were admitted in February 2023 or earlier, a project in practice can count as either

  • restricted elective ECTS credits
  • elective ECTS credits.

You may do a project that counts as both types of ECTS credits when your curriculum allows both types.
