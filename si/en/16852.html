When do I withdraw from an exam at the Department of Economics?

Placering: Exams › Registration and withdrawal › Exam withdrawal

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Mathematics-Economics - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/exam-withdrawal-ECON-SAMF.aspx

When

The exam registration period is the same as the withdrawal period. This means that you can withdraw from exams in the same periods you would register for the exams.

Find the registration periods here

How

You withdraw from exams in Self Service via:

  • Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Examination > Cancel.

Go to Self Service 

You can only withdraw during the withdrawal periods. Please check the academic progress requirements before withdrawing from an exam.

Check your exam withdrawal

When you have withdrawn your registration from an exam, you can see it immediately in Self Service via:

  • Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Examination > View registrations and cancellations.

You are responsible for ensuring that you are registered for the right exams when the deadline for withdrawal expires.

If you withdraw from a wrong course, you can re-register for the course. The exam registration period is the same as the withdrawal period.

If you notice errors in your registrations after the deadline for withdrawal, you must contact the UCPH Study Guidance.
