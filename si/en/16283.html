You withdraw from your study programme in Self Service. 

What to do

  1. Go to Self Service under the 'Enrolment' tab and select 'Withdrawal'. 
  2. Have your SU payments stopped so you do not risk having to repay your SU. The SU Office will also automatically be notified of your withdrawal, but often with a slight delay.
     Stop SU payments at the SU Office website

You will recieve a letter that confirms your withdrawal.

Go to Self Service

Your access to UCPH after withdrawing from your study programme

You have access to Self Service for 2 years after withdrawing from your study programme. In Self Service you can find and print a transcript of your grades.
You can order a transcript of your grades certified (stamped) at UCPH Study Guidance.
