There are several ways in which you can prevent stress in your student life, and it is worth investing some energy in this. What is relevant for you depends on the difficulties you may be experiencing in your student life. 

4 things you might like to look at: 

  1. How do you plan your study day? – do you make realistic plans that allow you to take time off and recharge?
    Get tips for how to plan your study day
  2. Are you using the study techniques that are right for you? It can be very stressful if you often find it difficult to achieve what you aimed to do in the reading room.
    Get inspiration for different study techniques
  3. Do you experience performance pressure in your student life? Perhaps you put yourself under too much pressure, without taking into account what else is happening in your life.
    Read more about performance priorities
  4. Are you taking enough care of yourself – physically and mentally? Is your foundation in place, so you have the energy to study?
    Read more about mental health
