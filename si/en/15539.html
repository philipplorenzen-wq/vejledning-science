Before submitting a complaint, you should always talk to your lecturer about the issues or problems. It is in everyone's best interests to try to resolve the issues before they ruin the course.


Talk to a study and career guidance counsellor

If you are unsure how to address issues or your dissatisfaction with a course, you are welcome to talk to a study and career guidance counsellor at UCPH Study Guidance.
