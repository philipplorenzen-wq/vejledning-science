On the list of alumni networks, you can see if there is already a network on your programme.

If there is not, you can start a network yourself. In this way, you can create a forum and a network that focus on your academic skills, and through which you can stay in touch with your, soon-to-be former, fellow students.

Get advice and support to get started by writing to alumne@adm.ku.dk.
