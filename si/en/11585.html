You can apply for a dispensation for either special conditions at an exam or a change of the exam type, if you cannot take the exam equally with your fellow students.

This can e.g. be in relation to:

  • physical or mental illness/decreased functionality
    E.g. dyslexia, sight impairment, stress, concussion, ADHD
  • temporary functionality issues
    E.g. pregnancy, physical injuries or language challenges.
