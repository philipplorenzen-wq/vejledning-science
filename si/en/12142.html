Everyone should feel welcome at UCPH regardless of background, orientation or needs. That is why UCPH conducted an inclusion survey among all students and employees in 2024. The more than 8,000 responses make up the basis for the survey’s result reports, including two reports based on responses from nearly 4,000 students.
