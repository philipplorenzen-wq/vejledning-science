SCIENCE should be a place for everyone. You, your fellow students, and SCIENCE's staff have a collective responsibility to create a study environment where we

  • respect each other's differences
  • engage in dialogue with one another
  • reflect on how we interact with other people.


At SCIENCE, we have formulated 6 Principles of Interaction that set the framework for a safe and inclusive study environment in and outside the classroom.

Use these principles as a guideline for how you and others can contribute to ensuring an inclusive and safe study environment.


Principles of Interaction at SCIENCE

These principles apply both in the social study environment and in classes and lectures. In classes and lectures, the academic framework determines what you will be taught – even if some topics may be perceived as offensive.
