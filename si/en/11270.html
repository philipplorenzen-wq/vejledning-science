I'm Karsten Olsen and I'm your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic contents of your programme in detail and have contributed to determining your curriculum. My research areas are food chemistry, food physics, gastronomy and food innovation in relation to food processing and storage. I teach on the courses Food Science and Culinary Techniques as well as Thematic course in Food Innovation & Health in your programme.
As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make. 
My role is to guide you academically through your study:• I can guide you about the academic content of your program and relevant areas of research. • I can help you with choosing relevant courses and finding supervisors for your projects. • I approve your contract for the Master thesis as well as potential projects in practice and projects outside the course scope.• I cannot help you with the rules and structure of your programme. For these questions, please contact UCPH Study Guidance.
Here are my advice and my recommendations for completing your master's programme in Food Innovation and Health successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.
