You may need to print your results, for example if you're applying for a job, housing, or a stay abroad.

Documents with signature

We can send the following certified documents (signed and stamped) to your KUmail within 24 hours:

  • Transcript – This shows the courses you have completed or confirms that you have graduated from your study programme.
  • Documentation for enrolment – This verifies that you are currently enrolled at UCPH/your study programme and indicates the expected semester of completion for your degree (e.g. for use in student accommodation applications).

Order documentation for enrolment and transcript


Documents Without Signature

You can always download a non-certified transcript or a confirmation of enrolment yourself in Self service.
