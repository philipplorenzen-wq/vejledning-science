How do we create a good working relationship?

For the cooperation between you and your supervisor to work, it is a prerequisite that you align your expectations regarding

  • the project – academic focus, level and ambitions
  • the supervision – the kind of supervision and how much supervision you need.


Two important areas to address early on

When initiating your cooperation, it is a good idea to address these two issues.

1) Framework for supervision and cooperation

  • Will the supervision take place face to face, by email, Teams, phone or a mix?
  • How often will you need to meet/be in contact. Do you need a firm plan or agree as you go along?
  • Are there periods when your supervisor or you are not available? (due to holidays, etc.)
  • What kind of academic sparring and feedback can you expect from your supervisor, and which decisions do you need to involve your supervisor in?
  • Do you have access to facilities at the department? (If you need laboratory space, equipment, IT, etc.)


2) Expectations and assumptions

  • What is your level of ambition regarding the project, and is it aligned with your supervisor's level of ambition?
  • In doing the project, do you have a specific aim, which your supervisor should know about?
  • Are there any particular challenges or concerns related to the project work?
