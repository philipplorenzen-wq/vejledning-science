Which other scholarships can I apply for?

As a student at UCPH, you have the opportunity to apply for a number of scholarships. Each of these scholarships must be applied for separately.

The scholarships vary in their requirements for applicants, and you can only apply for scholarships that have been announced.

See the list of individual scholarships and when they are expected to be announced.
