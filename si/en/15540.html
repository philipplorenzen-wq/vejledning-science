Are there academic requirements which must be met by courses outside SCIENCE?

There are requirements for your courses outside SCIENCE. Some of them depend on the type of course.

Requirements for elective courses

The only academic requirement to be met by elective courses outside SCIENCE is that they must be at master's programme level or higher.

In other words, you decide whether the course content is relevant to your study programme.


Courses at Bachelor's programme level

You can take a restricted number of courses at bachelor's programme level.

See how many elevtive courses at Bachelor's programme level you can fit into your programme under Programme structure.


Requirements for compulsory or restricted elective courses

If you want to take one of your compulsory courses outside SCIENCE, the new course must match the academic standards of the SCIENCE course.

If you want to take a restricted elective course outside SCIENCE, the course you choose must fall within the core academic disciplines of your study programme.
