What will my diploma show?

Placering: Exams › Grades and complaints › Diploma

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-will-my-diploma-show-SCI.aspx

Your diploma is formal proof that you have completed your study programme. It will list all the courses and projects you have completed, along with your grades.
The diploma will not show 

  • how many exam attempts you have used 
  • exams you may have failed
  • if you had special conditions in class or for the exam.

Your diploma will be in both Danish and English.
