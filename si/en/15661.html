The library is located on Dyrelægevej 10, 1870 Frederiksberg C.

You will find

  • Quiet reading rooms with 50-74 study places
  • Space for group work and reading in the open library area

The library has capacity for around 300 people. A large part of the library's public area is furnished as a study environment – divided into either conversation or quiet zones.
