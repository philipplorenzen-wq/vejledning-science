As a student at DIKU, you can become a member of the Teaching Committee. The committee works to strengthen the quality of teaching and advises the head of department on the strategic development in the field of teaching. 
Interested in becoming a student representative in the Teaching Committee? Please write to vilu@di.ku.dk
