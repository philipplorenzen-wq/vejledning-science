Written exam - where and when?

You can find the date, time and place of written exams and re-exams in the exam schedule.

See the exam schedule

You can also always see your exam registrations in Self Service.

Find your exam room and your exam number in Digital Exam

You can see the specific exams which you are registered for in Digital Exam at least one week before your exam.

In Digital Exam, you can see your exam room and your exam number.

Go to Digital Exam

Oral exam - where and when?

You can find the time for your oral exam in the exam schedule.

See the exam schedule

You can also always see your exam registrations and your exam number in Self Service.

You will receive an email about time and place

When you are due to take an oral exam, you will receive information about the time of the exam via your KUmail.

Often, the schedule for oral exams will also be made available in Digital Exam before the exam.
