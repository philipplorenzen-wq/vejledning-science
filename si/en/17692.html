Can I apply for special conditions for my exam after the deadline?

Gælder for målgrupperne: Gæstestuderende SCI - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-apply-for-special-conditions-for-my-exam-after-the-deadline-EX-SCI.aspx

Did you not have – or did you not discover – your need for special conditions until after the deadline, you still have a chance to get your application processed even though your application is late.


How to apply after the deadline:

  1. Apply for a dispensation by following the normal procedure.
  2. Make it clear in your explanation why you have not been able to apply before now.
  3. State in the commentary field that it is urgent as you have an upcoming exam.
