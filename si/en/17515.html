The project in practice can help you to become clearer about your skills and your future working life.
You can also use your project to acquire

  • specific experience from a professional context, which you can use on your CV when applying for a student job or a job following graduation
  • new knowledge and possibly inspiration for other major projects on your study programme or for possible career paths
  • contacts and networks in the 'real world' outside the University, which may help you in your career.
  • experience with entrepreneurship, if you are working with your own idea/company.
