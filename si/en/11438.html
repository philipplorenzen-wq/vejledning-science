You will have access to information about when and where your exams are held no later than 7 days before each exam.


Before you receive information about the time and place of your exams, you can:

  • see the exam dates for your course in the general exam schedule for SCIENCE
  • use the schedule group for your course to see approximately when your exam will take place.
