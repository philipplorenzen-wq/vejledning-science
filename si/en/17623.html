How to calculate your GPA if you are enrolled in a Danish university:

When you fill in your application, you must state your simple grade-point average:

1. Add up all your grades 
2. Divide the sum with the number of grades you have received on your overall study programme (Bachelor's programme and Master's programme)


You have to include all graded from 02 and above.
Pass/Fail exams do not count.

Special situations:

  • If you are a bachelor student and you have not yet received any grades, you must enter 0 in the application.
  • If you have an international bachelor's degree, you are only to state the average of your Danish grades and not use your foreign grade-point average. However, you still need to upload your foreign transcript of grades along with your transcript of grades from the University of Copenhagen.
  • If you still do not have any Danish grades, because you have taken your bachelor's degree abroad, you must write '15' in the GPA field and upload your foreign BA-transcript. We will convert your GPA for you.
