As a student, you have no doubt at times felt very busy and under pressure. 

You may also briefly have experienced various signs of stress during peak periods, such as up to an exam. This is completely normal and harmless.

However, if you are under too much strain over an extended period, it could develop into a serious stress reaction. 

By being aware of the various types of stress, you can: 

  • prevent and reduce stress 
  • seek help in good time when you need it.
