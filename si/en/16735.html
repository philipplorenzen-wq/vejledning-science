When you fill out the application form, you must choose whether you want to apply for

  • dispensation to a single or a few exams (not courses with ongoing tests) – and perhaps extra time for the Master's thesis
  • dispensation for exams in courses with ongoing tests
  • only extra time for the Master's thesis
  • a general dispensation for exams in all upcoming courses at SCIENCE (except courses with ongoing tests) – including extra time for the Master's thesis, if you need it.


How a general dispensation applies

A general dispensation is

  • valid for your future exams for the rest of your studies at SCIENCE – or until the dispensation's end date
  • not valid for projects and courses with ongoing tests
  • in most cases, also valid even if you start another study programme at SCIENCE.

You must apply for a dispensation where your course is offered if you

  • take courses at another faculty at UCPH or another university
  • start a study programme outside of SCIENCE.


If you have courses with ongoing tests

If you need a dispensation for exams in courses with ongoing tests, you must apply for a dispensation for each course. 

If you already have a general dispensation, you do not need to apply again but notify SCIENCE when you need special conditions for a course with ongoing tests.


Extended time for projects

You do not need to apply for a dispensation if you need extended time for your

  • project outside the course scope
  • project in practice
  • thesis preparation project.


Instead, you can do the project without taking courses at the same time.
