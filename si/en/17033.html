Who may act as my supervisor?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Who-may-act-as-my-supervisor-SCI-IS.aspx

Your main supervisor must be employed at SCIENCE.
Your supervisor must be employed in one of the following job categories:

  • professor
  • associate professor/external lecturer
  • teaching associate professor
  • teaching lecturer 
  • post doc
  • assistant professor
  • senior researcher
  • senior consultant
  • senior adviser 
  • affiliate associate professor
  • affiliate professor
  • OR with PhD qualification or similar


Possibility for co-supervisor with a different background

It is not required that a potential co-supervisor is from SCIENCE.
