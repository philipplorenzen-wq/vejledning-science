You need to apply for pre-approval if you wish to take ECTS credits at

  • a UCPH faculty other than SCIENCE
  • another university in Denmark or abroad

The pre-approval is your guarantee that the ECTS credits you take elsewhere can be transferred to your study programme at SCIENCE. The pre-approval is also needed for you to be admitted to another faculty or university.
