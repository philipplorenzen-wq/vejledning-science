My name is Ole Mertz and I am your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic content of your programme in detail and I have contributed in determining your curriculum.

My own research is focused on the intersection between human and physical geography and I am specifically interested human-environment interactions in the Global South. I primarily teach on the courses Kulturgeografisk Feltkursus (BSc), Land System Science – Arealanvendelse og Fødevarer (BSc), Advanced Land System Science  and Environment, Society and Development in the Global South.

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the content of your study plan. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here is my advice and my recommendations for completing your Master's programme in Geography and Geoinformatics successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are based on the latest programme structure, unless I have specifically written otherwise.
