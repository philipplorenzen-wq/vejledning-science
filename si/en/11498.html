As a student at UCPH, you have your own UCPH email account – the socalled KUmail or "webmail" .

All official emails from UCPH will be sent to your KUmail, so remember to check it regularly.

  • Your KUmail account has the address [UCPH username]@alumni.ku.dk. 
  • The password for your KUmail account is the same as for KUnet. Read about the UCPH password here. 
  • For security reasons you must use multi-factor authentication (MFA) to access your KUmail. For help with multi-factor authentication, visit the page Help for login.
