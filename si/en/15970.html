Studenterhuset on Købmagergade is both a café and a music venue – a vibrant cultural and academic hub for students in the Copenhagen area. 

Your UCPH student identity card makes you eligible for discounts on: 

  • food and drink 
  • admission prices for events.

Daily activities and parties 

On weekdays, you can enjoy cheap coffee, food, cake and beer – and perhaps play a game of foosball. 

On Fridays and Saturdays there is live music and DJs. 


Study in common and meeting rooms 

During the day, you can freely use the peaceful rooms on the first and second floor as study and group rooms. 

In the evenings, you can book the rooms for debates, courses, events etc.
