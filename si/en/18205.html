What do I do when the exam is over?

When the exam ends, you must stay in your seat until the invigilators have collected all the exam papers. You may not leave the room until the invigilators give you permission.


Before leaving the exam room, make sure that

  1. you have handed in your whole exam paper according to the invigilators' instructions
  2. the invigilators have registered that you have handed in your paper. You will be given a receipt confirming that you have handed in your paper.
