I’m taking parental leave (without leave of absence) during my Master’s thesis

If you are taking parental leave (with SU) without taking leave of absence while writing your thesis, you must apply to have your submission postponed.

You apply to have your submission postponed when you give notice of parental leave by

  • checking the box 'I apply to extend my Master's thesis contract'
  • write how many months that you would like your deadline for submission extended for.

Please be aware that you are not ensured the number of months that you apply for.


See how to give notice of parental leave under 'When and how should I give notice of parental leave with SU?'
