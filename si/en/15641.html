What are the requirements for my Master’s thesis report?

Formal rules and requirements for your Master's thesis report:

  • You must write in English.
     
  • You must write an abstract (a summary) in Danish or English.
  • You must attach published/unpublished articles as appendices. Articles may only be included directly in the thesis if you/your group are the sole authors.
  • You must fill out and submit an AI declaration, regardless of whether you have used generative AI or not.
    How to fill out and submit the AI declaration
  • You must avoid plagiarism. You must be aware of this both if you use your own as well as others' academic work.
    Read about good scientific practice and plagiarism

Make sure to write clearly. One grading point may be abducted in the assessment if the quality of your spelling, writing proficiency and communication makes it difficult to assess your project.


Talk to your supervisor about scope and content

There are no formal requirements regarding how your report should be structured.

Talk to your supervisor about

  • what your report should contain
  • how much you need to write.
