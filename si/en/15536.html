When and how can I withdraw from courses?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/When-and-how-can-I-withdraw-from-courses-SCI.aspx

You can withdraw from courses via Self Service > Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Courses > Withdraw.

The deadline for course withdrawal is the same as the deadline for the late-registration period for the block.

Your course registration is binding after expiry of the deadline for withdrawal.


Deadlines for withdraw

Blocks 1 and 1–225 August 2026
Blocks 2 and 2-3 10 November 2026
Blocks 3 and 3–42 February 2027
Blocks 4 and 4-520 April 2027
Blocks 5 and 5-1, 20261 June 2027
You must withdraw by 23:59 on the closing date at the latest.

Check that your withdrawals are correct

When you have withdrawn from the course, you will receive a receipt via your KUmail.

Check your course withdrawal by selecting Self Service > Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Courses > Registrations and withdrawals.

You are responsible for ensuring that you have registered for and withdrawn from the correct courses.


Do you still comply with the study activity requirements?

Always be sure that you still meet the study activity requirements when you withdraw from a course without replacing it with another one in the same block.
