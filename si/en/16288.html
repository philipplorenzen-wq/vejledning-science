Do I automatically withdraw from the exam when I withdraw from a course?

Placering: Courses and teaching › Registration and withdrawal › Course withdrawal

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Do-I-automatically-withdraw-from-the-exam-when-I-withdraw-from-a-course-SCI.aspx

When you withdraw from a course, you will be asked if you want to withdraw from the exam too. If you answer 'yes', you will also be withdrawn from the exam.

Check that you have withdrawn from both the exam and the course in Self Service before the expiry of the deadline for late-registration.
