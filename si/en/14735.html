Should I apply for SU for my master's degree programme?

It is important that you remember to apply for SU for your master's degree programme. If SU does not receive your application, you will be perceived as opting out of SU during your master's degree programme.

For your master's degree programme you can apply via minSU on www.su.dk.

Go to minSU


Please note that your application for SU cannot be processed until you are enrolled in a master's degree programme.
Find information on how to apply for admission to a master's degree programme on your faculty's website or in the student counselling service.
Expect extended processing time, when changing your enrolment from bachelor's to master's degree.
Check Self Service, to see if you are enrolled correctly.

Please note! If you have applied for admission to a master's degree programme, but do not pass your bachelor's degree, you must immediately inform your faculty and SU at UCPH so that the SU allocation can be changed back to the bachelor's degree programme.
