You can grant another person a self service power of attorney on minSU.
You can read more at www.su.dk.
If you want to grant another person power of attorney to handle your SU matters that cannot be done at minSU, you can fill out a power of attorney to the SU office at UCPH. The power of attorney can be found at this link.
