If you fail or do not take the ordinary exam, you must register for re-examination by writing to studyguidance-science@adm.ku.dk.

You will not be automatically registered.

Consider your options before you register for re-exam

As the re-exam might be scheduled after your official exchange study enrolment has ended, you should always make sure to discuss your options with your home university if you are planning on taking a re-exam.
If you have any questions about your options for re-exams at UCPH, please write to incomingmobility@adm.ku.dk.


Your possibilities for retaking the exam 

You can choose the exam period in which you would like to retake the exam.

You can retake the exam in:

  • the first re-exam period for the block to which your course belongs.
  • a later re-exam period in which your exam is offered. This can be either an ordinary exam period or a re-exam period.
