To receive unemployment benefits from your unemployment insurance fund, you must register as a job seeker at your local job centre or on Jobnet.dk on the first day of your unemployment.

Find your local job centre


Other than registering as unemployed you need to contact your

  • unemployment fund
  • trade union

If you are an international student

As an international student, you should check your visa and if you need to apply for a job-seeking permit.

Find information about your Visa and tips on getting a career in Denmark as an international candidate
