Here are two ways to get help with your appeal:


UCPH Study Guidance

If you are unsure about anything or need advice about your appeal process, you may benefit from talking to a study and career guidance counsellor from UCPH Study Guidance.


The student ambassador

The student ambassador can also help explain your rights and assess your case.
