#4 Contact people in relevant companies or job positions

With LinkedIn you can reach out to people in relevant companies or job positions. This way you can learn about both

  • the job and the company
  • how your contact uses her/his skills in the job.
If you are interested in learning about a specific job position: 

Dear Louise

I'm a student at the MSc programme in Geography & Geoinformatics at UCPH. 
I'm interested in working with climate adaptation and urban development, for example, at a place like COWI. Do you have 20 minutes to share a bit about your tasks? Perhaps over the phone or in an online meeting.
Kind regards, Anna

If you are interested in doing a project collaboration:
Dear Martin
I am writing to you because you work at Ørsted, and I am very interested in doing a project collaboration with the department at Ørsted that works with software development.
Can you assist me with the name of the department and/or a person I can contact?
Kind regards, Peter
