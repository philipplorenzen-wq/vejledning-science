Students who are admitted to an SU-qualifying programme also qualify for an Ungdomskort, a student-discount card valid for public transport between your official home address and your faculty.

It is recommended that you apply for your Ungdomskort as soon as possible. However, you may first apply two weekdays after you have applied for SU. If you apply sooner, your application will be rejected and you will need to apply again.

Submit your application on-line at www.ungdomskort.dk, where you can also find more information about the card.

Please note: The fees for an Ungdomskort can be found at www.ungdomskort.dk  

More information is available on DSB’s website www.dsb.dk.

Students may under certain circumstances be eligible to be compensated per kilometre travelled. Learn more at www.ungdomskort.dk.

Please be aware: If you are a non-EU student and have been granted a residence permit as a student, you are not entitled to use an Ungdomskort according to the terms in your residence permit, as it is considered support from an authority.

You can read more at this link.

 If you have any questions, you must contact the Danish authority directly at New to Denmark.
