You must take 30 ECTS per semester during your stay at your host university. This means you must be pre-approved for 30 ECTS per semester, follow courses and take examinations equivalent to 30 ECTS per term.

If you have been granted an Erasmus scholarship during your stay in Europe, but you are pre-approved and take less than 30 ECTS per semester, the amount of the grant will be reduced.

The exchange spots are given according to how many ECTS-points you intend to get pre-approved for and obtain during your stay.

Special situations
If you have a valid reason for not being able to take 30 ECTS per semester during your stay, the above does not apply.


Reasons:

  • Studying part time due to a special or medical condition (E.g. you are receiving SPS or have medical certificate for studying part time)
  • Not having enough ECTS due to mandatory credit transfer
  • Writting BA thesis at the University of Copenhagen with an online supervision (Only applies for students enrolled at the Faculty of Humanities and the Faculty of Theology)
