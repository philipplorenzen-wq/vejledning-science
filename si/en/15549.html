How the Study Board will process your application for a dispensation

Placering: Planning your studies › Rules and dispensations › Processing of your application

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-the-Study-Board-will-process-your-application-for-an-dispensation-SCI-2.aspx

  1. You apply for an exemption
    Fill in the form, attach any attachments and press ‘Send’.
  2. We check if anything is missing
    We will check if you have completed and attached what we need. If anything is missing, we will contact you.
  3. We may send your application for academic assessment
    If relevant, we will send your application for academic assessment to your Head of studies or course coordinator. We do this to obtain an academic assessment, e.g. if you apply for approval of a course as restricted elective or an alternative form of examination.
  4. We prepare your application
    We check whether we can process your application administratively on behalf of the Study Board, or whether we need to send it to the Study Board for consideration at an exemption meeting. If we handle it administratively, you can go directly to step 6.
  5. The Study Board will process your application
    As a rule, we will process your application within 6 weeks when we have received all relevant information. During the summer holidays and Christmas periods, the processing time is slightly longer than normal.
  6. You’ll get a mail
    You will receive an answer on your KUmail.

The entire Study Board will process your application if your application is rejected, and you will be disenrolled from your study programme.
