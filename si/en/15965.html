Firstly, you need to plan your study time to make sure you spend your time how you want to. Because there is rarely time to do everything you want to do and feel you should be doing as a student. 


What you gain from making a good plan with room for both study and leisure: 

  • A better chance of being able to prioritise your time and achieve what is important to you. 
  • Less stressful days and a better overview. 
  • Less of a guilty conscience and wrestling with yourself about how you spend your time. 

Read about the need to prioritise as a student

Different needs for study planning 

Most students benefit from planning their study day. But the optimal degree of planning varies for each individual: 

  • For some, it is reassuring to put everything in a timetable that they regularly update. 
  • Others find it frustrating if the plan is too detailed when things often change. 

So use the methods on this page as inspiration and adjust them to suit your preferences.
