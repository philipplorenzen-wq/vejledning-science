As part of your education, you can do a project in collaboration with a private company, an organization, or a public institution. A project collaboration is a great opportunity to:

  • test your educational expertise in practice
  • build a professional network
  • immerse yourself in a topic or field of work that interests you.

This way, you can learn more about what you can and want to do in your professional life, both in terms of study jobs and after you graduate.
