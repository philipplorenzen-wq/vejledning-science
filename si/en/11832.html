When you cultivate and actively use your network, it can be of great value to you – both during your studies and in your future career.

And now is the time to start, while you still have plenty of time to

  • practice – without too much at stake
  • build a network that you can use when you need to apply for your first job after graduation.

Networking may seem a bit challenging at first – but as you will soon discover, it has become an integrated part of modern work life.
