The study environment representatives take part in the University of Copenhagen’s efforts to improve the study environment for you as a student. They function as a link between students and management and do tasks at several levels. There are study environment representatives attached to each faculty, where they are included in a dialogue forum/student forum with the dean and can contribute to the faculty’s efforts to improve the study environment.
