How do we fill out a group contract?

How you do the contract, depends your study programme(s):

  • If you're from the same study programme, you must do a joint contract which everyone then approves individually via Self Service.
  • If you collaborate with students from other study programmes at SCIENCE, you must each fill out your individual contract and then add the names of the other members under 'Other information'.

2 things, you must consider carefully

  1. The thesis report: Do you wish to compose a collective report or a report where you each make specific contributions? 
  2. The defense: Do you want to do it as a group examination or as an individual examination? 

You must state both decisions under "Other information" in your thesis contract.

Please remember that everything you write in the contract is binding and can only be changed if you are granted a dispensation.
