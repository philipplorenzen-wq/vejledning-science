How do I take elective courses at DTU?

You need to follow this guide to take elective courses at DTU and make it part of your study programme at SCIENCE.


1) Apply for pre-approval at SCIENCE

You must apply in advance for approval of a specific course at another faculty or university. You do so by applying for pre-approval.

A pre-approval from SCIENCE does not ensure that you meet the admission requirements for the course. Check whether you meet the requirements at the faculty or university that offers the course.

How to apply for pre-approval at SCIENCE


2) Apply for a place at DTU

When applying for elective courses at DTU you must apply as a guest student.

You must comply with the deadlines and procedures applicable at DTU.

Read about how to apply at DTU, admission requirements and deadlines

Find courses and course schedules at DTU


3) Have credits transferred or notify about failed courses at DTU

Whether or not you pass the course at DTU, you need to complete and submit a credit transfer application as soon as you have received documentation for the results from the other university.

Your credit transfer application may be key to ensuring that you meet the study activity requirements for receiving your student grant (SU).
