In the week that leads up to each block your personal timetable will be available in the MyUCPH app and on the front page of KUnet.
Your personal timetable is based on the courses you have registered for and on which you have been granted a place.

Your personal timetable shows the place and time of

  • lectures
  • class instruction
  • laboratory exercises, etc.


Find your personal timetable on the myUCPH app

How to find your timetable on the myUCPH app:

  1. Download myUCPH in App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android). 
  2. Sign in using your UCPH username and password.
  3. Choose 'Personal timetable'.

Import your personal timetable to your own calendar

You can import your personal timetable to your calendar on your smartphone, tablet or computer. 

Import your timetable with this guide


Find timetables for the individual courses

The timetable for each individual course is available before your personal timetable is.

You can view the timetable for each course in the course description by clicking on the link on the right-hand side of the page.
