Your role as an MSc Student Ambassador

You will welcome the students to the department and plan interesting and engaging social activities throughout the first study year. 

You will also become the natural link between the first- and second-year master’s degree students, creating a more cohesive and coherent student environment.

The purpose of the student ambassadors is to

  • contribute to a smooth transition into the master’s programme, with particular focus on international students
  • support students in establishing social and academic relationships
  • help foster a positive study culture within the cohort and across nationalities

You will be guided by the Study Environment Consultant for DIKU.
