Your programme includes a “mobility window" in block 3+4 in the first year, which makes it possible to study one semester abroad.

You need to follow some study activities equivalent to compulsory or restricted elective courses, when you are abroad, and you have to get a pre-approval of the courses taken abroad. As it takes time to arrange a study abroad, you must start the planning as soon as possible and often even before you start the education.

  • Consider a stay abroad as early as possible 
  • Look for SPOT on events on studying abroad
  • Consult the study and career guidance counsellors from UCPH Study Guidance
  • Read reports from other students having studied abroad
  • Look for Financing your stay

Once you have passed the pre-approved study activities, you need to apply to the Study Board to have the ECTS credits transferred to your Master's programme.
