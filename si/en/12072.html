You might sometimes feel pressured by your own and other people’s expectations of: 

  • what you should achieve during your studies 
  • how well you should do on your study programme 
  • how well everyone else is doing in their studies.

Unfortunately, a lot of students experience such pressures during their studies. 

Below you will find some perspectives, tips and exercises that can hopefully help you reduce and handle the pressure you feel – either from the outside world or from within.
