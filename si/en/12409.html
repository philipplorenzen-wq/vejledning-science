If you are invited to a job interview, the employer has probably already decided that you are qualified for the job.

The dual purpose of the job interview:

  • The employer must find out whether you and your way of working are a good match for the job and the other workplace staff.
  • You must find out whether the job content, working conditions and the workplace as a whole suit you.
