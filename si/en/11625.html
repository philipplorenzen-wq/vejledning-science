The purpose of the project is to allow students to demonstrate their ability to work independently with an academic topic, which is key to the academic profile of their study programme.

MSc Project is only an option for 

  • master level or 3rd or 4th year bachelor exchange students 
  • non-EU guest students.

The research project may be accepted as an MSc thesis by your home university. Please make sure to confirm with your home university that you can have it transferred so that it can count towards your degree.
