If you have to leave Copenhagen due to semester start at your home university, or other inflexible condition, it might be possible to arrange for you to take the exam at a different time, from a different place, or by doing an alternative form of exam, e.g., a written take home exam, which must be submitted online.

There are no guarantees that it is possible to accommodate your request, since it depends on the availability of the professor in the affected course(s).

Therefore, please start the below process as early as possible, and have alternative courses at hand, if it is not possible to meet your request in your preferred course(s).


Procedure

Contact your professor in the affected course or courses as early as possible, and cc the exam office: eksamen-frbplus@adm.ku.dk.

The arrangement is made between you and the professor, with the aid of the exam office, who oversees the practical aspects of your exam, and of ensuring that the quality of your alternative exam meets the requirements from the university.


Written on-site exams 

Especially for students with a home university in a different time zone, written on-site exams, including ITX exams, are difficult to change. Therefore, generally avoid courses with this type of exam if you know that you will need an alternative exam date, -place or -form.

If you cannot avoid a course with this type of exam, be extra vigilant of contacting the professor early, and be prepared to possibly change your course to an Individual Study project instead of the official course.

If you have questions regarding this option, please write to incomingmobility@adm.ku.dk.
