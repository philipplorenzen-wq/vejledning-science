DICON Brætspil is DIKU's (un)official board game association!

DICON is an association which facilitates board games at DIKU, with events, help for individuals who wish to hold events involving board games. All students at SCIENCE are welcome and we hope to see you at our events!

If you have a good idea for an event, want to help out with our monthly event, or want us to acquire board games we don't have, then write to dicon@di.ku.dk.
