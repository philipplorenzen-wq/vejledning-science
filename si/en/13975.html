Who is entitled to a provider supplement?

If you are a single parent or if you cohabitate with an SU recipient, you are eligible for a provider supplement in addition to your SU grant.
Please note! You cannot receive childbirth portions and supplementary SU loans/provider supplement at the same time.
Read more about childbirth portions, provider supplements and the conditions you must fulfil on www.su.dk.
Application deadlines
When you apply for childbirth portions or provider supplement, you must observe the application deadlines.
See the application deadlines at www.su.dk.
