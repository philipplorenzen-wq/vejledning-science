Gælder for målgrupperne: Landscape Architecture - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Your-options-after-graduating-SCI-LA.aspx

Study-related jobs and internships are widely recognized by the profession, and many new landscape architects get their first job where they worked as students. In the past, private landscape architecture firms and municipal park management were the most important job market, and this is still an important occupation. In recent years, several architectural firms and engineering firms have established landscape departments. Since the municipal reform in 2007, fewer and larger Danish municipalities have the planning authority for both cities and the open countryside. As a result, the municipal job market has expanded considerably. Today, Danish municipalities employ landscape architects for a variety of landscape design and planning tasks at multiple scales in both cities and rural areas. New tasks such as adaptation to climate change and biodiversity make landscape architecture skills an important asset in an increasingly interdisciplinary job market. Governmental agencies and NGOs also offer job opportunities for landscape architects. Private contractors and cemeteries also offer some job opportunities for landscape architects.At the universities PhD programs are the first step towards research and teaching in landscape architecture. If you plan to do a PhD, please consult researchers in your desired subject area during the Master's degree.The Danish Association of Landscape Architects (DL) with around 800 members is an important actor for the profession. JA and Landskabsarkitekters Forening is the specific union for landscape architects from the UCPH in Denmark.
