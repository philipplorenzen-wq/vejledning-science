Unless otherwise stated in your course description, your approval covers the first 4 times the exam is held after you have been approved, e.g.:

  1. Ordinary exam in the current year of study
  2. Re-exam in the current year of study
  3. Ordinary exam in the year after you were approved
  4. Re-exam in the year after you were approved


If you need to take an exam after the first 4 possible exams

If you need to take the exam at a later date because you have been ill, have taken leave of absence, etc., contact the course coordinator and ask if you still meet the prerequisites for taking the exam.
