UCPH Alumni Association is for all students at the University of Copenhagen including all exchange students who have passed a minimum of one exam.

UCPH Alumni Association is a way to stay in touch with fellow students, alumni and the university.

The focus of the association is to

  • create social and professional networks with other alumni
  • participate in career activities and/or events with prominent public figures.

Exchange students need to sign up while still enrolled at the University of Copenhagen.
