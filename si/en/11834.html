Unemployment insurance is a voluntary insurance scheme administrated by the unemployment funds.
You must be a member of a Danish unemployment fund (a-kasse) and pay contributions to the unemployment fund if you want to be covered by unemployment insurance.

Check the different criteria and deadlines for registration before you graduate.
