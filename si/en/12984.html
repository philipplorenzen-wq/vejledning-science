How can my student counsellor help?

Please contact your student counselling in order to receive input on whether your plan will work.

The student counselling will know about the possibilities for help and support, and they know how and when you should apply. They also have extensive knowledge and experience of how other students with functional impairments have planned their semesters, and what worked well for them.
