On the Master's programme in Biochemistry, your Study Board recommends that you study abroad in

  • year 1, blocks 3 and 4 in your recommended course of study.

This period is your 'mobility window' and may contain both elective, compulsory and restricted elective courses.

See the courses offered during  the mobility window on your study programme by selecting your year of enrolment under Planning your studies
