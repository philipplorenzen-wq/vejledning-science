You can apply throughout the year

How to apply for funding:

  • Make a budget for your application 
  • Fill out the online application form and upload the budget

 Fill out the application form 

The Department strives to respond to your application within 2 weeks via email. There may be a longer response time.

If you have any questions about your application or the procedure, you can send them to the Department at trivsel@math.ku.dk

Requirement for the budget

The budget can be very simple, but must show the administration exactly what expenses the event entails – also the elements you are not applying funds for.
