Here you have the opportunity to get really close to your education - and to have an influence on your study environment and your studies. The Biology Association has representatives in the department's teaching committees, where you collaborate with 
  • teachers 
  • Deputy Heads of Department of Education 
  • other members from other studies at the department. 

You will also have the opportunity to attend the management meetings at the department (app. 4 times a year) together with the 
  • head of department deputy 
  • heads of research and teaching 
  • the head of administration 
  • the team leader for PR and Communication 
  • the study start coordinator 
  • other council members from other studies at the department. 

If interested, contact the teaching committee at the department to hear how you can become an active part of the Biology Association.
