How do I pass a compulsory course after the last exam option?

Placering: Courses and teaching › Course changes › Discontinued courses

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-pass-a-compulsory-course-after-the-last-exam-option-SCI.aspx

When a last exam option has been offered, you must register for the course or assignment stated in the interim arrangement for the discontinued course in order to pass it.

Find the list of discontinued courses forming part of your study programme under Programme structure.


Under the interim arrangement, your course may be replaced by

  • an identical course
  • a replacement course (equivalent course)
  • a written assignment.


If your course is replaced by an identical course with the same exam form, you can register for the exam via Self Service. You still need to meet any relevant examination prerequisites.
Read more about when you may register directly for the exam
