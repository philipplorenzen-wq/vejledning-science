What are the requirements for my exam?

You decide the structure of your exam in an Individual Study Project together with your supervisor.

Your exam can be

  • oral
  • written
  • a written assignment followed by an oral exam.

The exam may also be in the form of an article or public presentation. You may be eager to sharpen your presentation skills or your ability to clearly and concisely summarize results.

Work with your supervisor to develop an examination form that suits your needs.

Requirements for a report 

If you do a report, you can arrange the specific details about e.g. form, content, length and focus for what you submit and/or present orally with your supervisor.
