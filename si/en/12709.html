Can my registered address be with my parents during my stay abroad?

You need to talk to the Danish Folkeregister on what to do with your address while abroad.
If you move your address abroad, please remember to state if you live with your parents or not while being abroad.
This can be done at minSU choosing Ret din SU > Oplys særlige bopælsforhold.
Read more about the conditions for SU while studying abroad on www.su.dk.
