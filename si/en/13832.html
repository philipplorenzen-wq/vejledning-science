You must be admitted to a programme and have accepted the place before you can apply for SU.

On your master's degree programme, you can receive SU from July if you complete your bachelor's degree programme at the summer exams in June (including any re-exams in August).

You must apply for SU between 1 and 15 June if you want your SU for July to be paid on the last banking day in June.  You can apply for SU for July up until 31 July.

Apply via 'minSU' at www.su.dk.
