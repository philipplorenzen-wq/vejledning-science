The MSc programme in Agriculture with specialisation in Production and Environment consists of four elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 22.5 ECTS credits
  2. Restricted elective courses, 
    • 45 ECTS credits (thesis 30 ECTS)
    • 30 ECTS credits (thesis 45 ECTS)

3. Elective subjects, 22.5 ECTS credits

4. Thesis, 30 or 45 ECTS credits
