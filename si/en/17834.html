Your credit transfers affect some of your study activity requirements and your SU.

This is how your credit transfers affect your study activity and SU:

  • The maximum duration of study which you must meet does not change as a result of any credit transfer you apply for yourself. If you have gotten 30 ECTS credits or more transferred from previous studies, it might affect your maximum duration of study.
    Contact UCPH Study Guidance if you are in doubt about your maximum duration of study
  • Your SU may be affected by all credits tranferred from previous studies if the credit transfers mean that your completion time can be shortened by 6 months or more.
    Read more about your monthly SU grants portions
