What is the maximum duration of study for my study programme?

Gælder for målgrupperne: Food Science and Technology - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-is-the-maximum-duration-of-study-for-my-study-programme-SCI-FST.aspx

You have a maximum of 3 years (36 months) to complete your Master's programme.

  • The prescribed duration of study (2 years)
  • + 1 year

If your study programme include an internship, your study programme will be extended by 6 months. This means that you have a maximum of 3.5 years (42 months) to complete your Master's programme.
