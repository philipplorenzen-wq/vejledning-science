How and when do I apply for pre-approval?

Placering: Study abroad › Plan and apply › Pre-approval of courses

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-apply-for-pre-approval-SCI2.aspx

You must apply for pre‑approval as soon as you have been nominated for an exchange place. This applies even if you do not yet know for certain which courses will be offered.
Once you have arrived at the host university and know exactly which courses you will be taking, you must apply to change your pre‑approval if you end up taking other courses than those originally approved.
If you have not applied for an exchange place but are organising your stay abroad entirely on your own, you must apply for pre‑approval no later than six weeks before you need it.
