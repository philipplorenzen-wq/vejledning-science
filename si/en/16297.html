The deadline for applying for pre-approval depends on where you want to take courses.


Courses and projects at other UCPH faculties

Autumn (blocks 1 and 2)Deadline 1 June
Spring (blocks 3, 4 and 5)Deadline 1 December

After the deadline you can apply for a dispensation to be allowed to apply for pre-approval after the deadline. Follow the normal procedure, but enter the following in the comments box in the form: “I wish to apply for a dispensation for applying for pre-approval after the deadline." 


Courses and projects at other universities

Apply at the latest 6 weeks before you need the pre-approval. You will need a pre-approval to be admitted to the other university. Check the deadline for submitting your pre-approval to the other university.


How to apply for pre-approval

You apply by

  1. filling in the relevant online form
  2. uploading the course description (stating ECTS credits)
  3. uploading documentation for a different course weighting system to ECTS, if applicable.

Reply to your application

As a general rule, you will receive an answer within 6 weeks from when the Study Board has received all relevant information about your application. If you need to send supplementary material, you will be contacted via your KUmail.
The Study Board will process your application as soon as possible.
