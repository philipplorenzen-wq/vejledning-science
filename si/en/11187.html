The University of Copenhagen has a number of scholarships, which can be applied for only by students at UCPH.

There are two different types of scholarships: The joint scholarship and other scholarships.

The joint scholarship consists of several small scholarships but you apply for it as one scholarship.

Other scholarships you can apply for individually.
