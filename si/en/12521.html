One week before your oral exam at the latest, you will receive a KUmail with a link to Digital Exam where you will find:

  • The date and time of your exam
  • A link to the digital exam room in Zoom or Teams

When you follow the Zoom or Teams link, you will enter an online waiting room where you can test your sound and video. Your teacher/examiner will give you access to the exam room when it is your turn.

Read the guidelines for online oral exams

Read more about how your exam will take place on Zoom and Teams, and get help to install and test the two programs.

  • Guide for exams, installation and test in Zoom
  • Guide for exams, installation and test in Teams

Questions

If you have any questions about the content and form of your exam, please contact your teacher.

The guidelines explain how an online oral exam takes place in practice. However, screenshots and icons in the guides may vary from that of the actual platform.
