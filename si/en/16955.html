Can I apply for pre-approval of multiple courses?

Placering: Courses and teaching › Registration and withdrawal › Pre-approval

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-apply-for-pre-approval-of-multiple-courses-SCI.aspx

You may apply for pre-approval of multiple courses at the same time using the same form. How many you can apply for depends on whether the courses are in Denmark or abroad.


You must have enough ECTS credits to do the courses you are applying for in Denmark

You must have enough ECTS credits to do all the courses in Denmark for which you apply for pre-approval. The reason is that all your pre-approved courses will take up credits in your study programme.


You may apply for more courses if going abroad

You can apply for pre-approval of more courses than you need and have room for in connection with studies abroad.

You can apply for more courses to have a plan B in case of changes to the range of courses offered by the university you are going to.


Annul pre-approvals that you do not use

Make sure to annul all pre-approvals for courses you are not going to take. Otherwise, your pre-approved course will take up ECTS credits in your study programme and prevent you from using them for other courses or projects.
