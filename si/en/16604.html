A self-arranged stay is for students who would like to study at a university with which UCPH does not have an agreement.

Please note that a self-arranged stay requires more preparation than applying for an exchange place through UCPH.

A self-arranged stay may be an option for students who

  • want to decide to a greater extent what their stay will look like – within the framework that their curriculum allows
  • failed to apply for an exchange place within the deadlines for applications.
