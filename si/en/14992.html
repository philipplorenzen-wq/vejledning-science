DIKU Dialogue Forum is a meeting forum between students and the management at DIKU, where we hold four annual meetings.

At the meetings, we discuss the initiatives and challenges at the department, and we try to figure out how we can best support each other in creating an inclusive and open department.

It is an opportunity for you as a student to gain real influence at the department and insight into current and upcoming decisions made by the department.

In addition, we also have student representatives serving on DIKU's Activities & Added Value Fund (the DAMP committee), which evaluates students' applications for financial support.

Would you like to be part of the DIKU Dialogue Forum?

All enrolled students at the Department of Computer Science can participate, with up to eight students serving at a time. In addition, we also have two alternates.

If you are interested in attending the meetings or would like to learn more about the DIKU Dialogue Forum, please write to managementsupport@di.ku.dk.
