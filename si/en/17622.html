A summer course is for students who would like to replace some of their courses with courses at a university abroad during the summer.

There may be several reasons for taking a summer course.

  • You might have an elective course that you would like to take during the summer to have a smaller workload during the ordinary semester.
  • Or maybe you are dreaming of studying abroad, but you are unable to go for a whole semester.

Regardless of the reason, you can either apply for a summer course through UCPH's agreements or plan it yourself.
