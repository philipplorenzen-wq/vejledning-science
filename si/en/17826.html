Plan your holiday during the holiday period if you want to be absolutely sure that your holiday doesn't clash with important study activities. If you are due to retake an exam after the holidays, you may have to study for the exam during the holidays.


Holiday during scheduled study breaks

If you are planning to go on holiday during a scheduled study break, this may mean that you cannot register for and retake exams in the relevant block.

It is your responsibility to plan your holidays so they don't interfere with any teaching activities, exams or re-exams.
