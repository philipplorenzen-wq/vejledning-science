myUCPH is the official app for students at the University of Copenhagen. Here you can find important information that is good to have at hand during your studies.

The app includes:

  • Your personal timetable
  • Messages from Absalon
  • Study messages from KUnet
  • News from KUnet
  • Information about the libraries

Log in by using your UCPH username (3 letters + 3 numbers) and your password.
