If you lose something on campus, there are different places you can enquire locally.

There is no single place where all lost property at SCIENCE is collected.

Lost property at Frederiksberg Campus

Contact either

  • SCIENCE Campus Services Help Desk
    Tel.: +45 35 33 13 33
    E-mail: FrbPlus-Helpdesk@adm.ku.dk
  • Operations service at Bülowsvej 21

Lost property at the rest of campus

Contact either

  • The reception at the nearest department
    Find contact information on the different departments
  • The canteen where you have lost something
    Find the different canteens at SCIENCE
