Your teacher invites you to remote teaching in Zoom by either sharing a link or a meeting ID. In order to participate, you must install the Zoom app on your computer. 

Download the Zoom app

  1. Go to https://ucph-ku.zoom.us/download
  2. Download and install Zoom Client for Meetings
  3. Follow the installation steps on your computer

Log in on the Zoom app

  1. Open the Zoom app on your computer
  2. Select log in with SSO login. Enter ucph-ku > press continue
  3. You will now enter UCPH's login page, where you must log in using your UCPH username, e.g. abc123, and your UCPH password. 
  4. To particpate in online teaching in the future, you can follow the link provided by your teacher after which the app will open automatically. If you have been provided with a Meeting ID, you must open the app manually, press join, enter the Meeting ID and press join again.
