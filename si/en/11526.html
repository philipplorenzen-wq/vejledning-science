If you have signed a contract with the Housing Foundation Copenhagen, please visit their website, when your studies at SCIENCE are coming to an end.

On the website you will find all of the practical information you need before moving out, e.g.,

  • when you have to be out of your room/apartment
  • where to hand in your keys
  • a cleaning checklist
  • how to get your deposit back.

For further information please contact the Housing Foundation Copenhagen directly.
