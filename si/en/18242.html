When you have completed your Erasmus exchange you must evaluate your Erasmus grant.

The evaluation of your Erasmus grant consists of:

  • An online evaluation to UCPH
  • An online evaluation to the EU (by link sent to your KUmail)
  • Upload of Transcript of Records and Proof of Attendance

Documentation for the actual stay at the host university:

UCPH is only allowed to give you an Erasmus grant for the period of time where you are physically present at the host university and have obligations at the host university (meaning: intro weeks, lecture period, exams). If the actual dates are not stated on your Transcript of Records from your host university you will also need a Proof of Attendance (or a similar document).

The Proof of Attendance should contain: 

  • The actual start and end date of your physical exchange (NOT enrollment period)
  • Your full name and the name of the host university
  • Signature from the host university. Signature must be dated

Signature: Signatures must be made by hand on paper or via verified digital signature (e.g. Adobe or host institution's own verified system). Signatures copied/pasted into the document cannot be accepted.

Signature date: The date of the signature must be maximum 5 days BEFORE the end date, e.g. end date is 5 January then the signature must be made earliest on the 1 January.

Download template to Proof of Attendance

You can upload a Proo of Attendance/Certificate of Attendance from your host university, if it holds the information described above.
