If you wish to use your elective options to obtain a specific academic profile, I have made these recommended study tracks for you. The study tracks are written from the point of view of the course offerings in 2025-26 only, meaning that the courses listed below may not be offered in 2026-27.
When you plan your studies, make sure you comply with the number of ECTS credits, which constitutes your programme structure.
