My name is Lars Hemmingsen and I am your Head of Studies. As such, I know the academic contents of your programme in detail and have contributed in determining your curriculum. My research areas are physical chemistry and biological chemistry.

The study program is quite flexible, and you are free to define your profile as a student.

Here are my advice and recommendations for the successful completion of your master's programme in Nanoscience. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are written on the basis of the latest programme structure unless I have specifically stated otherwise.
