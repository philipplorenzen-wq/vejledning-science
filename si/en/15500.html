  • Students are coming from all over the globe. Most come from Denmark, a lot from Europe, but also students from Asia, America and Africa are here every year. This gives an international atmosphere and a unique opportunity to create international networks. Take your time to get friends forever.
  • Your study programme is a full time workload. There will be time for a student job, but only part time and it is best if the working hours are flexible.
  • Help us by evaluating your courses. You will get a reminder by e-mail in due time.
  • Use your UCPH-mail account (frequently). All e-mails from the university will be sent to your official UCPH-mail. If you don't read this mail frequently you may miss important information.
  • Read your study messages and study events not to miss important messages and events.
  • You can find almost all information on KUnet. Make sure to read the Curricula and rules, and read other official rules documents here.
