Special educational support can be anything from physical aids to personal support. However, you cannot receive financial support or extra academic support for a subject you find difficult.

To obtain SPS, you must have a diagnosed functional impairment.

You can apply for SPS throughout the duration of your studies. However, it is a good idea to apply as soon as possible, as it may take a couple of months before the support is fully in place.  

Read more about SPS at The Ministry of Children and Education's website (note that the site is in Danish)
