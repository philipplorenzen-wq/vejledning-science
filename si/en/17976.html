Can I apply for Erasmus Traineeship as recent graduate?

You can apply for Erasmus Traineeship as Recent Graduate and get an Erasmus Traineeship grant from UCPH, even though you have graduated from UCPH. You can apply, if you  graduate with a Bachelor's Degree or a Master's Degree.

The rules, application process and application deadlines are the same as for Erasmus Traineeship. The only rule not applying is that Erasmus Traineeship as Recent Graduate isn't credit bearing (meaning: you do not earn ECTS as recent graduate).

 Specific rules for applying for Erasmus Traineehip as Recent Graduate:

  • You must apply minimum one month before you graduate from UCPH, meaning you must apply one month before you hand in or defend your Bachelor assignment / Master's Thesis at UCPH
  • Academic approval: you should not upload an academic approval. Instead you must upload your Thesis contract or proof that you have singed up for the Bachelor assignment. We need to see proof of the date, you will hand in / defend you final project/thesis UCPH
  • You must apply and evaluate via UCPH's application portal (Mobility-Online). This means that you must check your Kumail on a regular basis also after your graduation and that you must log in to the application portal by the end of your Erasmus Traineeship to evaluate the Erasmus grant
  • Duration of the Erasmus Traineeship: your Erasmus Traineeship must end no later than 12 month after you graduate from UCPH. Example: If you hand in/defend your final assignement on 1 August 2023 your Erasmus Traineeship must end no later than 31 July 2024
  • You can participate in Erasmus mobility (studies + traineeship) for max 12 months per Bachelor- and Master, this also includes Erasmus Traineeship as Recent Graduate. For example: if you have been on Erasmus study exchange for 6 months during your Master's Degree, you can participate in Erasmus Traineeship as Recent Graduate for max 6 months.
