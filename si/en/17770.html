Can I register for a course I've previously been registered for at LAW?

You are not able to register for a course at LAW if you have been registered before. 

You cannot

  • register for the course again via Self Service
  • attend classes if you are not formally registered for the course.

If you were sick for a substantial part of the term in which you were originally registered for the course, or you were unable to participate in classes for other reasons beyond your control, you can apply for dispensation to re-register for the course. In such cases, please contact at guidance cousellor at LAW Student Services.
