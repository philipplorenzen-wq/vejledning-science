Why should I attend career fairs?

Placering: Your career › Explore the job market during your studies › Career fairs

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Why-should-I-attend-career-fairs-SCI.aspx

At career fairs you can gain relevant knowledge that may help you
  • learn more about your own options and wishes for your work life
  • target the choices you make during your programme towards the possible career paths that you are interested in.

Use the fairs to e.g. 
  • find project opportunities 
  • find a student job 
  • find a full-time job 
  • acquire information about what employers demand from their employees. 
The knowledge that you acquire at the fairs can help you target your choices during your studies towards the career opportunities that interest you.
