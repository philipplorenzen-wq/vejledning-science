Dairy Forum - University of Copenhagen 

Dairy Forum is a student club for students at The University of Copenhagen that share an interest in dairy! We arrange events with social and professional purposes, along relevant discussions regarding the collaboration between the University and the industry. Use the group for sharing info on interesting events.  

Keep up with the Dairy Forum on:  

  • Facebook 
  • Instagram 

Dairy Forum is part of FEF. 

Beverage Forum - University of Copenhagen 

Beverage Forum is a student organisation at The University of Copenhagen. The group is created to explore beverages through social events. Previous events have included: 

  • Visit to breweries such as Mikkeller Baghaven and Warpigs 
  • Visiting Decideret Cider 
  • Kombucha Workshop 
  • Visit at coffee roastery 
  • Wine tasting 
  • Brewing beer using the FOOD pilot plant. 

Keep up with Beverage Forum on Facebook 

Beverage Forum is part of FEF.
