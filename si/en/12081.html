As a student, you will be faced with having to make a number of choices and decisions – some easier than others. Which decisions are most challenging varies from person to person. 

If you are struggling to choose – whether it be a course, a master’s programme or a career path: 

  • you are sure to learn something from your decision 
  • there are a number of things you can consider to help you make your decision.
