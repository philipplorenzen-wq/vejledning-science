Conservative Students (KS) is a centre-right student political association. We believe that the individual student's well-being should be in focus, which is why we work to ensure that all students have the freedom to organise and plan their studies in a way that suits them. We believe that freedom of research and thought must be given high priority and that the Chicago principles should be included in the University Act.

Conservative Students represent student interests in study boards, academic councils and in various dialogue forums across the University's faculties. We also organise a number of social and academic events every semester.
