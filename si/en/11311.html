UCPH has a set of evacuation procedures that aim to ensure that as a student you can vacate the building quickly and in a coordinated way in the case of a critical event at UCPH.

Employees will be in charge of an evacuation, but with knowledge of the evacuation procedures you can contribute to a fast and efficient evacuation.
