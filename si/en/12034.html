The MSc programme in Biotechnology consists of four elements:

  • Compulsory courses, 7.5 ECTS credits
  • Restricted elective courses, 37.5 ECTS credits
  • Elective courses, 15 ECTS credits
  • Thesis, 60 ECTS credits

The programme is two years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Biotechnology / Cand. scient. i bioteknologi
