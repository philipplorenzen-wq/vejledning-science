All students are welcome at Lighthouse. With interdisciplinarity being a key feature, you will meet students from many different backgrounds.

You can become part of UCPH Lighthouse if you or your team are interested in innovation and perhaps want to start your own business, develop a good idea or do a project.

You can book an intro meeting for yourself, your team or a group of students. You can also join a team that is already working on an idea.

Students from other educational institutions are welcome as long as they are in a team with at least one UCPH student.
