Do credit transfers affect my maximum duration of study?

If you have gotten at least 30 ECTS credits transferred from previous studies, it might affect your maximum duration of study.

If you have previously studied at the same study programme, it affects your maximum duration of study. You maximum duration of study will be calculated collectively for all enrolments at the same study programme.

Contact UCPH Study Guidance if you are in doubt about your maximum duration of study


Your credit transfers may have an impact on your monthly SU grant portions

If your completion time can be reduced by 6 months or more as a result of credit transfers, the SU Office will reduce your monthly grant portions.
