Across the educational institutions at Kalundborg, you will find activities arranged by the educational institutions and the municipality of Kalundborg aimed at students. Activities will be held in Kalundborg on an ongoing basis, e.g. bike rides or other social activities for students.

Student Café Kalundborg

Banegårdspladsen 2
4400 Kalundborg

Every Thursday from 16.00 to 21.00 you can visit the local communal Student Café. The café is the perfect place to hang out, meet fellow students or join the different events. The Student Café hosts a variety of events like quiz nights, karaoke nights, and board games among others.

You will find the cafe in a cosy environment at the community house SYMB near the central station in Kalundborg.

Friday bar at University College Absalon

The Friday bar's opening hours are Thursday and Friday from 14.00 to 23.30, where alcoholic beverages may be purchased. The Friday bar is in the canteen at University College Absalon.
