Your submission of an assignment is not final until the deadline for submission for your exam. If you regret submitting before the deadline, you may edit your paper or completely remove it to upload a new version.


Editing your paper:

  • Go to the 'My Exams' menu and find the relevant exam
  • Select 'Edit hand-in' or 'Delete hand-in', depending on your chosen method of editing
  • Upload a new paper if this is what you want to do.

You cannot edit your Master's thesis report

You cannot edit or submit a new version of your Master's thesis report once you have submitted it the first time.
