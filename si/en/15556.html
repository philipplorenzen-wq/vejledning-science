If you follow the recommended course of study of your programme, you must register for the compulsory and restricted elective courses of the relevant blocks.

See what courses to take in your programme's structure

You also need to register for any elective courses you decide to take at SCIENCE.


Courses at other faculties or universities

You must also register via Self Service for any compulsory or restricted elective courses which you take at another faculty or university that are part of your study programme.

You will find your course on a designated list of courses for the relevant faculty or university.


Alternative study plan

If you follow your own study plan, you are responsible for ensuring that the courses you register for are approved under your curriculum and can be taken in the order and at the times of your choice.
