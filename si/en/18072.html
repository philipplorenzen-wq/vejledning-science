You must submit your assignment via Digital Exam.


Submitting via Digital Exam:

  1. Log into Digital Exam using your UCPH login
  2. Select the relevant exam under 'My exams'
  3. Press 'Next' in the right-hand corner to fill in the required information and upload your paper.
     

Go to Digital Exam


If you want to submit a blank paper

In the bottom left-hand corner in your exam tab, select 'Submit a blank exam' if you do not want to submit a completed paper.
If you submit a blank paper, you use 1 exam attempt and your grade will be registered as 'EB' (meaning 'ej bedømt'/'not graded'). You have formally participated in the exam.

Receipt for your submission

You get an automatic receipt after submitting your assignment via Digital Exam.
You can always view your submission receipt and a copy of your submitted exam paper in the 'My Exams' menu item.
