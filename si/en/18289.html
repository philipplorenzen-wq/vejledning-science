Talk regularly with your supervisor about academic, practical and more personal challenges related to your project. For the most part, you will be able to find out how to proceed together.


A study and career guidance counsellor from UCPH Study Guidance can provide help with

  • getting an overview of your Master's thesis and your study plan
  • what to do in the event of illness or other delays to your thesis
  • how to handle problems with your supervisor, group, etc.
  • what is important if you need to apply for a dispensation.

Keep an eye on the thesis preparation study events which are organised by UCPH Study Guidance


Your doctor may (if you fall ill during your thesis) be able to help you with

  • how much studying you can do, given the state of your health
  • how long you should be absent due to illness
  • a medical certificate – which you will need as documentation if you are applying for a dispensation.
