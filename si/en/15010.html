No, you are not allowed to record or stream online teaching or share recorded teaching from a course room in Absalon without the consent of the teacher and your fellow students. This applies to online teaching, in-person teaching, and supervision sessions.

What happens if I record or stream teaching without consent?

If you record, stream, or share without consent, it is a violation of the university’s rules of conduct and a violation of the teacher’s copyright. Violations of the rules are handled by the faculties and by UCPH Education in the Central Administration.

The sanctions range from a warning to half a year suspension. Repeated violations may result in permanent expulsion from UCPH.
