Why should I update my devices.?

Placering: IT and facilities on campus › IT and support › IT-security

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/update-devices.aspx

You should regularly update all your devices e.g. smartphone, computer and tablet.
When you update , the latest security updates will be installed. This means that your devices are better protected against e.g. hackers.
If you look up the manufacturer of your device on the internet, you will typically be able to find instructions on how to update your device.
You should also, remember to update the apps or programs you have installed.
