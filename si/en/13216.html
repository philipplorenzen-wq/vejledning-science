If you are an international student and need to change your placeholder civil registration number (pseudo CPR number) to your official one, please fill out the form below and include documentation of your new civil registration number.


You can also use the form if you have gotten a new civil registration number in connection with legal gender reassignment. You can see what documentation is needed when logging into the form.
