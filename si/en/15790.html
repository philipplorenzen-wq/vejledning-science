The MSc programme in Biotechnology with a minor subject in Biology consist of the following:

  1. Compulsory courses (major subject), 7.5 ECTS credits
  2. Compulsory courses (minor subject), 22.5 ECTS credits
  3. Restricted elective courses (major subject), 30 ECTS credits
  4. Elective subjects, 30 ECTS credits
  5. Thesis, 30 ECTS credits
