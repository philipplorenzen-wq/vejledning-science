Two ways in which you can improve your language competences before a stay abroad:
  • Participate in a preparatory workshop
    The Centre for Internationalisation and Parallel Language Use (CIP) at UCPH offers preparatory workshops on communication and language targeted at students going on a stay abroad.
    Read more on CIP's website
  • Take a language course at your host university
    Some foreign universities offer language courses to their coming exchange students before the semester start. Find information about this on the host university's website or by contacting them.
