If you would like to try testing an exam meeting, you may choose to create your own Zoom or Teams meeting with a co-student. This way, you can test the sound, microphone and possible screen sharing.

If you have questions or problems before the exam concerning:

1. Access to the platform

  • Contact Support on 3532 3232 from 7:30 to 16:00 every weekday.
  • State which platform you are trying to access (Zoom or MS Teams) and describe the issue in as much detail as possible, including any error messages and what you did just before the error occurred.

2. Platform settings or general IT functionality of the platform:

  • Contact Support on 3532 3232 from 7:30 to 16:00 every weekday.
  • State which platform you need information about and what you want to do.

3. The actual exam, such as your registration, scheduled time of the exam, whether it is a group exam:

  • Contact the exam administration on your course.
