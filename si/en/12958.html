How do I get in touch with the job market?

As a student with an impairment, it can be difficult to find the time or energy to have a job or do volunteer work. Therefore, it may be a good idea to make use of the opportunities that are built into the degree programme. In that way, it is not an extra task, but rather something you do as part of your studies.

Contact with the job market built into the programme:

  • Academic internship
  • Bachelor project or Master's thesis in collaboration with a company
  • Project work in collaboration with companies
  • Courses with integrated cases or contact with companies
  • Other options such as clinical training, fieldwork etc.

Contact with the job market outside the programme:

  • Student jobs. See for example KU Project & Jobs
  • Volunteer work
  • Career activities in the programme, with the union or elsewhere
