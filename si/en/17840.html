How much extra time do we get for an oral group exam?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-much-extra-time-do-we-get-for-an-oral-group-exam-SCI.aspx

All examinees are entitled to at least 12 minutes of exam time (excluding grading).

As a group, you will therefore be given a minimum of 12 additional minutes per group member. How long the exam will last in addition to this will be decided by the internal and external examiners based on how the exam is proceeding.
