You can choose between five different types of stay abroad:

  • Exchange stay
  • Self-arranged stay
  • Academic internship
  • Summer courses
  • Traineeship after completion of study programme.

The type of stay that is best suited to you and your specific study programme will depend on your curriculum, your preferences and the desired outcome.
