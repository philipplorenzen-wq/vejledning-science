At UCPH, we prevent and handle transgressive behaviour. Together we are responsible for looking out for each other and the study environment.

You are not alone.

Your student counsellors and the University's diversity and inclusion consultants are here to listen, help and guide you if you experience harassment, discrimination, violations, bullying or other forms of offensive behavior in your student life. Regardless of whether you feel targeted, have witnessed something you found unacceptable or have unintentionally overstepped someone else's boundaries. We take all enquiries seriously and deal with all cases as quickly as possible.
