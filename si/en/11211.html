The MSc programme in Agricultural Economics consists of four elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 15 ECTS credits
  2. Restricted elective courses, 60 ECTS credits
  3. Elective subjects, 15 ECTS credits
  4. Thesis, 30 ECTS credits

The programme is two years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Agricultural Economics / Cand.oecon.agro. i jordbrugsøkonomi
