Exams in courses are only offered twice a year in fixed exam periods.

It's up to you to schedule your exam/re-exam in either the

  • ordinary exam period connected to the course
  • re-exam period connected to the course.


Different exam form in re-exam periods

The exam form may be different for exams in re-exam periods than for exams in the ordinary exam periods. See if this applies to your course in the course description under 'Exams'.
