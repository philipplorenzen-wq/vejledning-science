When do I not meet the maximum duration of study?

You exceed the maximum duration of study if you have

  • been enrolled as an active student for your maximum duration of study
  • and have not passed all 120 ECTS credits on your study programme.

You must not have courses, projects or exams that exceed your maximum duration of study – not even if you have not used your 3 exam attempts.


If you are about to exceed the maximum duration of study

Approximately 1 year before you exceed your maximum period of study, you will receive a reminder via your KUmail that you have 1 year to complete your study programme.
Shortly before the deadline, you will be notified via your KUmail that you will be disenrolled if you do not apply for a dispensation.
