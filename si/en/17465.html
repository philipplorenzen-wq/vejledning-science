What do I do with exams, courses and projects during my leave of absence?

Placering: Planning your studies › Changes to your course of study › Leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-do-I-do-with-exams,-courses-and-projects-during-my-leave-of-absence-SCI.aspx

You must not be studying actively while on leave of absence. During your leave of absence, you must not 

  • take courses
  • take exams
  • receive project supervision.

However, you may register for and take exams in the last week of re-exams before resuming your studies.
Remember that while on leave, you must register for courses, projects and any exams that you intend to take after your leave of absence.

You are withdrawn from courses and exams during leave of absence

When your leave of absence has been approved, you will be withdrawn from all courses and exams during your period of leave.
When you return to your studies, you must still pass courses which you have registered for and where the deadline for withdrawal has passed.
