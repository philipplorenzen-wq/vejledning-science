At the Student Counselling Service, you can get free help to thrive in your student life. You can get help with anything from psychological challenges to more practical questions about parental leave or illness.

Psychological challenges may include symptoms of stress or anxiety, perfectionism or feeling worried, concentration difficulty, family issues or loneliness.  

The Student Counselling Service offers for example:

  • Online therapy
  • Workshops
  • Help for study groups or thesis partners who want the best possible collaboration

All offers from the Student Counselling Service are free of charge.

Contact the Student Counselling Service


Please bear in mind that you must be enrolled and currently studying on an SU approved higher education programme to get help from the Student Counselling Service.
