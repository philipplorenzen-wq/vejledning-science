You will receive a KUmail with a link to the evaluation form and a deadline when it is time to evaluate

  • teaching and lecturers
  • exams.

You can also find the evaluation form in Absalon under the individual course.

It takes about 5 minutes per evaluation, and your response is anonymous.


Lecturers follow up on evaluations

The lecturer is responsible for oral follow-up on the results of the teaching and lecturer evaluation at the end of each course.
