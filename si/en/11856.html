I'm David Skovmand and I'm your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies I know the academic contents of your programme in detail, and have contributed in determining your curriculum. My research area is Financial Mathematics and I teach on the courses Term Structure Models and Finansiering 1.

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

You are always free to contact me about choosing your electives and designing your curriculum.

If you have question about specific rules, credit transfer and legal matters I will refer you to UCPH Study Guidance.
