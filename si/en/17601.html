If you do not want to do a group thesis, it is not an advantage for you to do a group thesis preparation project.

A group project is, among other things, a good option for those who

  • know that they want to write a thesis in the same group and therefore want to prepare together
  • feel energised and benefit academically from sparring with others
  • enjoy working with people who possess other strengths and perspectives than themselves
  • would like to improve and challenge their cooperation skills.

Preparing for working life

Working in a group can help prepare you for your future working life. In both companies and research groups, projects usually involve cooperation between different persons and professional/academic skills.
