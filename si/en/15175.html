How will the University act on the survey results?

Diversity, equity and inclusion are strategic prerequisites in the University's strategy. A new strategic framework sets the direction for the work in this area, which also leads to new initiatives – these initiatives are inspired and qualified by the survey results.
Faculties, departments, campus administrations and group units will now have their own diversity milestones and initiatives. Cross-university initiatives are handled by the University's diversity team in collaboration with relevant units, committees and coordinators at the faculties and in the university administration.
In the fall of 2025, a new online toolbox will provide further inspiration and suggestions for the work and is particularly relevant to managers, staff and students involved in diversity, equity and inclusion efforts. New tools will be added regularly, including good examples from the work internally at UCPH.
