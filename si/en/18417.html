You are not allowed to receive a salary for any work you do for the company as part of your project. 

Some companies offer gifts or contributions toward expenses in connection to your stay in the company. You are allowed to receive these within certain limits.

Gifts and contributions you are allowed to accept from the company:

  • Gifts of up to DKK 3,000 kroner a month – as long as it is not a kind of 'income' which has been agreed in advance.

  • Contributions toward any expenses you have in connection with your time in the company. This could be travel expenses, rent or other expenses you might have if, for example, your company is far from your home. You must be able to document that the expenses are connected directly to your stay in the company.
