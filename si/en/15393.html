What benefits or services am I entitled to when I am covered by Team Danmark?

Placering: Planning your studies › Changes to your course of study › Elitesport

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-benefits-or-services-am-I-entitled-to-when-I-am-covered-by-Team-Danmark-KU.aspx

  • Guidance that focuses on your sport
  • Substitute teaching if you are absent due to your sport
  • Option of a study buddy (a fellow student who can help you take notes if you are absent due to your sport). Send an email to UCPH Professionals for more information
  • Opportunity for selected athletes to get financial support from Team Denmark
  • Special exam arrangements
  • Read more on Team Denmark's website or contact their career and education consultants.
