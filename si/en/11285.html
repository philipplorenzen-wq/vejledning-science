I am Anders Svensson and Head of Studies for the MSc Climate Change programme. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic content of your programme in detail, and have contributed in determining your curriculum. My research area is within ice core research and I teach the restricted elective course Climate Change Mechanisms and Tipping Points.

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here are my suggestion and recommendations for completing your master's programme in Climate Change successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.
