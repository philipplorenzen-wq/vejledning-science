  1. Please contact the SPS unit at sps@adm.ku.dk to inform that you are going to study abroad. We will inform you of any special conditions that you should be aware of in relation to your SPS.
  2. Request aconfirmation of your approval for SPS in English from the SPS unit. Then, you can show the confirmation to your host university, if necessary.
  3. Contact your host university and inform them about your functional impairment and need for support. Ask them what support offers they have for you as a future student, what you should be aware of, what dispensations to apply for and what kind of documentation they need. In general, you should try to gather as much information as possible about how your host university can help you best. 
PLEASE NOTE: Contact the SPS unit as soon as possible if any changes occur in your need for support as you are going to study abroad. Then we can apply for new support from the Danish Agency for Education and Quality.


If you order or buy aids outside the SPS scheme, meaning without approval from the Danish Agency for Education and Quality, you must pay for the aids yourself.
