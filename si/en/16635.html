When and how can I apply for funding?

There are five application rounds each year, with a deadline every two months.

The application deadline always falls on the Sunday of weeks 5, 14, 22, 32, 40 and 48.

If we receive your application after the deadline, it will be considered in the next application round, i.e. approximately two months later.

You may apply for funding retrospectively, provided that the initiative or event took place within the previous application period. For example, if you submit your application in week 14 for an event held in week 10, this is acceptable.

How to apply

You must complete an application form to apply.

Before you begin filling in the form, you should have a clear overview of matters such as the budget and target group, as you will be required to:

  • state how many participants you expect
  • indicate whether there is a participation fee (per person)
  • describe the target group
  • describe what you are applying for funding for, i.e. briefly outline the initiative/event (2–4 lines)

You must also attach a budget. Here is a template that you can use for your budget.

The institute attempts to respond to your application within 2 weeks. You will get the answer via email.
