You cannot change your supervisor or subject during your project except in case of very extraordinary circumstances such as

  • your supervisor falling ill 
  • serious cooperation problems 
  • technical/practical problems that constitute obstacles to your specific project. 

In such case, try finding a solution together with your supervisor – or your head of studies – so that you can submit your project by the deadline stated in your contract. 

You can only change the subject or supervisor within the same contract if both your head of department and head of studies approve this. 


If you cannot meet the deadline for submission, you may be able to apply for a dispensation

If it is impossible to find a solution enabling you to submit your project within the deadline, you may be able to apply for a dispensation to be withdrawn from the exam due to extraordinary circumstances. 

If there are no extraordinary circumstances, you can only start from scratch with a new project with a new subject and/or supervisor if you do not pass the exam or do not submit the project. 

Read about both these scenarios on this page under “Can I withdraw from the whole project or postpone my exam?”.
