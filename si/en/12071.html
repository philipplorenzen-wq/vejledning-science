Motivation is what drives you as a student – even when something is difficult or simply less interesting. You can work with your own motivation, for example when it feels as if it is gone, or simply when you have the time and energy to do something to nurture it. 

Your motivation is nourished when you: 

  • feel that you master your role as a student and are on top of things academically 
  • find that student life is meaningful 
  • have a sense of community with other people on or associated with your degree programme. 

Reflect on which of these aspects it would make sense for you to work with right now.
