From the Student Counselling Service, you can get free help from psychologists and social workers to tackle any challenges you might be experiencing in your student life.

They help with anything from psychological challenges such as stress, anxiety or loneliness, to more practical questions about parental leave, illness or the like.

They offer:

  • workshops and group sessions
  • individual appointments.
