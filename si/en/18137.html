There are many opportunities for you to acquire knowledge about and work academically with sustainability when you study at the University of Copenhagen. 

The University of Copenhagen has more than 300 courses in the field of sustainability. The full list of courses is available at the UCPH One Planet website, where you can also see the content of the courses and the requirements for signing up: UCPH One Planet

On the UCPH One Planet website, you can also find an overview of UCPH's research within sustainability and much more.
