When and how do I register for exams at the Department of Economics?

Placering: Exams › Registration and withdrawal › Exam registration

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Mathematics-Economics - Kandidat

When

When registering for a course, you will automatically be registered for the exam.

If you are only registering for exams without also registering for the course, for instance if you need to retake an exam, the following registration deadlines apply:

Exam periodWhen you have to register
Ordinary exam, winter 2025/2026
10-17 September 2025
Re-exam, winter 2025/2026

2 December 2025 - 4 February 2026

Ordinary exam, summer 2026
4-11 March 2026
Re-exam, summer 2026
2 June - 22 July 2026

How

You can register for a re-exam at the Department of Economics by contacting the study administration at bad@adm.ku.dk

Check your registrations

You can view your exam registrations by selecting Self Service > Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Exams > Show registrations and withdrawals.

Always check your exam registrations before the end of the registration period. It is your responsibility to spot any errors in your exam registrations.

If you find errors in your registrations, you must contact the UCPH Study Guidance.
