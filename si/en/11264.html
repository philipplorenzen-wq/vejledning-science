My name is Iben Nathan, and I am your Head of Studies. In this role, I have a detailed understanding of the academic content of your program and have been actively involved in shaping your curriculum. My research focuses on natural resources, politics, and governance, particularly in East Africa. I also teach and coordinate the Course Global Environmental Challenges in Environment and Development. 

As a student, you have many opportunities to influence the direction and content of your studies. While I oversee the academic framework of the program, your own engagement plays a crucial role in shaping your learning experience. With this in mind, I would like to offer a few recommendations for how you can make the most of your Master program in Environment and Development. Please regard these as suggestions to inspire you rather than as rules you must follow.
