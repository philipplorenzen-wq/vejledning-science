When and how should I give notice of parental leave with SU?

You give notice of maternity/paternity leave to the university through ‘Report parental leave’.

  • If you are the parent giving birth:
    You can give notice of childbirth before or after your child is born, though no earlier than 3 months before your child's expected date of delivery.
  • If you are the parent not giving birth:
    You can give notice of taking leave or begin your period of leave only when the child is born or adopted.

For more information, please see ‘When can I start my leave, and how much leave am I entitled to?’ on this page.

Enclose documentation of parenthood when you give notice 
  • Before your child is born: Documentation in the form of a maternity record which states your child's expected date of delivery.
  • Once your child is born: Documentation in the form of a birth certificate.
