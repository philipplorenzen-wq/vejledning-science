You must first and foremost choose a topic and a company that interest you, and which you will want to work with for an extended period of time. 

However, the project must satisfy certain requirements.


Your topic must

  • fall within your field of study so that your head of studies can approve it as part of your study programme.
  • live up to the description of the learning outcome for projects in practice.
  • fall outside your usual programme structure and must therefore not deal with research and teaching at the University.
