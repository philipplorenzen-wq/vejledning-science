You can find the date of submission in SCIENCE's general list of exam dates.

See SCIENCE's list of exam dates 

You can see the exact time of submission when it is displayed in Digital Exam about 2 weeks before your exam.

You will be notified via your KUmail when a new exam is displayed.


Allow enough time to upload the assignment

Make sure to log into Digital Exam so that you have enough time to 

  • log in
  • fill in the required information about your assignment
  • upload the assignment and submit.

Make sure you have enough time so you don't get into trouble if your Internet connection plays up, you have forgotten your password or something else unforeseen happens. If you do not submit before the deadline, you do not pass and you use an exam attempt.
