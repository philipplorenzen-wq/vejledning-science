Can I do a project outside the course scope outside SCIENCE/GLOBE?

A project outside the course scope must be anchored at SCIENCE or GLOBE. This means that you must

  • have a supervisor at SCIENCE or GLOBE
  • hand in the project and have it assessed at SCIENCE or GLOBE.

You are welcome to collect empirical data outside SCIENCE or GLOBE, for example at a laboratory, at another educational institution or at a company.

Discuss with your supervisor whether it can be used for your project.
