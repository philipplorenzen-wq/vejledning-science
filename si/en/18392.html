If you want to study abroad while on your programme, you have to get a pre-approval of the courses you want to enrol in during your stay.
The study board at your department or faculty can issue the pre-approval. The period of your stay abroad and the number of ECTS credits you intend to obtain, must be stated in the approval.
You do not need to notify SU at the UCPH of your period of studying abroad, as long as it has been pre-approved by the study board.
