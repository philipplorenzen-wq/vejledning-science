Here you will find information on what to do if you face a major or minor crisis during your study or internship period. This is an overview of how to handle the situation.


Finding a safe spot
Make sure you find a safe spot and move yourself and other fellow trevellers there.


Contact information
You can always get in touch with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Global Watch Office who can help you during a crisis. Phone: +45 3392 1112

You can also seek advice and guidance from the nearest Danish embassy or representation.

Here is an overview of their locations around the globe: https://um.dk/om-os/kontakt/find-os-i-verden.

You should also inform your family, friends and KU about your where abouts, so make sure to    contact:

  • Your host university or place of internship
  • Friends and fellow students
  • Family
  • Your faculty, department or International Education at the University of Copenhagen.

Stay informed
It's importamt to stay informed and alert if a crisis is about to emerge.

  • Stay informed about the situation through the media you trust
  • Follow the authority's recommendations
  • Follow the recommendations of your host university or internship and ask if you are in doubt

Insurance
It is a good idea to contact your own insurance company regarding home transportion, hospital costs or coverage of damaged belongings.


Danskerlisten
If you haven't aleardy done so, it is a good idea to sign up for Danskerlisten.
