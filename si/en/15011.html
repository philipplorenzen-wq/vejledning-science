How do I get consent to record or stream online teaching?

You can only record or stream online teaching if you have obtained consent from your teacher and your fellow students.

There are no special formal requirements for the form of consent, but your teacher and fellow students must express that they agree that you are recording/streaming the teaching, and that they might appear in the recording/streaming.

The consent must also state:

  • Where the recording is stored
  • How long the recording will be stored
  • The purpose of the recording, e.g. reading for exams, preparation for teaching, etc.

If you get consent, you can only use the recording privately. Your fellow students and teachers can withdraw their consent at any time, and the recording/streaming must be deleted.
