At HCØ and DIKU you will find several study spaces that are accessible for students who are for associated with Department of Computer Science. 

You have access to the rooms with your student ID card.

DIKU, Universitetsparken 1

Room 01-01-08 in the basement of the north wing (building 1).

Here you will find

  • 32 quiet reading rooms with peace for concentration
  • high tables with study spaces
  • space for group work
  • 3 boxes for meetings.

The room has a kitchenette and an eating area. Keeping the room and kitchenette clean is a shared responsibility.


DIKU Universitetsparken 1

Room 2-2-03 on the second floor in the middle wing (building 2).

Here you will find

  • study spaces
  • space for group work.

The room has a student canteen and a lounge area. Keeping the room and the canteen clean is a shared responsibility. The room is also student room and social gathering point for students at the Department of Computer Science.
Find more info and rules for using the canteen (in Danish only)


HCØ, Universitetsparken 5

Room 07-0-007 in Vandrehallen near the reception.

Here you will find

  • space for group work
  • high tables with study spaces.

The room has a kitchen and an eating area. Keeping the room and kitchen clean is a shared responsibility.
