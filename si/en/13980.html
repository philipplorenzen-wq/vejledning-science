When is the application deadline?

Placering: SU and grants › SU and studying abroad › Scholarships for studies abroad

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: gxd · Københavns Universitet - Kandidat · Tandplejeruddannelsen - P-Bachelor

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/When-is-the-application-deadline-SU-KU.aspx

You can apply for a study-abroad scholarship no earlier than 12 months prior to starting your stay. However, you must apply before the end of your term abroad, and before you come back home.
Please note! If you apply after the end of your stay abroad, your application will be rejected.
