The registration procedure is the same no matter why you need to register for the exam. 

You register for your exam at Self service by choosing

  1. 'Enrolment, Courses and Exams'
  2. 'Examination'
  3. 'Register'
  4. the relevant exam in the drop-down menu
  5. 'Confirm'

You can only register during the registration periods.

Go to Self Service

Other procedure for ordinary exam period in block 5, 4-5 and 5-1

You cannot register for exams in the ordinary exam periods for block 5, 4-5 and 5-1 yourself at Self Service.

Instead do as follows:

  1. Ask the course coordinator if you can take your exam in the relevant period without having followed or been registered for the course again in the current academic year.  Find contact info in the course description
  2. (If the course coordinator allows it) Write UCPH Study Guidance and ask them to register you for the exam.

Check if your registrations are correct

You can view your exam registrations by selecting Self Service > Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Examination > View registrations and cancellations.

Always check your exam registrations before the end of the registration period. It is your responsibility to identify any errors in your exam registrations.

If you find errors in your registrations, you must contact UCPH Study Guidance.
