Oral examination

You must appeal no later than 14 days after receiving the result.

Written exam

You must appeal no later than 14 days after the deadline for the grade publication. This deadline will be extended if the grades are delayed but not decreased if the grades are given earlier.

Dispensations to the time limit for appeal

If you could not complain by the deadline due to extraordinary circumstances, the faculty may grant a dispensation from the appeal deadline. Whether a dispensation from the deadline is granted, is assessed on an individual basis.

As a rule, extraordinary circumstances should have a sufficient scope and temporal placement which prevents you from meeting the deadline. Extraordinary circumstances could include:

  • Documented physical or psychological illness
  • Severe disease or death in the nearest family or circle of close friends
  • Particularly difficult personal circumstances
  • Other extraordinary circumstances, in which you have no blame
