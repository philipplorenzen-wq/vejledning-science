If you are employed as a student assistant for the performance of tasks for a research project, you are not required to enter into a data processing agreement. However, as a student assistant, you are obliged to treat data as confidential. This means that you are not allowed to show or forward data to others or keep them on your computer after your task has been completed.

As an employed student assistant, you are obliged to comply with University of Copenhagen's information security policy.
