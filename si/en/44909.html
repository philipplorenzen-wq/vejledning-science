Previously, the Medicinal and Biostructural Chemistry exam represented 5 ECTS credits (SFAK20011E) and the course certificate 2.5 ECTS credits (SFAK20010E).

As of the autumn semester 2024, students enrolled in Medicinal and Biostructural Chemistry no longer receive a course certificate, and therefore, the exam now represents 7.5 ECTS credits (SFAK24001E).

Unless you have passed the 2.5-ECTS course certificate by 1 September 2024, you need to take the new exam.

For students who need to take the 5-ECTS exam

If you have passed the Medicinal and Biostructural Chemistry course certificate by 1 September 2024, you need to take the 5-ECTS exam (SFAK20011E) at one of the following times:

  • Ordinary winter exams 2024
  • Winter re-examinations 2024
  • Ordinary winter exams 2025

You need to register for the exam. Please use the following exam code when you register for the 5-ECTS exam: SFAK20011E.
