Your Study Board decides applications for exemptions, pre-approvals and credit transfers.

Your Study Board consists of an equal number of representatives from

  • the student body
  • the scientific staff.

You application and documentation are not anonymized for the members of the study board but treated confidentially. The members of the study board are all bound by professional secrecy.
