Would you like to be part of the SPS club at UCPH? 

The club is for you who need support in your student life. You can be part of a social community where you can be together, have fun, share experiences and help the SPS counsellors understand your needs and interests. 

The SPS club offers: 

  • Social events with other students with special needs, for example at Christmas or evenings of playing boardgames. 
  • Invitation to take part in short surveys about your life as a student.
  • The opportunity to participate in the University's SPS campaigns. 
  • Emails with questions from SPS counsellors about, for example, input for new services that will improve your daily life as a student with special needs.

The SPS club is for everyone, and you can sign up via the link below.
