Gælder for målgrupperne: Molecular Biomedicine - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Molecular-Biomedicin-Association-SCI.aspx

Here you have the opportunity to get really close to your education - and to have an influence on your study environment and your studies. The Molecular Biomedicine Association has representatives in the department's teaching committees, where you collaborate with 
  • teachers 
  • Deputy Heads of the Department of Education 
  • other members from other studies in the department 
  • The study environment consultant

You will also have the opportunity to attend the management meetings at the department (app. 4 times a year) together with 
  • the head of department 
  • deputy heads of research and teaching 
  • the head of administration 
  • other council members from other studies in the department 

If interested, contact the teaching committee at the department to hear how you can become an active part of the Molecular Biomedicine Association.
