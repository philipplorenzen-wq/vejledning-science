This is what happens if you do not comply with the maximum duration of study:

  • You will receive a warning via your KUmail when you have about 1 year left to complete your study programme.
  • You will receive a warning via your KUmail before being disenrolled and be offered the chance to apply for a dispensation.
  • You will be disenrolled when you have used up the maximum duration of study (as an active student) on your Master's programme without passing all ECTS credits.


Dispensations possible

You may apply for a dispensation if you can't meet the maximum duration of study due to exceptional circumstances.

You can apply for a dispensation if you

    1. can demonstrate with a study plan that you will not be able to complete your study programme within the maximum duration of study
    2. can document the exceptional circumstances.

See how to make a study plan that fits your needs


Important to note when applying for a dispensation from the maximum duration of study

  1. Explain clearly how the exceptional circumstances have meant that you are unable to meet the maximum duration of study.
  2. Specify in your application and study plan how much extra time you need.
  3. Show in your study plan that you will be able to complete your study programme if granted the extension you are applying for.
