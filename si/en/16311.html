How much of the exam should we do together as a group?

You have a say in how much of your report/paper and the oral exam (if there is one) you want to do as a group.


Report/paper

For a project report or paper that includes an oral exam, you can choose between writing

  • a joint report in which everyone is equally responsible for all sections
  • an individualised report in which each group member is responsible for a section and is named as the author of that section. Each section may only have one named author.

If your exam only consists of a written exam, the report must be divided into individually authored sections for you to be given individual assessments.


Oral exams

For oral exams, there are two possible exam forms:

  • A joint exam in which all the group members are examined at the same time (not always an option).
  • An individual exam in which the group members are examined separately.
