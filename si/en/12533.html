Where can I find study spaces and group rooms at Kalundborg?

The teaching buildings at Kalundborg have study environments. Here you will find several group rooms and study spaces, which you are free to use for project and group work.

You are also allowed to use the teaching rooms when no teaching or other events are taking place.

Besides the teaching buildings, you will find study spaces and group rooms at University College Absalon in Kalundborg.

The canteen at University College Absalon is also a great spot for study activities where you can study together with your group or alone.

The library at University College Absalon is open every Tuesday. You can also visit the local communal libraries in the city center of Kalundborg.
