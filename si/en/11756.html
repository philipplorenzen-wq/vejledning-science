Prepare yourself for working life and start clarifying your strengths and opportunities while you are studying.

You can get in touch with the job market in several ways:

  • Activities that are built into your degree programme
  • Activities outside your degree programme

If you have a functional impairment, such as a mental disorder, autism, reading difficulties or a physical disorder, it is particularly important to make use of the opportunities that are built into your degree programme.

Students and graduates explain what it has meant for them to gain job experience
