Your stay at the University of Copenhagen was made possible thanks to the exchange agreement that has been established between your home university and the University of Copenhagen/Faculty of SCIENCE.

To uphold the good collaboration and exchange agreement we need good ambassadors – for the University of Copenhagen and for your home university. We therefore encourage you to:

  • Tell fellow students back home about your experiences abroad.
  • Tell fellow students back home about SCIENCE and UCPH.
  • Help out at study abroad fairs and other international events back home as an experienced study abroad alumni and ambassador.
  • Help welcome SCIENCE and UCPH students arriving at your home university.
