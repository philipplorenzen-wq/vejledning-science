Can I get financial support?

Placering: Master's thesis and other projects › Master's thesis › Registration and contract

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Environment and Development - Kandidat

You can apply for funding to cover expenses related to the compulsory field work that you have to carry out in connection with your thesis. 

Financial support is available to students who fulfil both prerequisites: 

  • have their main supervisor at IFRO or PLEN
  • do not travel to destinations listed as orange or red by the Danish Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Financial support is:

  • none if done in Denmark
  • DKK 5,000 if done in Europe
  • DKK 10,000 if done outside of Europe.

How to apply

Read the application form carefully before submitting it to your Head of studies to ensure that you fill it in correctly and include all required enclosures.
