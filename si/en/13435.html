The size of your study-abroad scholarship depends on:

  • what you are going to study
  • how many ECTS have been pre-approved
  • the tuition fee cost at your foreign host university

The three rates of study-aboard scholarships depend on what you will study at your stay abroad.

The rates are fixed annually in the Finance Act.
In 2026, the rates are as follows:

Rate 1: DKK 50,000 per 60 ECTS, equivalent to DKK 833.33 per ECTS

Rate 2: DKK 71,300 per 60 ECTS, equivalent to DKK 1,188.33 per ECTS

Rate 3: DKK 104,100 per 60 ECTS, equivalent to DKK 1,735.00 per ECTS
The programme areas are generally divided as follows:

• Programmes within the Social Sciences or Law: rate 1

• Programmes within the Humanities: Rate 1 and occasionally rate 2

• Scientific, technical or programmes within health sciences: rate 2 or 3.
The three rates indicate the maximum amount granted for a study-abroad scholarship, equivalent to 60 ECTS.
If the tuition fee is less than the scholarship rate, you will only be awarded the amount, equivalent to the tuition fee.
If the tuition fee is more than the amount awarded, you can apply for study-abroad student loans.
