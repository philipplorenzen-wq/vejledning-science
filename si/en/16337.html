How can I receive special conditions in class?

Who you shall address depends on the nature of your special conditions:

  • Regarding practical and physical conditions in class:
    Contact your department – or departments – where the classes are held as soon as you can.
    This could be in relation to e.g. room size or arrangement, schedules or the need for a smaller class.
    Find contact information for the responsible department(s)
     
  • Regarding special conditions for the content of the classes:
    Ask and coordinate with the course responsible.
    This could be in relation to e.g. distribution of class material, allergic consideration, group work, practical relations regarding field trips.
    Find contact information for the course responsible in the course description


Supportive assistants in laboratories need security clearance

If you need to bring interpreters or other supportive assistants to laboratories, they need a security clearance. Contact the responsible department about the procedure.

Find contact information for the responsible department


Permission to set up or store SPS aids

In some cases, The SPS Counselling is responsible for coordinating your special conditions with your department when it is about setting up and storing SPS aids. Talk to the SPS Counselling if you are unsure about whether you have to do something.
