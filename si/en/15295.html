In the list you will find universities outside Europe you can apply for with a GPA below 7. All UCPH students can apply for these universities.
Most universities in Europa do not require a minimum GPA.

Please notice:

  • The GPA's in the list are for undergraduate/bachelor students. If the universitie require a higher GPA for master students it appears from the fact sheet.
  • All places are allocated according to the highest GPA
