Your study programme is planned in such a way that it is possible to study at reduced hours at certain times instead of full time (which is equivalent to 15 ECTS credits per block).

The extent to which you can study at reduced hours depends on

  1. how quick you have been in your studies so far
  2. whether you may be able to apply for a dispensation due to exceptional circumstances, or whether you just need to slow down a bit for a while.
