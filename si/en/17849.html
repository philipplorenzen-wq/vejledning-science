What if I discover an error in the exam assignment?

Placering: Exams › Exam types and rules › Written on-site exam

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat · Gæstestuderende SCI - Alle uddannelsesniveauer · Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Betalende Deltid

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-if-I-discover-an-error-in-the-exam-assignment-SCI.aspx

Contact the exam invigilators as soon as possible and point out the possible error. The invigilators will then look into the matter and come back with an answer as soon as possible. 

If an error is found, it is up to the invigilators to assess whether to give the group of examinees extra time to complete the exam.
