The SU Office at the University of Copenhagen offers guidance on the Danish State Education Grant and Loan Scheme for students, based on their situation.

You can get help with things like:

  • state education grants (SU)
  • youth travel pass
  • study-abroad scholarships.

Contact the SU office at the University of Copenhagen
