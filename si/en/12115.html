The MSc programme in Mathematics-Economics consists of four elements:

  • Compulsory courses, 15 ECTS credits
  • Restricted elective courses, 45 ECTS credits
  • Elective courses, 30 ECTS credits
  • Thesis, 30 ECTS credits

The programme is two years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Mathematics-Economics / Cand. scient.oecon i matematik-økonomi.
