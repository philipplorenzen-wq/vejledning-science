When your stay abroad is approaching, it is time to sort out your accommodation (where and how ) while you are away.
You can do this in different ways:
  • Some students prefer to find accommodation for their entire stay abroad from home.
  • Others simply rent a room for the first period, and then find accommodation for the rest of their stay after they have arrived in the host city.
Three tips for your home hunting:
  1. Examine whether the host university can help you find accommodation.
  2. Find good accommodation tips in the evaluations from students who have written about their stay in the same city in which you will be living.
  3.  It is a good idea to look for accommodation for the start of (or all of) your stay in pages for private landlords.
