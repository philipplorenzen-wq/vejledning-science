Associations and organisations affiliated with the University can hire premises at UCPH.

Organisers of commercial, religious or political activities cannot hire rooms.
What can hired rooms be used for?
Events must be: 
  • compatible with democratic values
  • compatible with the University's reputation and academic spirit
  • carried out safely.  
If you hire a room, you are not allowed to pass on the user rights to others, and it cannot be used for other purposes than what is agreed in the hire agreement..

Smoking Policy

As of January 1, 2026, the University of Copenhagen has a new smoking policy. If you hire a room at the University, you must, of course, comply with the smoking policy. Read more on this page under “Am I allowed to smoke on campus?”
