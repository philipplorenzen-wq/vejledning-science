At the end of all SCIENCE courses, you are invited to share your experience of the course in an evaluation.

The course evaluation covers:

  • Teaching and lecturers
  • Exams

The feedback from you and your fellow students has a direct impact on the design and quality of courses being offered at SCIENCE.
