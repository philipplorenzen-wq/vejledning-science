Gælder for målgrupperne: Integrated Food Studies - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Recommended-study-tracks-SCI-IFS.aspx

If you are interested in specific topics within integrated food studies, I have made a list of courses related to your programme to a certain degree. The courses selected are assessed as relevant for the programme but also selected based on their availability in block one,two or five (block five are summer courses) which fits the structure of your programme regarding electives. If you do not follow the ordinary structure of the programme there are also courses to examine in the other blocks (block three and four). Remember to check the recommended academic qualifications of each course and contact the course responsible teacher(s) if you have questions regarding course content or you are unsure whether you are qualified to follow the course. You are also welcome to find other courses of your own specific interest both within or outside the Faculty of Science.

You can also contact me, if you have questions to a specific course.
