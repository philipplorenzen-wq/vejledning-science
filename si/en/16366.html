When can I do my project?

You must start your project at the start of the block. 

The deadline for submission of an MSc Research Projects varies depending on the size of the project:

  • 30 ECTS: 4 months after the start date
  • 45 ECTS:  6.5 months after the start date
  • 60 ECTS: 9 months after the start date


You can do the project part-time

It is possible to do the project part-time. If you are taking other courses concurrently with the project, the deadline for submission may be extended by one and a half months for each study activity equalling 7.5 ECTS credits.


Conclude your project within the official study abroad period 

The exchange agreement sets the maximum duration of the project period. If the exchange agreement only allows for, e.g., 6 months of exchange studies, the project must be concluded within that timeframe.
