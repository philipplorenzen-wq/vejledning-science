You can change your registration during the registration periods.

How to change your registration during the registration periods:

  1. Withdraw from course.
  2. Register for another course with vacancies.


After the end of the late-registration period, your registration for the course is binding and you need to pass the course to graduate. After the end of the late-registration period, you can only change your registration if you are granted a dispensation. Only then will you no longer be bound by your registration.
