The Master's thesis is a practical research project corresponding to a year worth of academic work. 

When you know which part of the geology you're interested in, you contact a teacher or researcher in that area and talk to them about a topic, outline for the project and a plan for the project. You then draw up a contract and send it for approval. 

A thesis projects can be made in corporation with external partners from the industry, private companies, GEUS, ministerial departments, museums and so on. 

Read more about the Master's thesis.

Other project options

You can also do a Project in Practice or a Project outside the course scope as part of your programme.
