What should I do if I am delayed during my thesis writing?

If you are delayed during your thesis writing and only complete your degree upon submitting your thesis, you must — in addition to the receipt from MinSU, your personal statement, and your medical certificate — provide documentation of the delay.

This may include, for example:
  • A granted exemption for postponing the submission deadline
  • Your thesis contract as well as an updated/revised contract showing both the original and the new submission dates
  • Other official documentation of your expected submission date
