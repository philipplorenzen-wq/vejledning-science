As a student at UCPH, you are required to follow the UCPH' information security and IT security guidelines.

When you familiarize yourself with and follow the guidelines, you protect yourself, research, your fellow students and the University.

Information security is about protecting information so that everyone at UCPH can be absolutely safe when we store and share information and data. Information security is dependent on the individual's attention and knowledge.
