Your appeal will be processed as follows:

  1. The appeal will be sent to the internal and external examiners (if the appeal concerns the assessment or other academic issues).
  2. The examiners must send their statement on the matter to the SCIENCE Study Administration within 2 weeks of receiving the appeal.
  3. The statement will be forwarded to you, and you then have 2 weeks to comment on it.
  4. The University will then make a decision based on the examiners' statement and your comments.
  5. The decision will be sent to your KUmail.


Response to the appeal and next steps

From the time you have submitted your appeal and we have received all relevant information, it typically takes 6-8 weeks before you receive a decision. However, it may take a little longer during holiday periods.

You will receive the decision on your KUmail. The email will explain the reasons for the decision. Your appeal will either be upheld or not upheld.


If your appeal is upheld

You may be offered either a retake or a re-assessment of your exam.
You have 2 weeks from when you receive the offer to decide whether to accept or reject it.


If your appeal is not upheld

You have the option of appealing the decision.
You have 2 weeks from when you are informed that your appeal has not been upheld to submit a further appeal against the decision.

You have the option to complain about legal issues to the Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science.
