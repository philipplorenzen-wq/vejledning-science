During your studies, you will be working with fellow students in various types of study groups. Study groups can have different purposes, such as:  

  • collaborating on a project 
  • assignments and presentations for specific courses 
  • preparation for classes and exams. 

No matter how long your study group has been working together, various tools and exercises can help you get the most out of your collaboration.
