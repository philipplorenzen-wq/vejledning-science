Is my dispensation for special exam conditions valid outside SCIENCE?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Is-my-dispensation-for-special-exam-conditions-valid-outside-SCIENCE-EX-SCI.aspx

Your dispensations for special exam conditions is only valid at SCIENCE.

You must apply for a new dispensation at the relevant faculty/university if you are taking exams outside SCIENCE.
