The specialisation in Ecology, double degree at a 4EU+ partner university, is set at 120 ECTS credits and consists of the following:

  • Compulsory courses:
    • 7.5 ECTS credits at UCPH
    • 11 ECTS credits at partner university
  • Restricted elective courses:
    • 22.5 ECTS credits at UCPH
    • 18 ECTS credits at partner university
  • Elective subjects at partner university, 16 ECTS credits
  • Thesis, 45 ECTS credits

You can apply for the double degree option in the first semester of your MSc programme when your specialisation is Ecology.

4EU+ partner universities

Current 4EU+ partner universities available:
  • University of Warsaw (UW), Poland

Elective subjects

You have 16 ECTS credits to strengthen your own profile at University of Warsaw (UW). You can choose courses for your elective subjects available at the Faculty of Biology in University of Warsaw (UW), Poland.

You have 0 ECTS elective credits to use at UCPH.
