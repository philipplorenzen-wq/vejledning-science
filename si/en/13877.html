Hanging of posters is allowed on bulletin boards and advertising pillars in the common areas of UCPH. Hanging must be done with pins, magnets or other non-sticky material - masking tape is allowed. 


It is not allowed to hang posters on walls or doors due to damage to the surfaces.
Bulletin boards allocated to student/employee clubs may not be used for hanging posters. 

What can I hang?

Posters at campus must be relevant to UCPH or our studies and must support a pleasant tone of communications. Practically, this means all posters containing hateful or inappropriate language will be removed. Non hateful posts, related to conflicts are allowed.
If in doubt concerning the content of your posters, contact Campus Service. See contact information to the right.
