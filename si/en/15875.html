Whether you need to take a language test prior to your stay depends on your host university’s admission requirements.
See the language requirements for the various universities in the fact sheets under Exchange places.
The language requirements are typically stated as:
  • a CEFR level
    Read about CEFR
  • a minimum number of points in a TOEFL test or an IELTS test.
    Read about TOEFL
    Read about IELTS
Some universities merely require a confirmation of your language proficiency.
You must take a language assessment before Erasmus+
It is a requirement that you take a language assessment (OLS) before going on an Erasmus+ stay.
You will receive a language assessment link immediately before you go on your exchange stay. There is no fail for the language assessment.
