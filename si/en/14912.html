When you have taken the courses at the partner institution assigned to your mobility track, you must apply for a credit transfer to make it an official part of your study programme.

If you study at UCPH as your second year of your programme, you are adviced to apply for credit transfer for the courses you took on your first year at your home university as soon as you arrive at UCPH. This will speed up the diploma process once you have defended your thesis.

How to apply for credit transfer of your courses, for your double degree diploma:

  1. Get certified documentation of your exam results  from your home university.
  2. Fill in the application and upload your exam results via 'Apply for credit transfer - double degree'.
