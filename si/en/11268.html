I am Hans Jørgen Lyngs Jørgensen and I am your Head of Studies. As such, I know the academic contents of your programme in detail and have contributed in determining your curriculum. My research area is plant pathology and how to avoid diseases, among others by biological control and I teach in the following courses:

MSc-courses:
  • Plant Infection and Disease Management
  • Advanced Crop Production
  • Tropical Crop Production
  • Pesticide Use, Mode of Action and Ecotoxicology
  • Plant Ecophysiology in a Changing Climate

BSc-courses:
  • Plant Science
  • Basic Course in Plant Protection
  • Plant Pathology: Crop Diseases and Food Security
  • Diagnosis of Diseases of Agricultural and Horticultural Plants

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here is my advice and my recommendations for completing your master's programme in Agriculture successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are based on the latest programme structure, unless I have specifically written otherwise.
