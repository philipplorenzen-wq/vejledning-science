You must be in the exam room no later than 30 minutes before your exam begins.
UCPH encourages you to be there 1 hour before the exam starts so that you won't be prevented from sitting the exam bacause of unforeseen delays.
If you arrive after your exam has started, you may not sit the exam. If you arrive too late, you will be registered as "absent" and will have used an exam attempt.

When should you be logged in to the exam PC

The IT support and the invigilators will give important information about the exam before it starts.  Therefore you should be logged in to the exam computer no later than 15 minutes before the exam.
