You must be admitted to a programme and have accepted the place before you can apply for SU.

On your master's degree programme, you can receive SU from February if you complete your bachelor's programme at the winter exams (including any re-exams in February).
You must apply for SU between 1 and 15 January if you want your SU for February to be paid on the last banking day in January.  You can apply for SU for February up until 28/29 February. 

Apply via 'minSU' at www.su.dk.
