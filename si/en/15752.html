Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Expectations-of-you-SCI-BBT.aspx

  • Consider your study as full-time work and use the time it requires
  • Structure your time and plan ahead, so you will have time for both studies and leisure. Participate in SPOT events on study techniques.
  • Read your study messages and study events so you do not miss important messages and events.  
  • Beware of the rules regarding exam cheating and good scientific conduct. Do not cheat or plagiarize - neither intentional nor non-intentional. It can have very serious consequences for you. Learn the rules.  
  • Participate in the social activities and note that there is an Assosiation of Biotechnology students: Foreningen af Bioteknologistuderende (FB) that arranges several events. You can also find them on facebook. 
  • Use your influence to improve your studies and study environment. Remember to evaluate courses, as this is a very important tool to improve our courses and teaching.Through the Association for Biotechnology students (FB) you have a voice in e.g. departmental education committees, study boards etc.
  • Include your fellow students, seek their help, and assist them when needed.
  • Remember always to communicate in a language that all persons present understand.
  • Familiarize yourself with the curriculum for the study. These are the official rules that regulate your study.  
  • Avoid negative stress. You must find strategies to get relaxed and refilled with energy. Read more about positive and negative stress.
