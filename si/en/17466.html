Each academic year has 6 planned one-week study breaks. During these weeks, you can

  • spend time on independent study
  • take re-exams
  • go on holiday. 


Events during scheduled one-week study breaks

Academic and/or social events are often scheduled during the planned one-week study breaks.
Examples of events during study breaks:

  • Research presentations
  • Literature search courses
  • Company visits
