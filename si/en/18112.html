Can I do a Master's thesis about my own startup?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-do-a-Master%27s-thesis-about-my-own-startup-SCI.aspx

Yes, your master's thesis can be about your own company that you want to start or already have started. You might have an idea or problem that you have found a solution to and want to turn it into reality.

You can do your master's thesis about your own idea in collaboration with a hub or incubator – e.g., UCPH's innovationc centre, UCPH Lighthouse.

UCPH Lighthouse offers advice, networking and office space.
