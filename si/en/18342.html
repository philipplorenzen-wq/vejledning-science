For how long is my student ID card valid?

Your student ID card is valid for as long as you are enrolled at the University of Copenhagen.
However, the card is marked with an expiration date 3 1/2 years from the day the card was ordered. Eg. if you have ordered your card on 1 September 2020, the expiry date will be 31 January 2024. 

As the expiry date is not of any significance to you as long as you are enrolled at the University, the card will not automatically be renewed after 3 1/2 years. Despite having passed the expiry date, the card will still grant you access to the UCPH building relevant to you until you end your studies.

Student Discounts

If it is important to you that the date on your student ID card is not exceeded, because you use it to obtain various student discounts, you can order a new card via Self Service on KUnet. When you order a new card, the old student card is automatically blocked.
Your new student ID card is valid for 3 1/2 years or until you stop at the University of Copenhagen.
