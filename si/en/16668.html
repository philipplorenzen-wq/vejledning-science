It is important that you regularly adjust your expectations of yourself and your student life in light of your current situation. While developing as a student, you also have many other roles. 

During your studies, you may experience: 

  • times when you have to give less priority to your studies for various reasons, in favour of other aspects of your life 
  • events and circumstances in your life that have a bearing on your studies 
  • bumps on the road, both big and small, like almost everybody else. 

The events encountered by students vary from person to person. It also varies how much things affect you and your studies.

Examples of factors that can affect your academic and social focus and energy:

  • Great heartache or falling in love 
  • Moving house 
  • Illness (you or your immediate family) 
  • Having children/becoming a parent 
  • Chronic illness 

Note that you can apply for various types of assistance at UCPH if external or internal circumstances are impacting your ability to study.
