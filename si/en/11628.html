You must find a main supervisor. It is your own responsibility to identify and contact a possible supervisor to reach an agreement.

Your supervisor must be

  • employed at SCIENCE
  • the person who provides you with academic support and acts as a sounding board on the project
  • your examiner.

You decide whether you want to first choose your topic or your supervisor.
