How long am I entitled to SU?

Placering: SU and grants › Your SU › SU and completion time

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: gxd · Københavns Universitet - Kandidat · Tandplejeruddannelsen - P-Bachelor

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-long-am-I-entitled-to-SU-SU-KU.aspx

You are entitled to SU throughout the month in which you become a bachelor/master's graduate – however, this does not apply if you complete the programme on the first day of the month.
In the context of SU, your programme is considered complete when the last grade has been granted.
If you finish your programme with an oral exam, your programme is officially complete on the day of the last oral exam, because you receive the grade immediately after the examination.
If you finish your programme with a written exam (for example a thesis), your programme is officially completed on the date when the grade (or the assessment: passed) is available to you.
