When considering a study abroad
Contact your study counselor to help you find out more informations regarding:
  • when a study abroad fits into your study program
  • which subjects you study while abroad
  • how to get started with the process
When you are assigned a place
Contact International Education if you:
  • is awarded an exchange place abroad
  • has been awarded a scholarship for your study abroad

You will automatically receive information about the progress when you are assigned for an exchange.
