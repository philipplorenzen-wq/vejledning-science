In the canteens, you will find a wide selection of hot dishes, salad bar, sandwiches, soups, breakfast, something for the sweet tooth, as well as cold and hot drinks. 

The buffet (salad, hot dishes etc.) costs DKK 8,50 per 100 grams in all canteens.

The canteens are all operated by Compass Group Chartwells. Assortment and prices therefore resemble each other - with room for variation from canteen to canteen.
