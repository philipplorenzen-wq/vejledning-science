How do I provide documentation of my functional impairment or medical condition?

If you have a functional impairment or medical condition, you must provide documentation in the form of a doctor's note, which clearly describes:

  1. The given functional impairment or medical condition.
  2. When it began, and how long the doctor expects it to last.
  3. How the functional impairment or medical condition affects your ability to participate in class.

You need a doctor's note to apply for special conditions or other exemption due to a functional impairment or medical condition.
