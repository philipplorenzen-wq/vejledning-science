Do I have to take leave of absence if I can’t study for a period of time?

There are alternatives to taking leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances. Leave of absence may be a good solution for you if you have other sources of income to replace the SU which you do not receive during your leave of absence.


If you need to receive SU while being absent from your studies, it is a good idea to look into the various alternatives to taking leave of absence.


Read about the alternatives to taking leave of absence in connection with

  • parental leave
  • long-term illness
  • illness or death in the family


Choose leave of absence due to military service or UN service

If you are doing military service or UN service, taking leave of absence is generally the best solution. As you receive pay during your military service, it is not an advantage/option for you formally to meet the study activity requirements.
