An AI declaration is a template in which you state whether you have used generative AI and, if so, for which purpose.

When generative AI is allowed to a limited extent, you must always fill out the AI declaration – regardless of whether you have used generative AI or not. If you forget to attach an AI declaration, there is a risk that your exam will not be assessed.

For ongoing tests, include an AI declaration for each written component you submit.

Start early when filling out UCPH’s AI declaration

It may take some time to get it right. Consider early on how you will describe your use of generative AI, e.g.:

  • Your key prompts and any source material (what you feed into the tool)
  • The output (the responses you received from the tool)
  • The process (if you engage in a dialogue with the tool)

The template includes a guide on how to fill it out.

How to:

  1. Download the Word template
  2. Fill it out and follow the guide
  3. Include it at the end of your assignment or report – before submitting
