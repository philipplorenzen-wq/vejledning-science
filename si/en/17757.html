Can I register for a course I've previously been registered for at SUND?

For your courses at SUND your option to re-register for a course depends on whether you're required to pass the course – or whether you only need to pass the exam associated with the course.

Course you must pass (with compulsory elements you're required to pass during the course, e.g. exercises)

  • You must re-register for the course if you haven't passed it.

Course where you only need to pass the exam(s) associated with the course

  • You can re-register for the course if you've been granted a dispensation to withdraw from the course.
  • You cannot re-register for the course, because you haven't passed the exam (no matter whether this is due to a too low assessment, illness, withdrawal from the exam or non-attendance).

You can
  • register for the course in the course room in Absalon
  • attend lectures
  • often, you can make an agreement with the lecturer to attend classes even if you are not formally enrolled on the course.

Send a KUmail to the Study Secretary on the course to be registered for Absalaon course room.


You cannot

  • register for the course again via Self Service
  • participate in compulsory parts of the course which you have already passed.

If you don't think you can pass the exam without being registered for the course again, please contact at study and career guidance cousellor at SCIENCE Studentservices.
