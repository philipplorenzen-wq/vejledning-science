There are several options for career support for you as an international student both while you study in Denmark and after you have graduated.


Places where you can seek support and inspiration:

  • Use the services and offers provided by UCPH Study Guidance
  • Visit the website for international students in Denmark: The State of Denmark
  • Join a free career programme:
    • Young Professionals in Denmark for first year students
    • Greater Copenhagen Career Programme for last year students and graduates
    • First Job Copenhagen for students and graduates living in City of Copenhagen
    • Career Kick Start for first year students who wish to find a student job
  • Use services and participate in events from your trade union and/or unemployment fund – if you have a membership.
