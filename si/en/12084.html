All over UCPH you will find pleasant cafés and Friday bars run by student volunteers. This is where you can relax, study or party with your fellow students – or play an active role in the community as a volunteer. 

All UCPH students are normally welcome in all cafés and bars on campus. 

You can also participate in or even arrange parties on campus – both at or in addition to the regular Friday bars.
