If you want to appeal against the decision, you must send your appeal to UCPH Education no later than two weeks after you have received the decision.


You can only appeal against the decision on legal grounds, that is to say, if you find that the decision has not been processed in accordance with applicable law. Among other things, legal aspects are the administrative rules and principles, such as hearing of the parties, grounds and appeals guidelines. However, your appeal may not concern academic issues or the University’s assessments unless the assessment is outside the legal framework.


When UCPH Education have received your appeal, a statement will be issued, that you will have at least one week to comment on.


UCPH Education will send the statement, your comments on the statement and the other documents of the case to the Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science.


The Agency will then process your appeal and you will receive a response directly from the Agency.
