My recommendations:

  • Do you need help planning your project? Here are some guides for you to use: project in practice, project outside the course scope, master thesis.
  • You can find inspiration for projects and supervisors at Welcome MSc students.pdf
  • You should start as soon as possible to find an advisor for your projects.
  • We recommend that you make a project outside the course scope before you start on master thesis. This enables you get to know your advisor/research group.
  • You can consider to also carry out a thesis preparation project.
  • Do you want to do a project in practice? You can find inspiration on UCPH Projects and Jobs.
  • Do you want an external collaboration for you master thesis? You can find inspiration in UCPH Projects and Jobs.
  • Former students did projects with external collaboration at Novo Nordisk, Lundbeck, Topsøe, AirScapel, FRS-Systems, KU dyes and HPNow.
