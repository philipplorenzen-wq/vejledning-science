There are in general three types of projects in the program. 

• Projects outside course scope• Projects in practice• Thesis preparation projects• Master thesis
Only the master thesis is mandatory, but depending on the thesis you want to write and the direction you want to go into it may be necessary to do a thesis preparation project. There are two versions of the program with either a 30 ECTS thesis or a 45 ECS thesis. The 45 ECTS option is mainly if you decide to write your thesis with a supervisor from the Niels Bohr Institute at the University of Copenhagen. If you choose to write your thesis at the Technical University or with a supervisor at the University of Copenhagen but not at the Niels Bohr Institute, it would usually be a 30 ECTS thesis, but can be 45 ECTS. You should keep in mind that depending on your topic and whether experimental or not a thesis preparation project may be needed. If you do a 45 ECTS thesis, the only option to take a thesis preparation project is in the first teaching block in the second year. It is therefore recommended to think about this before the beginning of the second year of studies. 
The project outside course scope is a very flexible option that can replace an elective course and you are free to make arrangements with any faculty member about a topic of your interest. 
Projects in practice will be in collaboration with a company. We will be organizing internship matchmaking events with selected companies. A project in practice replaces elective courses.
