What can I expect from the Buddy Programme?

You will be part of a group consisting of 2-4 Buddies and 10-20 new exchange students. In the group you will be a part of various social and cultural activities throughout the semester.

The Buddies are all students at SCIENCE who are interested in helping and meeting fellow students from around the world.


The Buddies can offer you help with

  • how to get from the airport to your accommodation
  • practical matters that arise during the first weeks after your arrival e.g., finding your way around town, places to shop for groceries and a bike, etc. 
  • showing you the university and the city of Copenhagen.  


Availability and limitations of your SCIENCE Buddies

The SCIENCE Buddies are volunteers with busy study programmes, jobs, etc. They all strive to be there for you when you need their help, but they are not always able help you out.

A SCIENCE Buddy cannot

  • be a personal assistant – they can always say 'no' if they are unavailable
  • act as study guidance counselor or academic advisor  
  • offer you accommodation or lend you money.
