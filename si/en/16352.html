Your primary supervisor is

  • your academic supporter and sounding board, from the ideas development phase and start-up to the actual project work
  • the examiner on your report and for your oral exam.


The supervisor's responsibility for the project

The supervisor has the overall responsibility for ensuring that your project scope and the workload involved match the project framework.
