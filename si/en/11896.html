There are five student chaplains affiliated with the University of Copenhagen. The university chaplains are employed by the Danish Lutheran State Church to be available to students for free counselling. 

All students can get counselling sessions, regardless of whether you are a member of the Danish Lutheran State Church and a believer or not. The university chaplains talk to everyone, respect your point of view and take a point of departure in your situation.
