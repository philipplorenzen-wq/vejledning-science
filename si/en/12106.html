Here you can find answers to exactly how you will be given support and/or aids – and not least who will help you with what in the process.

You start by selecting the functional impairment you have.

Then you can see the SPS programme step by step for your functional impairment. And you can see who will be helping you when.

The timeline starts when you book an interview, and it ends when you have received your aids or support.
