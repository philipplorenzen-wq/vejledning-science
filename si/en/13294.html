Can I use the premises or the campus area if I want to do a fundraiser?

The UCPH rooms are as a rule only used for university purposes and not activities regarding civil society, including fundraisers. 

In case of doubt, Campus Service will look into the specific enquiry. If you want to do a fundraiser at UCPH you must:

  1. Apply for permission from UCPH

    You have to contact Campus Service. The application should include what the fundraiser is for, and how it relates to local research or teaching. In case of doubt, the academic management at the Faculty will make the decision to approve or reject the application. If the application is approved, the fundraiser must then be formally approved by the Collection Board under the Danish Agency for Civil Affairs


  2. Apply for permission from The Collection Board

    If, in exceptional cases, UCPH decides that a fundraiser can be authorized, the guidelines of the Danish Fundraising Board must be followed. Apply for and get more information from the Collection Board under the Danish Agency for Civil Affairs (in Danish). In your application, you must be able to prove that the collected funds will be used for the purposes you have stated. Notice that approved fundraisers, will generally be held outdoors on the UCPH campus in an area designated by Campus Service.

No fundraisers without permission

If a fundraiser is organized without the approval of UCPH and the Fundraising Board, the fundraiser is illegal and the Fundraising Board can report the fundraiser to the police.
