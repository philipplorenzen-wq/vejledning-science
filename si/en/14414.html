The SU activity controls are conducted twice a year in March and September. In these two months, you need to meet the SU activity requirement. If you do not meet the requirement, your SU will be terminated.

In the table below, you can see how many ECTS you need to pass in relation to how many grant portions you have received.

 *No later than 7 months after your programme has started, the SU administration at the UCPH will check whether you have started your studies. As a starting point, it will be checked whether you have passed the exams or as a mnimum, participated in them. Your SU will be terminated if it is found that you have not started your studies.

If you have not started your programme due to illness or similar, you can apply for additional grant portions.

Example of inactivity:

At the time of the September payment, Mark has received 25 grant portions on his programme. Mark has passed 80 ECTS. Since Mark has not passed a minimum of 90 ECTS, he does not meet the SU activity requirements. He is therefore declared inactive, and his SU will be terminated from November onwards.
