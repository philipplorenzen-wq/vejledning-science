Kantinen (the Canteen) at DIKU is a lively place, where professors and students from all year groups meet. It functions as DIKU's social gathering spot - we eat, drink, study, and have a cozy time.

We have a student-driven kitchen, where you as a user have to take part in the daily operation. It is always possible to buy cheap drinks, and you are welcome to prepare food, as long as you remember to clean after yourself.

In addition to this, Kantinen also hosts events at DIKU e.g. the Christmas Dinner.

Kantinen is for now located at the DIKU-pavillon in the middle of Universitetsparken 11.
