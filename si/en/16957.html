I've taken other courses than my pre-approved ones?

If, for one reason or another, you have passed other courses than your pre-approved ones, you should point this out when applying for credit transfer. Accordingly, you do not need to submit an application to change or annul your pre-approval at this time.

What to do:

  1. Apply for credit transfer by complying with the usual procedures and deadlines.
  2. Indicate in the form whether the individual courses have been pre-approved or not, and whether they are to replace compulsory or restricted elective courses.

The Study Board will then decide on credit transfer for the courses.
