If you are currently having to make a specific decision, there are several things you may like to think about in the process.

5 tips for making good decisions: 

  1. Base your decision on what is important to you. It is wise to seek advice from others who know you well or have experience or knowledge of the subject. But see their advice as inspiration. In the end, only you know which decision is most right for you.
  2. Think back on a previous decision. What helped you to make your final choice? What insights did you gain from it, for example about which direction you want to take? Remember that you learn from all the decisions you make – even if they are not perfect.
  3. Decisions are not just about hopes and dreams, but also about personal and practical factors. What is possible? What best matches your present energy levels and competences?
  4. Your choices will always depend on the situation. You cannot predict the future, but when you have reflected on your decision, you can be at peace that you did what you could based on what you knew at the time.
  5. Find satisfaction in the choice – a lot has probably been decided for you earlier in your schooling and education – now you are being allowed to decide for yourself.

Choosing one thing over another is often a matter of priorities.
