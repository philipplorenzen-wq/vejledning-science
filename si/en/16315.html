Your deadline for appealing depends on the type of exam you have taken.

Exam types Deadline for appeals
Oral exams 2 weeks after the exam date
Written exams 6 weeks after the exam date
Written take-home assignments
6 weeks after the submission date
Courses with ongoing tests
6 weeks after the exam week is over

As a general rule, if you miss the deadline for appeals, your appeal will not be considered.


Special exemptions for appeal deadlines

In very special cases, you may be granted a postponement of the appeal deadline if you apply before the deadline. Applications for postponement of appeal deadlines are assessed on an individual basis.


You can apply for a postponement of the deadline if you, for example:

  • wish to appeal against a grade and have not received the reasoning behind your grade from your lecturer
  • have been unable to access your submitted exam paper in time.


How to apply for permission to appeal after the deadline

Submit your application for postponement of the deadline before the deadline for appeals. Follow the normal procedure for appeals and write the following in the comments field:

  1. that you also want to apply for a postponement of the deadline
  2. why you want to apply for a postponement.
