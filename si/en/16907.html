Gælder for målgrupperne: Bioinformatik - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Projects-SCI-BINF.aspx

Apart from the master's thesis, you can also choose one or more Bioinformatics Projects and an Individual Project in Bioinformatics as part of the restricted elective courses, and you can choose a Project in Practice, a Project outside the course scope or do a thesis preparation project before you start on your thesis as part of your elective credits.

  • Start to think about your interests and look for a thesis project early.
  • Bioinformatics projects can prepare you for your future thesis project and guide you to find your interests.
  • Use UCPH Projects & Jobs.
  • If you are interested in projects outside the university you can try to find a thesis project or project in practice in the industry (e.g. Novozymes, Chr. Hansen etc.).
  • Some students know exactly what kind of projects they are looking for, but if you don't - don't worry - most students end up thinking that their project is the most interesting project, when they get deep down into the details, even if they didn't have any ideas from the beginning.
