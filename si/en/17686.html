If you reside abroad for more than 6 months, it is a requirement to report a change of address to a foreign one in the Civil Registration System. Therefore, before leaving Denmark, make sure to report your departure to the municipality you reside in. You can do this through Borger.dk (https://www.borger.dk/bolig-og-flytning/flytning_oversigt/udrejse-fra-danmark-i-mere-end-6-maaneder)
