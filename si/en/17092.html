You also have the possibility to carry out a Project in Practice or a Project outside the course scope, but you have to consider carefully if you wish to replace the learning outcomes from a course with a Project in Practice or a Project outside the course scope. Please note that a Project in Practice can only be done with a supervisor from SCIENCE.

Your Master's thesis project is an important part of your education. It is your own responsibility to find a project and a supervisor, but I refer you to look at departmental databases over project proposals and the departments' research homepages. You are free to choose a project with a supervisor from either SCIENCE or SUND.
If you do not know in which topic to carry out your thesis project, you might consider the following:

  • Several departments have project databases, in which you can search or browse. You can also look at the homepages of the sections and individual researchers. See for example UCPH Projects and Jobs.
  • Even if a research group has not posted a vacant project, it is possible to contact the group leader to hear about the possibilities for doing a project in the group.
  • Did you meet a good researcher presenting an interesting field e.g. at a course? Then reach out to them.
  • Before you contact a group leader about a project, it is highly recommended that you read a little about the area of research and the work carried out in the group (homepage or publications). When contacting the group leader, make sure to explain why you find the area of research interesting.
  • It is also possible to carry out your Master's thesis project in a company. In this case, you need an internal supervisor from either SCIENCE or SUND.
