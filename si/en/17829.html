Can and should I take leave of absence?

If you fall ill, you can apply for leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances. If you do not need to receive SU while taking a break from your studies, taking leave of absence may be a good solution. It could be, for example, that you have another source of income (e.g. unemployment benefits) or that you do not need an income during the period of leave.


The disadvantages of taking leave of absence are that you:

  • cannot receive SU while on leave
  • must not be studying actively
  • may lose your right to live in student accommodation (ask at your hall of residence).


The advantages of taking leave of absence are that:

  • you do not have to worry about your studies while you are away
  • your study activity requirements are suspended
  • you do not use your monthly SU grant portions.
