How do I write a written assignment?

If you need to write a written assignment, this will be stated in your curriculum under the interim arrangement for your discontinued course.

A written assignment is based on the content of the discontinued course.

Supervisor for written assignment

Your supervisor for the paper is usually the course coordinator for the discontinued course.

If it is not possible for the course coordinator to act as your supervisor, your head of studies may help you find a new supervisor.


Time and registration

You may decide yourself which block you want to do your written assignment in. But please contact your supervisor first and ask if she or he is available the preferred block.

You must write and submit your written assignment within the block structure. This means

  • Courses equivalent to 7.5 ECTS: during blocks 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5.
  • Courses equivalent to 15 ECTS: in 2 blocks spread over blocks 1+2, 2+3, 3+4, 4+5 or 5+1. Alternatively, you can write your written assignment full-time in 1 block.

How to register for a written assignment

You must register for the written assignment by the end of the late-registration period for the block in which you want to write your paper.

Register for a written assignment


Contact your supervisor about content, syllabus, etc.

You must contact your supervisor yourself for guidance on the content, syllabus, time schedule, structure and submission of your paper.
The forms of assessment and grading of your written assignment must be identical to those used in the discontinued course.
