how do I change my name at UCPH?

If you change your name officially, it will automatically be changed in all UCPH systems after a few days.

If you wish to use a different name than the name UCPH automatically retrieves from the civil registration office, you can change it by filling out the form 'Get Study Administrative Help'

Use the topic 'Other'. Remember to write:

  • Your name
  • The name you want to appear in UCPH's systems
  • A brief explanation of your name change

Get Study Administrative Help

Please note that the name you changed to will be shown in all UCPH study administrative systems, e.g. Absalon and on your student card.

If you have name protection in the National Registry, you can register a name in self-service. 

If you no longer wish to have name protection

If you want to remove your name and address protection, you need to do it on borger.dk (in Danish).
