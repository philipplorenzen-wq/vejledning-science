As a new student – and with a functional impairment  – there are many things to keep track of: your studies, your social life and maybe even a new city. Because of your impairment, you may have to see your doctor and you may be less productive from time to time meaning that you cannot study at the same pace as your fellow students.

One way to get through your studies is to get an overview of your specific needs – and then plan accordingly. If you draw up a study plan, it can help you prioritise and remember, among other things, application deadlines.
