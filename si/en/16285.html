How much leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances may I take?

How much leave of absence you can get depends on your reason(s) for applying for leave of absence.


Leave of absence due to childbirth or adoption

In case of childbirth, you can get up to 1 year's parental leave.

If you want to take leave of absence due to childbirth, you must do so before the child is 18 months old.


Leave of absence due to military service or UN service

In case of leave of absence due to military service or UN service, you can be granted leave of absence during the period in which you have been called up.


Leave of absence due to illness, death, etc.

In case of leave of absence due to serious illness, death, etc., an assessment of your specific situation will determine how much leave you can get.

You should initially apply for the leave of absence you need and can document that you need here and now. If necessary, you can then apply for extended leave of absence later on.
