An academic Internship abroad is a stay for which you establish contact to a workplace yourself and agree on how the stay will proceed.

Before arranging an academic internship, it is, however, a good idea to examine:

  • whether your curriculum allows you to do an academic internship
  • what the requirements are for transferring credit for the stay.


An academic internship abroad can, for example, take place in:

  • large or small companies
  • organisations and NGOs
  • embassies, the European Parliament and trade offices
  • or as part of a research group at a university.

An academic internship usually lasts 3-6 months, but it is completely up to you to agree on the duration of your stay with the internship site in question.
