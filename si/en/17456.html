Your complaint will be processed as follows:

  1. The complaint will be sent to the relevant recipients. For example, examiner and co-examiner if it is about an assessment.
  2. Within 14 days (not including July and public holidays) of receiving the complaint, the recipients shall send their statement on the case to Student Affairs.
  3. You will receive the examiner's statement for review and have 1 week to comment afterwards, if relevant.
  4. Your comments will be submitted to the complaint recipient if there is new information or arguments.
  5. Statements and comments may be given until the matter is adequately clarified.
  6. The university decides based on the other stakeholders' statements and your comments on those statements.
  7. You will receive a decision in the case via your KUmail.
