Mathematicians find jobs in many parts of society

  • Going into the private sector.
    Often in banks, coding, statistics, insurance, pharma, start-ups and many many other places.
  • Teaching in high school (gymnasium), etc.
    This is a very important part of ensuring that the next generation appreciates mathematics.
  • Continuing doing research.
    This usually requires doing a PhD.

But you should not feel limited by this list. Mathematicians learn to think analytically, and can be great problem solver;  these are skills which are in demand in many places.
