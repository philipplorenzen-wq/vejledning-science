From the outset, you must be aware of whether your absence will have consequences for your ability to meet the examination prerequisites for the course.

See if your course has examination prerequisites in the course description 

What to do in case of absence

  1. Talk to your course coordinator and continuously assess with him or her whether you can still meet the examination prerequisites.
  2. Submit a notification of illness for the exam if you and the course coordinator assess that your illness will prevent you from taking the exam.
Alternatively, you may apply to withdraw from the whole course if this is an elective or a restricted elective course for you and you do not want to take the course again.


If you need to submit notification of illness

  1. contact your doctor immediately to obtain documentation of your illness. Read more on this page under 'What are the requirements for documentation from my doctor?'
  2. submit a notification of illness as soon as possible by filling out an online form via the button below and uploading your documentation no later than 8 calendar days from and including the final day of the course.
    If your documentation isn’t ready by the deadline, you’ll need to upload a document instead, stating when you expect to be able to submit it. 
Notify of illness
If your notification of illness is approved, you will avoid using an exam attempt. You will usually receive a reply within 6 weeks. 

You must assess whether you have to

  • register for the course again
  • register for the exam without following the course.

Read the course description and talk to your cours e coordinator about what is possible for you.

If you don't want to take an elective/restricted elective course again

If you do not want to take the course again and it is either an elective or a restricted elective course, you do not have to submit a notification of illness. Instead, you must apply for a dispensation to withdraw from the course after the deadline.
If you use or have used an exam attempt for the course, it is very difficult to withdraw.

Read more about how to apply to be withdrawn from a course after the deadline
