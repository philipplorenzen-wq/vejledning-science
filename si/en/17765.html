How much extra time do I get, when I take parental leave with SU?

When you give notify of parental leave with SU, your maximum duration of study will be prolonged, so that you have more time to focus on your family. This will enter into force at the earliest from the date you announce your parental leave.


Your maximum duration of study is prolonged with 1 year.

If you start your maternity leave/become a parent during a block, your maximum duration of study will be extended further with an extra block.
