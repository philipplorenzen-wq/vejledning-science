What if I suddenly fall ill during an exam at SUND?

If you fall ill during an exam, you must

  • Give notice of absence due to illness as soon as possible via the form below
  • Attach valid documentation.

Important – be aware that: 

  • The deadline to give notice depends on your exam type
  • If you fall ill during an exam at South Campus, you must also follow the procedure described below in addition to giving notice via the form.
  • You lose an exam attempt if you
    • fail to submit documentation of your illness or submit it after the deadline.
    • submit any assignment - even if you give notice of absence due to illness in time afterwards.
Give notice of absence
Your deadline depends on your exam type

Please note that your deadline to give notice of absence due to illness depends on the exam type:

Exams at South Campus AND oral exams

You must give notice of absence due to illness no later than 8 calendar days (i.e. the same weekday the following week) after your

  • exam at South Campus, or
  • oral exam

Take-home assignments

You can provide notice of absence due to illness until your exam submission deadline.

South Campus exams: You must also do the following

  • Summon an invigilator
  • "Submit" your exam digitally with the invigilator: 
    • Under 'Submit', select 'Do not submit the exam for assessment'
    • Then select 'Not submitting for assessment due to illness'.
