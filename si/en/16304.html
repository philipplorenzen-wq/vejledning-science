Which courses can I take in the same block?

You can combine courses in different schedule groups – e.g. schedule groups A and C.

If your courses are in the same schedule group or the same subgroup, classes and perhaps also exams in these courses are likely to clash.   


Courses equivalent to 15 ECTS may take up a whole block, and no account is therefore taken of the schedule groups when planning these courses.


You are responsible for ensuring that your courses and exams do not clash.
