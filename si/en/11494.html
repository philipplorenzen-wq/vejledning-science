If you are deferred in your programme due to other special circumstances, you can apply for additional grant portions in accordance with the SU regulations.

Moreover, you can apply for additional grant portions in the event of:

  • Council work
  • Illness

The provision on other special conditions applies to cases in which there have been exceptional circumstances, in the sense that a situation has arisen which must be characterised as being completely unexpected.

This means that conditions that are serious for you as a student, but which nevertheless must be considered as part of life, usually does not award additional grant portions.
