Stop/restrict the fire if possible

  • Put out smaller fires if possible.
  • Use carbon dioxide fire extinguishers (CO2) on oil, liquids and electrical installations.
  • Use water fire extinguishers and hoses on solid materials.
                • Close doors and windows, if possible.

                               NB: Do not expose yourself to danger.

Call 112 and alert the fire brigade

  • Even though you've pressed the alarm, it is always a good idea to call and explain the situation.
  • Call and provide additional information if possible, even though the automatic fire alarm is activated.

Evacuate the building if necessary

  • Follow instructions for evacuation – see Evacuation elsewhere on the page.


Call UCPH Emergency service 35 33 88 00

  • Call and describe the situation even if the alarm button has been pressed.
