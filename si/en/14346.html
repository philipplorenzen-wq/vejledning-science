Are scholarships taxable?

Placering: SU and grants › Grants › UCPH grants

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Are-scholarships-taxable-ku.aspx

As a rule, you must pay tax on scholarships. The majority of UCPH's scholarships are what is known as needs scholarships and are therefore taxable.

Travel grants are reported as tax-free.
