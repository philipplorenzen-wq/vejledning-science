When can I apply for SU for my new programme?

Placering: SU and grants › Your SU › SU and change of programme

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: gxd · Københavns Universitet - Kandidat · Tandplejeruddannelsen - P-Bachelor

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/When-can-I-apply-for-SU-for-my-new-programme-SU-KU.aspx

At the earliest, you can apply for SU one month before you start the programme. This means that if you are enrolled as of 1 September, you can apply for SU on 1 August.

Please note that your application for SU will not be processed until your acceptance of the place is registered.

You must fill out the application electronically via minSU on www.su.dk.
