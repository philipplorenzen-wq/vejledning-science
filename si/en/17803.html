Gælder for målgrupperne: Biologi - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Your-options-after-graduating-SCI-BIOL.aspx

Biologists find jobs within many different fields: Medical industry, Environmental organizations, NGO's, Biotech companies, Private consultancies, Dissemination and communication, IT, Public administration (Ministries, Agencies, Regions and Municipalities), High School teaching, Governmental research institutions, Universities, etc. Keep an eye on events where biologists and employers tell about career paths and requested competences. You also have the possibility to follow the elective Science course “Entrepreneurship and Innovation".

It is an advantage to be able to demonstrate experience in the “real world". This can be obtained by a relevant student job, but you may also choose to make a Project in Practice with a private company or a public institution. Additionally, you can choose to do your Master's thesis in collaboration with a private company or a public institution. Many of the supervisors have relevant contacts.

Some of you will enter a PhD-program, typical with the offset in the thesis work and resulting publications. This is the entrance to continued research work, but many PhD's also later obtain jobs far from their research specialization, taking advantage of the general competences acquired (data and literature handling, report writing etc.).

  • Start building your CV during your master program - do not forget that you after five years of studies have many general academic qualifications: data analysis, literature handling, presentations graphically as well as different report formats, writing and discussing academic problems in English, computer skills etc.
  • Note that in your Study start modules in Absalon is a number of specific career modules about career paths, work experience during your studies, networking and how to get help and inspiration to work on your career
  • Networking is extremely important, so join the Alumni network and relevant LinkedIn groups.
  • Note that there are specific deadlines for applying to Erasmus support for your travel, 15 May and 15 November
