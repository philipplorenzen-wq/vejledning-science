How do I get a student card for University College Absalon?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Student-card-Absalon-BIOS-SCI.aspx

At Kalundborg you need another student card, besides your UCPH student ID, if you want to access University College Absalon outside the ordinary opening hours.

During the first weeks of your programme, you will receive your student card for University College Absalon from staff at UCPH.
