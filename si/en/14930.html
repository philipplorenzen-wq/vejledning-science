You can go on a student exchange program in most parts of the world.

You can get an overview of all the places you can go to in the application portal Mobility Online. You can apply for an exchange spot among all the universities that are listed in the system.

For more information click on the fact sheets and read about each university and exchange agreements. You can also read about:

  • What requirements you must meet
  • How many exchange spots does University of Copenhagen have at the host university
