Why has UCPH conducted the inclusion survey?

Placering: Your student life › Well-being in your study life › Inclusion survey

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Why-is-UCPH-conducting-the-survey-KU.aspx

Everyone at UCPH should feel welcome regardless of background, orientation and needs. That is why the inclusion survey has examined students’ and employees’ experiences of inclusion. 
The survey provides a new knowledge base for initiatives and the continued diversity, equity and inclusion efforts, which is one of the prerequisites in UCPH’s 2030 strategy, Creating benefit for more people. 
The survey supplements existing surveys of students’ well-being and the study environment at UCPH, including the Study Environment Survey. It identifies experiences of exclusion, difficulties with belonging, study/career barriers and accessibility issues. The survey covers questions about slightly different background variables and areas than it is possible to cover in the Study Environment Survey.
