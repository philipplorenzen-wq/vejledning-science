If you wish to use your elective options to obtain a specific academic profile, I have made recommended study tracks for inspiration.

When you plan your studies, make sure you comply with the number of ECTS credits, which constitutes your programme structure.

See the structure of your programme and the recommended study plan.

In the following, I give some examples of how you may choose courses in the first year. 

Please note that there are many other possible combinations. You may pursue a clear profile or acquire broad competencies or a combination.

If you are admitted to the programme between September 2019 and February 2022, the course Advanced Biotechnology and Intellectual Property Rights is compulsory for you.
