MinSU is the name of the self-service system on SU.dk.

Here you can apply for the State Education Grant and Loan Scheme (in Denmark known by its acronym 'SU') for your programme at the University of Copenhagen, view information about your SU, and make necessary changes.

To access the system, you must use NemID.

On MinSU, you can, among other things:

  • Apply for and deselect SU and student loans
  • Get an overview of your SU, end date, etc.
  • Extend your programme
  • Track your study progress
  • Apply for extra monthly grants due to childbirth
  • Apply for the addition of monthly grants
