Block 3 and 4 the first year is the best option to go abroad since this is where most elective/restricted elective courses are placed. However, you do need to change your study plan a bit because the Major Subject Project is normally placed in block 4.

The Major Subject Project can be done in all 5 blocks (including the summer block), so it is easy to make an alternative study plan that will enable you to study abroad.

  • You can make an alternative study plan using block 5, year 1 for the Major Subject Project
  • Another possibility I would recommend, is that you find a thesis project where it is possible to carry out part of the project in a lab outside SCIENCE and Denmark. In this case, you should find a supervisor who has collaborations with other laboratories or universities.
