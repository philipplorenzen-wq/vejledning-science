3 places where you can go for professional guidance about your decision and your options:

  • Study and Career Guidance at UCPH Study Guidance offers guidance about, for example, any doubts you may have about your studies as well as available options and solutions for you as a student at SCIENCE in your current situation.
    Contact UCPH Study Guidance
     
  • Studievalg Danmark, Center Hovedstaden og Bornholm – the regional education and career guidance centre in the Copenhagen area – offers guidance about your study options in Denmark and the important choice you need to make if you are considering a new study programme.
    Contact Studievalg Danmark, Center Hovedstaden og Bornholm
      
  • The eGuidance of UddannelsesGuiden (the Education Guide) can provide you with digital answers and guidance about study options and jobs.
    Receive eGuidance from UddannelsesGuiden
