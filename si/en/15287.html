I have been hacked. What should I do?

Placering: IT and facilities on campus › IT and support › IT-security

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Hacked.aspx

If you suspect that others have gained unauthorized access to your university account and services such as e-mail, contact IT Support as soon as possible. 

Make sure to create a new and secure password once you get access to your account again.
