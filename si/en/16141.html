How do I submit a notification of illness to SUND?

If you fall ill for an exam, you must:

  • Give notice of absence due to illness via the form below - no later than 8 calendar days after the exam (i.e., the same weekday in the following week)
  • Ensure that you attach your doctor's note to your notice of illness.

If you are unable to upload the documentation before the deadline, you must indicate in the form when you will be able to submit it via the form.

Give notice of absence 

A doctor's note is necessary documentation

The doctor's note must describe that you are prevented from attending the exam due to illness. 

The doctor's stamp and signature must be stated in the doctor's note.

The documentation may also be an emergency room report or an admissions report.
