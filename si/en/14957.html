FEF is the Student Association for Nutrition and Food Science Students: 

  • Bachelor students in Fødevarer & Ernæring (Foods and Nutrition)

Master students in

  • Food Science and Technology
  • Integrated Food Studies
  • Food Innovation and Health
  • Human Nutrition 

FEF hosts social and study-related events. They also act as a spokesperson on behalf of the students to both FOOD, NEXS and IFRO. FEF works on improving the overall satisfaction of students at Frederiksberg Campus by being represented in the SCIENCE Students Association and collaborating with the four other student associations at the campus. 

There is a one off fee, but this gives you: 

  • Participation in events – often at a reduced price or even free 
  • Possibility of meeting and getting to know students from other fields of study and new and older students 
  • Membership in interest groups like: Dairy Forum and Beverage Forum
