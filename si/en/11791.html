When showing your student card it gives you discounts on the entrance to the University's many museums:

  • Botanical Garden
  • The gardens at Frederiksberg Campus
  • Geological Museum
  • Medical Museion
  • Museum of Herbal Medicine
  • Veterinary Historical Museum
  • Zoological Museum
  • The Arboretum
  • The Pometum
  • The Øresund Aquarium
