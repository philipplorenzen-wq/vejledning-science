Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-happens-if-I-break-the-exam-rules-SCI.aspx

You may be expelled from the exam and, at worst, from the University if you do not follow the University's and SCIENCE's rules for exams. These include:

  • UCPH's rules and regulations
  • SCIENCE's course and exam rules
  • the instructions in the course description
  • the examiner's/invigilator's instructions.

If you are expelled from the exam, it will count as an exam attempt.
