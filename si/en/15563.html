You can see the dates of the exam in your course in the general list of exams at SCIENCE.
The exam dates are published twice a year:
For blocks 1, 1-2 and 2: 15 May  
For blocks 3, 3-4, 4 and 5: 15 November
See the list of exam dates at SCIENCE 

See the list of exam rooms at SCIENCE


Oral exam

You can see precise information about time and place on Digital Exam no later than 7 days before the first exam day.


Written exam

You can find exam number, exam date, time, and place for your written exams:

Go to Self service > Exam plan – time and place > Digital exams

You can see the information no later than 7 days before your exam.


Written take-home assignment

You will recieve the exact times and deadlines on digital exam. When the information is visible on Digital Exam depends on the type of assignment.

Written take-home assignments with both a specific start and submissiontime:
The exact times of start (receiving your exam question) will be visible at the latest 7 days before the start date.

Written take-home assignments with (only) a submissiontime:
The exact deadline will be visible at the latest 7 days before the submission date.


You will be notified via your KUmail when information about a new exam is available on Digital Exam.

Go to Digital Exam
