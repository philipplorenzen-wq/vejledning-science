It might be an advantage for you to take leave of absence due to childbirth (without SU) if you

  • have other sources of income while on leave of absence as you are not entitled to receive SU (e.g. maternity/paternity benefit)
    Read about the maternity/paternity benefit rules at borger.dk
      
  • want to take a complete break from your studies, and do not want to be able to participate in any kind of study activity (exams, classes and lectures, supervision, etc.).

If you want to apply for leave of absence

You can read all about how to apply, and about the applicable rules under Leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances.
