What do I do about the study activity requirements?

If you are ill for a long time and do not take leave of absence, you may be unable to live up to the UCPH and SU study activity requirements. This applies to:

  • Maximum duration of study
  • The SU study activity requirements

When to apply for a dispensation from the University's requirements or contact the Danish Students' Grants and Loans Scheme (SU) depends on the requirement(s) that you are unable to comply with. 


Get an overview with a study plan

Start by preparing a study plan based on how long you – and possibly your doctor/psychologist – expect you to be away from your studies. This will give you an overview of where you may run into difficulties with the study activity requirements.

Prepare a study plan for how you can study on reduced hours and possibly take a break while you are ill


Maximum duration of study

You must apply for a dispensation if you can see and document that you cannot complete your study programme within the maximum duration of study.

When applying for a dispensation, please note:

  • Specify in your application and study plan how much extra time you need.
  • Show in your study plan that you will be able to complete your study programme if granted the extension you are applying for.

Read more about the maximum duration of study


Be aware of the SU study activity requirements

If you are delayed in your studies by six months or more, it may affect your right to receive SU.
