Relevant jobbanks where you can find predefined job or project proposals:

  • UCPH Projects and Jobs is the official project and job portal of the University of Copenhagen.
  • Jobindex is the biggest jobportal in Denmark.
  • Jobfinder lists jobs and internships relevant for engineers, science graduates and IT professionals.
  • Greater Copenhagen Career portal lists jobs and internships at companies in the Greater Copenhagen region, especially suited for international students and graduates.
  • Work in Denmark is a job portal targeted international students and graduates.
