If I don’t pass the re-exam, will it affect my ability to meet my study activity requirements?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/If-I-don%E2%80%99t-pass-the-re-exam,-will-it-affect-my-ability-to-meet-my-study-activity-requirements-SCI.aspx

Taking a re-exam will not affect your ability to meet your study activity requirements if you register for and pass the re-exam in the re-exam period that is in the same block.


You need to pay particular attention to your study activity requirements if you

  • choose to postpone your re-exam to a later exam period
  • do not pass the re-exam.

If your re-exam means that you cannot meet your study activity requirements, you need to apply for a dispensation .
