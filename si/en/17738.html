The master's thesis (30 ECTS credits) completes your study programme. As a general rule, you are responsible for finding your own thesis supervisor. It is often best to ask a teacher from a course that you like or who works on your favorite topics personally. Have a look at the research being done at the department  and contact a group member for advice and recommendations. Denmark is known for its casual style, so feel free to talk to your teachers. If you cannot find any supervisor, I may help you to find one. My advice: start looking 1-2 blocks before your planned thesis to find your supervisor. Popular supervisors and areas are quickly "booked".

Consider doing a project outside the course scope before starting on your master's thesis. This is a good way to explore an interesting topic and to practice your research and writing skills before you start your ,master's thesis. You may also work in a small group on a project, which is sometimes more enjoyable. Who knows, maybe you get inspiration for an exciting research topic to work on in your thesis?
