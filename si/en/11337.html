Contact UCPH Study Guidance if you need
  • special conditions in class
  • special exam conditions
  • information about your possibilities for special educational support (SPS)
  • to apply for dispensation for study activity requirements 
  • help with challenges related to physical accessibility, e.g. access to buildings, rooms or facilities
  • a confidential conversation about your student life.

Contact SU at UCPH if you
  • need to apply for extra SU grant portions
  • want to know about the possibility of receiving a disability supplement (if your impairment prevents you from having a student job) 
  • have made a study plan and wish to know whether it affects the SU study activity requirements

You can also seek support from

  • The University Chaplains
  • The Student Ambassador
  • The Student Counselling Service/Studenterrådgivningen
  • Disability organisations (such as SUHM)
  • Student associations and networks.
