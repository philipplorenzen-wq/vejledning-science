My recommendations:

  • You should make a LinkedIn profile as soon as possible. Keep it updated! Many recruiters would check your LinkedIn profile.
  • Get to know the companies - join a career event.
  • You should think about what kind of job you would like before you find you master thesis.


Career paths, including PhD:

You have the opportunity to hear about different career paths on one of the first Fridays in block 2 at the event "You first job as chemist". Here you will hear 3-4 alumni with the Master's degree in Chemistry from the University talk about their careers.


Where are the alumni working?

You can read about the transition to work life in the report Kemikandidaters møde med arbejdslivet (pdf, in Danish). The research is based on 179 alumni from the Master Programme in Chemistry. 75 % worked in the public sector and 25 % in private sector.
