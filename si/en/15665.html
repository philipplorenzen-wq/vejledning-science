You must first and foremost choose a topic that interests you and which you will want to work with for an extended period of time. However, the project must satisfy certain requirements.


Requirements for your topic:

  • You project must live up to the description of the learning outcome for project outside the course scope on your study programme.
    View the shared section of your curriculum
  • If you only use elective ECTS credits, your are free to choose a topic that you and your supervisor agree on.

  • If you use restrictive elective ECTS credits for your project (entirely or partly), your topic must fall within the academic scope of the group of the restricted electives.
