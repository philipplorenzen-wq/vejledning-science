Can I be withdrawn from a course after the deadline if my course is unsuitable for pregnant women?

You can apply for a dispensation to be withdrawn from an exam or course after the deadline if your course is found not to be compatible with being pregnant.


This may be relevant in connection with courses involving

  • experiments and harmful chemistry
  • particularly challenging physical activities.


You can use your maternity record as documentation when your reason for being withdrawn is solely due to your pregnancy and not to pregnancy-related discomfort or illness.
