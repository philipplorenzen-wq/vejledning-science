There are several things you need to have ready to complete the contract. So prepare well in advance of the registration deadline.

Before completing the contract, you must have discussed the project with your supervisor. Read more about what else you need to prepare before you can complete the contract in ‘What should I do before starting the project?’.

How to complete the contract for a thesis preparation project 
  • Under ‘The project counts as…’ select ‘Elective’ (only choose ‘Restricted elective’ if your curriculum allows it or you have been granted a dispensation).
  • Under ‘Project type’ select ‘Thesis preparation project’.
  • Under ‘ECTS credits’ select either ‘7.5 ECTS’ or ‘15 ECTS credits’.
  • Under ‘Assessment’ select '7-point grading scale'
  • Under ‘Exam format’ select the format you have agreed on with your supervisor. 

Complete the remaining fields so they match your situation.
If you are writing in a group, remember that you only need to complete one shared contract.

What happens after I have submitted my project contract?

Once you have submitted your contract, we will obtain signatures from the head of studies, the deputy head of department for education, and your supervisor.
When your project contract has been approved, you will be notified via your KUmail. Only then are you officially registered for your project.
