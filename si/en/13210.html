If you lose something at Kalundborg, there are different places you can enquire locally.

Teaching buildings

Contact Facility Service by

  • visiting Frontdesk at University College Absalon in Kalundborg
  • writing to facilityservice@pha.dk
  • calling +45 72 48 37 08 (Monday - Friday 8:00-13:00).


University College Absalon

Lost property can be seen in the cupboard in the canteen by the stairs leading up to the A1. Access is permitted by contacting Uddannelsesservice, room A1.11 on the 1st floor.
