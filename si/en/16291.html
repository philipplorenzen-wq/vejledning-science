Send your written complaint via your KUmail to UCPH Study Guidance (as an attachment).

Your complaint must include:

  1. A factual and thorough description of your specific points of dissatisfaction and why you are dissatisfied.
  2. The course title and course number, and the name of the lecturer.

You can complain either during or after the completion of the course.


How your complaint is processed

Your complaint is considered by the department offering the course, and they will send you a response. The relevant study board and head of studies are informed about the complaint and the department's response.
