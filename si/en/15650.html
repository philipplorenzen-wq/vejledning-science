You need to find up to 3 people you would like to work with. A project can have a maximum of 4 participants.

To write your project as a group you must:

  • work on the project during the same period
  • choose the same supervisor
  • complete and submit the project contract together  
  • (if you come from different study programmes) have the topic approved by your respective heads of studies.
