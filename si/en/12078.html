Study techniques are the methods you use – consciously or subconsciously – when you learn something new. 

Finding the right study techniques gives you: 

  • a better overview, sense of control and peace of mind – even during busy periods 
  • greater academic learning outcome 
  • more effective and prioritised preparation 
  • knowledge of how you work best, which will be useful – both while studying and in your future working life. 

What works for one person may not work for another, so experiment to find what works for you.
