There are many ways to become a successful Human Nutrition student. Find your own way in the programme. 
You can use elective courses, your Master's thesis and other projects to create a specific or more general profile of your Human Nutrition programme. I recommend that you make your choices based on your own interests. If you need sparring in order to qualify your choices, you can contact me or UCPH Study Guidance.
Before you sign up for an elective course, please remember to check the recommended academic qualifications and contact the course responsible if you have any questions regarding the course content or you are unsure you are qualified to follow the course.
