How early should I begin preparing to start my Master’s thesis?

It may take 3-4 months to prepare your Master's thesis.


Before starting the project, you must:

  • Choose a topic
  • Find a supervisor
  • Find a company (if you want to work with a company). Start approx. four months beforehand.
  • Fill out your contract and have it ready for approval before the registration deadline.


If you have a dispensation that gives you extra time for your thesis

What you should be aware of:

  • Your supervisor doesn't know that you have extra time for your Master's thesis. It is important that you tell your supervisor to be sure that your supervisor is available during the whole period.
  • You don't have to worry about your extra time when filling out your thesis contract. Your extra time will automatically be stated in your approved contract.
