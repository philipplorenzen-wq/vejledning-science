The course catalogue is a great tool for you to explore and manage the many elective courses on offer at UCPH.


Finding inspiration in the course catalogue

  1. Narrow down your search based on practicalities
    Select/fill in the basic categories that are already known, i.e.:
    • Academic year
    • Semester/block (Note: Select a semester and not a block if you are also looking for courses outside SCIENCE)
    • Level (Bachelor, Master, etc.)
  2. Specify the academic field
    Select a preferred department or faculty for your course subject.
    Skim the list of course names, which should now be more manageable, and click on any courses that catch your interest.
  3. Narrow down your search based on areas of interest
    Enter specific search words/topics in the search field if you want the course to include particular academic interests.

For example, you can search for words like: 'statistics', 'communication' or 'climate change'.
Your search will now show all available course descriptions that include your search word.


Check if the course fits your timetable

Before selecting a course, make sure that it fits with

  1. your available number of ECTS credits
  2. the schedule groups for your other courses and projects.

Find information about ECTS credits and schedule groups under 'Course information' on the right-hand side of the course description.
See what the schedule groups mean when planning your block


Read about formal requirements and recommended qualifications

If there are formal requirements that you must meet, or recommended academic qualifications that you should preferably have to follow the course, they are stated in a separate section in the course description.
Read it carefully to avoid being bound to a course which you do not have the academic qualifications to pass.


Consult the course coordinator

If you are unsure whether you meet the requirements or whether you have the necessary academic qualifications, you can consult the course coordinator. Find the coordinator's email address under 'Course information' on the right-hand side.
