I'm Kirsten Jørgensen and I'm your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies I know the academic contents of your programme in detail, and have contributed in determining your curriculum. My research area is Biotechnology finding biosolutions using plants and I teach on the courses in plant biology and will be involved in the courses focusing on the regulations for production and GMO approval.

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here is my advice and my recommendations for completing your master's programme in Biosolutions successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.
