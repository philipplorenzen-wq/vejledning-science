Is leave of absence a good solution for me?

If you do not need to receive SU while taking a break from your studies, taking leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances may be the best solution for you. It might be, for example, that you have another source of income (e.g. unemployment benefits) or that you do not need an income during this period.


The disadvantages of taking leave of absence are that you

  • cannot receive SU while on leave
  • must not be studying actively
  • may lose your right to live in student accommodation (ask at your hall of residence).


The advantages of taking leave of absence are that

  • you do not have to worry about your studies while you are away
  • your study activity requirements are suspended
  • you do not use your monthly SU grant portions.

The alternative to taking leave of absence is to study at reduced hours while you receive SU and apply for any exemptions you may need.
