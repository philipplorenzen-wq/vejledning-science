If you are ill for more than one block, you can reorganise your studies to suit your needs.


You have 2 options when you are ill for a long time:

  • Apply for leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances without SU (possibly with other income).
  • Continue studying as an 'active student' with SU, but without taking courses or sitting exams in the period.

If you choose to continue studying as an 'active student', you may need one or more exemptions, which you can apply for using a single form.
