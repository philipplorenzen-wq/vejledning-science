How can I receive disability supplement?

To receive the disability supplement your impairment must cause significant limitations in your ability to have a student job alongside your study programme.
In addition, you must:

  • Receive SU or a completion loan
  • Provide documentation that your impairment is permanent

A permanent impairment can, for example, be:

  • Blindness and strong visual impairment
  • Deafness and strong hearing impairment
  • A psychological or psychosocial disorder (for example, as a result of anxiety, phobias, autism or personality disorders)
  • Mobility deficits due to severe muscle and neuronal disorders (e.g. , muscular dystrophy, multiple sclerosis, severe back disorders and spastic paralysis)
