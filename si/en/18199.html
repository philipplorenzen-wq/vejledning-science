Can I change my leave of absence?

You may extend, shorten and completely cancel your leave of absence before it has begun.


If you want to extend your leave of absence

When your leave of absence is coming to an end, you must submit a new application following the same procedure as when you first applied.
Your leave of absence will start at the earliest on the date you submit the application


If you want to cancel your leave of absence – before it has started

You can only cancel your leave of absence if you do so before the end of the late-registration period for courses and projects for the block in which you want to take a leave of absence.


What to do:

  1. Send an email stating that you wish to cancel your leave of absence to studyguidance-science@adm.ku.dk  
  2. Register for courses/projects in the block before the end of the late-registration period.

Find the late registration period under Course registration


If you want to interrupt your leave of absence – after it has started

If you wish to resume your studies prematurely, you may interrupt your leave of absence when you wish to start studying again.

What to do:

  1. Send an email to studyguidance-science@adm.ku.dk stating that you want to resume your studies and from which block. You cannot interrupt your leave of absence retroactively.
  2. Notify the SU Office when you interrupt your leave of absence so that you can start receiving SU again.
  3. Register for exams and courses/projects. You may take any exams outstanding from previous courses in the same block – if you can register in time.

You can only register for courses in the next block.
