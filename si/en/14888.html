My role is to guide you academically through your study:

  • I can guide you about the academic content of your program and relevant areas of research. 
  • I can help you in choosing relevant courses and finding supervisors for your projects. 
  • I approve your contract for the Master’s thesis as well as potential projects in practice and projects outside the course scope.

I cannot help you with the rules and structure of your programme. For these questions, please contact UCPH Study Guidance.
