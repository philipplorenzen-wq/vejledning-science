If you do not apply for credit transfer before the deadline due to delayed documentation (transcript):
  1. The Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science will contact you if you are registered as being delayed by more than 30 ECTS according to the SU rules.
  2. You must reply to the agency explaining the reason for the delay and attaching your pre-approval.
It may affect your student grant (SU) if your credit transfer application, and thus the transfer of credits to your programme, is delayed.
