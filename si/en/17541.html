If you have any questions, you are always welcome to contact the information desk. It is possible to get both professional assistance, guidance to the printers and to lending questions.

The libraries are a natural part of your student life. There are printing and scanning facilities on both campus libraries, and you can find group study spaces or quiet areas.

Reading desks, whiteboards, and various technical equipment for use.
At KUB Nord you can also use the 3d printers, computers with special datalab software, or play with VR equipment.
