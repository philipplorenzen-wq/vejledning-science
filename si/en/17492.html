Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Do-I-need-to-bring-my-own-computer-to-the-exam-SCI.aspx

If it is a requirement that you bring and use your own computer, this will be stated in the course description under 'Exams'.

If you are not required to bring your own computer, you may only do so if

  •  the course description expressly lists it as a permitted exam aid
  •  or all exam aids are permitted.


Power supply for your computer

In exams where computers are permitted, the University will provide plug sockets. You must bring your own power cord.  


You are responsible for your aids and devices

The exam invigilators cannot help you if you have left something behind or have technical problems with your own computer.
