As a student with one or more neurological impairments, you may experience study difficulties in the form of, for example:
  • Concentration and memory problems
  • Difficulties with starting, planning, structure and overview
  • Sensitivity to light and sounds
  • Speech disorders
  • Binocular vision disorders
  • Dizziness and gait disturbances
  • Severe migraine attacks
  • Constant feeling of fatigue and hypersomnia
  • Pain

In order to be able to study on equal terms with other students, you may need support or aids.
