When you fill out and send the contract 

What you should know when filling out the contract:

  • The date on which you would like to start your Master's thesis.
  • Who you will be working with (supervisor, any host company, group members, etc.).
  • The Master's thesis problem formulation (max. 2,000 characters).
  • A plan for the supervision that you make with your primary supervisor.

Your plan for the supervision must describe the following:
  • How often and how the supervision will be carried out
  • What is expected from each of you at your meetings
  • Conditions concerning the collection of primary data/experimental work
  • General mutual expectations for the working relationship.

Guide to fill out your contract


3 steps to submitting your Master's thesis contract:

  1. Find the digital Master's thesis contract via Self Service under the 'Exams' tab.
  2. Fill out the contract with the help of your supervisor. 
    You can save or edit the contract until you press 'Ready for approval'. The contract is not valid, as long as the status is 'In progress'.
  3. Press 'Ready for approval' to submit the contract for approval.

Approval of thesis contract:

Your Master's thesis contract is only finally approved once you get a notification of the approval via KUmail a few days before your start date - at the latest. This rule also applies if you get the status “Approved" at Self service before the KUmail.
