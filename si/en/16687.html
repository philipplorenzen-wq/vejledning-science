We know that it can be difficult to ask for help in situations of discrimination, offensive behaviour or an unhealthy culture. In a safe environment, we offer to listen, support and guide you in moving forward. Some find it easiest to talk to people who are close, others prefer to talk to an outsider. You can also do both.

At UCPH, we are very keen to help and guide you if you need:

  • to discuss your situation with an outsider
  • information about your options and rights.

People who can help you at UCPH:

  • The student counsellors for your degree programme
    All student counsellors at UCPH are subject to a duty of confidentiality and are trained in listening and talking to students – even about difficult issues. They can also guide you on next steps, for example if you want to submit a complaint.
  • KUallies
    The UCPH diversity and inclusion consultants in KUallies provide the same services as the student counselling. They provide the opportunity to talk to someone outside your study environment. 
  • UCPH's student ambassador
    With impartial guidance and support, the student ambassador can help you understand your options if you decide to submit a complaint.
