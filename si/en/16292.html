When planning to take a course outside SCIENCE, you need to find out in advance 

  1. whether the faculty/university and the course allow you to take courses as a credit student, guest student, visiting student or exchange student (Different designations in different places)
  2. the deadline for applying to do courses at the other university/faculty
  3. when the course is offered – and whether the timing fits in with your study plan. 
    Find a study plan template in which the structure of your study programme has already been entered under programme structure
  4. whether you can fit the course into your programme depends on the number of ECTS credits.
    See your programme structure
  5. whether you meet any academic requirements or prerequisites applying to the course – if in doubt, check the course description or ask the lecturer.


Find information about your courses outside SCIENCE

  • at the home page of the relevant faculty or university
  • by contacting the student counsellors or the administration at the educational institution.
