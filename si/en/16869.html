You can do the following types of projects:

  • Project outside the course scope
  • Project in Practice 
    (not if you have the specialisation Soil, Water and Biodiversity)
  • Thesis preparation project 
    (only if you have the specialisation Soil, Water and Biodiversity EnvEuro, 2nd year at UCPH).

You can always use elective ECTS credits on your projects. In some cases, you can (also) use restricted elective ECTS credits.

Look under the different projects to see how many and which ECTS credits you can use on each project.

If you have the specialisation Chemistry, Toxicology and Health, projects may not exceed 30 ECTS credits in total.
If you have the specialisation Soil and Water, projects may not exceed 22.5 ECTS credits in total.
