If you are not happy with the outcome of your appeal, you can submit a further appeal against the decision.

You have 2 weeks from when you are informed of the decision to submit a well-founded written appeal. 


What to do

Send your appeal as an attached PDF file from your KUmail to klager-science@adm.ku.dk.

In the subject field of the email, write: 'Appeal of a complaint decision'.

You must use your KUmail to send your appeal digitally; otherwise the appeal will not be accepted.


Appeal procedure and possible outcomes

Your appeal will be handled by a board of appeals consisting of two external examiners, one lecturer and one student.


Possible outcomes of the case

  • your appeal is not upheld
  • your appeal is upheld and you are offered either:
    • a re-assessment by new examiners (only for written exams), or
    • a retake of the exam with new examiners.


If your appeal is not upheld

If you are unhappy with the academic assessment

You cannot complain to other authorities about the board of appeals' academic assessment of the case. The board of appeals has the final academic jurisdiction in relation to exam appeals.


If you are unhappy with legal issues

You can appeal to the Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science against the board of appeal's decision in the case.
