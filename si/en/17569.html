There are many different career fairs – and it is not possible to create an exhaustive list of relevant fairs.

The list of fairs below is therefore only for inspiration.

Go on a hunt for other relevant fairs by searching for career fairs, job fairs, or similar search words for example on Google.

Career fairs at SCIENCE:

  • SCIENCE Career Horizons – a whole career week in week 40 which includes  a career fair
  • DIKU holds an annual IT Career Day

Other fairs and events where the faculty is involved will be shared as study events on KUnet.
See the current study events on KUnet

Other relevant career fairs:

There are also career fairs at other faculties, universities, etc. You can, for example, check:

  • ITU's Career Week
  • The Faculty of Health and Medical Sciences' career fair for students
  • DTU Career Week
  • Synapse Life Science Career Fair
  • CBS Career Fair
  • Career Days


Don't hesitate to participate

In general, you should not be afraid to participate, even if you are not directly in the target group for the fair. Most major companies need various types of profiles.
