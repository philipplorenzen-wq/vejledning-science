You submit the project report in Digital Exam at the deadline written in your digital contract after it has been approved.

A couple of days before your deadline please ensure that it is possible for you to submit your project report in Digital Exam.

The submission of your project report is not final until the deadline for submission. If you regret submitting before the deadline, you may edit your project report or completely remove it to upload a new version.

Go to Digital exam

You submit your abstract by
  • attaching it to an email and sending it to studyguidance-science@adm.ku.dk 
  • write in the subject line: Abstract project in practice

How to submit as a group

Only one of you needs to submit the project report in Digital Exam on behalf of the group. This applies regardless of whether you are enrolled in the same or different study programmes.

All group members will be able to see that the report has been submitted when logging into Digital Exam.

If you are prevented from submitting by the deadline

If you e.g. fall ill in a way that prevents you from submitting the report by the deadline, you can apply for a dispensation to withdraw from the exam.
