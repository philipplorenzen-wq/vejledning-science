As an active student, you can create a Microsoft Azure subscription (Azure for Students) with a $100 credit. This subscription gives you free access to work with a wide range of Microsoft's Azure services until the credit has been used.

Please note the following:
  • The $100 credit expires after 12 months.
  • You can renew your subscription annually for as long as you remain a student.
  • If you do not use the service for 90 days, certain compute resources (processing power, memory, and storage capacity) may be removed by Microsoft in accordance with their terms and conditions.
  • Your access will be terminated when you leave your studies. Therefore, remember to transfer any setups, configurations, and data you wish to keep before then.
  • You must not use your subscription to store or process personal data.
  • You can find training materials and support for Microsoft Azure services on Microsoft Learn.
Read more and sign up online using your KU username at:
https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free/students/
