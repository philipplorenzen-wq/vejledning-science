Besides projects organized by the course coordinators as part of the individual courses, we have three major types of projects:

The Master's thesisis the main project of the study programme. Already in block 3 of the first year you should start thinking about which topic you want to work with in your thesis project, possibly already contacting some relevant researchers at the department, external companies or organizations for guidance and input. We will arrange a workshop with ideas for master thesis projects.

It is also possible do a Project in Practice with a public or private organization outside the university. It is a great opportunity to use some of the knowledge you have gained during your bachelor and master programmes in a professional context and to gain some work experience which you can use in job applications and in your later career.

Finally, it is also possible to do a Project outside the course scope, which is a small research project. You are responsible for finding a project and a supervisor. I'm happy to help out with suggestions on who to contact.
