You can receive the following scholarship rate for a stay outside Europe:
  • One semester: DKK 8,000
  • Two semesters: DKK 12,000
You can receive the following scholarship rate for a stay in Europe (the UK)
  • One semester: DKK 5,000
  • Two semesters: DKK 8,200

The internationalisation scholarship rates are fixed and are paid as a lump sum into your NemKonto account before you start your stay.
