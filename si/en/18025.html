You cannot apply for a new exchange spot if you have already been nominated for another spot.

You may cancel your allocated spot and apply for a new one. To cancel your spot, please send an email to outgoingstudents@adm.ku.dk.

Deadlines for cancelling an allocated spot:

  • 10 January if you wish to apply for an exchange spot in the 15 February application round.
  • 26 April if you wish to apply in the 15 May application round.

You may apply for exchange at two different host universities in the autumn and spring semesters of the same academic year.

However, you cannot apply for both in the same application round. If you wish to apply for an additional exchange spot within the same academic year, you must contact International Education (outgoingstudents@adm.ku.dk), who will open the application form for you if relevant. If you apply for exchange in two semesters, you must have 60 ECTS available, and we expect you to make use of both spots.

If you have already been nominated for a spot, students who have not yet been allocated a spot will be prioritized before you.
