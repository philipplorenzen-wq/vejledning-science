Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Your-options-after-graduating-SCI-BIOS.aspx

When you have completed your studies, there are many career possibilities. There will be career possibilities especially in the industry but also in the public job market. This will typically be in biotech companies within Industrial production of bio-based products and processes for production purposes, refinement and utilization of biological raw materials and enzymes for processing and optimizing bio-based products. Following business functions and/or areas such as 

  • Product development in biosolutions industry
  • Quality control and development
  • Engineering and process design
  • Proces development and optimization – automatization and robot technology
  • Regulatory processes
  • Analysis and product optimization
  • Advisors and consultants in governmental agencies
  • A PhD programme

Keep an eye on events where employers inform about career paths and requested competencies.

It is an advantage to be able to demonstrate experience in the “real world". This can be obtained by a relevant student job, but you may also choose to make a Project in Practice with a private company or a public institution. Additionally, you can choose to do your Master's thesis in collaboration with a private company or a public institution. Many of the supervisors have relevant contacts.
