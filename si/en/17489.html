As a general rule, you may not leave an oral exam until the examiners begin grading.

Be sure to get permission from the invigilator or examiners if you need to leave the preparation room or exam room.


If you leave the room without permission

If you leave the exam without getting permission, you will not be able to complete the exam. It will still count as a used exam attempt.

If you need to leave because of acute illness, you can apply to have the exam attempt cancelled due to illness.
