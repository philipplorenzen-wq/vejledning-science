Your primary supervisor must be employed at SCIENCE.
Your supervisor must be employed in one of the following job categories:

  • professor
  • associate professor/external lecturer
  • teaching associate professor
  • teaching lecturer 
  • post doc
  • assistant professor
  • senior researcher
  • senior consultant
  • senior adviser 
  • affiliate associate professor
  • affiliate professor


Possibility for co-supervisor with a different background

You and your primary supervisor might find it beneficial to have a co-supervisor for practical and academic support on your Master's thesis. Co-supervisors can have other titles than mentioned above, e.g. PhD student.

A co-supervisor can be from another faculty or university. Discuss your options with your primary supervisor.
