How do I start studying at reduced hours?

Placering: Planning your studies › Changes to your course of study › Study at reduced hours

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-start-studying-at-reduced-hours-SCI.aspx

Once you have prepared your study plan (see the other sections on this page) and found out the extent to which you need to study at reduced hours, and once you have been granted the necessary exemptions, all you need to do is to withdraw from the courses, projects and exams that you have decided to postpone, including:

  • Exams you have registered for.
  • Courses/projects you have registered for.

You should also check that your SU is not affected by you studying at reduced hours.
