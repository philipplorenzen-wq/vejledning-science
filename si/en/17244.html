Contact UCPH Study Guidance


How to lodge a complaint 

  1. Write a complaint, describing what has happened. Please be as specific as possible. 
  2. Save your complaint as a PDF file and attach it in a KUmail.
  3. Write in the subject field either "Complaint about employee" or "Complaint about fellow student". 
  4. Send your KUmail to klager-science@adm.ku.dk. 


The person who is the subject of your complaint (complainee) will be informed about the complaint and given an opportunity to respond. 

The entire process is described on this page.
