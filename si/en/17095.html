Make it easier for yourself by preparing

  • a brief presentation of yourself
    Practice from home how to present yourself and your competences tailored to the needs of the employer in question, in just 30 seconds. Then you will be ready to do it whenever the opportunity arises – be it by the company's booths or in the coffee line.
  • a few questions for the companies
    Find out who is attending and what you would like to know about the ones who you are interested in.
Questions you can ask
  • What is your experience with graduates with my educational background?
  • What does a typical working day look like in your company?
  • What are the 3 most important competencies of your employees?
  • How do I become a good employee and colleague?
  • What do you think of my project collaboration idea?

You can also ask questions about the companies' work culture, visions, values, specific work areas or something else altogether.
