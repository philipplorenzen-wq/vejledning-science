How long will it take to process my application for a dispensation?

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-long-will-it-take-to-process-my-application-for-a-dispensation-SCI.aspx

It may take up to 6 weeks from when you have submitted all relevant documents before you receive a reply to your application.

If you receive an email alerting you that you will be disenrolled from your study programme within 14 days while your application is being processed, you will not be disenrolled until you have received a reply to your application.

Read more about the processing of your application.
