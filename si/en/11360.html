The curriculum for the MSc programme in Physics is governed by the Study Board of Physics, Chemistry and Nanoscience at the Faculty of Science, University of Copenhagen. 

Curriculum 2010 (revised 2026): Applies to students enrolled at the start of the 2010/11 academic year or later. The curricula consists of

  • Shared section (2026)
  • Programme specific section (2010, revised 2026)

Curriculum 2010 – minor subject (revised 2026): Applies to MSc students of physics with a minor subject enrolled at the start of the 2010/11 academic year or later The curricula consists of

  • Shared section (2026)
  • Programme specific section (2010, revised 2026)
