We have a group on LinkedIn (PLEN Alumni) for previous students of the institute. This is a good place to look, to get ideas about the type of job you can get after graduation. Our "old" students are employed in a range of different public institutions and private companies working with environmental monitoring and analysis, sustainable production, pollutant remediation and nature restoration, environmental regulation and modelling, environmental analytical chemistry and more. 

See an interview from a former student.

Some of the old students also use the LinkedIn page to post job advertisements, when they seek a new employee with your competences. Hence, sign up at LinkedIn with your profile and search for the group.
