What special conditions can I receive in class?

UCPH tries to meet you and your fellow students’ needs. Therefore, there is not a complete list of what the special conditions are.

 
Examples of special conditions in class:

  • Another light setting or room arrangement in class
  • Respect of allergies at experiments
  • Classes with fewer students
  • Special schedule of classes
  • Permission as well as room for SPS aids in the classroom
  • Permission to bring supportive assistants into the classroom
  • Distribution of class materials or handing in of papers in a certain format
  • Dispensation from – or limited participation – in field trips and study tours

The solution depends on your specific situation as well as the practical and physical conditions at the responsible department.
