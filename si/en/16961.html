How many courses should I register for per block?

At SCIENCE, it is recommended that you study full-time – i.e. do 15 ECTS per block.

You may take fewer courses in some periods and more in others.


If you register for more than 15 ECTS per block, make sure that

  1. you do not take on too much
  2. your courses are not in the same schedule group.


If you register for less than 15 ECTS per block, make sure that

  1. you meet the UCPH study activity requirements
  2. you meet the SU study activity requirements
  3. you will not get too busy later on in your study programme.
