In order to present yourself in the best possible way, start by analysing:

  • What your strengths are.
  • How your impairment is of importance in relation to a job or when engaging with a company.

You can use the strengths model above. 
 

Must you talk about your functional impairment?

You also need to consider whether you want to mention your impairment in your application or CV. Surveys and experiences in this area suggest that it limits the chances of being invited for an interview if you mention the impairment directly. See the report From education to job market (in Danish only) prepared by the Association of young people with disabilities.

Open the report From education to job market (in Danish only) 

If you choose to mention your impairment directly, you might consider asking for priority access to an interview. Contact your municipality to hear about the possibilities, for example the municipality of Copenhagen.
