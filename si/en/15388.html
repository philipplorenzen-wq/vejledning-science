You can spend some of your ECTS credits on a course in the field of sustainability. UCPH has compiled a list of all courses in the field of sustainability on the UCPH One Planet website, where you can also see the content of the courses and the requirements for signing up: UCPH One Planet

On the UCPH One Planet website, you can also find an overview of UCPH's research within sustainability and much more.

You can also work with sustainability through projects and cases or participate in events by becoming part of the UCPH sustainability space, Actory.
