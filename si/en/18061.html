Can I receive SU during my leave of absence?

Placering: Planning your studies › Changes to your course of study › Leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-receive-SU-during-my-leave-of-absence-SCI1.aspx

If you take leave of absence, you cannot receive regular SU or extra SU due to parental leave or illness.
Payment of your SU will automatically stop when you take leave of absence. When you return from your leave, you must yourself remember to notify the SU Office.
