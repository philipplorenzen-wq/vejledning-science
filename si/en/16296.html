When is the last chance to pass the exam in a discontinued course?

You will be notified directly of the last chance to pass the exam in the discontinued course if you have

  • used one or more exam attempts on the course
  • been unable to take the exam due to illness.


Exceptions for identical courses with the same exam form

You will not be offered a last chance to pass the exam if your course is replaced by a course with identical content and exam form. Instead, you must register for the exam (or classes) in the identical course.   

You will be notified directly via your KUmail if your course is replaced by an identical course.


Exceptions for courses replaced by a non-identical course

You will be offered a last chance to take the exam if your course is replaced by a new course.

You can choose to register for the course (or in some cases the exam) that replaces your discontinued course – even before the deadline for registering for the last exam in the discontinued course expires. If you do so, you will be bound to the new course. 


When is the last chance to pass the exam in a discontinued course?

The last chance to pass the exam in a discontinued course is in the academic year following the last time the course was taught.

The exact exam dates are published twice a year.
