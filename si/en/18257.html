As a general rule, you do not have to pay tax on a scholarship awarded to cover expenses during a study abroad. Should you be taxed on your scholarship, you must contact SKAT (Danish tax authorities) to inform them that the scholarship has been awarded to cover expenses related to a study abroad. 

UCPH will report your scholarship as B-income and you therefore do not need to report your scholarship for a study abroad to SKAT yourself. 

Please find more information on SKAT’s website: https://skat.dk/borger/unge-og-studerende/su-og-skoleydelse/stipendier-og-legater
