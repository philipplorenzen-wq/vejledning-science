Are you interested in pursuing a career in research? Are you interested in an academic or industrial career? Is a PhD something for you? What is the advantage of doing a PhD?

I have prepared a list of job titles of Molecular Biomedicine graduates. The list shows a very broad array of jobs within research, non-research, and entrepreneurship. Moreover, I have prepared a list of corresponding workplaces demonstrating a wide spectrum of interesting employers. I will present these lists at the introductory day in August before you commence your Master’s programme. Finally, graduate unemployment rates for Molecular Biomedicine are low.

To prepare for your working life after graduating from the Master's programme, the following student-driven organization offer various events aimed at facilitating the transition between student and working life:

  • The Molecular Biomedicine Student Council
  • Synapse 

Your Absalon Study Start modules include career-related modules, some with information specifically for Molecular Biomedicine about possible career paths. Make sure to study those for inspiration!

As mentioned above, in August, before you begin your Master's programme, I will invite you to an introductory day during which I will tell you about career opportunities after graduation from the Master's programme in Molecular Biomedicine.
