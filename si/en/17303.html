What am I not allowed to use generative AI for?

Placering: Exams › Exam types and rules › Use of Generative AI

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-am-I-not-allowed-to-use-generative-AI-for-SCI.aspx

You must write your project or written take-home assignment yourself, and it must be the result of your own intellectual work.

You are not allowed to use generative AI tools to

  • translate your work or parts of it from one language to another
  • generate a draft which you then revise
  • improve a draft you have written yourself. You may use generative AI for proofreading, provided that it only corrects spelling and grammar and doesn't alter the academic content or makes significant linguistic changes.
  • create or write parts of your assignment without referencing and citing generative AI as a source (read more under 'How do I reference generative AI as a source?')


It is considered plagiarism if you use generative AI in a way that is not allowed.
