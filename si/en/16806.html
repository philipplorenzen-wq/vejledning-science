If you wish to use your elective options to obtain a specific academic profile, I have made these recommended study tracks for you.

When you plan your studies, make sure you comply with the number of ECTS credits, which constitutes your programme structure.

See the structure of your programme and the recommended study plan.

Recommended study tracks for Food Science and Technology

All the recommended tracks are depicted with start in September. If your start in February, you can be inspired by the same study tracks as the September start, an example of start in February is listed as the last suggestion below.

The structure always includes 3 compulsory courses (light grey), and five restricted elective courses (dark grey). Additionally, you can choose either i) four elective courses (white), and a 30 ECTS thesis (light grey), or ii) two elective courses and a 45 ECTS thesis.

You can always choose additional restricted elective courses to replace elective courses, but if you want an elective course to replace a restricted elective course, you must apply to the study board.

All the recommended study tracks below are shown with 30 ECTS thesis and four elective courses. For some of the recommended study tracks below, it will be easy to fit a 45 ECTS thesis, and for some of the others it may require a little shuffling of courses, and maybe not all the suggested courses can be followed.

Besides the recommended study tracks, you can also look at the different specialisations to get inspiration for your study plan.
