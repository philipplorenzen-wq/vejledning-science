You have 3 attempts to pass an exam.

When you register for a course, you will automatically be registered for the first exam attempt. You must register for the other attempts yourself.


What counts as an exam attempt?


You will use an exam attempt if you

  • fail the exam (receive a grade of less than 02)
  • submit a blank paper at the exam
  • do not attend the exam
  • do not meet the requirements for taking the exam
  • are ill at the time of the exam and do not submit a notification of illness or your notification of illness is not approved


You can avoid using an exam attempt if

  • you withdraw from the exam before the deadline for withdrawal
  • your notification of illness is approved.


More than 3 exam attempts

You can apply for a dispensation for an extra exam attempt if you can prove you need it due to exceptional circumstances.
