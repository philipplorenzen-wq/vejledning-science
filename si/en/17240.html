You can get help with Special Educational Support (SPS) from UCPH Study Guidance if you need special support (such as exam aids or support people) due to a functional impairment. 

They can help you:

  • identify your needs and what special educational support you may be eligible for
  • with the application procedure – from start to finish

Read more about how to apply for SPS at Study information.
