Remember to bring the following on the day of the exam:

  • Valid photo ID – for example your student ID card with a picture, driving licence, the ID app AltID (requires a CPR number and MitID), passport, national identity card or a residence card issued in conjunction with a residence permit. Without a valid photo ID, you will be rejected at the exam. If you are rejected, you will be registered as 'absent' and use an exam attempt.
  • Your UCPH username and password
  • Information about exam time and room number. You will find it on Digital Exam.
You can obtain draft paper in the exam room if you need it.

Valuables

As far as possible, leave objects of value at home when you take exams. However, if you have things with you that you do not need for the exam, the exam supervisors can tell you where you can place them.

Food and drinks in the exam room

You are allowed to bring food and drinks. Be considerate of the other students and avoid food with strong smells and noisy packaging.

Headphones and hearing protectors

If you want to use your own headphones or hearing protectors at exams, you must apply for a dispensation from your study board. If you need help to apply for a dispensation or have any doubts about the exam rules, please contact UCPH Study Guidance.

The exam house has a selection of standard headphones that you can borrow. Borrowing headphones at the exam house does not require a dispensation from your study board.
If you want to borrow hearing protectors at the exam house, you must apply for a dispensation. Earplugs are available at the exam house without a dispensation.
