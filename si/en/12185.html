If you need guidance about your student life, you can call us, write an email, or book an appointment. 

If you need study administrative help with something you cannot resolve yourself, you need to fill out a form.  

You will get help faster by using the right way to contact us. 


Remember, you can always find rules, deadlines and tips for student life on your Study Information page, and you can solve many things via Self Service.
