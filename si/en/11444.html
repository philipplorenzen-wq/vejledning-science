On some courses, you have to meet specific requirements for taking exams. These requirements are called examination prerequisites.


Examination prerequisites can be i.e. that you have to

  • submit and pass assignments during the course
  • take part in laboratory experiments
  • pass tests during the course
  • participate actively in classes.
