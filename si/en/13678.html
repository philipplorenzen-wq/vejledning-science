The study environment survey is your channel to the UCPH management.

In order to be able to boost the study environment where needed, it's important to know how you feel – both physically and mentally. You can, for example, provide feedback on the following:

  • Is it easy to contact student counselling and can they answer your questions?
  • Can you find the information you need on KUnet and can the administration answer your questions?
  • Are there enough power outlets and does the wireless network function?
  • Are there enough study spaces for your needs?
  • Are you stressed before an exam and are you thriving with your classmates?
