When is best time to go abroad to study?

On the Master's programme in Agriculture, your Study Board recommends that you study abroad in

  • year 1, blocks 3 and 4 for you with a specialisation in Plant Science.
  • year 1, blocks 3 and 4 (for you with a specialisation in Production and Environment, admitted in September, with a 45 ECTS thesis).
  • year 1, blocks 1 and 2 (for you with a specialisation in Production and Environment, admitted in February, with a 45 ECTS thesis).
  • year 2, blocks 1 and 2 (for you with a specialisation in Production and environment, admitted in September, with a 30 ECTS thesis).
  • year 2, blocks 3 and 4 (for you with a specialisation in Production and Environment, admitted in February, with a 30 ECTS thesis).

This period is your 'mobility window' and may contain both elective, compulsory and restricted elective courses.

See the courses offered during  the mobility window on your study programme by selecting your year of enrolment under Planning your studies
