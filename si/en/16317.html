You need to show up at least 30 minutes before your exam starts.

Take into account possible delays when planning your transport.


If you are late

If you are late for the exam you may not be able to take it. If you are refused admittance to the exam, you will be registered for 'non-attendance' and use up 1 exam attempt.

Avoid arriving late by familiarising yourself with the time and place of the exam
