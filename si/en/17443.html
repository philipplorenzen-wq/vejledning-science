The Master's programme does not include a predefined mobility window, but it is possible to study abroad in the first or the second semester during the first year. 

If intending to study abroad, it is important to obtain pre-approval from the Study Board for the courses you plan to take, including the courses to replace the compulsory course(s) placed in the semester in question. You also have the possibility to attend summer schools or intensive courses abroad. Such activities can replace elective (and sometimes restricted elective) courses. 

For inspiration, present focus areas of the University of Copenhagen are digital competencies (e.g. analysis of large data sets, bioinformatics, modelling and coding) as well as competencies within innovation and entrepreneurship.
