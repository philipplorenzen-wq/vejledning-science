Your feedback is used directly in the efforts to develop and improve the quality of teaching activities and exams at SCIENCE.

Evaluation – from you to the course catalogue:

  1. The course coordinator presents the results of an evaluation together with proposals for improvements to the departmental teaching committee.
  2. The teaching committee (lecturers and students) gives the lecturer feedback on and advice about e.g. teaching methods or written material used during the course. The teaching committee presents the results in a report to the Study Board.
  3. The Study Board reviews the feedback about the course and may make recommendations for further action. The results of the course evaluation are taken into account in the Study Board's consideration and approval of course descriptions for the forthcoming academic year/upcoming blocks.
