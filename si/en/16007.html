As an Erasmus+ student, you must have your Learning Agreement signed by International Education at UCPH.

There are 2 ways to get it signed:

  • Through an online portal: Please ask your home university how to do this.
  • By sending it to incomingmobility@adm.ku.dk.


Your home university decides when and how you need to get it signed. Please ask them if you are unsure about it.


If you send it by e-mail

Please fill out as much as you can before sending your document.

You will receive an answer within 10 working days – after all the courses from your Learning Agreement are approved in Mobility Online.

If you have an early deadline for getting your Learning Agreement signed, you should write it in the subject field of your e-mail.
