The form of your examination (oral, written, take-home assignment, etc.) determines when and how you submit a notification of illness. 

Read about notification of illness for the relevant exam form on this page.

Read about how to take re-exams when your exam consists of multiple examinations

If your course runs over 2 blocks

If you submit a notification of illness for one of your examinations on a course that runs over two blocks, you can't sit the remaining examinations – neither in the first nor second block.

This is because your notification of illness covers the whole exam with multiple examinations and not only for the single examination.
