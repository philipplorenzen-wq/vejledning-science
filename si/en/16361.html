The library is located close to North Campus on Nørre Allé 49, 2200 København N.

You will find

  • Quiet reading rooms with a capacity for more than 220 students
  • Space for group work in shared areas and group rooms

The library has in total 489 study places divided into quiet, group, PC places and lounge areas.
