You may do one project in practice for 15 ECTS credits (workload: 412 hours) during your course of study.

Depending on your specialisation, your project can count as either

  • restricted elective ECTS credits
  • elective ECTS credits.

You may do a project that counts as both types of ECTS credits if your curriculum allows both types.

See your options in the recommended course of study under Programme structure.
