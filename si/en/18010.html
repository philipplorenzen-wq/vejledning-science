We are looking for 3-4 new MSc student ambassadors to start 15 June 2026 until end of May 2027. 

The expected workload is a maximum of 35 hours, unevenly distributed during the period. Most of the workload is concentrated at the start of the academic year, in blocks 1 and 2. 

You will be placed as a student study advisor, and salary and terms of employment are in accordance with the applicable agreement concluded between the Ministry of Finance, the National Union of Student Instructors, and the Association of Danish Medical Students (SUL). Placement and remuneration are as a student study advisor. The hourly wage is approximately DKK 220.97.
