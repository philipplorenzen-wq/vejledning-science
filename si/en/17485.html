Why haven’t I received my diploma?

If more than two months have passed since your last exam, please make sure that SCIENCE has registered all your completed courses and projects.


Check whether all your ECTS credits have been registered:

  1. Go to Self Service under 'Enrolment'
  2. Check whether your 'Education setup status' is set to 'Completed'.

If your status is set to completed and you have not received your diploma or if your education setup status is not set to completed, please fill out the form 'Get Study Administrative Help'
