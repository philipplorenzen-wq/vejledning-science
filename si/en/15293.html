UCPH has some advice on how you can push forward the green transition on campus.

  • Reuse and recycle and buy your books used
  • Bring your own thermo mug and save money on your coffee
  • Choose beans instead of beef
  • Use your bike to get from campus to campus
  • Drink water from the tap
  • Turn off the light and laboratory equipment when you leave
  • Remember to close the window when you leave
  • Join your local green student organisation. See a list of green student organisations at UCPH.

Read more about what you can do, for example, in connection with sorting your waste, transportation and printing.
