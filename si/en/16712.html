If your home university requires a confirmation of your stay or departure – or any other documents, you can get them signed by sending them to incomingmobility@adm.ku.dk. 

Please fill out as much as you can before sending your documents.

You will receive an answer within 10 working days.

If you have an early deadline for getting your document signed, you should write it in the subject field of your e-mail.
