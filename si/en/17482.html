Can I be present at my group members’ exams?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-be-present-at-my-group-members%E2%80%99-exams-SCI.aspx

You may only be present at the exams of the other members of your group after your own exam. This is to ensure that all members are examined on equal terms.
