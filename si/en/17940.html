There are many other ways in which to acquire labour market experience than through a student job.


Engage in project collaboration during your study programme

During your study programme at SCIENCE, you can
  • engage in a project collaboration with an external partner in a project in practice, a project outside the course scope or in connection with your BA project or your Master's thesis
  • work with your own business idea in a project in practice in UCPH Lighthouse 
Read more about your project collaboration opportunities
Get experience from volunteer work
You may engage in voluntary work and acquire many of the same experiences and competences that you can get through a student job.
You can find posts and inspiration for volunteer work on the portal frivilligjob.dk,
