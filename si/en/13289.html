No, smoking is not permitted on UCPH’s campus areas. According to UCPH’s smoking policy, smoking is prohibited both indoors and outdoors on all university campuses. This applies to all products that produce smoke or vapour, including e-cigarettes, vapes, and similar products.

Exception: Events after 6:00 pm

If you are attending an event after 6:00 pm, smoking outdoors is permitted. This applies to all kinds of events held at UCPH.

If the smoking ban is violated repeatedly, the management will handle the violation in accordance with the disciplinary regulations for students. You can find the disciplinary regulations here.

If you are hosting a social event at UCPH

If you are the organiser of a social event at UCPH, you and any co-organisers are responsible for ensuring that smoking can take place without posing a fire hazard and that no cigarette butts or similar waste are left behind after the event.

If you would like to quit smoking

If you would like to quit smoking, you can sign up for free smoking cessation courses. See the offers from the City of Copenhagen here, or check the options in your own municipality.
