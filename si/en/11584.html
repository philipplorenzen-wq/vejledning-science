If you have a decreased functionality or illness that prevents you from participating in class on equal terms with your fellow students, you might receive special conditions.


It could be that you experience challenges with

  • sound and light conditions
  • the arrangement and placement of rooms
  • the schedule for courses
  • a certain form of class or exercise.
