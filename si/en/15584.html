Good exam techniques can enable you to perform at your best and to tailor your performance to what is expected of you at the exam.


Good exam techniques are about

  • being able to communicate your knowledge – in writing and orally
  • planning your preparation time
  • preparing for what is expected at the exam
  • preventing nervousness and exam anxiety.

Exam techniques cannot be learnt from one exercise or one presentation – it takes time and practice.
