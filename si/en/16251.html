Your Master’s programme in Molecular Biomedicine is composed of compulsory, elective and restricted elective courses as well as your Master’s thesis project. You can use the elective and restricted elective courses to individualize your Master’s programme. Notice that you can place the elective and restricted elective courses as you wish, as long as you comply with the study activity requirements.

See the structure of the programme and the list of restricted elective courses.
