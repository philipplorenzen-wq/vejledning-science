Apart from the Master's thesis you can also choose Project in Practice or Project outside course scope instead of elective courses and Thesis preparation project as a restricted elective element.

  • Start to think about your interests and look for a thesis project very early and before you have to write the Major Subject Project.
  • Use UCPH Projects and Jobs and contact me or your professors for support. You can also find projects in our alumni association.
  • If you are interested in projects outside the university you can try to find a thesis project or project in practice in the industry (e.g. Novozymes, Chr. Hansen etc.).
  • Some students know exactly what kind of projects they are looking for. But if you don't, there is no need to worry, most students end up thinking that their project is the most interesting project, when they get into the details, even if they didn't have any ideas from the beginning.
