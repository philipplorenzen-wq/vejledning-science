Are you considering studying abroad, but lack the clinching argument? Try asking yourself the following questions:

  • Would you like to add an international academic dimension to your study programme?
  • Would you like to experience new ways of studying?
  • Would you like to increase your cultural insight and understanding?
  • Would you like to improve your language skills?
  • Would you like to make new friends from all over the world?
  • Would you like a unique and unforgettable experience for life?

If you can answer yes to one or more of the above questions, you should consider a study abroad period during your study programme.
