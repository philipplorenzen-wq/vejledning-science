Is my maternity record valid documentation of illness during pregnancy?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Is-my-maternity-record-valid-documentation-of-illness-during-pregnancy-SCI.aspx

Your maternity record is not valid documentation if you want to apply for a dispensation due to pregnancy-related discomfort or illness.


You will need a medical certificate documenting the exact reasons why you are applying for a dispensation.

You pay for the medical certificate yourself.

See a guide to what your documentation should include


When will you need documentation?

You will need documentation whenever you want to apply for a dispensation for something that falls outside the normal rules or framework for your study programme.
