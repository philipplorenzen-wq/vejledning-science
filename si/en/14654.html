If your need for support changes, please contact UCPH Study Guidance as soon as possible so we can find out if there are other forms of support you may benefit from.

For example, if:

  • You get a new diagnosis
  • Suspect that you have reading and writing issues
  • You are going on an internship or on an exchange abroad

See how to book an appointment with UCPH Study Guidance here.
