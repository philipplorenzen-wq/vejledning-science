When you give notice of your parental leave without leave of absence, you can choose to withdraw from the exam and consequently also from the submission of your report, if any.

Read more about parental leave without leave of absence (with SU)

Continue with the same project after your maternity leave

If you want to continue with the same project after your maternity leave, you must:

  • agree with your supervisor on when you will restart your project
  • register for the for the same project
  • submit your report – possibly in a revised version that stays within your original problem formulation
  • attend the oral exam.

Start over with a new project

If you choose to start a new project, you must follow the same procedure as last time:

  • Find a new topic and possibly a company.
  • Find a new supervisor, if necessary.
  • Complete and submit a new contract.
