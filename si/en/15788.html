The specialisation is set at 120 ECTS credits and consists of the following:

  • Compulsory courses, 30 ECTS credits
  • Restricted elective courses, 30 ECTS credits
  • Elective subjects, 15 ECTS credits
  • Thesis, 45 ECTS credits
