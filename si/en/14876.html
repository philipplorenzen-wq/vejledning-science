Previous surveys have led to concrete improvements of the study environment at UCPH.

Examples from the Faculty of Science:

  • 200 extra study spaces and lounge areas with light and heat-shielding curtains by the large glass panels.
  • Lockers.

(North Campus, lobby/vandrehal at the HCØ institute)

• More electricity outlets, new lockers, high tables and booth sofas as study spaces.

(Frederiksberg Campus, Marble Hall and lobby of the tower block)

It's important that as many students as possible take the survey in order to give the management the best insights into where to take action.
