Rules for sharing teaching material

Teaching material includes written and non-written materials, such as recordings. As a student, you need to be aware of the following:

  • If you want to share written teaching material with people who are not part of the Absalon course room, you must obtain consent from the teacher.
  • If you want to share teaching recordings in which no other than the teacher can be identified, you must obtain consent from the teacher.
  • If you want to share teaching recordings in which other participants can be identified, you must also obtain consent from these participants.

When sharing teaching material, you must use a secure distribution channel – even if you have obtained consent. A secure distribution channel may, for example, be KUnet.

You must comply with copyright and GDPR rules.

Consequences of sharing teaching material without consent

If you share written teaching material or recordings with people who are not part of the Absalon course room without obtaining consent, it will be considered a breach of the disciplinary measures towards students at the University of Copenhagen in force from 1 March 2023.

The consequence of breaking the disciplinary measures by sharing teaching material without consent may be a warning or expulsion from the University for a limited period of time or permanently.
