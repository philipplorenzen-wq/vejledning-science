How many and which ECTS credits can I use on a project in practice?

Gælder for målgrupperne: Global Environment And Development - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-many-and-which-ECTS-credits-can-I-use-on-a-project-in-practice-15-BV-SCI.aspx

You may do one project in practice for 15 ECTS credits (workload: 412 hours) during your course of study.

A project in practice can only count as restricted elective ECTS credits on your study programme.
