As a non-EU citizen your student visa only allows you to work maximum 20 hours per week (except for the months of June, July and August where you can work full-time).
The 20 hours per week include both
  • internships and project- and thesis collaboration
  • student jobs and volunteering.
You risk having your student visa revoked, if you violate its conditions.
Apply for work permit if you exceed 20 hours
You must apply for a work permit for internships (a so called ST4) if your student job, internship and/or project- and thesis collaboration exceeds more than more than 20 hours a week in total.
Application is free of charge and it's not a long process.
The application process: 
  1. You apply before you start the project collaboration/internship by sending a KUmail to admissions@ku.dk.
  2. UCPH Study Guidance will then start the ST4 process for you.
