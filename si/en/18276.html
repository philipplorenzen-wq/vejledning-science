If you did not get a place on the course you will be notified via KUmail at the latest by

  • 1 July  for courses in block 1 and 2
  • 1 January for courses in block 3, 4 and 5

If you got a place on the course, you'll be able to see your class in Self service by the same deadline.

Read more about not getting a place on a course  


How places are allocated during the ordinary registration period

You are guaranteed a place when:

  • the course is compulsory for you to take.
  • you have previously taken the course without passing the exam. 


Remaining places are allocated according to the following priorities:

  1. The course is a restricted elective course for you.
  2. The course is an elective course for you, or you have been granted permission to take courses from your future Master's programme while completing your Bachelor's programme.

Any additional places will be allocated to those students who

  1. are enrolled at the same programme level as the course - bachelor's students are prioritized higher for bachelor's courses and master's students for master's courses.  
  2. have passed the highest number of ECTS credits on their study programme. If 2 students have the same number of ECTS credits, lots are drawn.

You will be notified via your KUmail if you did not get a place during the ordinary registration period.


How places are allocated during the late-registration period

During the late-registration period, places are allocated on a first come, first served basis.

Even though a course is compulsory to you, you are not guaranteed place in the late-registration period where all places may be filled.
