Your bank might require you to close your account and/or provide them with your personal contact information in your home country when leaving Denmark.

If you have a bank account Denmark, we recommend that you:

  • Contact your bank to let them know that you are leaving the country.
  • Contact your bank to close your account – if you know that this is required or if you wish to do so.
  • Contact your bank to take care of any account or money transfers you might need to take care of before leaving Denmark.
