Leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances

You may suspend your studies completely for a period if necessitated by exceptional circumstances in your life. You are not entitled to receive SU during your leave of absence.


You may apply for leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances such as

  • childbirth or adoption
  • military service (værnepligt/værneret) or UN service
  • your own serious illness
  • serious illness or death in your immediate family.

Based on your specific situation, an assessment will be made of whether you can be granted a leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances.
