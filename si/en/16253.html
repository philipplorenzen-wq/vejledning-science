Your study programme's progression from foundations to advanced courses

1st year: The compulsory courses in blocks 1+2 provide a broad and solid foundation. Already in blocks 1 and 3+4 you have a choice among a variety of Computer Science course to suit your interests (your restricted elective courses). You can also choose to take elective courses here.

2nd year: You can freely choose your courses in blocks 1+2 (your elective courses) also at other departments (e.g. Mathematics) and at another faculties (e.g. Humanities) to complement your studies and your specialization. These two blocks are the ideal time to study abroad for a semester. You can also take restricted elective courses here. 

The last blocks (block 3+4) are reserved for working on your Master's thesis and completing your programme.

Read more about the programme structure here.

To make your (restricted) elective choices easy and to guide your specialization, follow the courses suggested by a study track. I recommend that you choose a track that interests you most. The tracks are recommendations and you may follow and combine them to suit your interests.
