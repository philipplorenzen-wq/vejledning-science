During the compulsory and selected courses, you have the chance to identify future project opportunities and supervisors.

  • See the study programme.
  • You have the chance to change specialisation during block 1. If you change your mind later, it may be difficult due to the courses you are bound to.
  • You must choose a total of 45 ECTS compulsory and restricted elective courses from the list for each specialization. The last 15 ECTS are elective courses, which can be used for projects or all other courses. You are free to place restricted electives and electives wherever you want during the study programme (i.e. the placement on the study programme tables are only suggestions).
  • In the course database all courses at University of Copenhagen are described.
  • At the University of Copenhagen Project and Job bank you find potential projects both shorter termed as well as Master thesis suggestions.
  • Remember that courses outside SCIENCE needs pre-approval - so be in good time before the normal deadlines (at least two weeks before).
  • You can choose elective courses from Globe and find a supervisor for the Master's thesis at the GLOBE institute (SUND) without applying for it.
  • The Master's thesis is 45 or 60 ECTS, full-time during 2nd year. Find the strict deadlines for drawing up the master thesis contract. If you decide on master thesis projects that will involve e.g. fieldwork during the summer before the official start of the thesis work, consider using a Thesis preparation project (PREP) for this either in block 4 of 5 before starting the thesis in block 1.
  • Consult your coming supervisor and be careful not run into trouble with potential plagiarism between a PREP project and your Master's thesis.  

If you have doubts about how to structure your programme or the rules regarding it, find the information here in your study webpage and do not hesitate to contact UCPH Study Guidance.
