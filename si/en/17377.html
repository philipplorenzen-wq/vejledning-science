Just like your student counsellors, the diversity and inclusion consultants in KUallies are ready to listen, help and guide you if you experience harassment, offensive behaviour or bullying during your studies.
This applies regardless of whether you have experienced offensive behaviour, witnessed an incident you did not think was okay, or if you perhaps have violated someone else's boundaries.

We offer someone to talk to outside your study environment. 

You can find us at Søndre Campus.

Fill in the form or send us an email to KUallies@ku.dk
