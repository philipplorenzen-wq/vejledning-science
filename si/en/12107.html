What's it like to study at UCPH when you have a functional impairment?

A number of students will tell you in the videos below.

They talk about how they best cope with their studies, what is challenging for them and what help they receive.

You will meet a student who has a physical functional impairment - and you will meet students who are dyslexic, have mental challenges or neuro-divergence.
