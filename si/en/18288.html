The deadline for applying is Thursday 31 May, 23:59.

You can apply to become a SCIENCE Buddy if you

  • have been studying at SCIENCE, for at least 1 year 
  • speak conversational level English
  • take an interest in helping new international students

  • are responsible and open to meeting new people.

Both Danish and international students are welcome to apply.
 Apply to become a BUDDY 

You will be contacted on 1 June. 

Interviews are expected to be held in-person on 3 June and 4 June, 2026 on Dyrlægevej 10, Frederiksberg Campus through Zoom, between 13-16 PM.

If you have any questions, please contact studiemiljoe-studiestart-science@adm.ku.dk.
