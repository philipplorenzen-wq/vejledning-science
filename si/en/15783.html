Why should I join an unemployment fund?

If you are unemployed after graduation, you may become eligible for unemployment benefits if you are

  • a member of an unemployment fund
  • meet the criteria laid down by the unemployment fund.

The unemployment fund also offers help and guidance in your job search process.
