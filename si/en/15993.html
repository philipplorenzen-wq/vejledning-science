How many and which ECTS credits can I use on a project in practice?

Placering: Master's thesis and other projects › Project in Practice › Purpose, requirements, ECTS and timing

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Environmental and Natural Resource Economics - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-many-and-which-ECTS-credits-can-I-use-on-a-project-in-practice-30-BV-V-SCI.aspx

Your project in practice may have 2 different sizes:

  • 15 ECTS credits in total (workload: 412 hours)
  • 30 ECTS credits in total (workload: 825 hours).

The project can count as

  • restricted elective ECTS credits: 15
  • elective ECTS credits: 15.

You may do a project that counts as both types of ECTS credits when your curriculum allows both types.
