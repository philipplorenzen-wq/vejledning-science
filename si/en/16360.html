You can do a project together with students from other study programmes at SCIENCE if your joint project is approved by all the group members' heads of studies.

Three steps to getting started with an interdisciplinary project

  • Start preparations early to establish if your project is feasible.
  • First consult with the person you want as your joint supervisor to establish whether he or she believes the project is possible.
  • Contact your respective heads of studies and find out whether they can approve the interdisciplinary project.

Your fellow student, who is enrolled at another faculty, must register at their own faculty, but you must also add them to your project contract.
