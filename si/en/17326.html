The MSc programme in Mathematics is flexible enough to be tailored in many directions, depending on your interests and goals. It is designed to ensure a certain breadth in the topics you study, as well as a certain depth in some of these topics. The short list of restrictive elective courses (List 1 in your Programme Structure from which you need 30 ECTS) ensures breadth, and should help you identify a direction in which you want to shape your MSc.

After taking a few List 1 courses you might know what you want and how to get there. But if not, I recommend that you talk to a professor in a topic that particularly interests you. He or she can then recommend courses that will allow you to go deeper in this direction while also maintaining a balanced course program. Note that you need a certain number of ECTS from List 2 (how many depends on whether you are on the general profile or with a minor.)

You are also welcome to talk to me. Then I can either make recommendations for which courses to take, or point you to another professor who can, depending on your interests.

All available courses (elective and non-elective) can be found at kurser.ku.dk.
