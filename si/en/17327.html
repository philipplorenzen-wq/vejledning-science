We are looking for upcoming 2nd year MSc students at the Department of Computer Science, who are passionate about creating a thriving student environment, which includes safe and inspiring spaces for International and Danish students alike.

It is not a requirement to have previous experience within this field; however, volunteer or paid work in the areas of study start, study environment and/or social student environments are regarded as strong attributes for your candidacy. 

We welcome everyone to apply.
