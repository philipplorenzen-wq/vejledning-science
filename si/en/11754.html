Competences are about what you can do – knowledge and skills you can use in different situations.

You build up competences on your study programme and through your leisure activities, student jobs, study abroad etc.
You have both

  • academic competences
  • personal competences.

Once you can identify your own competences, you have a good starting point for applying for (student) jobs, business collaborations etc.
