If you do not pass your thesis, you have used one exam attempt. 

You (and your supervisor) must do this in order to move on with your thesis:

  • Make a new problem formulation within the same subject area.
  • Draw up a new Master's thesis contract. You will receive a KUmail when you can access the digital contract. If you have not received this mail 1 week after your original deadline of submission, contact UCPH Study Guidance.
  • Submit the new contract for approval 14 days at the latest after the Master's thesis defence. Your latest start date can be this date. If you submit your contract before the 14 days, your start date will be the date of approval.

How to draw up and submit your contract 

Your new deadline for submitting your thesis report


You must submit the new Master's thesis report 3 months after having had the revised contract approved – no later than 3½ months after the Master's thesis defence.

This new deadline applies regardless of whether you are attending courses concurrently with writing your thesis.


Your new oral defence will be planned and handled in the same way as the first.
