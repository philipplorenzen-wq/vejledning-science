When do I register for an exam at LAW?

You are automatically registered for the ordinary exam. 
If you need to take the re-exam, you can decide whether you want to register for the re-exam in the ordinary exam period or the re-exam period.
You can register for exams via email to studycontact@jur.ku.dk during the relevant registration periods: 
Semester Exams in the ordinary exam period Exams in the re-exam period
Autumn 2024
11 - 18 September 2024

1 - 8 January 2025 (period 1*) 
22 - 29 January 2025 (period 2**)

Spring 2025
5 - 12 March 
2 - 9 July 2025

* Period 1: Applies to exams or re-exams for courses with ordinary exams in October, November and December.

** Period 2: Applies to exams or re-exams for courses with ordinary exams in January.
