How will my Master’s thesis be assessed?

You will get your grade by the end of your oral defense. The grade is based on both the Master's thesis report and the oral defence.
The Master's thesis is assessed by

  • one or more examiners (your supervisor and possibly your co-supervisors)
  • an external examiner.  


If you are doing a Master's thesis with a company, your contact at the company is not involved in assessing you.
