You must find up to 3 people you would like to work with. A project can have a maximum of 4 participants.

To write your project as a group, you must:
  • work on the project during the same period
  • choose a common supervisor
  • complete and submit the project contract together
  • (if you come from different programmes) have the topic approved by your respective head of studies.
