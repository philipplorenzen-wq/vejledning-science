The MSc programme in Human Nutrition consists of three elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 52.5 ECTS credits
  2. Elective subjects, 22.5 ECTS credits
  3. Thesis, 45 ECTS credits

The programme is two years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Human Nutrition / Cand.scient. i human ernæring
