On Study information, you can see a list of the grants and scholarships administered by either UCPH or your faculty:

  • See scholarships at your faculty and UCPH
  • Read about scholarships and other ways of financing your stay

To find relevant foundations and scholarships, you can also

  • consult websites – for example www.legatbogen.dk, www.fonde.dk, www.so.dk/legater, www.legatnet.dk
  • check News on KUnet and in the University Post
  • read evaluations from students who have been abroad
  • ask friends or fellow students if they know of any
  • check if your local municipality is offering grants you may be able to apply for.
