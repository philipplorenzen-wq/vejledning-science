How do I find relevant companies or organisations to contact unsolicited?

3 good places to begin your search for a relevant company or organisation to contact about a job or project:

1) Talk to your network – professors, fellow students etc.

You already have a network who might have contacts in interesting companies or organisations. Talk to e.g. 

  • your lecturers – many of them work with companies and organisations as a part of their research and have an interesting network outside academia. Maybe you have had an interesting guest lecturer, you could contact?
  • fellow students who have done project collaborations.

2) Find out where other students have done a project in practice

Read the abstracts from your fellow student's projects in practice and see which companies and organisations they have collaborated with. Maybe you get inspired.

Read abstracts from fellow student's projects in practice

3) Use LinkedIn in your research

LinkedIn is a great tool for finding relevant companies and organisations to contact. You can e.g. find alumni with the same background as you and find out where they have worked – both during their programme and after graduation.
