A project outside the course scope can to a very large extent be shaped according to your interests.

Your project must include:

  1. Your independent work on a selected methodological/theoretical topic.
  2. Your academic reflections on and communication of an academic problem/issue.
  3. A written or oral exam – or both. You can decide on the exam type yourself.
    Read more about options for the exam


Project purpose and requirements


Generally speaking, you must demonstrate your ability to

  • acquire knowledge within your subject area
  • formulate and work with an academic problem/issue
  • disseminate your work in one form or another.

See which specific purposes and requirements your project outside the course scope must meet in the shared section of your curriculum.
