How do I know if my course is discontinued?

Placering: Courses and teaching › Course changes › Discontinued courses

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-know-if-my-course-is-discontinued-SCI.aspx

If a course is discontinued, you will be notified directly to your KUmail if you have

  • used one or more exam attempts on the course
  • been unable to take the exam due to illness
  • been registered for a course that is discontinued and replaced by an identical course.

In the same email, you will also be informed of the ways in which you can pass the course.
Find the list of discontinued courses forming part of your study programme under Programme structure.
