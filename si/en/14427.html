You can apply for additional grant portions if you are delayed as a result of being or having been an ordinary member of a UCPH council, committee or board established under the governing law.

What documentation must you submit?

1. Documentation of your membership

A letter or document from the council's secretary stating:

  • The membership period
  • The scope of the work (expected time commitment)

2. Your own description of the work

You must describe:

  • The tasks you carried out (the nature of the work)
  • How much time you spent per week on average
  • Meeting preparation and meetings with your constituency

Important: Participation in meetings alone does not entitle you to additional grant portions. The overall time commitment is assessed. You must therefore document that your activities were extensive enough to delay your studies during a period in which you received SU.


You must apply for additional grant portions via minSU on www.su.dk.
