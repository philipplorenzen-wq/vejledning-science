How do I resume my studies?

Placering: Special support, Illness and parental leave › Pregnancy and parental leave › Parental leave

You can resume your studies when you want to – as long as you comply with your modified study activity requirements.

You can resume your studies

  • either on a full-time basis
  • or gradually until your extra time has been used up.


Pay attention to the registration periods

Remember to register for exams, courses and projects in the registration periods for the block in which you want to resume your studies. You register on Self Service.
