If you apply for an extra exam attempt to be used after one of the deadlines which must be met for you to comply with your study activity requirements, you also need to apply for dispensation from the study activity requirements.

The study board can only process your application if you apply for both.


How to apply for an extra exam attempt and dispensation from the study activity requirements at the same time

  1. Follow the application procedures for applying for dispensation for extra exam attempts and dispensation from the study activity requirements.
  2.  In the comments field in the online form for both applications, write that you are also applying for dispensation from the study activity requirements/for an extra exam attempt.


If you have already applied for an extra exam attempt

  1. Submit your application for dispensation from the study activity requirements as soon as possible.
  2. In the comments field on the online form, write that you have already submitted an application for an extra exam attempt.

See how and when to apply for dispensation from the study activity requirements
