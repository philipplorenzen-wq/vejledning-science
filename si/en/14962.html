Fysisk Fagråd is a political student organisation consisting of all students at Physics at the University of Copenhagen. 
The primary task of Fagrådet is to promote the interests of the students in the different boards across NBI, Science and KU, the Student Council and the Science Student’s association plus the teaching committee at NBI.
Fagrådet has the potential and opportunity to have a direct impact on your place of study and influence your study environment in a positive way. Members of different councils are elected democratically at the semi-annual general assemblies. When participating in the meetings in Fagrådet, you can voice your opinion so board members can work on promoting it further on in the University system.
