If you apply for one of UCPH’s exchange places outside Europe,  Great Britain, Greenland and Faroe Island, you will automatically be considered for UCPH’s Internationalisation scholarship.
You automatically apply for the University of Copenhagen's Internationalization Scholarship in the application round on November 1st. In the other application rounds, you cannot apply for the scholarship.
