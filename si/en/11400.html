You are welcome to arrange to take courses at another faculty or university, but you need to plan well in advance.


Before taking courses at another faculty or university, you must

  1. have the courses pre-approved as part of your study programme at SCIENCE
  2. be admitted to the other faculty at UCPH or the other university.
