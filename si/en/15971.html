At UCPH Study Guidance, study and career guidance counsellors are ready to help and guide you through your studies – right up until you graduate. 


They can help with things like:

  • your course of study – planning and course choices
  • changed plans, e.g. due to parental leave, illness or functional impairment
  • well-being, motivation and uncertainties about your studies
  • experiences of offensive behaviour
  • your future plans and career.


You can get administrative support with

  • systems – e.g. Self Service, Digital Exam and Absalon
  • registering for courses and exams
  • an application you've already submitted.
