There is almost no unemployment among statisticians. Many of our candidates proceed to find jobs in the private sector. Primarily the pharmaceutical or financial sector. Many also continue in public sector, for example health care research.

If you want to continue a career as a researcher at the university, you must earn PhD degree.

Find more information about the PhD programme at UCPH.
