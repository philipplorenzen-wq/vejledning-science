If you don't pass your first re-exam (your second exam attempt), you're still entitled to one more attempt. You have a total of 3 exam attempts.


Before you register for the re-exam again

Before you register for another re-exam, it's a good idea to consider whether there are specific challenges concerning the course material or the exam form that you need to focus on.

You can talk through your situation and options with a study and career guidance counsellor in UCPH Study Guidance.
