The sooner you begin cultivating your network, the better equipped you'll be when you eventually enter the job market. And you can already benefit from your network as a student.

Your network can be your way to

  • new knowledge
  • project collaborations
  • a student jobs/ jobs
  • new, relevant acquaintances.

Opportunities arise in many different places through your network. However, it requires initiative and initiating contact.


Half of all vacancies are never posted

In Ballisager's Candidate Analysis from 2023, 50% of the candidates state that their latest job was not posted as a job advertisement. 

This means that half of the jobs were filled by candidates 

  • the employer already knew or found through their network
  • who applied unsolicited.
