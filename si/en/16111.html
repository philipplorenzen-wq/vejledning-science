Can I take a leave of absence during my Master’s thesis?

As a general rule, you cannot apply for leave of abscence as you normally would, but there are these following exceptions:

  • parental leave
  • adoption
  • military service, UN service, etc.

See how to apply for leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances 

If you are ill for a long time, you cannot be given leave of absence during your thesis. 

Instead, you must apply for permission to postpone the submission of your thesis. Read more on this page under ‘Can I postpone submitting my thesis?’

How to return from taking leave of absence

When you take leave, you will be given a new deadline for submission. Your period of leave will be added to your original deadline.

You continue with the same contract and supervisor following your leave.
