The password for your UCPH username must be changed at least once a year. You will automatically be notified via your KUmail when the time comes.

If you suspect that your password has been intercepted by someone else, you must change it immediately.

If you've forgotten your password or need help with multi factor authentification, visit Help for login.

To change your password click here:

Change password


After changing your password, remember to update it on:

  1. KUmail
    If you have set up your KUmail in a mail app on, for example, your mobile, tablet or computer, remember to change the password to your new one.

  2. eduroam (WiFi)
    Change the password for eduroam on the devices where you saved your login.

    Depending on how you have installed eduroam, you must log in with your new password and either your UCPH username, or your UCPH username followed by @ku.dk (e.g. abc123@ku.dk).
