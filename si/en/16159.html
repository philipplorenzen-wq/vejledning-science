The MSc programme in Climate Change with a specialisation in The Physical Climate System consists of four elements: 
  1. Compulsory courses, 30 ECTS credits 
  2. Restricted elective courses 
    • 37.5 ECTS credits (thesis 30 ECTS credits)
    • 30 ECTS credits (thesis 45 ECTS credits)
  3. Elective subjects 
    • 22,5 ECTS credits (thesis 30 ECTS credits)
    • 15 ECTS credits (thesis 45 ECTS credits)
  4. Thesis, 30 ECTS credits or 45 ECTS credits

Elective subjects

You have 22,5 ECTS credits or 15 ECTS credits depending on the size of your thesis to strengthen your own profile at the programme.
Read more at 'How can I use my elective ECTS credits?'
