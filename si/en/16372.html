How will my project be assessed?

Placering: Projects › MSc Research Project › Report and exam

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Gæstestuderende SCI - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

You are given a single grade for the report and the oral defence (7-point grading scale).

Your project will be assessed by

  • an examiner (your supervisor)
  • one or more external co-examiners.

In addition to assessing the academic content of the project, where the abstract is included on a par with the other components, the examiners will also assess your spelling and writing proficiency, as capacity to communicate the subject matter is included in the overall assessment.

If you are a 3rd or 4th year bachelor student at the time of writing the project, the project will still be assessed as a master level project.

Registering the exam result

When the project has been handed in, assessed, defended and graded, your supervisor must use the standardized MSc Research Project for Exchange or Guest Students – Report Form to have your grade registered. All the necessary information is listed in the form.

Your supervisor must send the form to eksamen-frbplus@adm.ku.dk for the grade to be registered.
