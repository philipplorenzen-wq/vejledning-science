How do I change classes?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-change-classes-SCI.aspx

You can only change classes during the late-registration period for the course, and only if there are vacancies.

To change classes, go to Self Service > Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Courses > Select/change classes.

Go to Self Service

You can view your new class in Self Service immediately.

Check the late-registration periods under Course registration.


Swap with another student if the class is full

If the class is full, you may swap classes with another student. You can only swap classes during the late-registration period.

You swap classes by sending a KUmail to studyguidance-science@adm.ku.dk, where the person you swap with is Cc on the email.
