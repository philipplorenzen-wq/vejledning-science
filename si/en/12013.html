If you are doing an academic internship, a thesis preparation project stay or fieldwork abroad, you must plan the stay yourself. Therefore, it is a good idea to consider:

  • what type of stay is best suited to you
  • where you would like to go
  • what criteria you must meet
  • how to plan your stay.
