When will I receive a reply to my application?

Placering: SU and grants › Extra grant portions › Other special circumstances

Gælder for målgrupperne: gxd · Københavns Universitet - Kandidat · Københavns Universitet - P-Bachelor

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/When-will-I-receive-a-response-to-my-application-other-SU-KU.aspx

Usually, you can expect a reply to your application within 12 weeks, from the day you get a receipt of your application.

During holiday periods, process time may be longer.
