You can apply for an Erasmus Traineeship grant, to finance your Academic Internship/Traineeship in an EU Member country or a country participating in the Erasmus+ programme:

Belgium, Bulgaria, Czech Republic, Germany, Estonia, Ireland, Greece, Spain, France, Croatia, Italy, Cyprus, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Hungary, Malta, Netherlands, Austria, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovenia, Slovakia, Finland, Sweden, North Macedonia, Serbia, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway and Turkey.

You cannot receive an Erasmus Traineeship grant for an academic internship that takes place in Denmark, United Kingdom, Greenland or the Faroe Islands.

IMPORTANT for spring 2027: Due to limited Erasmus funds, it is uncertain whether UCPH can provide Erasmus Traineeship grant for academic internships in the EU/Europe in the spring semester 2027. A decision is expected in December 2026 at the earliest and will be announced here. If you are in doubt whether you can complete your academic internships in the EU/Europe in the spring semester 2027 without the Erasmus Traineeship grant, we recommend that you find another solution.

Please note: University of Copenhagen does not give dispensation for the application deadline.
