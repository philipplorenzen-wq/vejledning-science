Most of the time, you will not get a decision on your exam complaint about an ordinary exam before the re-exam. Therefore you can retake the exam while you have a complaint pending.

If you receive a re-assessment based on your complaint and at the same time take a re-exam, you will be able to choose between the two grades.
Can I participate in my next semester while my complaint is processed?
We encourgage you to continue your studies as planned while your complaint is processed. 
However, you may only continue in the courses where you meet the formal requirements.
