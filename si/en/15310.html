How do I change which tax card SU uses?

If you have a part-time job and also receive SU, it is important to check which tax card SU is using.

The SU Office at the University of Copenhagen automatically receives information about your tax details.

If you want to change the tax card used for your SU payments, you must update it yourself in MinSU under Change your SU and TAX.

Log on to MinSU here

If you receive SU, the Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science and your possible employer may both retrieve and use your primary tax card from SKAT simultaneously. Your employer should therefore not experience any issues when retrieving your tax card – even if the agency is also listed as a user.

In the event that you are informed that the agency is “holding on your primary tax card," the matter must be resolved between your employer and SKAT.

Everything is handled digitally, so neither the agency nor the SU Office at the University of Copenhagen can “hold on to" or “release" a tax card.

If you have any questions about your tax matters, you must contact SKAT.
