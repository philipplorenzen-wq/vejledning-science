DatØk Fodbold is Computer Science & Economics Football team. We participate in the faculty's football tournament twice a year in block 1 and block 4.

Attending the matches and workouts is a great way to meet students across different classes and year groups. At matches and workouts we enjoy being together outside our studies. For this reason, we are open for everyone with interest in football, no matter experience and gender, and hope you will show up to our matches and workouts. Contact: datokfodbold@di.ku.dk
