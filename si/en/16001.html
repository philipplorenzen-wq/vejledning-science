The specialisation is set at 120 ECTS credits and consists of the following:

  • Compulsory courses, 25.5 ECTS credits
  • Restricted elective courses, 37.5 ECTS credits
  • Elective subjects, 15 or 30 ECTS credits
  • Thesis, 30 or 45 ECTS credits
