  1. Book an appointment 
    Start by making an appointment with UCPH Study Guidance. We will help you get started with support.
    See how to book an appointment with UCPH Study Guidance here. 
    Remember to prepare for the interview by obtaining documentation from your doctor of your functional impairment.
  2. Message in your e-Boks after 3-6 weeks
    Your counsellor applies for the support and/or aids that you have discussed during the interview.
    3-6 weeks later, you will be notified via e-Boks when your application has been processed.
  3. UCPH will order support and aids for you
    UCPH will order support and aids for you.
  4. Notification in your e-Boks
    3-6 weeks later, you will be notified in your e-Boks when your application has been processed.
  5. Now your aids are ready
    You must pick up the study aids yourself at Njalsgade 72, 7. sal (vest), 2300 København K.
    Please note that when using a dictaphone, you must obtain permission from the lecturer and your fellow students before you can use it in classes.
