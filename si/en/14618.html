Violence can be both verbal and physical.


Death threats, threats of violence and the like should be reported to the police.

Physical violence should be reported to the police.

If you are a witness


Call 112 before you intervene. Provide the address.

Call out: Say that you have called the police, and that they are on their way.


Call UCPH Emergency service 35 33 88 00, and someone from operations or the emergency service will help you.

Make sure that somebody meets the police and shows them the way.

  • If possible, send someone to receive the police.


If possible, try to calm the threatening individual down, but do not expose yourself to danger.


Write down what happened as soon as possible.
