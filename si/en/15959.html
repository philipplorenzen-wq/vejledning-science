Study surveys unfortunately show that more and more students at UCPH are feeling stressed.
If you are not thriving as a student, this will naturally affect your general well-being. Similarly, your health – mental and physical – has a big impact on your well-being as a student. 
When you are generally feeling well, you are better able to: 
  • realise your academic potential and get the most out of your studies
  • deal with the challenges and workload normally associated with being a student 
  • join the important communities centred around your studies that play a key role in your academic well-being and motivation. 

It is therefore worth giving priority to doing things that benefit your health and well-being – even when you are feeling perfectly fine. This will help you tackle the challenges you may meet along the way.
