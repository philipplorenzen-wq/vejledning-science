You must contact the university you want to apply to for information about how to apply.
For example, you can find information on their website or by contacting their international office.

You should start planning your stay approximately one year before you want to go.

Six steps in your planning:

  1. Check the foreign university's admission requirements, application procedure, tuition fees, course descriptions, course catalogues, etc.
  2. Complete the host university's application documents and submit the application.
  3. Apply for pre-approval from your study board.
  4. Sort out insurance, travel safety and any visa
  5. Examine the possibility of applying for a study-abroad scholarship to pay the tuition fee at your host university.
  6. Apply for grants, scholarships and to private foundations, etc.
