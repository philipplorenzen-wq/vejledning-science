If your assignment is based on an exam question, you will receive the question in Digital Exam.

When you get the question depends on the deadline set for your assignment. For one-week assignments, for instance, your exam question will be uploaded exactly 1 week before the date of submission.

See the timeframe for your assignment in your course description 


Notification with the exact time

You will receive a KUmail when your exam is displayed in Digital Exam. When your exam is displayed, you can see the exact time when you get your exam question.
