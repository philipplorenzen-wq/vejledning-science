Can my Erasmus Traineeship be in a blended format (virtuel + physical)?

It is possible to make an Erasmus Traineeship in a blended format. It means that you will complete a part of the traineeship where you will be phycially present at the host insitution and the rest of the traineeship will be in a virtual format.

The part where you are physically present at the host institution must be for a period of min. 2 consecutive months and the virtual part must be either before or after. You cannot take a break between the two parts. 

You will recieve an Erasmus grant for the period, where you are phycially present at the host institution.

The complete traineeship (physical + virtuel) cannot excede a 12 months period.

Application deadline: the deadlines and application proces described in the section "How do I apply for a grant for my academic internship/traineeship" are applicable for Erasmus Traineeship as a blended mobility.

The start date of your Erasmus Traineehip is the first date, where you need to be present either virtuel or phycially at your host institution.  

Learning Agreement for Traineeship: When you apply for Erasmus Traineeship as blended mobility, you must state in your Learning Agreeent the start and end date of the mobility, where you will be phycially present and the start and end date of the mobility where you will be virtually present.

You must also include a short description of the tasks, you will be responsible for during the virtual part of the traineeship
