How can I get additional monthly grant portions?

As a parent/prospective parent you can apply for extra monthly grant portions (known as student birth grant).
If you want to receive student birth grant, you must have applied for SU.
Read more about the rules for student birth grant (in Danish)
Apply for SU via minSU at www.su.dk.

Disbursement of student birth grant

Student birth grants can be paid out in three different ways:

  • single portions
  • double portions
  • mixed portions

Read more about the different types of grant portions (in Danish)
Apply for student birth grant via minSU at www.su.dk.

Transfer of student birth grant

You can choose to transfer up to 3 months of student birth grant to the other parent, if you are both students and meet the conditions for receiving SU.
You can apply for the transfer of student birth grant via minSU.
Student birth grant as a single parent

As a single parent, you will receive 12 months of student birth grants instead of 9 months. You apply for this via minSU.
