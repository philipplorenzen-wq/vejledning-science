If you are accepted into UCPH Professionals, you will receive a declaration documenting that you are considered an elite athlete at UCPH. Upload the declaration as an attachment to your dispensation application (e.g. for maximum completion time) along with any other documentation requested by the study board.

According to section 18(4) of the Danish University Act (danish), only the study board on your programme can decide whether you can be granted a dispensation. They will do this on the basis of an individual assessment.

UCPH Professionals cannot guarantee in advance that you will be given the extent of flexibility in your studies that you want.

This also applies if, for example, you find it difficult to meet the compulsory attendance requirements, or if you have special needs for assigning of classes.

However, UCPH Professionals encourages you to apply for everything you need, and the study board will then consider your requests.

UCPH Study Guidance can help you draft an individual study plan to give you an overview of which dispensations you may need to apply for.
