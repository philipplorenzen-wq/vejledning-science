Programme for International Welcome Days

The International Welcome Days will provide you with all the practical information and tools necessary for a rewarding academic, cultural, and personal experience.
Download full programme for International Welcome Days Summer (PDF)

You will get

  • an informative welcome at your new faculty, SCIENCE, and a chance to meet some of its representatives 
  • a guide on study life at SCIENCE 
  • a guide to your new city, Copenhagen 
  • an opportunity to ask any questions you might have 
  • a chance to meet other exchange students at SCIENCE.
