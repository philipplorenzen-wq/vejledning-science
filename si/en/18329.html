What happens if I register for a course I’m not allowed to take?

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-happens-if-I-register-for-a-course-I%E2%80%99m-not-allowed-to-take-SCI.aspx

Your registration will be rejected if you register for a course:

  • which your curriculum does not allow you to take
  • for which you do not meet the formal requirements
  • for which you do not have enough ECTS credits (for instance, if you have a pre-approval that you need to annul).


If your registration is rejected during the ordinary registration period

You will be notified by UCPH Study Guidance via your KUmail that your registration was rejected.

You may register for another course with vacancies during the late-registration period.

If your registration is rejected during the late-registration period

If you receive a receipt via your KUmail, stating that your registration was rejected it means that you cannot fit the course into your study programme.

You should always write to UCPH Study Guidance at studyguidance-science@adm.ku.dk by forwarding your receipt.
