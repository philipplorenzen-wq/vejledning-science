Your Master's thesis project is a very important part of the programme and is placed in the second year of the Master's programme. It is your responsibility to find a project and a supervisor, and you are free to choose between a project at SCIENCE or SUND. In August, before you begin your Master's programme, I will invite you to an introductory day during which I will tell you about the thesis project.

You also have the possibility to carry out a Project in Practice or a Project outside the course scope. You must consider carefully if you wish to replace the learning outcomes from one or more courses with a Project in Practice or a Project outside the course scope.

If you do not know in which topic to carry out your thesis project, you might consider the following:

  • At the Department of Biology, you can study the homepages of the individual researchers.
  • Projects can also be conducted at other SCIENCE departments.
  • Check the KU Project and Jobbank.
  • At the homepage of SUND, you can find available projects at the individual homepages of departments and centres. 
  • Even if a research group has not posted a vacant project, it is possible to contact the group leader to hear about the possibility of doing a project in the group.
  • Before you contact a group leader about a project, it is highly recommended that you read a little about the area of research and the work carried out in the group (homepage or publications). When contacting the group leader, make sure to explain why you find the area of research interesting.
  • Some research groups are very popular among students, so it is a good idea to start looking for a project at an early stage. It is also possible to carry out your Master's thesis project in a company. In this case, you need an internal supervisor from SCIENCE or SUND.
  • You can also carry out the Master's thesis project abroad.
