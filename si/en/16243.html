At FOOD many projects are conducted in collaboration with external companies or partners – and the possibilities for doing this are very good. Usually this works well and to the benefit of all parties. Contact me if you have further questions. 

Regarding confidentiality agreements

There are some extra, concerns you should be aware of in this connection, though - particularly linked to confidentiality. If possible, please use the standard agreement made by the faculty. If the company does not want to use that one, I recommend that you send their suggestion to me before you sign it so the university's lawyers can check that your interests are still sufficiently protected. This process usually takes 2-3 weeks.

Finding suitable projects

You may find a project through UCPH Projects and Jobs where all open projects are uploaded. It is also possible to come up with projects yourself and then contact a possible supervisor. The department will also host a block meeting once a year where relevant researchers will present an array of possibilities. You can also make projects at other departments, e.g. NEXS and IFRO.
