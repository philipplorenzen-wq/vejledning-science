  1. Contact UCPH Study Guidance 
    Start by making an appointment with UCPH Stydu Guidacne. We will then help you apply for funding.
    See how to book an appointment with UCPH Study Guidance here.
    Remember to prepare for the interview by obtaining documentation from your doctor of your functional impairment.
  2. Message in your e-Boks after 3-6 weeks
    UCPH applies for the support and/or aids that you have discussed.
    3-6 weeks later, you will be notified via e-Boks when your application has been processed.
    UCPH sends your application to the Student Counselling Service.
  3. The Student Counselling Service contacts you
    The Student Counselling Service will contact you within 10 days to arrange for an interview. This is known as a clarification interview.
  4. Assessment and/or testing of study aids
    If you have long-term effects of concussion, the IBOS Institute for the Blind and partially sighted will contact you within 10 days to arrange for an interview.
    Here they will do an assessment. On the basis of the assessment, IBOS will recommend the aids that are best suited for you, and you can test them.
  5. You receive a report
    The Student Counselling Service (and IBOS if you have concussion), will draw up a report describing the recommended aids and support.
    The report can be used as documentation for applications for dispensation and/or additional support.
  6. UCPH applies for the recommended support for you
    UCPH applies for the recommended support.
  7. Notification in your e-Boks
    You will be notified in your e-Boks when your application has been processed. This typically takes 3-6 weeks.
  8. Now your aids are ready
    You must pick up the study aids at Njalsgade 72, 7. sal (vest), 2300 København K.
