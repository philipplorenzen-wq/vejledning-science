You can withdraw from an exam at DTU before the following deadlines:

Withdrawal-deadline from exams in the study year 2025/26

Exam period
 Withdrawal period
Ordinary exams and re-exams in May (re-exams for courses with ordinary exams in December and January)
1 April - 1 May 2026
Re-exams in August (re-exams for courses with ordinary exams in May, June, and July)
By 23 July 2026
Ordinary exams and re-exams in December (re-exams for courses with ordinary exams in August)
By 15 November 2025
You need to withdraw your registration for an exam by 23:59 on the last day of the withdrawal period.
How to withdraw from an exam at DTU

You withdraw from an exam at DTU at DTU's Self Service.

If you want to withdraw from the course (including the exam), you must withdraw at SCIENCE's Self Service in the same periods and in the same way as for your courses at SCIENCE.
