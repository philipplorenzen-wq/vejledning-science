What if I have to leave Copenhagen before the exam or re-exam?

If you have to leave before the exam or the re-exam period e.g. because the semester at your home university starts early it might be possible for you to take your exam

  • at a different time
  • from a different place
  • in an alternative exam form, e.g. handing in a written paper before departure.

There are no guarantees that you can get an earlier or different exam. It depends on the type of course and the availability of the person responsible for the course.


How to apply for changing your exam

4 steps when you apply for a different exam form:

  1. Ask your course coordinator in the affected course or courses as soon as possible if it is possible to change your exam and how.
  2. Get an agreement from your course coordinator by e-mail. 
  3. Send the argeement to eksamen-frbplus@adm.ku.dk and Cc the course coordinator. Use the subject line "Exchange student, alternative exam". 
  4. The exam office will send a reply as soon as possible, when they have assessed whether the quality of your alternative exam meets the requirements from the university.


Written exams under invigilation can be difficult to change

If you must leave before a written exam under invigilation (e.g. ITX exams) you need to contact the course coordinator as soon as possible in order to find a solution.

You might have to change your course to an Individual Study Project instead of the official course. You can ask the Study Guidance about this option by writing to studyguidance-science@adm.ku.dk.
