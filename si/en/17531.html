Call, write or stop by a possible supervisor in order to find out whether he or she

  • is the right supervisor for you
  • has the academic qualifications for supervising you
  • is available


Contact potential supervisors 2-3 months before the start of the project so that the person in question is able to include your project in his/her planning.


When contacting a potential supervisor

Before contacting a potential supervisor, it is a good idea to consider the following:

  • When do you want to do the project?
  • What do you want to achieve with your project? (Any specific ideas on the drawing board.)
  • Why could this particular person be the right supervisor for your project?
  • What particularly do you want out of the supervision?
  • Is there anything you want to know about the person in question?
    For example, has the person in question tried to supervise this type of project before, how busy is he or she in the period, etc.

If the person in question is unable to supervise you, you can ask if they can recommend an alternative supervisor.
