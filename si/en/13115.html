As a student, you have the opportunity to book rooms for your exam reading.

Per. week you can at most book:

  • 1 day on the weekend for a desired timeslot 
  • 1/2 half day during teaching time (8-13 or 13-18) 
  • 2 weekday evenings after kl. 18 

Please note that there is a case processing time of 7 working days.

Contact the room administration:

  • Frederiksberg+: lokaler-frbplus@adm.ku.dk 
  • North: lokaler-noerre@adm.ku.dk
  • South: lokaler-soendre@adm.ku.dk
