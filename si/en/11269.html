I'm Karen Skriver and I'm your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic contents of your programme in detail and have contributed to determining your curriculum. My research area is protein chemistry, protein intrinsic disorder, and transcriptional networks and I teach the Project Courses: Biochemistry, Protein Science, Integrative Structural Biology, and Protein Chemistry & Enzymology.
As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.
Here is my advice and my recommendations for completing your master's programme in Biochemistry successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.
