Can I prolong the duration of an exam because of language challenges?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-prolong-the-duration-of-an-exam-because-of-language-challenges-SCI.aspx

You can apply for prolonged duration of exams in Danish if

  1. Danish is not your first language
  2. you have been enrolled at UCPH for less than two years.


When you have been enrolled at UCPH for more than 2 years, you no longer have a valid reason to apply for special conditions.

You cannot apply for a dispensation for English exams with the reason that it is not your first language.
