There is not an actual Absalon app, but the Canvas App which gives you access to Absalon via a tablet or smartphone. The app is not an official UCPH app and therefore not adapted to UCPH's needs as the desktop version of Absalon is.

If you want to use the Canvas App, please be aware of the following:

  • You cannot use it to receive UCPH information about other things than what goes on in Absalon. You will, for example, not receive study messages in the app. If you want to receive study messages on your tablet or smartphone, you must download UCPH's own app, myUCPH.
  • You will see differences in appearance, language and available functionality compared to how Absalon normally appears.

You can download the Canvas App from App Store and Google Play.

How to log on the app

When you have downloaded the app you will:

  1. be asked to enter your ‘school or district’. Please write 'Absalon’.
  2. then you must log in with your UCPH username and password. Depending on the operating system, it is possible that you will have to add @alumni.ku.dk after your username. If you are a student and an employee at UCPH at the same time, you will then have to add @ku.dk after your username.
