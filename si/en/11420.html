You have 3 attempts to pass an exam.

You use 1 exam attempt (whether or not you attend the exam) if you:

  • are still registered for a course (and therefore the exam) after the deadline for withdrawing from the exam
  • are still registered for the exam (without being registered for the course) after the deadline for withdrawing from the exam.
