You must apply for pre-approval if you want to take courses at

  • PhD or professional bachelor level at SCIENCE
  • a UCPH faculty other than SCIENCE
  • another university in Denmark or abroad.


No pre-approval required for compulsory or restricted elective courses outside SCIENCE

If your study programme includes compulsory or restricted elective courses at another faculty or university, you will not be required to apply for pre-approval – unless it is clearly stated in the description of your study programme.

Find your programme structure

You register for all compulsory and restricted elective courses on your study programme via Self Service.
