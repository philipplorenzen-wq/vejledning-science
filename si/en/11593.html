Up to four students can do a Master's thesis together based on a joint problem formulation and with a joint supervisor.

A Master's thesis in a group can, among other things

  • strengthen your cooperative skills
  • provide you with close academic sparring and new perspectives on your subject
  • prepare you for a career with project work.
