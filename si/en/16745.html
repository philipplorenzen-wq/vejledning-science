The MSc programme in Bioinformatics with a specialisation in Computer Science consists of four elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 37.5 ECTS credits
  2. Restricted elective courses, 37.5 ECTS credits
  3. Elective subjects, 15 ECTS credits
  4. Thesis, 30 ECTS credits
