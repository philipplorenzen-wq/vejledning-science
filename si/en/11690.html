You can apply for changing your exam at SCIENCE, if it takes place at the same time as your study start, courses, or exams at your host university.


You can apply for changing

  • the time and place for your exam. You can e.g. apply to do your oral exam over Teams while you are away, or move the date of your exam to be able to attend before your departure.
  • the exam type. You can e.g. apply to change an on-site exam to a written take-home assignment.


Ask your lecturer for a written suggestion for a changed exam

Talk with your lecturer/examiner on the course about which solution would fit the course and your situation most ideally, and ask her or him to formulate a written suggestion for a changed exam. You need to attach the written suggestion to your application.


How to apply for a changed exam

Apply for a dispensation as soon as you find out you need to change your exam.

4 steps when you apply for dispensation to change your exam:

  1. Get documentation of why you need to change your exam – for example papers which document when you will start your study abroad so you cannot participate in the present exam.
  2. Get a written statement from your lecturer/examiner which describes the exam she/he recommends in your case.
  3. Write a statement where you describe why and how you need to have your exam changed.
  4. Fill out an application by using “Apply for a dispensation " and enclose documentation, statement and description.
Apply for a dispensation

As a general rule, you will receive an answer within 6 weeks from when the Study Board has received all relevant information about your application. If you need to send supplementary material, you will be contacted via your KUmail.
