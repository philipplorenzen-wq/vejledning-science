My name is Amelie and I am your head of studies. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic contents of your programme in detail and have contributed to determining your curriculum. My research area is protein structures and mutations, and I teach in the courses Biological Sequence Analysis, Structural Bioinformatics and Advanced Topics for NGS sequencing.

As a Bioinformatics student, you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here are my advice and my recommendations for completing your master's programme in Bioinformatics successfully. Read it as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are written on the basis of the latest programme structure unless I have specifically written otherwise.

Heads-up: From 2027/2028 on, the Bioinformatics MSc will be majorly restructured, because the first generation of UCPH BSc Bioinformatik students will finish their degree, and the new MSc will build upon that. This may affect what courses are available for you to take in the second year. For example, I would advise against delaying first-year courses to the second year.
