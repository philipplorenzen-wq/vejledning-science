If you expect to return to the same sporting level, you can, of course, still be part of UCPH Professionals.

If you suffer from a long-term injury, please notify UCPH Professionals. You do that by sending an email to KUprof@ku.dk. Make sure to send the email from your KUmail.

If you want to take more course than planned while injured, please contact UCPH Study Guidance to get help drafting a new study plan.
