I cannot submit my Master's thesis by the deadline

You will use 1 of your 3 exam attempts if you fail to submit by the deadline stated in your thesis contract.

You (and your supervisor) have 14 days – after your original submission date – to:

  • Change your problem formulation so it remains within the same topic area, but with a slightly new angle.
  • Draw up a new Master's thesis contract. You will receive a KUmail when you can access the digital contract. If you have not received this KUmail one week after your original submission deadline, contact:
    - if your supervisor is from SCIENCE: UCPH Study Guidance at SCIENCE
    - if your supervisor is from SUND: UCPH Study Guidance at SUND.
  • Submit the new contract for approval no later than 14 days after the original deadline for submitting your thesis.
    See how to complete and submit your contract.

Even if you do not submit a new thesis contract, your new exam attempt will automatically begin no later than 14 days after your original deadline.

You have 3 months for your new attempt

Your new deadline for submission will therefore be no later than 3 months and 14 days after the Master's thesis defence.

This new deadline applies regardless of whether you are attending courses concurrently while working on your thesis. The deadline is stated under 'Thesis start' in the contract.

Your new oral defence will be planned and handled in the same way as the first.
