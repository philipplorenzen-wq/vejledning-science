A thesis preparation project is your opportunity to get tuned into the topic you are considering writing a thesis on before you commence the thesis period. You can do this from various angles – depending on your needs and interests.

For example, you can use your thesis preparation project to

  • investigate an issue that you are considering writing a thesis on  
  • immerse yourself in the theory you intend to use in your thesis
  • try out a special method
  • collect specific empirical data for the thesis.

You decide whether you want to first choose your topic, or your supervisor.

Get inspiration for your topic from

  • potential supervisors
  • interesting research groups
  • courses you would have liked to have followed.
