When you are at an exam or hand in assignments for assessment that are a precondition for or form the basis of an exam, it must be the result of your own independent work within the framework of the exam in question.

Exam cheating is when you violate the rules for your exam or the UCPH regulations, regardless of whether it is intentional or unintentional.

You are responsible for knowing and following the rules when you take an exam. So please familiarise yourself with the rules regarding exam cheating as well as the rules for the specific exam.

If you still find the rules unclear, you can ask your teacher. 

UCPH's regulations
