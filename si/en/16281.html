If you want to complain about legal issues, you must elaborate a written complaint to the Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science.

In your complaint, you must explain exactly which parts of the Study Board's decision you do not find comply with the rules.

Send your complaint to klager-science@adm.ku.dk.


How your complaint is processed

This is what happens after you have submitted your complaint:

  1. The Study Board writes an account of its reasons for the rejection.
  2. University Education Services will send the Study Board's statement to you, giving you the opportunity to comment on it before they forward the case.
  3. Your complaint, the Study Board's statement and your comments will be sent to the Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science for processing.
  4. It takes at least 2 months before you receive a reply after you have submitted your complaint. However, the processing time may be longer. University Education Services will process your case as soon as possible.
