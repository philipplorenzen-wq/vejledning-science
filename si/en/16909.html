The Master's thesis is a dominating part of the MSc study programme. As soon as possible, you should try out different project idea with potential future supervisors. One opportunity is during one of the compulsory courses, where you meet many of the relevant teachers.

Note that the master thesis is not bound to your chosen specialization i.e. you may be on the Microbiology specialization and do you master project with Marin Biology etc. 

Before the Master's thesis it is possible to conduct a Project outside the course scope (a small research project), a Project in Practice in collaboration with a public or private company or a Thesis preparation project. You are responsible for finding a project and a supervisor, but eventually I may help you.

For inspiration check out the UCPH Project and Job bank with project suggestions and supervisors.

You are always welcome to show up at or send a mail to the researchers and teachers about projects. Some laboratories are very popular, so be in good time and be prepared when contacting the researchers. 

  • Find a project and a potential supervisor: Use the course database, UCPH Projects & Jobs, project meetings, the researcher's publication lists and message board advertisements.
  • Contact possible supervisors for a meeting.
  • Be prepared and interested.
  • Contact me, if you have problems finding a project and a supervisor.
  • Remember, projects and courses outside SCIENCE requires pre-approval so be in good time.
