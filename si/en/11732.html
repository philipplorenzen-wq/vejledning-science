If you obtain access to personal data as a student, it is your responsibility to take good care of the data. Personal data may be:

  • information about test subjects
  • register data
  • interviews
  • questionnaires
  • other types of data about persons.

There are some requirements for secure storage of personal data which you must know and comply with. In some situations, you must enter into a data processing agreement with University of Copenhagen or enter into a confidentiality agreement.
