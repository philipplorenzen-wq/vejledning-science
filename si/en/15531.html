What can I complain to Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science about?

Placering: Planning your studies › Rules and dispensations › Appealing against a decision

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-can-I-complain-about-SCI.aspx

You can only complain to the Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science about legal issues. This may, for example, be the case if you think the Study Board has

  • applied incorrect rules
  • has attached importance to factually incorrect information
  • has not considered important arguments in your application.


The Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science cannot take a position on the Study Board's assessment of whether exceptional circumstances exist in your case. Nor does the Danish Agency for Higher Education and Science normally assess whether the decision is generally reasonable or appropriate.
