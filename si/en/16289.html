What should I do as an ‘active student’ while on leave?

Placering: Special support, Illness and parental leave › Illness › Long-term illness

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-should-I-do-as-an-%E2%80%98active-student%E2%80%99-while-on-leave-SCI.aspx

If you want to receive SU and be considered an active student while on leave, you should

  1. withdraw from any courses, projects and exams in the near future and avoid registering for new ones.
     
  2. apply for an extension to your Master's thesis period – if you are doing your Master's thesis.
     
  3. apply for extra monthly SU grant portions – if you can see that you will be studying for longer than the period covered by your monthly SU grant portions.
    Read more about extra monthly SU grant portions due to illness
     
  4. apply for a dispensation from the study activity requirements – if your illness prevents you from meeting your study activity requirements.
     
  5. keep an eye on your KUmail. This is where you will be notified if there is anything pressing you need to deal with.
