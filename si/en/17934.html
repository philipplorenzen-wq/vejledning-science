When you will receive a reply depends on when you have applied:

  • If you have applied November 10th., you will receive a reply to your application no later than in week 48.
  • If you have applied February 15. or May 15., you will receive a reply to your application no later than four weeks after the deadline for applications.

The reply is sent to your KUmail

All communication about your application will take place in your KUmail. Make sure to check your KUmail regularly so that you do not risk missing one of the typically short deadlines for replying and thus losing your exchange place.

If you get a place

You will be nominated by UCPH to the foreign university when you have been nominated for a place. You must then apply for admission to the foreign university. You will receive information about the admissions process at the foreign university after you have been nominated for a place.

If you do not get a place

If you do not get a place, you will receive a rejection email in your KUmail. You can still apply for an exchange place in the next application round.
