If you are going to study abroad, you can apply for a study-abroad scholarship if you are either:

  • going on a credit-awarding stay
  • taking a full programme abroad

If you are paying tuition fees on a credit-awarding stay, as part of your programme at the UCPH, you can apply for a study-abroad scholarship through the SU office at the UCPH.
You must be eligible to receive at least 1 SU-clip/1 final loan installment when you start your study abroad period, in order to be eligible for a study-abroad scholarship.
A Credit-awarding stay, is studies abroad where your study board has pre-approved the subjects you want to enrol in, so that they can be transferred with the credits you obtain, on to your programme, when you come back home to the UCPH.
