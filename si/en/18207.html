You can get help to improve your exam techniques in several ways.


SPOT ON events on exam techniques

Study and Career Guidance arranges several events a year on exam techniques. The events include presentations and exercises.


Join an event and get:

  • tips on tackling nervousness and being more confident in the exam situation
  • advice on structuring your preparation for exams
  • advice on how to present what you know at the exam.

Keep track of upcoming SPOT ON events


Exam Stress app

The Student Counselling Service has created an app called 'Exam Stress'. The app gives you digital support via your smartphone.
The app gives you tips and exercises that can help you

  • structure your time and preparation for the exam
  • stay calm and contain your nervousness
  • avoid negative, spiralling thoughts.

You can find the app on Google Play or App Store.


Talk to a study and career guidance counsellor

A study and career guidance counsellor from UCPH Study Guidance can give you

  • advice on how to handle nervousness at exams
  • help with planning your time when preparing for the exam so you feel calmer and more confident.
