The Geo Council can be your way into student politics, but it does not have to be. It is also your way of staying up to date if you like to know what is happening politically at IGN and in the Geology department specifically. It is where students have a chance to influence the structure of the programme both
  • materially
  • academically
  • socially.
Apart from that, it is also highly social and good fun. 
You are automatically a part of the council, so you only need to show up to meetings, listen, and share your opinions. If you want to have a more direct impact, you can further pursue the option of joining the various boards. 
For more information, visit the Geo Council Facebook site
