Must I be registered for a course in order to attend it?

You must generally always be registered for the course in order to attend it.
This you may not participate in without being registered for the course:

  • Laboratory-based teaching activities
  • Exercises
  • Calculation classes, etc.


You may attend lectures without being registered for a course in order to e.g. prepare for a re-exam.

If you need to take a re-exam in the course you can join the course room in Absalon. Contact the course coordinator, if you are interested in this.

Remember to register for the exam

If you want to take the exam without being registered for the course in the same block, you need to register for it yourself. However, this is only possible if the exam form allows it.
