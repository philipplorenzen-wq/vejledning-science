What are the requirements for documentation from my doctor?

The documentation from your doctor or psychologist may be

  • a medical certificate
  • a case record
  • an epicrisis
  • other documentation that your illness has prevented you from taking the exam.


The medical certificate from your doctor must state

  • that you have been ill
  • the date on which you fell ill and became unable to attend/be ready for the exam
  • your doctor's estimate of the duration of your illness
  • how your illness has affected your ability to participate in lessons and/or take the exam.

See a guide to what your documentation should include

You pay for the medical certificate yourself.
