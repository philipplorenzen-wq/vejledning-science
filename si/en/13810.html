In the video below, you will meet Emma, Sabrina, Amanda, Asger and Nikolas, who are all students at UCPH and who have a mental challenge or are neuro-divergent. 

You will hear about their thoughts and challenges when starting at the university and, for example, have PTSD, schizophrenia or Asperger's syndrome.

But you also hear how they deal with the challenges and what help they receive.
