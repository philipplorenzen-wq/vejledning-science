Which trade union should I join?

You can freely choose whether you want to be a member of a trade union and which trade union you wish to join.

Many trade unions safeguard the interests of special professional groups and only admit members from specific sectors and/or study programmes. 

Other trade unions are interdisciplinary organisations and have no admission restrictions.


4 things to consider when choosing a trade union:

  • Should it be a trade union specialising in your field?
  • What activities and services would you like a trade union to offer (e.g. legal assistance, career activities and safeguarding of political interests)?
  • What price would you like to pay?
  • Which trade union have your fellow students chosen and why?

5 unions within the area of natural science

SCIENCE collaborates with the following 5 unions – all working within the area of natural science:

  • IDA

  • DM 

  • Pharmadanmark

  • PROSA

Depending on your area of study and future career at least one of these unions could be relevant to you. But you can also look into the other unions available.
