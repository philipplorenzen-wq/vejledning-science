How do I gain access to buildings at Kalundborg?

At Kalundborg you don’t need your UCPH student ID for opening doors. The UCPH student ID is only for opening doors at the campus areas in Copenhagen.

However, you need a second student card for University College Absalon to get into the teaching buildings and the building at University College Absalon outside ordinary opening hours, e.g. in the evenings and at weekends.

You will receive a student card for University College Absalon at study start.

The student card for University College Absalon also gives you access to Helix Lab Research and Education Center. Here you must use your card, since the building is locked all time. You have access to some facilities in Helix, but not all. Use of the laboratories must be arranged by appointment.

Ordinary opening hours

Monday – Thursday
07:30-17:00
Friday
07:30-15:00

After 17:00 you have access to University College Absalon through the main entrance with your student card for University College Absalon.

The alarm is disconnected from 6:00 to 23:59 on weekdays and from 7:30 to 23:59 on weekends. You cannot access the different buildings beyond these times.

Closing days without access:

  • Weeks 27, 28 and 29 (July)
  • Christmas Eve

Closing days with access:

  • The days leading up to and during Easter
  • Friday after Ascension Day

From 25 December to 1 January, access will be from 8:00 to 18:00.

Some years, there may be days leading up to Christmas, depending on when Christmas falls.
