You are automatical registered for the ordinary exam.

You register for re-exam by contacting the Study Administration via the form below.

It's up to you to schedule your re-exam in either:

  • an ordinary exam period
  • a re-exam period

How to contact the study administration

Please contact the Study Administration using the form below if you

  • need to registrer for re-exam. Choose ‘Exam’ as the subject of your enquiry.
  • have problems with your registration.
