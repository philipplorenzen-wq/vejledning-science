I am Stephan P. A. Sauer - and I am your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies I know the academic contents of your programme in detail, and have contributed in determining your curriculum.

As a student, you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here is my advice and my recommendations for completing your master's programme in Chemistry successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are written on the basis of the latest programme structure, unless I have specifically written otherwise.
