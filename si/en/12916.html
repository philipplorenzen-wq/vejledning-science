Can I edit the time of completion?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-correct-the-time-of-completion-SU-KU.aspx

You can edit the time of completion of your programme on minSU on su.dk.
If you are a bachelor's degree student, you must provide the expected time of completion for your bachelor's programme as you have to apply for SU for your master's degree programme.
Please note! If you change the time of completion more than a month before your SU expires, you need to apply for SU from the current month. Simply put, if you want to extend your SU from July, you need to apply for SU from May.
