You can take a leave of absence during your project if you meet the requirements for taking leave of absence.

See how to take eave of absence for a particular reason (illness, parental leave, military service, etc.).

Continue with the same project after your leave of absence 

If you want to take the exam in the same project after your leave of absence, you must:

  • agree with your supervisor when you will restart your project
  • register for the for the same project
  • submit your report – possibly in a revised version that stays within your original problem formulation
  • attend the oral exam.

Start over with a new project

If you choose to start a new project, you must follow the same procedure as last time:

  • Find a new topic and possibly a company.
  • Find a new supervisor, if necessary.
  • Complete and submit a new project contract.
