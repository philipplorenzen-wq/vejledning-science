UCPH has five affiliated university chaplains whom you can contact, for example, if you need to talk to someone who is not university staff. You do not have to be a member of the Danish national church or be religious to speak to the university chaplain. 

The university chaplains offer:

  • personal conversations about issues large and small related to well-being and life. For example, if you feel inadequate, are grieving, or feel burnt-out, stressed, anxious or lonely.
  • help arranging religious ceremonies such as baptisms and weddings for students.
