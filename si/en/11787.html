I'm Henrik Siegumfeldt and I'm your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies I know the academic contents of your programme in detail, and have contributed in determining your curriculum. My research area is food microbiology and I teach on several courses within the curriculum, e.g. Microbiology of fermented food and beverages, Dairy microbiology and Brewing Process and Technology.

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here are my advice and my recommendations for completing your master's programme in Food Science and Technology successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are based on the latest programme structure unless I have specifically written otherwise.
