If you want to include courses from a previous study programme in your current study programme, you can apply for credit transfers for such courses. This applies no matter what university you took the courses at.


You can apply for credit transfers for:

  • courses from study programmes which you have already completed
  • excess ECTS credits that were not transferred from previous studies (see the letter you have received about credits transferred from previous studies)
  • ECTS credits from study programmes which you have not completed and which were not transferred from previous studies
  • courses you have taken as single subjects.
