if you plan to report a change of address to a foreign one during your study abroad, you must report the period of study abroad in minSU, so you can receive the SU-rate for students that do not live their parents (Udeboende SU).

If you plan to keep a Danish address in the Danish civil register while studying abroad, your residence status (living with or without parents) is determined, as usual, by your address registration in the civil register.
Please find more information about SU during study abroad on SU's website.
