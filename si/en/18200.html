Rules and options for the group

If a group member falls ill and cannot attend the group exam, the rest of the group must take the exam without that group member.

If the other group members choose not to take the exam, you will use one exam attempt each.


Rules and options for group members who cannot attend the exam due to illness

You can avoid using an exam attempt by notifying your faculty of your absence due to illness.
How to notify the faculty that you cannot attend an exam due to illness

Register for the re-exam and take it on your own, either in the next re-exam period or in another exam period. You can either resubmit the group's report/paper or submit a new report/paper for the re-exam.


Ask the examiner about the exam itself

Ask the examiner/course coordinator how exactly the exam will be conducted – both for the group that lacks a member and for the person who will be taking it alone.
