When do I register for courses?

You register for your courses either during

  • the ordinary registration period
  • or the late-registration period.

Ordinary registration periods

When you register during the ordinary registration period:

  • you are guaranteed a place on your compulsory course
  • you are guaranteed a place on courses with unlimited places (as in the late-registration, restrictions of places can be applied)
  • you can help to ensure that enough students register for the elective or restricted elective course of your choice for the course to be offered.

Late-registration periods

During the late-registration periods, you can only register for courses that still have available places after the ordinary registration period. Even if a course is compulsory to you, you are not guaranteed a place in the late-registration period, if it is full.

Courses with available places are grey in the course list at Self service. There might become available places during the late-registration period if someone withdraws from a course.
