What do I do, if I cannot gain access with my student ID card?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/I-cannot-gain-access-to-my-department-with-my-student-ID-card.aspx

If you think that you should have access to a room at campus – but your student ID card does not work – you can contact your campus administration with the following information: 
  • Name
  • Card number
  • Workplace/Institute/Department
  • Card reader number on the door/building you need access to
You can see which buildings and rooms you have access to in Self Service under ID card > Access rights for ID card
