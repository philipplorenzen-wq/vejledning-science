There are several places where you can find inspiration for your topic:

  • Your (potential) supervisor can act as a sounding board with insight into your field of work.
  • Courses and lecturers who have introduced you to areas you would like to work with again.
  • Courses you would like to have done about a topic that sparked your interest, but which did not tie in with your curriculum.
    Look for inspiration at courses.ku.dk
  • The departmental websites can provide information about a variety of research areas and units. See the pages on research.
  • UCPH Projects & Jobs is a database with specific project proposals from companies and research units.
