When and how do I register for exams at DTU?

You can register for exams in the following periods:

Registration for exams in the study year 2025/26

Exam period
Registration period
Ordinary exams and re-exams in May (re-exams for courses with ordinary exams in December and January)
1-15 April 2026
Re-exams in August (re-exams for courses with ordinary exams in May, June, and July)
26 June - 23 July 2026
Ordinary exams and re-exams in December (re-exams for courses with ordinary exams in August)
15 October - 1 November 2025

You need to register by 23:59 on the last day of the registration period.


How to register for exams at DTU

When you are registered for the course, you are automatically registered for the exam.

If you want to register for the exam without being registered for the course, you must

  • contact DTU.
