There is also support to be found outside UCPH if you need help tackling various issues that you may struggle with in your student life or in general. Many services are free.

If you are unsure which help or support is right for your situation, you are welcome to discuss this with your study and career guidance counsellors. Find them under Seek support and guidance at UCPH
