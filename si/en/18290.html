You can take a leave of absence during your project if you meet the requirements for taking leave of absence.

See how to take leave of absence for a particular reason (illness, parental leave, military service, etc.).

Continue with the same project after your leave of absence 

If you want to take the exam in the same project after your leave of absence, you must

  • agree with your supervisor on when you will restart you project
  • register for the same project
  • submit your report – possibly in a revised version that remains within your original problem formulation
  • attend the oral re-exam. 

Start over with a new project 

If you choose to do a new project, you must follow the same procedure as last time:

  • Find a new subject and possibly a new company.
  • Find a new supervisor, if necessary.
  • Complete and submit a new project contract.
