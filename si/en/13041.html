To be entitled to SU, you must:

  • Fulfil the activity requirements

SU activity requirements

The SU activity requirements differ from the activity requirements of your degree programme.

According to the SU rules, you are allowed on your degree programme, to be delayed up to a maximum of 30 ECTS as regards to your grant portions (one grant portion corresponds to one month's SU).

NOTE: If you started on your current programme before July 2016, you are allowed to be delayed up to 60 ECTS.

The study activity is calculated on your current programme. Childbirth portions and additional grant portions to make up for delays are not included in the review of used monthly grant portions.

Childbirth portion example

If you have used 18 grant portions and received  additional childbirth portions for 6 months, you are registered in total; of 18 grant portions.
