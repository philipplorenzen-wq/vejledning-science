You can find all your registered exam grades via Self Service > Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Grade results.

Your exam grades will be posted on Self Service no later than four weeks after:

  • you have submitted a written exam assignment
  • you have taken an oral exam
  • your course with ongoing tests is completed.
