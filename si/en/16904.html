As part of you studies you can also do a Project in practice, Project outside the course scope and/or a Thesis preparation project, for no more than 30 ECTS in total.

For students starting in 2021/22 or earlier, you can do projects for up to 45 ECTS in total.

Project in practice

A project in practice can count as 15 ECTS credits of electives or restricted electives. It is a group or individual project written in cooperation with a company or external organization. Note that it is your own responsibility to find a company to work with.
Many MSc students in Agricultural Economics have written a project in practice in relation to an internship at a Danish embassy, somewhere in the world.
Project outside the course scope

You also have the option of writing a project outside the course scope for up to 15 ECTS credits, which typically counts amongst the free electives (but, subject to approval, potentially also can count as restricted elective).

Thesis preparation project

Furthermore it is possible to write a thesis preparation project for either 7.5 or 15 ECTS (and it is possible to apply for a dispensation to try to get this to count as a restricted elective).
