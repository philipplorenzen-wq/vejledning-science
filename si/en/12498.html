FC SparC is the football(soccer) club for football-interested students of Computer Science and Machine Learning. We meet weekly for trainings, and play games in the season based SCIENCEcuppen. Which is the Science campus football tournament among the various educations on campus. The tournament runs twice a year. We have the best third period of all the clubs on campus.
The name derives from the club's origins in the eighties, and is based on a supercomputer architecture.
We are primarily a danish association, but everyone will be welcomed with open arms and a injury-prone slide tackle.

Contact: fcsparc@di.ku.dk
