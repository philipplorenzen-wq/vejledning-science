The study programme (1st year abroad, 2nd year at UCPH) is set at 120 ECTS credits and consists of the following:

  • Compulsory subject elements at partner university, 60 ECTS credits (1st year)
    Remember to transfer credits from your 1st year as soon as you start at UCPH
  • Compulsory subject elements, 7.5 ECTS credits 
  • Restricted elective courses, 15 ECTS credits.
  • Elective subjecs, 7.5 ECTS credits.
  • Thesis, 30 ECTS credits

Elective subjects

You have 7.5 ECTS credits to strengthen your own profile at the programme.
Read more at 'How can I use my elective ECTS credits?'
