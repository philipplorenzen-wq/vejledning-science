What does the Buddy Programme require of me?

As a participant in the Buddy Programme, you should

  • be prepared to get actively involved and getting to know your Buddies – your Buddies are in it to meet you and they expect a mutual cultural exchange
  • be responsive and proactive when communicating with your Buddies
  • keep your appointments
  • respect that there can be times when your Buddies will not be able to assist you.

Always keep in mind that the SCIENCE Buddies are volunteers who spend their free time helping new international exchange students.
