How do I document my decreased functionality or illness?

Placering: Exams › Get ready for your exams › Special exam conditions

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Gæstestuderende SCI - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-document-my-decreased-functionality-or-illness-EX-SCI.aspx

You must document your decreased functionality or illness with a doctor's certificate or statement from your psychologist or psychiatrist that states

  1. the nature of the decreased functionality or illness
  2. when it appeared and how long he/she expects it to last
  3. how your decreased functionality or illness affects your ability to take the exam.
See a guide to what your documentation should include

      
In these situations, you will need documentation

You will need the documentation when you apply for special conditions at the exam or other exemptions you apply for on the basis of your decreased functionality or illness.
