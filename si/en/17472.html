How do I pass a restricted elective course after the last exam option?

Placering: Courses and teaching › Course changes › Discontinued courses

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-pass-a-restricted-elective-course-after-the-last-exam-option-SCI.aspx

When a last exam option has been offered, you pass your discontinued restricted elective course by registering for the course(s) stated in the interim arrangement for the discontinued course.

Find the list of discontinued courses that apply to your study programme under Programme structure.


If an interim arrangement exists, your course will be replaced by

  • an identical course


If no interim arrangement with an identical course exists

You must choose and register for a new restricted elective course from your curriculum to replace the discontinued course when

  1. no identical course is being offered
  2. the registration period for the last exam option in the discontinued course has expired.
