Elections are held at UCPH once a year. Votes are cast electronically via KUnet.

The election follows the Danish University Act, which states that elections must be held for:

  • the University Board
  • the academic councils
  • the study boards
  • the PhD committees.

The election is segregated, such that teachers/researchers, administrative staff and students vote separately. Students have the opportunity to vote every year. You can also stand for election.
