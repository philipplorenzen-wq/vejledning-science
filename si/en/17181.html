Through UCPH, you have the opportunity to take summer courses at Nordic Centre in Shanghai, China.
The centre is an association of leading universities and research institutions in Denmark, Finland, Sweden, Norway and Iceland, and is located at Fudan University.

Read more about the courses

  • on Nordic Centre Shanghai's website

How to apply

You must apply by 6 March at the latest by

  1. completing the relevant application form (please find the application form via the websites above)
  2. submitting the form by email to Outgoingstudents@adm.ku.dk with the following attachments:
    • Transcript of grades from Self Service
    • One letter of recommendation from a lecturer/supervisor, former employer or similar.
    • Proof of English-language proficiency from, for example, upper secondary school.
