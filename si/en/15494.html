Gælder for målgrupperne: Chemistry - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Expectations-of-you-SCI-CHEM.aspx

There are several ways to get through your Master's degree in chemistry, and it is important that you take responsibility for how you study and your choices. I recommend you to choose what interests you.

If you need to qualify your choices then you can contact me or the UCPH Study Guidance.

Good advice:

  • You should participate in the Welcome Programme. See programme and details.
  • You should participate in the Safety Course in Welcome Programme at MSc Introduction at the Department of Chemistry. You need to pass both the practical and theoretical part of Safety course before you can start on your master's thesis.
  • Your study is your full-time work. You should expect to spend 35-45 hours per week. It is important that you get structure in your everyday life.
  • You need to know your study programme and its rules.
  • You could make a project outside course scope before you start your master's thesis. This enables you to get to know your supervisor/research group. Read more about Project outside the course scope.
  • You could also do a project in collaboration with a company. Read more about Project in Practice. 
  • If your thesis will involve long time experiments that cannot be carried out during the 9 month for your master's thesis or if you need to learn experimental or theoretical techniques before you can start on your master's thesis, which are not covered by any of the courses, you could make a thesis preparation project. Read more about Thesis preparation project.
  • You need to evaluate your courses after each block. Please, give feedback to your teachers.
  • You must avoid plagiarism and cheating in the examination. Read about cheating and plagiarism.
  • You should participate in academic events such. SPOT ON events, department seminars and conferences.
  • Read your study messages and study events not to miss important messages and events.  
  • You should involve yourself in student politics: Council of chemist students.
  • You should involve yourself in Social activities: International dinners, Christmas dinner, Galla party, etc.
  • Avoid stress. You must figure out how get relaxed and refilled with energy. Read more about positive and negative stress.
  • Are you an international student? You may experience you first oral exam after block 1. You may want to practice before the oral exam. Read about good exam technique.
  • Are you an international student? Do you want to learn basic Danish? See available courses.
