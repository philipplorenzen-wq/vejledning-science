Depending on the size of your project, you can work with it over one or more blocks:

  • A project worth 12,5 ECTS credits or less can be done full-time over one block – in block 1, 2, 3, 4 or 5.
  • A project worth 15 ECTS credits can either be done full-time over one block, or part-time over two blocks – in block 1+2, 2+3, 3+4, 4+5 or 5+1.

See what size your project may be under 'How many and which ECTS credits can I use on a project in practice?'.

You must start your project at the start of the block and finish it by the end of the exam week in the last block of the project.
