You arrange your academic internship yourself, and the process therefore depends on the actual internship site.
 

When you have found an internship company or organisation, you must:

  • enter into a contract with the host. The contract may contain details on tasks and duties, insurance, duration and contact. It is important that you enter into a contract, as this ensures that your stay meets both parties' expectations.
  • apply for pre-approval, accommodation, grants and scholarships, take out any required insurance and, if necessary, apply for a visa – in short, sorting out all the practical matters needed
