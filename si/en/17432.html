It is a good idea to study abroad at some point during your studies. Especially for Danish students who have not been on exchange during their Bachelor's programme (but applicable to everyone), we strongly encourage you to consider doing so during your Master programme.

The flexibility of the programme makes it easy to do so, especially in block 1 and 2 in your second year.

University of Copenhagen has formalised exchange agreements with many universities abroad.

Read about the different exchange places, application deadlines and procedures.
