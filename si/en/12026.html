There are 2 overall types of applications:
  • a solicited application in response to a specific job or project advertisement
  • an unsolicited application which you send to a company without a position having been advertised.   
Even though it may be outside your comfort zone, it is a good idea also to submit unsolicited applications. Up towards half of all vacant jobs are, in fact, filled without having been advertised, and the same applies to many project collaborations.
