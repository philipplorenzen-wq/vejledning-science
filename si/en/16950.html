By complaining, you can achieve the following:

  • You can help to improve conditions for fellow students.
  • If you complain during the course, the issue may be resolved while the course is still in progress – for the benefit of you and your fellow students.

Make your opinion known in the course evaluation


You can also help to improve conditions for future students by participating in the course evaluation.
