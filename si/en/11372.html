If you experience that you are being mistreated or incorrectly assessed for an exam, you have the opportunity to complain. You can complain about all exams, including written and oral exams, oral defences and course assessments.

If your complaint is upheld, you will be offered either

  • a new assessment (only written examinations)
  • a re-exam.
