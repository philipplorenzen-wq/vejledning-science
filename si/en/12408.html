Answer the KUmail you have received with the decision directly, if you e.g.

  • think a mistake has been made and you can back it up in e.g. in a course description or some form of documentation
  • want to send new relevant information or documents, which you think may change the decision.

You'll receive an answer from the Study Board when they have had the chance to consider your points.
