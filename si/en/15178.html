Rethink IFRO is a student activist organization at Frederiksberg campus, University of Copenhagen.

We recognize that neoliberal economics is again and again failing to solve major problems, such as the climate crisis and the increasing inequality.

Our organization aims to work towards a more pluralistic and inclusive view of economics at IFRO (Department of Food and Resource Economics) and engage students in more critical and pluralistic thinking during their studies.
