Why am I granted support for only six months at a time?

This is because your need for support may change from one semester to the next.

The SPS counsellors at UCPH reapply for support on your behalf after each semester. Therefore, you do not need to do anything yourself if your need for support has not changed.

The SPS counsellors reapply for your support around May–August and November–January. You will be notified via Digital Post when your support has been reapplied for.

If your need for support has changed, you must contact the UCPH Study Guidance so the counsellors can help you.

Contact UCPH Study Guidance

Please note:

If, for example, you have been on sick leave, taken a leave of absence, or have not needed support during a semester: when you resume your studies, you can apply for support again.

Contact the SPS advisors so they can apply for the support on your behalf.

Write to sps@adm.ku.dk
