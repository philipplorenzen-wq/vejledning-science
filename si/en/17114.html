Which unemployment fund should I join?

You can freely choose whether you want to be a member of an unemployment fund and which unemployment fund you wish to join.

Many unemployment funds only admit members from specific sectors and/or study programmes. Others are interdisciplinary organisations and have no admission restrictions. Choose the unemployment fund that you find makes the most sense for you.


You may consider the following when choosing an unemployment fund:

  • Should it be an unemployment fund specialised in your field? You may check whether your trade union collaborates with a specific unemployment fund.
  • Which services would you like to receive from an unemployment fund? Look at the services offered by various unemployment funds.
  • What price would you like to pay?
  • Which unemployment fund have your fellow students chosen and why?
