It can be difficult to choose between all the many courses, which you can use your elective ECTS credits on.

Study and Career guidance at UCPH Study Guidance has 3 tips for you to help you choose.

Tip #1: Research

Find out which possibilities you have on your programme.

  • How many elective ECTS credits do you have?
  • Do you have the possibility to do a project in practice?
  • Can you do a project outside the course scope?

See which possibilities you have under Programme structure

Tip #2: Reflect

Think about what you would like to do with your elective courses

Your electives can be used to influence your programme with your personal preferences. You can, for instance,

  • zoom out and create an overview of an academic field
  • dive into an interesting subject field
  • take your point of departure in a specific theory
  • use a specific method
  • try a course at another faculty or university.

Then research which concrete courses can give you the possibility to do exactly what you would like. And remember to be curious. Even courses that may seem wrong can teach you a lot about what you would like to work with later on. When you practice choosing and testing your own plans, you are learning how to become a good academic.

Find inspiration for choosing courses in recommendations from your Head of Studies

How to use courses.ku.dk to find exciting courses at UCPH

Tip #3: Register

When you know which possibilities you have to choose from and what you would like to choose, you are ready to register for the courses.

You can register in the course registration period.

Please note that you are bound to your course when the late-registration period for the block has ended.

If you would like to take courses outside SCIENCE, you need to apply for

  • a pre-approval
  • registration at the faculty or university where the course is offered.
