  My name is Morten S. Risager and I am a Professor of Mathematics and your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic content of your programme in detail and have contributed to determining your curriculum. My research area is analytic number theory, and I teach on the bachelor courses Introduction to the Mathematical Sciences and  Introduction to Number Theory, and the masters course Analytic Number Theory.

Our study programme is quite flexible, and allows you, to a large extent to choose courses that best fit with your interests and career plans. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here is my advice and my recommendations for successfully completing your master's programme in MSc programmes in Mathematics and Mathematics with a Minor (Sidefag). Read my advice as inspiration for your choices, not as strict rules.
