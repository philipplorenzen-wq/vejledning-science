Relevant insurance policies for students:

  • Accident insurance (full-time)
    Covers financial consequences of an accident.
  • Liability insurance
    Covers your financial losses, for example, if you are to pay compensation to others.

Contact your/an insurance company for further information and to take out insurance. 

If you are injured as an employee or someone performing a job for UCPH in another capacity

If you are employed by UCPH, for example as a student assistant or a tutor and you are paid for your work, you are covered by the University's industrial injury insurance in case of work-related injuries.

You may be covered by the University's industrial injury insurance if, at the time of the injury, you are performing a job for, under the instruction of and in the interest of the University of Copenhagen.

If you have been injured under such circumstances, you must contact the person who assigned you the job.
