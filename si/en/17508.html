How much time am I expected to spend on Buddy activities?

You are expected to spend an average of 4-6 hours per month during the Buddy period on various Buddy activities. However, you should expect to use more hours at the beginning of each semester, as the majority of the activities are placed when the exchange students arrive.

The number of hours will depend on how much help the students in your group need, and what your personal schedule allows.

Your tasks as a Buddy

  • greeting the students
  • helping out during the welcome programme
  • organising events and activities 
  • check-ins and meetings
  • participating in social and cultural events hosted by the Buddy coordinators 
  • participating in the kick-off events together with the other volunteer buddies.

Planned activities:

Autumn semester 2026

  • 14 August from 16-18 // Kick off event for all volunteer buddies at Frederiksberg Campus
  • 27 August  // Meet the new exchange students and help with campus tours in the Welcome programme at North Campus
  • 11 September from 16-20 // Sightseeing by boat and social night with food with all the exchange students and the other volunteer Buddies
  • Week 41 (5-9 October) // 15 minutes online check in with your Buddy Coordinators and your buddy group

To receive your official Certificate of Recognition that acknowledges your contribution you need to participate in as many events as possible. 

If you already know that you are unable to participate in some events, you are still welcome to apply, but please mention it when you apply.
