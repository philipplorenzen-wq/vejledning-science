Can I follow the teaching without having a place on the course?

If you have not been given a place on a course, you are only entitled to attend lectures. You are not entitled to participate in exercises/laboratory-based teaching activities or other classes, nor do you have access to the course room in Absalon.

If you want to take the exam

If you want to take the exam, you must register for it yourself.

You can only take the exam and pass the course if

  • allowed by the exam form
  • you are able to meet any examination prerequisites before the exam, or if you already meet these requirements.
