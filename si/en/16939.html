When will I receive a reply to my application?

As a general rule, you will receive a reply to your application within 6 weeks from when

  • the Study Board has received all the information (documentation etc.) necessary to process your application
  • the application deadline expires – where there is such a deadline.

If you receive an email alerting you that you will be disenrolled from your study programme within 14 days while your application is being processed, you will not be disenrolled until you have received a reply to your application.


Shorter or longer processing time

It will often take less than 6 weeks before you receive a reply to your application. But the processing time may also be longer if, for example, a special issue needs to be examined.
If it takes more than 6 weeks to process your application, you will be notified via your KUmail.
If your application is particularly urgent, you can state this and the reason why in the comments field in the online form. It is unfortunately not always possible to process your application more quickly.


Other deadlines in the summer period

The Study Board is closed for the summer holidays from late June until the end of August. If you submit an application less than 6 weeks before the summer holidays, you cannot expect a reply until September.
Urgent applications during the summer holiday may sometimes be processed by the chairmanship of the Study Board. Such applications may, for example, concern postponement of the deadline for submitting your Master's thesis and additional exam attempts for the re-exams in August.


Processing times for the 2024-25 academic year

Processing times for pre-approvals and credit transfers in 2024-25:

  • 97,8 % of all applications for pre-approvals and credit transfers were processed within 6 weeks.
  • The average application processing time was 7,6 days.

Processing times for applications for exemptions in 2024-25:

  • 89,5 % of all applications for exemptions were processed within 6 weeks.
  • The average application processing time was 15,5 days.
