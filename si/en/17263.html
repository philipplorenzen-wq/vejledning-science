You need a primary supervisor from UCPH when you undertake a project. Additionally, you'll also have a contact person in the company who doesn't have formal responsibility for your project.


Supervisor or topic first?

Whether you start by choosing a company and topic or a supervisor first depends on your approach and priorities regarding the project.

3 things to consider regarding a supervisor
  1. A supervisor can sometimes guide you towards a company/organization within their field of expertise or network.
  2. If you have already chosen a topic/company, you can find your supervisor based on the specific field you've chosen.
  3. If you have a particular supervisor in mind whom you would like to work with, it might be logical to start there.
