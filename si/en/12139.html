UCPH Professionals is a scheme for UCPH students who are elite athletes, professional coaches or referees at the highest national or international level in their sport.

If you are accepted into the scheme, you can apply to the study board on your programme for more flexibility in your day-to-day student life.

 That could be:

  •  If you need to extend your completion time or study at reduced hours for a period of time.
  • Applying for pre-approval of your study plan, which the UCPH Study Guidance can help you prepare. The study plan is approved for one year at a time.

 Scheme membership also applies for one year at a time.

If you’re curious about KU Professionals, you can watch the video to learn more.
