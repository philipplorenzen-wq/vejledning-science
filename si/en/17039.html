If you do not pass the exam, you might be able to do a re-exam. 

Contact UCPH Study Guidance at studyguidance-science@adm.ku.dk and your home university as soon as possible to work out a solution.

The exchange agreement that governs your admission may impose certain restrictions in terms of extending the deadline.
