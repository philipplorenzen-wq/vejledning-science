Students, student associations and other associations that are based at or academically affiliated with the University of Copenhagen can borrow a room.
What can rooms be used for?You can borrow rooms for meetings, lectures, seminars and conferences for work-related activities or student and association activities. You can also borrow rooms for social activities and other events that are in line with the University's objectives.  
You cannot borrow rooms for commercial or religious events or for private parties.
If you borrow a room, you are not allowed to lend it to others, neither fully nor partially, and it cannot be used for other purposes than what has been agreed.
Events must be:  
  • compatible with democratic values
  • compatible with the University's reputation and academic spirit
  • carried out safely.

Smoking Policy

As of January 1, 2026, the University of Copenhagen has a new smoking policy. If you borrow a room at the University, you must, of course, comply with the smoking policy. Read more on this page under “Am I allowed to smoke on campus?”
