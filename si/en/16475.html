When do I need to join?

To become eligible for unemployment benefits as a new graduate, you must join an unemployment fund within 14 days of graduating your study programme.

If you are already a member of an unemployment fund, you must notify the unemployment fund that you have graduated.


Examine the criteria laid down by the unemployment fund before you graduate

Before you graduate, it is a good idea to contact the unemployment fund you want to join and find out 

  • how to join
  • what criteria there are
  • what deadlines you must meet to be eligible for unemployment benefits. For example registering 1 year before graduation may give you earlier access unemployment funds.

Register with the job centre

You must also register with your local job centre in your municipality if you become unemployed.
