When am I eligible for a dispensation?

There may be grounds for a dispensation if your needs are based on exceptional circumstances. You must be able to document any exceptional circumstances stated in your application.


Examples of exceptional circumstances may be

  • death or serious illness in your immediate family
  • your own serious illness
  • a need for special conditions or aids and support materials for exams
  • other special circumstances that affect your studies.


Examples of circumstances which do not qualify as exceptional circumstances

  • Your student job has taken up too much time
  • Your housing situation or financial circumstances.

You can mention such circumstances in your application, but they cannot stand alone when you apply for a dispensation.
