The MSc programme in Mathematics with a general profile consists of three elements:

  1. Restricted elective courses, 60 ECTS credits
  2. Elective subjects, 30 ECTS credits
  3. Thesis, 30 ECTS credits

If you are on the MSc programme in Mathematics with a minor,  your programme consists of:

  1. Restricted elective courses, 45 ECTS credits
  2. Minor subject, 45 or 75 ECTS credits from your minor subject (depending on the minor)
  3. Thesis, 30 ECTS credits
