When can I do my Master's thesis?

Your study board has made a recommendation for when in your programme to place your Master’s thesis. See the recommended block scheduling of your Master's thesis under Programme structure.

You can choose to place your Master’s thesis differently, as long as you 

  • begin your Master’s thesis after your first year of study.
    It is not a requirement that you have passed all courses from the first year of study.
  • can still comply with the study activity requirements.
    Read more about the study activity requirements.

Your Master’s thesis does not have to be the last thing you pass on your programme.

Your Master's thesis is 30 ECTS credits (4 months).

If you follow courses and projects alongside the Master’s thesis 

You are allowed to take courses alongside your Master’s thesis. Just be aware that it will not extend the Master’s thesis period.


Consider doing a Master’s thesis preparation project 

You have the option of doing a Master’s thesis preparation project.
You do a Master’s thesis preparation project before you begin your Master’s thesis and you can use it for e.g.
  • collecting empirical data 
  • run experiments
  • doing field work.
Read more about how you can do a Master’s thesis preparation project
