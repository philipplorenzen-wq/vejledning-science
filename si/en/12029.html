A student job next to your studies is an excellent idea because it gives you
  • experiences and competences from working life
  • an opportunity to try out your expertise from your study programme in practice
  • knowledge about what is important to you in your coming career
  • contacts in the labour market in your network. 
All things that are highly valuable to you when looking for your first job as a graduate.
