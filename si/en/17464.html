Do I need to apply for admission all over again if I want to switch to another study programme?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Do-I-need-to-apply-for-admission-all-over-again-if-I-want-to-switch-to-another-study-programme-SCI.aspx

If you want to withdraw from your study programme in order to switch to another study programme at SCIENCE, you do not necessarily need to apply for admission all over again.


Read more about rules and procedures at:
www.science.ku.dk > Courses and programmes > 'Your educational level' > Choose the study programme you want to switch to > Application procedure > Are you studying or have you studied a Master's degree programme? > Change of study programme/transfer


If you want to switch to a different faculty at UCPH, or another university, you must inquire about the rules at the educational institution in question.
