You can annul your application for a dispensation. The procedure for doing so depends on whether you have received a reply to your application.


If you have not received a reply to your application

Fill out the form 'Get Study Administrative Help'.

Please specify clearly which application this concerns.


Get Study Administrative Help

If your application has been approved

Simply reply to the mail you have received via KUmail about the decision, stating that you wish to annul your dispensation.
