Do I need to meet the SU activity requirement if I have been granted dispensation from my programme?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Do-I-need-to-meet-the-SU-activity-requirement-if-I-have-been-granted-dispensation-from-my-programme.aspx

Yes. You must meet the SU activity requirement even though you have been granted dispensation from your programme.

The reason is that your degree programme grants dispensation from the rule of study progress. However, the Danish SU has its own regulations, which means that dispensations granted by your programme administration do not apply in SU contexts. 

If you are delayed on your programme due to illness, other special conditions or council work, you can apply for additional grant portions.

You can read more about SU and dispensations here.
