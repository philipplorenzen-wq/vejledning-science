When can I apply for scholarships?

Placering: SU and grants › Grants › UCPH grants

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/When-can-I-apply-for-scholarships-ku.aspx

You can only apply for scholarships when they have been announced, and you must apply electronically via a link in the call for applications.

Scholarships will be announced under 'News' on the front page of KUnet.

See Guideline for filling out the form.
