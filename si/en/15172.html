Being injured can be mentally hard. Maybe you worry that your injury will affect your sporting level? Or maybe you feel alone because you're currently not part of the training community in your club.

As a student at UCPH, you can get free psychological support at the Student Counselling Service. You can also ask your association or club if they can pay for a sports psychologist.
