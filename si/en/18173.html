How do I take an exam on a changed course?

Placering: Exams › If you do not pass › Re-exams

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-take-an-exam-on-a-changed-course-SCI.aspx

If you need to pass an exam on a course that has changed to a great extent since you attended it, it can be a challenge to take the exam.

Do you think that the exam type or exam basis has been changed to such an extent that you cannot take the exam without taking the course again, you can write to studyguidance-science@adm.ku.dk.

In that case, an academic assessment will be made to determine whether you can take the exam on the terms and conditions described in the course description from the previous academic year.
