At a career fair, you can meet and network with representatives from private and public workplaces.

This is relevant both during your studies and when you start seeking your first job after graduation.

You can attend a career fair to:

  • Establish connections with employers you are interested in and may want to work for or do a project collaboration with.
  • Discover new companies/organizations that work on topics you find exciting.
  • Gain insight into the skills different workplaces are seeking.
