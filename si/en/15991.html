You may do one project in practice for 15 ECTS credits (workload: 412 hours) during your course of study.

A project in practice can only count as elective ECTS credits on your study programme.

Check if there is a limit on how many ECTS credits you can use in total for different projects


Apply for a dispensation to use your restricted elective ECTS credits

You can apply to your Study Board for a dispensation to do your project in practice with restricted elective ECTS credits.

How to apply for a dispensation:

Prepare a document with a draft project contract. The draft must include:
  • the type of project it concerns
  • how many ECTS credits it gives
  • the title of the project
  • a project description.
Complete the form ‘Approval of a SCIENCE course as restricted elective’. Remember to attach the draft project contract.

Complete the dispensation form

Send your dispensation application well in advance

Usually, you will receive a response within 6 weeks from the date the study board has received all relevant information about your case.
You must have received a response to your dispensation application before you can start completing your project contract.
