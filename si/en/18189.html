If several students want to complain about the same situation during the exam, the complaint can be submitted as a joint exam complaint. One person must send the complaint, but all complainants must sign it.

In the case processing, the student who submitted the complaint will be the contact person. The contact person will comment on behalf of all complainants. The decision on the exam complaint is sent to all signatories.

Suppose the faculty upholds a complaint about exam conditions. In that case, the faculty will compensate all students whose exam suffered from the error or defect in question.
