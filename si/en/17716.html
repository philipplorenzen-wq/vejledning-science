How do I cite generative AI as a source?

The same requirements for quotation marks and source referencing apply as with any other source.

If you use content from generative AI in your project or written take-home assignment (directly, in edited form or in any other way), you should always quote and cite generative AI as a source.

How you should cite generative AI, and what the citation should look like, depends on the referencing style and type of reference required by your course coordinator.

It is considered plagiarism if you fail to cite correctly.

Ask your supervisor or course coordinator if you are in doubt about how to cite generative AI as a source.
