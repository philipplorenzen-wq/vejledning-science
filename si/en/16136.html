When will I receive my diploma?

You will automatically receive your digital diploma in your official Danish online mailbox ‘e-Boks’ no later than two months after your last exam has been assessed. The month of July is not included when calculating the two-month deadline due to the summer holiday.
You can only receive your diploma if your programme status at Self service is 'completed'.

How to check your programme status:
  1. Go to Self Service under 'Enrolment'
  2. Check whether your 'Education setup status' is set to 'Completed'.

Go to Self Service


If your status is set to completed and you have not received your diploma, you can get your status by calling UCPH Study Guidance.

Has your programme status not been updated to ‘Completed’?

If your programme status in Self Service is not set to completed, even though your final grade has been entered, you must fill in the form ‘Get Study Administrative Help’.

In the form, please state that you are still listed as an active student, even though your final grade has been registered.
