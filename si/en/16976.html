You may bring the exam aids and support materials stated in the course description.

Find your course description in the course database 


Explanation of the exam aids stated in the course description

Your course description will state 1 of the 4 following rules on exam aids:

  • No aids and support materials permitted
    You may only bring pens, markers or pencils to the exam.
    Unless otherwise indicated, you must write the exam with a ballpoint pen.
  • Certain aids and support materials permitted
    You may bring certain specific aids and support materials which will be listed in your course description, for example calculators, dictionaries, notes, etc.
  • Written aids and support materials permitted
    You may bring written materials such as books, notes, calculations, etc. NB: does not include tablets and computers.
  • All aids permitted
    All aids and support materials permitted, including computers, tablets, smartwatches, earphones, noise cancelling ear muffs and mobile phones, are permitted as aids, e.g. for looking up notes and making calculations.
    However, you may not bring your own printer and you are not allowed to be online during the exam.  


You are responsible for your aids and devices

The exam invigilators can't help you if you have left something behind or have technical problems with your devices.


Rules on phones, tablets, smartwatches and computers

You may only bring electronic devices if this is stated in the course description or if all aids are permitted at your exam.
You may never use your phone, tablet, smartwatch or computer to

  • communicate with others inside or outside the exam room
  • use the internet
  • set to anything other than flight mode for the duration of the exam.

If you are not allowed to bring your phone, you will usually be able to leave it with the invigilators during the exam.


Food and drink in the exam room

You are allowed to bring food and drink.
However, show consideration for the other examinees by not bringing food with a strong odour, loud wrapping, etc.


Apply for dispensation to bring your SPS aids

If you have received aids through the SPS Counselling, you can apply for special exam conditions to bring your SPS aids to the exam.
