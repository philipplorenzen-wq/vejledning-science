You can use the exam aids mentioned in the course description for the course you are to sit an exam in. Find the course description in the course database.

Programs on the exam PC

  • The following standard programs are available on the PCs for all exams:
  • Windows 10
  • Word 2021
  • PowerPoint 2021
  • Digital Pen (see Instruction for digital pen oct. 2022)

Please note that the IT supporters will help you start the programs, but, with the exception of Word and Digital Pen, they will not support the use of the programs.

Other programs and exam aids

In the PDF, you will find a detailed explanation of which software and aids are available for your exam if your course description includes one of the following five definitions of permitted aids:
  • No aids
  • Certain aids
  • Written aids
  • All aids except internet and generative AI
  • All aids
