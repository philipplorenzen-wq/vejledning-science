Your Erasmus+ grant will depend on the destination of your Erasmus exchange. The rates change every year and the Erasmus grant is calculated based on the period of time you are on exchange.

 International Education will notify you about the amount of your Erasmus grant directly through your KUmail. Exchanges starting in autumn semester will be notified in June, and those starting in spring semester will be notified in November.

Please note: UCPH is only allowed to give you an Erasmus grant for the period of time where you are physically present at the host university and have obligations at the host university (meaning: intro weeks, lecture period, exams). This means that you must expect that your Erasmus grants will be re-calculated when you have evaluated your Erasmus exchange at the end of your stay. You will therefore know the final Erasmus scholarship when the reporting of your Erasmus scholarship has been approved.

This means that you can never be granted a larger Erasmus grant than the one calculated based on the official semester dates prior to your Erasmus exchange.

Erasmus grant for students applying by the deadline 8 September 2023

If you apply for an Erasmus Exchange in the Spring 2024 in the application round in September, please be aware that you might receive a smaller Erasmus grant, than students who have applied by the application deadlines 1 November 2022 and 15 February 2023.

The reason is, that UCPH has received less Erasmus funding from the EU Commission than we applied for.

We will send information about the size of your Erasmus grant in November 2023, if you receive an Erasmus exchange.
