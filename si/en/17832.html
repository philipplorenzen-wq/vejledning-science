Will my grade be transferred together with my credits?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Will-my-grade-be-transferred-together-with-my-credits-SCI.aspx

If you have taken courses in Denmark which are graded, your grades will be transferred.

Courses which you have taken abroad are always registered as pass/fail.
