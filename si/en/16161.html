What are the rules when I am on campus?

You must behave properly and show consideration for others on campus – both towards fellow students and staff. This means, among other things, that you may not:

  • create nuisance to others
  • cause harm to others
  • destroy other people's or the university's property.

Overnight stays in SCIENCE's buildings are not allowed.

As a student, you must follow the instructions of UCPH staff and guards.
Read more in the regulations at SCIENCE (pdf)

You must wear your student ID card visibly

It is important that you always carry your student ID card with you and your card is visible.

You may be asked to show your student ID card by UCPH guards or other staff employed to monitor and look after the campus.

In special circumstances, it may be necessary to take a photo of your student ID card if your behaviour does not comply with the regulations at UCPH and SCIENCE. For example, if you sleep on campus, have left a lot of mess and garbage after an event or behave in other ways that do not comply with the rules of disciplinary measures on UCPH and SCIENCE.

Your personal information is subject to UCPH's general rules on information security.

Repeated or serious violation of the rules

Repeated and/or serious violations of the rules will be considered a breach of the UCPH's rules of disciplinary measures.

In such cases, it may be necessary to confiscate your student ID card and exclude you from campus.

Read more in the UCPH rules of disciplinary measures (pdf)
