When is best time to go abroad to study?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/When-is-best-time-to-go-abroad-to-study-SCI-AGECON.aspx

On the Master's programme in Agricultural Economics, your Study Board recommends that you study abroad in

  • year 2, blocks 1 and 2 (for you who was admitted in September).
  • year 2, blocks 3 and 4 (for you who was admitted in February).

This period is your 'mobility window' and may contain both elective, compulsory and restricted elective courses.

See the courses offered during  the mobility window on your study programme by selecting your year of enrolment under Planning your studies
