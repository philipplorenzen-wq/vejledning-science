There are many career options after finishing the program. More and more companies specialize specifically in quantum technology. There are also many sectors where quantum technology becomes increasingly relevant from communication to the medical industry. As the potential use of quantum technology increases, there is also an increased need for experts that can act as consultants for emerging applications. We will be organizing career events with selected companies. 

As the program is new, we do not yet have an alumni organization, but we hope that our graduates in the future will support the new generations of students in the program.
