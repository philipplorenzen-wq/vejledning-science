The MSc programme in Environmental and Natural Resource Economics consists of four elements:

  • Compulsory courses, 52.5 ECTS credits
  • Restricted elective courses
    • 22.5 ECTS credits (with thesis 30 ECTS credits)
    • 7.5 ECTS credits (with thesis 45 ECTS credits)
  • Elective courses, 15 ECTS credits
  • Thesis, 30 or 45 ECTS credits

The programme is two years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Environmental and Natural Resource Economics / Cand.scient.oecon. i miljø- og ressourceøkonomi.
