There are 3 ways your purchases can be made in practice:

Method 1 (always recommended): get the supplier to send an electronic invoice
The vast majority of companies in Denmark can send an electronic invoice, and this is by far the easiest way to get money out of the department. When ordering the granted, enter EAN 5798 000 422 391 and state "Shabnam Abbasi" as the recipient.
Method 2 (sometimes works): The Department helps you order the grant
In some cases, you can get help ordering the granted. This applies, for example, in cases where the Department has agreements, user accounts or access to internal UCPH ordering processes. If you are unsure and want to know about the possibility of having the Department order the granted items, you can always contact Shabnam Abbasi for more information.
Method 3 (not recommended): Out-of-pocket expenses and reimbursement
If you spend money yourself, you have the option to be reimbursed for your outlay.  However, this requires that you save all bills and receipts - and that you are registered in the RejsUd system. RejsUd is not a fun system to be in - especially not for a single invoice and it requires a lot of patience with the system. Reimbursement of expenses generally takes longer, so we always recommend avoiding individual expenses

If you are unsure about handling expenses and reimbursement, please speak to Man Yu Li for help.
