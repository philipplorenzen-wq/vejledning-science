The curriculum for the MSc programme in Chemistry is governed by the Study Board of Physics, Chemistry and Nanoscience at the Faculty of Science, University of Copenhagen.

Curriculum 2013 (revised 2026): Applies to students enrolled at the start of the 2013/14 academic year or later. The curricula consists of

  • Programme specific section (2013, revised 2025)

  • Programme specific section (2013, revised 2026)
