Your exam will be an oral exam based on a written report.

Agree on the specific details about, for example, form, content, length and focus for what you submit and/or present with your supervisor.
You can find your submission date in your project contract.

You can choose to do your oral exam online, if you and your supervisor agree on it.

If you do a report

If you do a report you need to know this:

  • It must be in english.
  • You must talk to your supervisor about the content of your report.
  • There are no fixed requirements regarding how your report is structured. Talk to your supervisor.
  • If your superviser is at a another faculty, you must ask him or her what the rules are there.
  • You must fill out and submit an AI declaration, regardless of whether you have used generative AI or not.
    How to fill out and submit the AI declaration
  • Submit your report via Digital Exam.

See what you need to do if you cannot meet the deadline for submission due to e.g. ilness


Avoid plagiarism

You must avoid plagiarism. You must be aware of this both if you use your own as well as others' academic work.
