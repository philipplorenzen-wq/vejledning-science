A stay abroad can, for example:

  • develop your academic standards
  • add an international dimension to you study programme
  • improve your language skills
  • give you a new perspective on your discipline
  • give you insight into other types of instruction and other ways of solving problems
  • strengthen you personally and socially
  • increase your cultural insight and understanding
  • improve your CV
  • lead to an international network
  • give you a unique and unforgettable experience with friends for life.
