Do I need to bring ID to the exam?

Placering: Exams › Exam types and rules › Oral exam

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Do-I-need-to-bring-ID-to-the-exam-SCI.aspx

You need to bring one of the following forms of ID to your oral exams:

  • UCPH student ID card
  • driver's licence
  • passport
  • the ID app AltID (requires a CPR number and MitID)
  • national ID card (issued by Borger.dk)
  • residence card issued on the basis of your recidence permit.

Without valid ID you will not be able to take the oral exam, and you will be registered for 'non-attendance' and use up 1 exam attempt.
