You may register after the deadline if you are granted an dispensation.
When applying for an dispensation, you must be able to document that you have not been able to register before the deadline due to exceptional circumstances.  

You can apply for dispensation on the grounds of e.g.:

  • illness (you or your immediate family) 
  • a death in your immediate family
  • other exceptional circumstances beyond your control

Circumstances to do with your student job, housing situation or finances are not regarded as exceptional circumstances.


How to apply for a dispensation

4 steps when you apply for a dispensation:

  1. Get documentation of your situation, e.g. a medical certificate from your doctor or an opinion from a psychologist. 
    See a guide to what your documentation should include
  2. Write a statement, which contains a description of the exceptional circumstances due to which you need a dispensation.
  3. Create a study plan for your entire course of study, which shows how you intend to complete your study programme. Find a study plan template in which the structure of your study programme has already been entered under Programme structure.       
  4. Fill out an application by using 'Apply for a dispensation' and enclose documentation, explanation, and study plan.

Reply to your application

As a general rule, you will receive an answer within 6 weeks of SCIENCE Study Administration having received all relevant information about your case. If you need to send supplementary material, you will be contacted via your KUmail.
