When will my study-abroad scholarship be approved?

There are three steps in the process of the study-abroad scholarship grant, before the final approval:

1. Send your complete application to udlandsstipendium@adm.ku.dk

SU at the UCPH will process your application as soon as possible and within four weeks. If any information is missing, you will be contacted via your KUmail.
Please note! If you start your studies abroad in the upcoming calendar year, we cannot process your application before the rates of the study-abroad scholarships have been determined in the Finance Act. So, studies abroad, beginning in the upcoming calendar year, can normally be processed no earlier than the beginning of January, within the year of your stay abroad.
2. Payment of grants

When SU at the UCPH has processed your application, the scholarship will be paid to your NemKonto/EasyAccount, but no earlier than three months prior to the start date of your stay abroad. You will be notified about the payment in your KUmail and in your e-Boks. Please note, that this is a provisional payment of the study-abroad scholarship.
3. Reporting from the stay abroad

Once your stay is complete, you must submit the following:

  • Receipt of the paid tuition fee. The receipt must be dated, stamped and signed by your foreign host institution.
  • Proof of completion of your stay, for example your transcript of records.

You must send the above documents to SU at UCPH. When the SU has received all documents, your study-abroad scholarship will be final, and you will receive a letter in your e-Boks.
Finally, you must notify SU at the UCPH, once you have received credits for your stay. You can apply for credit transfer at your study board.
When SU at the UCPH has registered your credit transfer, your case regarding the study-abroad scholarship is finalised and closed.
