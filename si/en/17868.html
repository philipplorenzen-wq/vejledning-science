You can use your project in practice to study an organisation or company that focuses on communicating knowledge about your subject area. This could, for example, be

  • at an upper secondary school, where you develop a course, a theme day, etc.
  • with an NGO etc., where you can work with the communication of an academic problem/issue.


Supervisor for a project focusing on knowledge communication

You may decide to find a supervisor from one of the two departments at SCIENCE that have knowledge communication/dissemination as their main focus area:

  • Department of Science Education
  • Natural History Museum Of Denmark
