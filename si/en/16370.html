How will my project be assessed?

Your project will be assessed and graded numerically (7-point grading scale). You are given a single overall grade for your project. 


Your project will be assessed by

  • an examiner (your supervisor)
  • one or more internal co-examiner from SCIENCE.

Registering the exam result

When the Individual Study Project has been handed in, assessed, perhaps defended and graded, your supervisor must register your grade using the standardized Individual Study Report Form. All the necessary information is listed in the form.
Your supervisor must send the form to eksamen-frbplus@adm.ku.dk for the grade to be registered.
