The first year of the programme comprises courses and maybe one or more small research projects. The course Structural tools in nanoscience is mandatory and introduces a range of techniques for obtaining structural information of nanosystems. You must take several of the restricted elective courses offered. These are core nanoscience courses which will ensure you a stronger academic level. The rest of the year will be elective courses.

The second year is for your thesis work. Begin yours search for a supervisor and a research group early. Talk to multiple professors and ask which projects are available in their research group.

You are always welcome to come by my office to discuss your choice of courses and projects.
