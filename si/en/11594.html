During your Master's thesis, you can face challenges as a result of

  • illness
  • issues to do with your cooperation
  • problems with the company you are cooperating with. 

You cannot, as a rule, withdraw from submitting your thesis. 

But you can apply for a dispensation to postpone the submission if extraordinary circumstances prevent you from submitting or completing your planned thesis.
