Where can I get help?

Your doctor can help you with issues like

  • how much studying you can do, given the state of your health
  • how much time you need to spend away from your studies
  • a referral to a psychologist (where necessary and possible)
  • a medical certificate – which you will need if you are applying for a dispensation.


The study and career guidance counsellors from UCPH Study Guidance can help you to

  • determine whether you are meeting the study activity requirements and other rules
  • get an overview of the ways in which your studies can be reorganised to take account of your situation
  • give input which you may need when applying for a dispensation.

Contact UCPH Study Guidance


The Student Counselling Service can assist you with

  • financial challenges, issues and possible solutions in your situation
  • mental issues affecting your life, e.g. depression, stress, anxiety or a general lack of motivation and energy for your studies.

Contact Student Counselling Service
