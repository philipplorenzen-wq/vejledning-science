How are group exams and assessments conducted?

How your group exam and assessment is conducted depends on whether you have worked on your report/paper as a group or individually.

If your exam only consists of a written report, you will be graded individually based on your contributions and the report as a whole.


Oral exams on joint reports/papers

In exams on a joint report/paper, you are always examined on the basis of the report as a whole.

Your grade is individual, but will be based both on the joint report and your individual performance in the exam.


Oral exams based on individualised reports/papers

If you are taking an oral exam based on a report in which your name is only on selected sections, you will still be examined on the whole report.
The assessment of the report/paper itself will be based on the sections with your name on.


The group members will be given individual grades based on both:

  • your individual contributions to the report/paper
  • your performances at the oral exam (if there is one).
