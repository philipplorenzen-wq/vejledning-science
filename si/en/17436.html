If you are considering going abroad, both specialisations have a semester where there are only elective and restricted elective courses, which is suited for an exchange.

In Computational Biology block 1 + 2 in the second year is the perfect time.

In Computer Science block 3 + 4 in the first year is the perfect time.

You can also consider doing your master thesis abroad. This will normally require that you find a supervisor at the Department of Biology or Department of Computer Science that have contacts abroad or that you find a project yourself.
