  • Mute your microphone when you do not need to contribute to the class
  • You can turn off your video, if your internet connection is slow, turning it off can improve the quality of the image + sound you receive
  • If you download the Zoom or Teams app, you get more options. With the app you can for example blur your background so others can not see it
  • If you are both a student and have a student job at UCPH, you will have to log in to Teams with an emailadress without "alumni": UCPH-username@ku.dk e.g. ABC123@ku.dk
