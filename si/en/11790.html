At UCPH you can try your hand at innovation and entrepreneurship in many ways.

If you have an idea or a problem that you have found a solution to and want to turn into reality, UCPH offers advice, networking, and office space in UCPH Lighthouse.
Here you can be inspired by other students who are working to turn their ideas into reality. You can also help find solutions to societal challenges or get assistance and networks to set up your own business.
