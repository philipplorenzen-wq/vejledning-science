Before the Master's thesis it is possible to conduct a Project outside the course scope (a small research project), a Project in Practice in collaboration with a public or private company or a Thesis preparation project. You are responsible for finding a project and a supervisor, but eventually I may help you.

Notice that you when you have a project in collaboration with a company will acquire generic competences and learn how your competences can be used.

Your Master's thesis project is an important part of your education. It is your own responsibility to find a project and a supervisor. Feel free to contact a relevant faculty member to inquire about supervision. This also applies to your final Master's thesis. All of these can be written in collaboration with an external partner, remember that you always need an internal supervisor.
