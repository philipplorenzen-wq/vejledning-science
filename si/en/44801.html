When you apply for exemption, you can either apply for:

  • Exemption to use special conditions in class for a limited period of time. This may be relevant if you are suffering from a temporary functional impairment.

Or

  • General exemption that applies to all classes on the programme where special conditions are required. This may be relevant if you are suffering from a permanent functional impairment.

Regardless of the type of application, the study board will base its decision on an individual assessment of the particulars of the case, including the medical report.

About general exemption

If you are granted general exemption, it will be valid for the entire programme.

The general exemption does not apply, if you

  • Take courses on other study programmes (e.g. optional courses).
  • Start a master's degree programme at UCPH.
  • Enrol in a different study programme at UCPH.

In these cases, you must submit a new application for exemption.
