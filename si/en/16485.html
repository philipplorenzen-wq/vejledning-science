The MSc programme in Biotechnology with a minor subject in Chemistry consist of the following:

  1. Compulsory courses (major subject), 7.5 ECTS credits
  2. Compulsory courses (minor subject), 60 ECTS credits
  3. Restricted elective courses, 22.5 ECTS credits
  4. Thesis, 30 ECTS credits
