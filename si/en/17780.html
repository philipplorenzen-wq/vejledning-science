Can I receive an extra Erasmus grant?

University of Copenhagen offers a Fewer Opportunities top-up grant for certain students going on Erasmus exchange or Erasmus Traineeship.

What it the Fewer Opportunities top-up grant?
The Fewer Opportunities top-up grant is an additional 250 Euro/month (8,33 Euro/day) that you can receive on top of your ordinary Erasmus grant.

Who can receive the Fewer Opportunities top-up grant?

  • Students with disabilities, chronic illnesses or conditions requiring treatment. Both physical and mental conditions are included

Documentation: Medicial certificate (lægeerklæring), SPS-documentation (recieved in your e-boks) or similar

  • Students with a civic duty or personnel of the reserve (borgerligt ombud eller indkaldte til tjeneste i Reserven), who needs to return to Denmark to take care of their duties during their Erasmus Exchange
Documentation: Summons (indkaldelse) with the dates
  • Care takers or resource persons (parent, adoptive parent, care taker for a diseased family member, mentor or support person). You do not need to bring your child with you on exchange.
Documentation: paternity/maternity certificate, adoption certificate or similar

You can receive the Fewer Opportunties top-up, if you also receive SPS-support or other support during your Erasmus exchange/traineeship.


How do I apply?

Please send documents to prove that you are eligible to receive this top-up grant to erasmus-topup@adm.ku.dk

Due to data protection please send all documents to this email address.

Applications for "fewer opportunities" for the autumn semester are accepted from May, and for the spring semester from October.

Application deadline for Erasmus exchange

You must send the documents no later than:

  • 15 June if your exchange begins in the autumn
  • 15 November if your exchange begins in the spring

Application deadline for Erasmus Traineeship

You must send the documents no later than 1 month before you start your Erasmus

Traineeship or 10 January / 10 August, if your traineeship starts 1 February / 1 September

What must I do to receive the the Fewer Opportunities top-up grant?

  • You must send documents before the deadline mentioned above
  • You must sign and upload a Solemn Declaration in Mobility-Online as part of the evaluation of your Erasmus grant when you return from your Erasmus study exchange.

Inclusion support

Besides the Fewer Opportunities top-up you can apply for an Inclusion support grant.

This grant is for students with a physical, mental or health related condition who need extra

financial support to allow their participation in Erasmus mobility.

You can receive the Inclusion support grant and the Fewer Opportunities top-up.

The Inclusion support grant should cover real costs not covered by other funding opportunties (e.g. SPS or similar) during the Erasmus exchange/traineeship.

 Please write an email to erasmus-topup@adm.ku.dk with a short description of your situtaion and what expenses you have, so we can guide you on what to do.

Please contact us as soon as possible so you can prepare your application before the deadline.

Application deadline for Erasmus exchange

Applications for "fewer opportunities" for the autumn semester are accepted from May, and for the spring semester from October.

You must send the application including documents no later than:

  • 15 June if your exchange begins in the autumn
  • 15 November if your exchange begins in the spring
    Please note: you cannot apply for inclusion support if the expenses are already covered by other sourses, e.g. SPS or similar. Double funding within Erasmus is not allowed.

Questions?
