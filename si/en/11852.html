Up to four students can do a project together with a joint problem formulation and a joint supervisor.

Doing a thesis preparation project as a group is primarily only an advantage if you also want to continue working together on the thesis.


A group project can, among other things

  • strengthen your cooperative skills
  • provide you with close academic sparring and new perspectives on your subject
  • prepare you for a career with project work.
