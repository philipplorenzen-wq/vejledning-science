How do I pass an elective course after the last exam option?

When you are notified in your KUmail about the last exam option, you will also be notified of whether your discontinued course will

  • be replaced by an identical course
  • not be replaced by anything.


If your course is replaced by an identical course, you must pass the identical course. This means that you must either register for the identical course or simply register directly for the exam – if the exam form is the same as for the discontinued course.
Read more about when you may register directly for the exam 


If the discontinued course is not replaced by an identical course

You must select and register for a new elective course to replace the discontinued course when

  1. no identical course is being offered
  2. the registration period for the last exam option in the discontinued course has expired.


If you were admitted before the 2015/2016 academic year, the choice is always yours

If you registered for the discontinued course before the 2015/16 academic year, and you had not started your Bachelor's programme by 1 September 2014, you may choose a new elective course – regardless of whether an identical course is offered or not.
