Here is an overview of group rooms on the Frederiksberg Campus.


How to bookAll students on FRB campus can log in with their UCPH username and UCPH password to book a group room.
Once you’ve reserved a room, you’ll receive a booking confirmation by e-mail.
The e-mail includes a link to your booking, in case you need to change or cancel it.
Book a group room

Rules for group rooms
  • You can make up to 5 bookings per week
  • Each booking can be a maximum of 4 hours
  • You can book up to 30 days in advance
  • Group rooms can be booked between 07:00 and 24:00
  • If a group room hasn’t been taken into use within 15 minutes of the booking start time, the booking is cancelled, and the room becomes available for everyone
