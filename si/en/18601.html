The exam takes place at the South Campus. For a written exam you will get your exam room number and exam date on Digital exam.

You can take the metro or you bicycle to the South Campus area. If you arrive by car, you can use the public parkingspaces in the area, as it is not possible to park on South Campus.

How to read your room number

  • Check your bulding number (5A.0.01 = building number 5A)
  • Check your floor number (5A.0.01 = floor 0 for ground floor)
  • Check your room number (5A.0.01 = room 01)

When you turn up for your exam:

  • Place bag, jacket, and other items in the wardrobe. If there is no wardrobe by the exam room, the supervisors will tell you where to place your things.
  • Turn off your mobile phone and put it in the belt bag on the chair.
  • Find your exam place and check in.
  • You may not, under any circumstances, change the setup of the exam computer. It is forbidden to unplug electrical cords, keyboards, or other equipment.

Use the belt bag

You can use the belt bags to keep small valuables such as your purse, phone, watch, etc. if you do not want to use the open, video-monitored cloakroom.

  • The belt bags must not hold larger items such as tablets or PCs; the bag must be zipped during the exam. Large items must still be placed in the video-monitored cloakroom.
  • The belt bags should be packed before the exam starts, closed/zipped and may not be accessed again until the exam is over.
  • If you need your bag during the exam, you must alert one of the invigilators and open your bag under their surveillance.

Read the exam procedure in the quick start guide

The quick start guide briefly describes the exam procedure. You can also find the quick start guide on your exam PC. It is a good idea to read the guide before the exam starts.

If you experience problems during the exam

If technical problems cause you to lose time at your exam, the IT supporters will together with the exam supervisors assess whether you should be given extra time to complete your assignment and how much.

If you experience a general problem, for example an error in the exam assignment, the supervisors will together with the faculty and the examiner decide whether you should be given extra time. You will be notified if general problems arise.

Remember to submit your assignment

When you have completed your exam assignment, remember to submit it for assessment. You can also submit a blank exam assignment. If you forget to submit your assignment, the latest version of your 'Examination Document' will be submitted automatically.

When you have submitted your assignment, you can pack your things and leave the exam room.

If you violate the rules 

You may be excluded from the exam and, in the worst case, from the University if you do not follow the University's exam rules, including:
  • UCPH's rules of conduct
  • Instructions from the exam supervisors 
  • The instructions in the course description
If you are excluded from the exam, it counts as an exam attempt.
