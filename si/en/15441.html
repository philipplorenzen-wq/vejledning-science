How to keep your UCPH-account safe

Placering: IT and facilities on campus › IT and support › IT-security

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

If you have been using a shared study computer on campus, you must always log out of your UCPH account and other personal services afterwards. 

If you forget to log out, your account is at risk of getting compromised. This poses a risk to yourself and to the entire university, as your account can be misused to e.g. send out phishing emails

You should also never leave your personal devices, such as computer, cellphone and tablet, unattended when traveling on campus or other public places.

If you find a USB stick left behind at the university, hand it in at your local IT Support Center at UCPH. Never put the USB stick in your computer it can contain malware and virus.

You can see a list of all the IT Support centers at the IT Support page.
