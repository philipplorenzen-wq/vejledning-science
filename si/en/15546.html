Once you know the conditions of your course, you are better prepared to plan your block and get ready for the exam.


The course description gives you important information about e.g.:

  • The content and objectives of the course
  • Formal requirements and recommended academic qualifications for following the course
  • Exams
  • Type of instruction
  • Workload
  • Block and timetable details and ECTS


Framework for course and exam

The course description clearly explains the framework for your course and exam. For example, you may need to know the content of the course description in connection with an application for a dispensation.
