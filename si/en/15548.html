How to apply for credit transfer for courses you'ver taken as a part of your current programme:

  1. Get certified documentation of your exam results from the other university or faculty.
  2. Fill in the application and upload your exam results via 'Apply for credit transfer'. 

Apply for credit transfer


Deadlines for applying for credit transfer

In order to ensure compliance with your UCPH and SU study activity requirements, you must submit your application for credit transfer within a certain deadline.

The deadline for submitting your application for credit transfer is:

  • For courses taken in blocks 1 + 2: by 1 March.
  • For courses taken in blocks 3 + 4 + 5: by 1 September.


Reply to your application

As a general rule, you will receive an answer within 6 weeks from when the Study Board has received all relevant information about your application. If you need to send supplementary material, you will be contacted via your KUmail.
The Study Board will process your application as soon as possible.
