You must make an agreement if you intend to collaborate with a company or organisation during your project.

Use the UCPH template (Word document) to ensure a transparent agreement that safeguards both your needs and those of the company.

You must upload the agreement to your project contract, so it needs to be ready before you complete and submit the contract.

Fill out the agreement with the company

Important if you are asked to sign an agreement drawn up by the company

Only sign a confidentiality agreement from the company if strictly necessary. Pay close attention to how the contract affects your right to write and talk about your results in your report and in the period after you complete your project.

Before you sign a confidentiality agreement from a company:

  1. Consider discussing the agreement with your supervisor.
  2. Try to reduce the confidentiality period to a minimum.
