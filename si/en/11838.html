The MSc programme in Nanoscience consists of four elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 7.5 ECTS credits
  2. Restricted elective courses
    • 22.5 ECTS credits (with thesis 60 ECTS credits)
    • 37.5 ECTS credits (with thesis 45 ECTS credits)
  3. Elective subjects, 30 ECTS credits
  4. Thesis, 45 or 60 ECTS credits

The programme is two years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Nanoscience / Cand.scient. i nanoscience
