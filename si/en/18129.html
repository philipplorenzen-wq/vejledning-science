You can apply for exchange during your leave of absence.

You will receive information on your KUmail, even if you are on leave.

Please note that if you do not start your MA directly after finishing you BA, you will no longer hold your enrollment at the KU and will not be eligible for leave of absence.

You can read more on your study information page on KUnet under "Can I apply for exchange if I am on leave of absence?"
