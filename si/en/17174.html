An academic internship is for students who want to test their competences in practice in a company or an organisation for a limited period of time.

There may be several reasons why you would like to do an academic internship:

  • Perhaps there is a specific type of company you would like to join?
  • Or a specific field you could imagine working in after you have graduated?

Regardless of your motivation for doing an academic internship, you can use the academic internship to test your competences and to get an idea of what to expect after graduation.
