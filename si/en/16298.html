How to find your course description:

  1. Go to kurser.ku.dk.
  2. Enter the name of your course or course number (if you know this) in the 'Search words' field.
  3. Press 'Search'. 


You can narrow down your search by selecting:

  • level (educational level)
  • faculty
  • department
  • academic year
  • block, etc.
