Most students experience some exam nerves. It can be frustrating and uncomfortable when you do not feel quite in control of your body and your thoughts, but a degree of nervousness actually helps you focus under pressure.  

When nervousness becomes anxiety

For some, nervousness – and the fear of its physical and mental symptoms – can be hard to cope with.

Exam anxiety is when the discomfort leading up to an exam becomes so intense that you find it very difficult to focus on your academic work. In severe cases, it can affect your general well-being – even long before the exam.
