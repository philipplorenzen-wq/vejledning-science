If you are still open to most possibilities and do not know a lot about your options, you can explore and learn about them in the following ways:

  • Think about when you are the most interested and motivated on your study programme. What subject areas and working methods do you like? 
  • Consider what is important to you when it comes to thriving in your student and working life. 
  • Participate in various career events to get inspired.
    See all upcoming study events on KUnet
  • Be curious about other people's working lives. For example, ask lecturers, guest lecturers and students further along than you on the study programme.
