What is my supervisor’s role?

Your primary supervisor is

  • your academic support and sounding board, from the ideas development phase and start-up to the actual project work
  • the person you go to when having problems with equipment, results, motivation, etc.
  • the examiner on your thesis report and for your oral defence.


Supervisor's responsibility for the Master's thesis

Your primary supervisor

  • has the overall responsibility for ensuring that your project scope and the workload involved match the project framework
  • must do at least 50% of the supervising you recieve during your Master's thesis process.


Contact when writing thesis with a company

If you are writing your Master's thesis in cooperation with a company, then you must have

  • a primary supervisor from the university
  • a contact at the company where you are doing your thesis.
