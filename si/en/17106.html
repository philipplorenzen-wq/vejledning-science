Gælder for målgrupperne: Integrated Food Studies - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Projects-SCI-IFS.aspx

The program concludes with a Master's thesis of 30 or 45 ECTS. There is no standard recipe on how you develop your thesis project, but during spring before your second year, we will organize meetings where we discuss opportunities and develop ideas together.

Please be aware that formulating the scope and research questions guiding your master thesis always will be an iterative process, and it will take time. During this process, you will test options; change your mind and get new inspiration. So the earlier you start thinking about the direction you would like your master's thesis to take, the easier this first phase of the process will be. Here, my advice is that you actively search possible problems to address in your thesis – talk to older students, your teachers or professionals from the food sector you meet during your studies or elsewhere.
