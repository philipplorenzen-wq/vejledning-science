UCPH defines offensive behaviour in line with the Danish Working Environment Authority's definition. Harassment includes:  

  • Bullying
    For example, hurtful remarks or being yelled at, ridiculed or slandered. Being disparaged unfairly, e.g. about work performance, competences etc. 
  • Sexual harassment
    For example unwanted touching, unwanted requests for sexual contact, obscene jokes and comments. 
  • Denigration
    Being treated as having less value due to race, skin colour, religion or faith, political views, sexual orientation, gender, age, disability or national, social or ethnic background.
