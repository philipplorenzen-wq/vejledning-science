Before you complain about an assessment or other academic issues, it is a good idea to check if you can get any feedback.

  1. If your examiner or course coordinator offers a lecture in which the exam paper is reviewed, we recommend that you take advantage of that offer before submitting a complaint.
  2. You can always check with the examiner if there is a possibility that you can get feedback on your exam answer. However, the examiner is not obliged to give you feedback.

In addition, it is a good idea to read up on what your grade means and what goals have been set for the current course or exam. You can find the academic goals in the course description.

How to complain about an exam at SUND

3 steps when you want to complain:

  1. Make written complaint

    When writing your complaint, it is important to describe

    • what you are complaining about
    • what you want to achieve.

    Describe objectively and concretely

    • why you disagree with the assessment,
    • the problems you experienced during the exam, or
    • how the design of the assignment or the like is problematic depending on what you are complaining about.


    If the complaint concerns the examination basis or assessment, you must substantiate your complaint with academically relevant references by comparing your assignment with, e.g.:

    • The course or exam description; reference to the criteria for assessment.
    • The course's recommended literature.
    • Grading Scale and the description of the individual grades in the Grading Scale Order.
    • Previous exam assignments/correction instructions (prepared only for specific exams). Please note that there may be changes in the learning outcome since the previous exam.


    When writing your complaint, it is advisable to remember that it is a good idea to describe a reason for your complaint that the complaint recipient can relate to in their statement.

  2. Obtain relevant documentation (if possible)
  3. Fill in an online form and send a complaint and possibly documentation through 'Send your exam complaint'.
