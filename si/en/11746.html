As a student, it is important that you think about maintaining good scientific practice. This applies when you:

  • write an assignment during a course or at an exam
  • give presentations
  • take an oral exam.  

Good scientific practice ensures a high academic level and credibility throughout your studies, as it strengthens your academic reflection and awareness of methodology and ethics.
