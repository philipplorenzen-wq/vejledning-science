If you have been deferred in your programme due to illness, you can apply for additional grant portions in accordance with SU regulations.

Moreover, you can apply for additional grant portions in the event of:

  • Council work
  • Other special circumstances

You must provide documentation of your illness. Read more about what documentation you need, below.

In accordance with the SU regulations, a deferral is measured in retrospect. This means, that you can only apply once a semester has ended.

If you are delayed with your final assignment on your programme (FX the thesis or dissertation), you can apply once you are able to document that the deadline for the assignment has been postponed.
