Your programme can lead to various career paths. During your studies, you can explore  

  • the different opportunities you have with your competences
  • what is important to you in a job
  • what motivates you professionally. 

You can benefit from exploring these aspects continuously in both your study and work life. The job market changes over time, and so do your desires and priorities, typically.

Where your exploration begins depends on where you currently stand in your career considerations.
