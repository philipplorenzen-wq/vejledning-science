The consequences of withdrawing from an exam depend on what stage of your study programme you are at and how many ECTS credits you have passed.


Pay attention to study activity requirements

Before withdrawing from an exam, you must make sure you can still pass the ECTS credits you need to meet the applicable study activity requirements, including:

  • UCPH Study activity requirements
  • SU study acticvity requirements

See when you may run into problems with UCPH's study activity requirements

Withdrawing may affect upcoming courses

Before withdrawing from an exam, you must check whether it will affect the courses you will be taking in upcoming blocks, since some courses require you to have passed another course first.  


See the course description for your upcoming courses under 'Formal requirements'.
