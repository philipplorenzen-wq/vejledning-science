The MSc programme in Integrated Food Studies consists of four elements:

  • Compulsory courses, 52.5 ECTS credits
  • Restricted elective courses, 7.5 ECTS
  • Elective courses
    • 30 ECTS credits (with thesis 30 ECTS credits)
    • 15 ECTS credits (with thesis 45 ECTS credits)
  • Thesis, 30 or 45 ECTS credits

The programme is two years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title:

  • Master of Science in Technology (Integrated Food Studies) / Cand.tech. i integrerede fødevarestudier.
