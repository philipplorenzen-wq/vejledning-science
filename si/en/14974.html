  1. Book an appointment
    Start by making an appointment with UCPH Study Guidance. We will help you get started with support.
    See how to book an appointment with UCPH Study Guidance here. 
    Remember to prepare for the interview by obtaining documentation for your mental functional impairment from your doctor. 
  2. Message in your e-Boks after 3-6 weeks
    UCPH applies for the support and/or aids that you have discussed during the interview.
    3-6 weeks later, you will be notified via e-Boks when your application has been processed.
    UCPH sends the application to the Student Counselling Service.
  3. The Student Counselling Service contacts you
    The Student Counselling Service will contact you within 10 days to arrange for an interview. This is known as a clarification interview.
  4. Clarification process at the Student Counselling Service
    You talk to a counsellor in the Student Counselling Service. Together, you will find the right support.
  5. UCPH applies for the recommended support for you
    The Student Counselling Service makes a report with recommendations for your support.
    UCPH applies for the recommended support.
  6. Notification in your e-Boks
    3-6 weeks later, you will be notified in your e-Boks when your application has been processed.
  7. Start-up meeting with student mentor
    The Student Counselling Service invites you to a start-up meeting with your student mentor and, if relevant, an academic support teacher.
