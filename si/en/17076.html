Our graduates have very high employment. Almost half continue to a Ph.D. position, often with a supervisor from the Nanoscience Center. Most graduates, some with a Ph.D. degree, go into the private sector. They get exciting jobs in biotech, IT, and other high-tech fields. Some even become business consultants. The cross-disciplinarity of nanoscience is in high demand.

The Nanoscience Center organizes an annual matchmaking event. Here invited companies present themselves and you will get a chance to have one-on-one discussions with them.
