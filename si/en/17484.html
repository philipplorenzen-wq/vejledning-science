Do I receive a new card when I change my name?

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

If you have changed your name, you must make sure that UCPH has registered the change. Normally, the University will automatically receive information through the civil registration office, but it's a good idea to check whether your name is correct. You can do this in Self Service under My data > Personal data

If your name is registered correctly, you need only to block your student ID card and a new card will be put into production. The name on the new card will be the name registered in Self Service.

You can block your card in Self Service under ID-card > Block ID cards
