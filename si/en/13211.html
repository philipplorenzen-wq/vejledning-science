If you want to apply for the scheme, you must:

  1.  Obtain documentation from Team Denmark, your association or club, head coach or similar that states your sporting level and weekly hours spent on training, getting changed, competitions etc.
  2. Complete the application form, upload the documentation and, if relevant, a personal statement/additional information about you. If you have a long-term training plan leading up to big national or international competitions, you can upload that as well.

When you have submitted your application including documentation, please check that you have received a receipt by email and a copy of your application in your KUmail.
