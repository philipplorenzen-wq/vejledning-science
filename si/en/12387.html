  1. Step 1: Apply on MinSU

    You must first apply for additional grant portions on MinSU.

    How to do it:
    • Go to: MinSU → Apply for SU → Additional clips
    • Fill out the application and state how many clips you are applying for
    You must apply for the period in which you were delayed:
    • Spring semester: 7 clips (February–August)
    • Autumn semester: 5 clips (September–January)
    In the application you must:
    • State your period of illness with start and end dates
    • Make sure the period corresponds, as far as possible, with your medical certificate
    • Finally, you must indicate when you expect to complete your studies.

    Once you have submitted your application, you will receive a receipt – remember to save it.

    Log in to MinSU

    If you are still ill:

    If you are still ill when you apply for grant portions for a semester that has just ended, you may enter the last month of the semester as the end date (the end date must include the re-examination period). In your personal statement, you can explain that you are still ill.

    Read more about additional grant portions (in Danish)

  2. Step 2: Upload the documents on KUnet

    Once you have applied for additional grant portions on MinSU and saved your receipt, you must upload your application on KUnet.

    Your application must include:

    1. Receipt from MinSU

    • The receipt page confirming your application for additional clips
    • You must apply for the period in which you were delayed

    2.  Personal statement

    Describe in your own words how your illness or special circumstances have affected your academic progress

    • Be specific and include:
      • Which courses you have not passed
      • Course names and ECTS credits
      • The semesters involved
      • When you expect to complete your studies

    3. Documentation

    • For example, a medical certificate or other relevant documentation
    • It is a good idea to show your doctor what information the certificate must include to meet the requirements
