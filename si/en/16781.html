The study programme (1st year UCPH, 2nd year at abroad) is set at 120 ECTS credits and consists of the following:

  • Compulsory courses, 22.5 ECTS credits
  • Restricted elective courses, 30 ECTS credits.
  • Elective subjects, 7.5 ECTS credits.
  • Subject elements (including thesis) at partner university, 60 ECTS credits (2nd year)
    Remember to transfer credits as soon as you complete your 2nd year
