What are the possible disadvantages of applying for credit transfers?

Whether or not it is a good idea for you to apply for credit transfers depends on what is important for you in relation to your study programme. Would you like to finish your degree quickly, or is it more important for you to take as many courses as possible from your current study programme?
Please note the following about credit transfers:

  • If your completion time can be reduced by 6 months or more as a result of credit transfers, the SU Office will reduce your monthly grant portions.
    Read more about the rules on your monthly grant portions
  • Your credit transfers replace other courses on your study programme. You should therefore make sure that any possible credit transfers do not prevent you from taking courses in which you are particularly interested.
  • If you apply for credit transfer from previous studies, you cannot have it cancelled later on.
