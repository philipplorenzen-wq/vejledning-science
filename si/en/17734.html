The specialisation is set at 120 ECTS credits and consists of the following:

  • Compulsory courses, 7.5 ECTS credits
  • Restricted elective courses:
    • 37.5 ECTS credits (with thesis 60 ECTS credits)
    • 52.5 ECTS credits (with thesis 45 ECTS credits)
  • Elective subjects, 15 ECTS credits
  • Thesis, 45 or 60 ECTS credits

Elective subjects

You have 15 ECTS credits to strengthen your own profile at the programme.
Read more at 'How can I use my elective ECTS credits?'

Restricted elective courses

If you consider applying for the double degree in Ecology, you must choose restricted elective courses from a specific list.
Read more at 'Specialisation: Ecology (double degree at a 4EU+ partner university)'
Depending on the size of your thesis you must choose
  • 37.5 ECTS restricted elective courses (thesis 60 ECTS credits)
  • 52.5 ECTS restricted elective courses (thesis 45 ECTS credits)
