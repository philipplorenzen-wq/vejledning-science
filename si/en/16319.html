When do I need to be in the exam room?

You need to be in the exam room at least 30 minutes before your exam starts – and preferably earlier.

Take into account possible delays and queuing to get into the exam room when planning your transport.


You will not be allowed into the exam room after the exam has begun

If you arrive after the exam has started, you will not be allowed in by the invigilators. You will be registered for 'non-attendance' and use up 1 exam attempt.

Avoid being late by familiarising yourself with the time and place of the exam beforehand.
