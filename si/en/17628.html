If you are considering getting a student job, you should think about how much time you can and realistically want to spend on it. In the end, you must decide and manage what you use your time for.


3 important points to include in your considerations

  • Your study programme at SCIENCE is a full-time study programme. This means that you must expect to use a full working week on your studies.
  • Your study and career guidance counsellors recommend that you work a maximum of 15 hours in addition to your studies, so that you have time to focus on your study programme.
  • It is important for your well-being that you allocate time to relax, see your family and friends and do the things that are important to you.
