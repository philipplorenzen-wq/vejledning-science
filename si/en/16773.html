As an MCs student ambassor your will

  • plan and carry out the Welcome Day for the new MSc students (Thursday in week 35) 
  • plan and carry out a workshop/meeting for the new students in September with a focus on their study start
  • be available for questions from the new students
  • plan one or more social activities taking place in Fall 2026 and carry out the planned activities – this will be planned together with an activity group contained of new MSc students
  • plan and carry out the MSc Dinner in Spring 2027
  • inform the new MSc students about important academic and social environments and events, including but not limited to Study Events, student clubs and associations, DIKU Galla, DIKU Revy, Christmas celebrations, etc.
