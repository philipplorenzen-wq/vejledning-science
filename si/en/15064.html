How do I report a faulty KUprinter?

Placering: IT and facilities on campus › IT and support › Printing and copying

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-report-a-faulty-KUprinter-IT.aspx

If you think there is an error on a printer, please contact UPCH IT on Tel. +45 35 32 32 32.

When you report a printer, please indicate which room the printer is in and the serial number of the printer, e.g. "MFP001".

If you think the error is on your computer or the server, or if you want help to use Webprint, please also contact UCPH IT on Tel. +45 35 32 32 32 or by visiting your local IT Support Center.
