How do I apply for a study-abroad scholarship for a credit-awarding stay?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-apply-for-a-study-abroad-scholarship-for-a-credit-awarding-stay-SU-KU.aspx

Fill in the application form for the study-abroad scholarship, print and sign it, scan it and send it together with all the appendices to udlandsstipendium@adm.ku.dk.
Application for study-abroad scholarship.pdf
Your application must include these appendices:

• Pre-approval of merit for your study abroad period.

• Documentation of your admission to the place of education (admission letter).

• Tuition fee documentation (invoice).
