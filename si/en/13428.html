When can I get additional SU loans?

Placering: SU and grants › Special support › Becoming a parent during studies

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: gxd · Københavns Universitet - Kandidat · Tandplejeruddannelsen - P-Bachelor

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/When-can-I-get-additional-SU-loans-SU-KU.aspx

If you are the provider of a child you can receive supplementary SU loans.
Please note! You cannot receive childbirth portions and supplementary SU loans/provider supplement at the same time.
SU at UCPH processes all applications, usually within 3-4 weeks.
You can only see your online application via minSU when it has been processed by SU at UCPH.
