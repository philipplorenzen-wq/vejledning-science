For many students, feeling part of a community centred around their studies is a key to: 

  • feeling happy and thriving as students 
  • finding joy, meaning and motivation – even at times when they may be growing weary of studying. 

Seeking out new communities can be hard – especially if you struggle with loneliness, or you don't feel particularly extroverted. 

Luckily there are many different ways of engaging socially – fitting for different types of people and situations. The most important thing is that you find the one that is right for you.
