How do I apply for a grant for my academic internship/traineeship?

You must apply for an Erasmus+ Traineeship grant 1 month before the start of your academic internship. This means that if your academic internship starts on the15th of October, you must apply before the 15th of September.

You cannot start on your academic internship and then apply for the Erasmus grant with another start date, if you cannot meet the application deadline. You must apply for the Erasmus+ grant with the official start date of your academic internship.*

We recommend that you start the application process for the Erasmus Traineeship grant as soon as you get your academic internship approved.


How long does it take to prepare the documents for the application?
It will take app. 2 months to prepare the two documents (Learning Agreement for Traineeship + pre-approval/academic approval), which you must upload to your application for the Erasmus Traineeship grant.

Please note that the response time for a pre-approval/academic approval can take up to 6 weeks and an additional 2 weeks to get a signature on your Learning Agreement for Traineeship from UCPH. Please remember, at your host institution must also sign the Learning Agreement for Traineeship.
UCPH will only sign the Learning Agreement for Traineeship, if you have a pre-approval/academic approval for the academic internship. 


Holidays at UCPH

Please note that the end of December + beginning of January and July a holiday period at UCPH. This means that there is fewer staff on duty during these periods. You cannot expect to get pre-approval/academic approval and signature on your Learning Agreement for Traineeship during these periods.

We recommend that you make sure to get pre-approval/academic approval and signature on your Learning Agreement for Traineeship before December/July. 

Extra ordinary application deadline for Academic Internship/Traineeship starting September 1st:
If your Academic Internship/Traineeship starts September 1st, you must apply on August 10th at the latest. Please note: we still recommend, that you apply as soon as possible 


Extra ordinary application deadline for Academic Internship/Traineeship starting February 1st:
If your Academic Internship/Traineeship starts February 1st, you must apply on January 10th. Please note: we still recommend, that you apply as soon as possible 

 
How to apply

Applications for traineeships starting in the autumn semester open at the end of June.
If you are going on traineeship during the autumn semester, you can fill out the application form from the end of June.

However, you can prepare your application earlier: we recommend that you prepare all the necessary documents (Learning Agreement, pre-approval/academic approval) before completing the application form.

When you have found a host for your academic internship and it has been approved by UCPH you can apply for the Erasmus Trainesship grant.

You must apply by:

  • Fill out the online application form in Mobility Tool (Part 1 + part 2)
  • Upload your Learning Agreement with all 3 signatures in Mobility Online
  • Upload your pre-approval/academic approval. The ECTS points you earn for the academic internship must be stated clearly in the document

Your application for the Erasmus Traineeship is considered complete when you have completed all of the above.

Can I send the Learning Agreement and the pre-approval/academic approval later, if I do not have the documents before the application deadline?

You cannot send the Learning Agreement and the pre-approval/academic approval after the application deadline.

Your application is assessed based on the Learning Agreement and the pre-approval/academic approval.

Other documents: Grant Agreement

When your application has been approved for an Erasmus Traineeship grant, you will get a Grant Agreement, that you must sign and upload in Mobility-Online.

Who at UCPH should sign my Learning Agreement for Traineeship?

You must indicate Shabnam Mushtaq as the signatory for the Learning Agreement at the University of Copenhagen (UCPH).
To obtain the signature, you should send your Learning Agreement to Outgoingstudents@adm.ku.dk along with your pre-approval/academic approval of the traineeship.

Please note that your Learning Agreement can only be signed after you have received your pre-approval/academic approval of the traineeship.
