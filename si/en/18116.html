The University Library can help you get your project started. 

The library offers individual guidance for all students who are writing their master’s thesis and bachelor project. On the library’s website you can book a session with the information specialist who knows the most about your field. 

Book an individual session here


The library can help you with:
  • Finding relevant literature within your field
  • Use of databases and other types of resources
  • Advice on your search process 
If you need inspiration on how to find new materials within your subject area, you can use the library subject guides, where you can find journals, databases, newspapers, encyclopedias and other resources. Find your subject here.
You can get help with references and bibliographies by taking a course in the reference management tool Zotero. You can register for the course here.
