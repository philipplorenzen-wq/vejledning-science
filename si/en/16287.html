What if my course is cancelled?

If your course is cancelled, you will be notified by UCPH Study Guidance via your KUmail.

The email gives you a new period – outside the normal registration periods – to choose another course.


Last chance to register in the late-registration

If you do not register for a new course during the period stated in the email, you have another chance in the late-registration. The available places are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.

See when the late-registration is under Course registration.
