If you are deferred in your programme due to council work, you can apply for additional grant portions in accordance with the SU regulations.

Moreover, you can apply for additional grant portions in the event of:

  • Illness
  • Other special circumstances

If you are deferred due to council work, you can get up to 12 additional grant portions if you have participated in:

  • UCPH management, meaning the University Board, Academic Council or the study board
  • work regarding students' social or educational conditions.

To apply for additional grant portions, you must be able to demonstrate that your council work has resulted in a delay in your programme.
