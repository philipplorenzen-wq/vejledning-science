It is possible to include a stay abroad in your studies. The ideal time to go is block 1 and 2 in your second year.

The Master's programmes that resemble ours the most are located in the capital cities of mainland Europe. But interesting electives in finance and statistics can be found in many places even if there is no official Master's programme in math-econ. 

UCPH holds collaboration agreements with (for example): 

  • TU Wien
  • University of Vienna
  • TU Berlin
  • Universität Zürich
  • ETH Zürich
  • LMU München
  • Université de Paris VII Denis Diderot
  • Université Pierre et Marie Curie (Paris VI).
