It can be difficult to choose between all the many courses, which you can use your elective ECTS credits on. Get 3 tips on how to do it.

You are free to plan your own blocks within the framework of your study programme.

Important to know when planning:

  • Not all courses can be mixed and matched freely
  • Some courses must be taken in a specific order.
