If you wish to include a stay abroad in your programme, we recommend that you plan your stay abroad during the first year of the programme. The mobility window of your study programme is in block 3 and 4 of the first year, where you have the most flexibility to plan a study abroad.

If you study abroad, you have to compose a study plan that still fulfills the requirements of your specialisation. If you go at a time when you were supposed to have compulsory courses, you must find courses that are equivalent to the compulsory courses (e.g. if you are away in the first semester of the programme). You must also find courses that can be approved as restricted electives. You can take any courses as your elective courses abroad. However, all courses must be pre-approved before you go.

The University of Copenhagen has a number of exchange agreements with universities worldwide.

If you are interested in learning about the opportunities, you are welcome to contact UCPH Study Guidance or the international coordinator for Physics, Lise Bech Christensen.
