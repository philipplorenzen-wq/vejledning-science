You must apply to change your exam type as early as possible – and at least 6 weeks before your exam. If you apply later, you risk not getting a response in time.


3 steps when applying to change the exam type

  1. Get documentation for illness or decreased functionality, e.g. doctor's certificate, test results.
    See a guide to what your documentation should include
  2. Write a statement describing why you apply for another exam type. 
  3. Fill out an application by clicking the button below and upload documentation and the written statement.
