What is the joint scholarship?

Placering: SU and grants › Grants › UCPH grants

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-is-the-joint-scholarship-ku.aspx

The joint scholarship is a collection of several small scholarships that are announced together. It is not possible to apply for them separately, i.e. you may only apply for them jointly as one scholarship.

If you are eligible for the joint scholarship, you will receive funding from the individual scholarships according to a special allocation procedure. Several of the small scholarships prioritise specific types of applicants, e.g. Højesteretsdommer Eyvind Olrik's scholarship for talented and needy law students who have completed their undergraduate studies.

 See the list of the scholarships included in the joint scholarship. Please remember that it is not possible to apply for the scholarships individually.
