During your thesis preparation project, you may face challenges or changes in the circumstances for your project which mean that you 

  • cannot complete the project 
  • do not want to complete the project. 

In case of extraordinary circumstances, you may apply for a dispensation to withdraw from the exam after expiry of the deadline for withdrawal. 

If you are not withdrawn from the exam, you will use an exam attempt if you do not complete your project.
