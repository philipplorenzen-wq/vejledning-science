Yes, your project can be about your own company that you want to start or already have started. You might have an idea or problem that you have found a solution to and want to turn it into reality.

You can do your project about your own idea in collaboration with a hub or incubator – e.g., UCPH's innovationc centre, UCPH Lighthouse.

UCPH Lighthouse offers advice, networking and office space.
