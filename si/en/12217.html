Gælder for målgrupperne: Geografi Og Geoinformatik - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Intro-SCI-GG-5.aspx

The MSc programme in Geography & Geoinformatics consists of four elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 15 ECTS credits
  2. Restricted elective courses, 30 or 45 ECTS credits
  3. Elective subjects, 15 ECTS credits
  4. Thesis, 45 or 60 ECTS credits

The programme is two years, if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Geography & Geoinformatics with a specialisation in [title of your specialisation] / Cand.scient. i geografi og geoinformatik med en specialisering i [titlen på din specialisering]
