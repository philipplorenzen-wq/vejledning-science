With an Individual Study Project you can pursue a topic of interest through independent study activities. 

Individual Study Project is only an option for 

  • exchange students
  • non-EU guest students.
We do encourage exchange students to follow normal courses while attending SCIENCE, as this will provide you with the best opportunities to meet fellow students and experience a different academic culture.
