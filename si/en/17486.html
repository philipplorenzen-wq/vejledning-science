What do the assessments (B), (I), (U), (MF) and (EB) mean?

Some courses are not assessed with grades, but with 'Bestået' (Pass) or 'Ikke bestået' (Fail). Your grade transcript will say either:

(B) = Bestået/Pass

(I) = Ikke bestået/Fail

If you are registered for an exam but do not attend, do not meet the exam requirements, or if you submit a blank exam, you will receive an assessment that is valid until you retake the exam. It can be either:

(U) = Udeblevet/Absent

(MF) = Manglende forudsætninger/Failed requirements (if you do not meet the requirements for taking the exam)

(EB) = Ej bedømt/not graded

Your diploma will only show passed exams.
