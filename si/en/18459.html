Working at the Department of Chemistry, UCPH provides a lot of opportunities but also responsibilities. As MSc-student one responsibility is to know the rules of safety regulating the department as a workplace, including both office space and laboratories.

It is therefore mandatory as a MSc-student in Chemistry to pass a safety course, and it is the responsibility of your supervisor to make sure you register for the safety course.
The course is held twice a year - typically in week 35 and week 6.

Questions and registration

If you have questions regarding the MSc safety course, contact your MSc supervisor.
