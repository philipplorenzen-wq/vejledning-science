Once you know how to prepare, you will be better able to listen, participate actively and take relevant notes during and after classes.

3 preparation strategies you can try out: 

  • Do your primary reading before class and some repetition after – possibly with your study group. 
  • Quickly skim the material before the lecture, and then read it thoroughly afterwards, with the focus you took away from class. 
  • Read very carefully before class and take good notes during and immediately after class. 

Consider which method best suits you, the specific course and the time you have available.
