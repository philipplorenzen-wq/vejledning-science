You can withdraw from an exam via Self Service > Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Examination > Cancel.

Remember to withdraw before the deadline.

Go to Self Service


Check your exam withdrawal

When you have withdrawn your registration from an exam, you can see it immediately via Self Service > Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Examination > View registrations and cancellations.

You are responsible for ensuring that you are only registered for the exams you can take when the deadline for withdrawal expires.

If you find mistakes in your registrations before the deadline for withdrawal, you must contact UCPH Study Guidance.
