Run

  • Escape if you can.
  • Insist that others escape with you.
  • Leave your belongings behind.

Familiarise yourself with the buildings and think in advance about escape routes and alternative escape routes.

Hide

  • If you can't escape, find a place to hide.
  • Go to the nearest room that can be locked. Lock the door and stay as quiet as possible.
  • Hide your presence in the room, e.g. by closing the curtains.
  • Block the door with furniture and objects if possible.
  • Stay quiet and turn off the sound on your mobile phone.
                • Stay away from the door.

Notify


  • Call 112 and tell the police what they need to know.
  • Stop others from entering the building if you can do so without exposing yourself to risk.

When the police arrive: Follow the police officer's instructions, stay calm and avoid sudden movements that may be interpreted as threatening.

Make sure that your hands are visible.
