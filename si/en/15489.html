My advice to you is:

  • Consider your studies as full-time work and use the time it requires
  • Structure your time so you will have time for both studies and fun. Participate in Study events on study techniques. 
  • Beware of the rules for good scientific conduct (don’t cheat or plagiate - neither intentionally nor non-intentionally), and make yourself well-acquainted with the rules for using AI for your respective course, project or thesis.  
  • Participate in the social activities (student cafés, parties and other social events)
  • Use your influence to improve your studies and the study environment (fill out course evaluations)
  • Read your study messages and study events not to miss important messages and events.
