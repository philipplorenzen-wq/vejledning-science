You can do your Master's thesis together with students from other study programmes at SCIENCE if your joint problem formulation is approved by all the group members' heads of studies.

Four steps to getting started with an interdisciplinary group Master's thesis

  • Start preparations early to establish if your project is feasible.
     
  • First consult with the person you want as your joint supervisor to establish whether he or she believes the Master's thesis you want to do is possible.
     
  • Prepare a description of the assignment (in the Master's thesis contract) based on the description of the learning outcome for Master's theses on each of your study programmes.
    Find the description of the learning outcome in the programme-specific section of the curriculum
     
  • Ask your respective heads of studies whether they can approve the interdisciplinary Master's thesis.
