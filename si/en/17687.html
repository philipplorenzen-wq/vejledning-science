When searching for a collaborator for your project, approach it as if you're searching for a job.
Set aside up to 3-4 months for this process – you will probably have to ask a few before you find a match.

You might:

  • Already have an idea of who your collaborator should be
  • Need to research who could be a relevant collaborator for you.

Companies can also benefit from collaborating with you

If you're hesitant to make contact, remember that the company also gains from:

  • meeting potential candidates within a relevant field
  • getting fresh perspectives on their company/organisation and work area.

 According to the Danish Industry, 6 out of 10 of their members are inclined or very inclined to collaborate with university students on a project
(source: Analyse om dimittenders vej til job – Dansk Industri 2022).

Get tips for finding a company and seeking a project collaboration

Consider before making contact:
  • What are the company's needs?
  • What can you offer the company?
