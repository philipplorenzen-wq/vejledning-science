How do credit transfers count with regard to the SU study activity requirement?

Placering: SU and grants › Your SU › SU study activity requirements

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: gxd · Københavns Universitet - Kandidat · Københavns Universitet - P-Bachelor

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-credit-transfers-count-with-regard-to-the-SU-study-activity-requirement.aspx

Mandatory credit transfer is divided into two types of credit.

  • Credit that can shorten your prescribed completion time 
  • Credit that cannot shorten your prescribed completion time 

Only credit that cannot shorten the prescribed completion time is valid with regard to the SU activity requirement. 

On minSU under See your SU > study progress, you can see the number of ECTS registered as passed on your programme with regard to the SU activity requirement.
