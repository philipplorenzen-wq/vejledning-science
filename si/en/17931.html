Frit Forum (Free Forum) is a social-democratic student association, which you can get involved in during your studies – and vote for in the university elections. 

A red university

Frit Forum is a political community based on social-democratic values of freedom, equality and solidarity. We work to improve the University and pull it in a solidary direction. As a member, you will have the opportunity to contribute to a better university for students and a better society for Denmark as a whole – now and in the future.

Debating

We are not only a student political association at the University of Copenhagen. We are a nationwide political movement, which influences the governing and political forces in society. Each semester, we host events with politicians, researchers and opinion-makers on current issues. We do this because we see political discussion as the core of our association. Everyone is welcome in Frit Forum. The goal is debating – not agreement.

Community

We meet up a couple of times a month for academic and social events. Among other things, we host presentations, debates, study trips, parties, campfire nights and many other activities in which members from all over the country are welcome. As a member of Frit Forum, you will be part of a strong political community where life-long friendships are formed. 

Sign up

Read more about Frit Forum at www.fritforum.dk (in Danish). If you are interested in hearing more about the association, please send an email to kobenhavn@fritforum.dk. Or we can meet for an informal chat over coffee or beer in our rooms near the Round Tower. You can also like our Facebook page 'Frit Forum København' (in Danish) to get information about all our events.
