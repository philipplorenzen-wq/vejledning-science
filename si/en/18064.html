If you are not granted dispensation for an extra exam attempt, you cannot continue your study programme. You will therefore be withdrawn from your study programme.

This means that you will not be able to participate in

  • classes
  • exams
  • programme-related events and offers.


Talk to a study and career guidance counsellor

If your application for dispensation for further exam attempts is not granted, it may be a good idea to talk to a study and career guidance counsellor from UCPH Study Guidance.

This is a chance to discuss, for example

  • the problems that have made it difficult for you to pass the exam
  • the possibilities for appealing
  • the possibilities for completing your studies
  • other educational opportunities.

Contact UCPH Study Guidance


Appeals and support

You can appeal against the decision if you feel your case has not been processed according to the rules.

How to appeal against a decision

You can also contact the Student Ambassador for impartial support or legal advice.
