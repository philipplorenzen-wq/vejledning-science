You have chosen a specialisation during your admission process. To see the structure of your programme please choose your specialisation.

The programme is two years, if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year) and choose a specialisation without internship. If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Food Science and Technology with a specialisation in [titel of your specialisation] / Cand.tech.al. i fødevarevidenskab med en specialisering i [titlen på din specialisering]

The programme is two and a half year if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year) and choose a specialisation with internship. If you complete all of the 150 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Food Science and Technology with a specialisation in [titel of your specialisation] / Cand.tech.al. i fødevarevidenskab med en specialisering i [titlen på din specialisering]
