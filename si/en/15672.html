First and foremost, carefully consider what you are looking for in a supervisor. You will have to work together for an extended period.


Your main supervisor should ideally have both

  • the academic background needed to provide guidance on your topic.
  • the personal qualities which are important for your cooperation. 

You can't always have it all in one and the same person. Therefore, consider what kind of support you need most for this particular project. 


How to find a supervisor

2 possible ways of choosing a supervisor:    

  1. Check out the various research groups at SCIENCE, and see if anyone is working on something that overlaps with your academic interests.
    Find research groups at SCIENCE
  2. Ask your fellow students – for example ask someone who has come further in their studies than you have if they have any recommendations or ideas.
