Minor subjects at the Faculty of Science

You must have 45 ECTS credits in the master's part of your minor subject.

If your minor subject is sports science, you must have 75 ECTS credits in the master's part. Your master's degree programme will thus be extended by 30 ECTS credits, which are eligible for a state education grant (SU).

The courses you need to take depend on the courses you've taken in the bachelor part of your minor subject. You are responsible for ensuring that you take all of the courses in your minor subject in your bachelor and master's programmes.

Minor subjects outside the Faculty of Science

You must have 75 ECTS credits in the master's part of your minor subject. Your master's degree programme will thus be extended by 30 ECTS credits, which are eligible for a state education grant (SU).

The courses you need to take depend on the courses you've taken in the bachelor part of your minor subject. You are responsible for ensuring that you take all of the courses in your minor subject in your bachelor and master's programmes.

You can register for courses via Self-Service during the registration periods.


If you're unsure about which courses make up your minor subject, and the order in which you can take them, contact the student and career guidance service for your minor subject.
