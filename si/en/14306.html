If you need help to clarify whether you are subject to the new rules and how they might affect your situation, there are two ways to get help: 

  • Read the information on the topic site for the SU reform (danish).
  • Contact the SU office at the University of Copenhagen. The SU office can guide you on how to plan your studies and your SU in regard to the new SU rules.
