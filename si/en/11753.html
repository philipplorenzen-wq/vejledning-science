My name is Carsten Pedersen, and I am your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic contents of your programme in detail and have contributed to shaping your curriculum.

My research area is in plant immunity, and we are studying how plants protect themself against pathogens and how pathogens have developed “weapons” to manipulate the host and fight back. The aim is to make resistant plant cultivars. As a side project, I am involved in genetic studies of the Danish apple collection at the Pometum.

I am teaching in Molecular Genetics at the first year of the bachelor’s programme in biotechnology, and some other courses including From Genomics to Plant Breeding.

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the content of your studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here are my advice and recommendations for completing your Master's programme in Biotechnology successfully. Read my advice as an inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are based on the latest programme structure unless I have specifically written otherwise.
