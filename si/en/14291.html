How do I best prepare for the interview?

Before your interview with UCPH Study Guidance, we recommend that you:

  • consider what challenges you encounter during your studies at UCPH.
  • obtain documentation of your functional impairment or diagnosis from your doctor. You send the documentation for your application for SPS before the interview. If you do not have the documentation before the interview, yousend it to sps@adm.ku.dk (preferably in PDF format).

Read the requirements for documentation here.
