Gælder for målgrupperne: Biochemistry - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Your-way-through-the-programme-SCI-BIOK.aspx

Principal Subjects in Biochemical is central to the Biochemistry programme and reflects that Biochemistry is interdisciplinary and bridges biology and chemistry. You learn to search for and critically analyze scientific literature and to use digital tools in biochemical research. This includes data management and the use of artificial intelligence. The course culminates in the ability to peer review manuscripts. Here, you choose among several themes such as protein science, molecular microbiology, molecular genetics, and cell biology. 

The Thesis is placed in the second year but before you start you need to learn how to plan an experimental project like your thesis. You learn this in the Major Subject Project placed just before you start your thesis. I recommend that you choose a thesis preparation project (PREP) in the block before your thesis so that you can do preliminary experiments, test methods etc.

The courses listed in the curriculum as restricted elective for Biochemistry are the courses that I recommend. However, you should follow your interests and also look for courses outside SCIENCE.

If you want to go abroad and take courses block 3 and 4 the first year is the best choice. You need to make a minor change in your study plan regarding the Major Subject Project, but this project can be done in all blocks including block 5. You can also consider doing your thesis or part of your thesis outside Denmark.

Remember that you can change the order of elective and restricted elective in your study plan. 

If you are interested in innovation and entrepreneurship, there are several courses to choose and you can check UCPH Innovation Hubs. You can also consider doing a project in practice in the industry. It may also be relevant to find courses about sustainability.

You should also pay attention to summer courses.
