What’s the structure of the academic year at SCIENCE?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What%E2%80%99s-the-structure-of-the-academic-year-at-SCIENCE-SCI.aspx

The academic year at SCIENCE consists of 4 blocks of 15 ECTS credits (full-time study programme).

A block is divided into:

  • Normal weeks
  • Exam weeks
  • Planned study breaks/re-exam weeks

The 5th block is for summer holidays or summer courses.
