The student ambassador is a kind of ombudsman for all students at UCPH.
You can contact them for impartial legal guidance on matters related to your course of study – such as your rights in connection with a specific matter involving the university.

This could for example be if you

  • feel that you have been treated unfairly
  • need mediation because a case is deadlocked
  • find yourself in a procedural grey area and don't know where to turn.
