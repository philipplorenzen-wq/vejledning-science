Where can I get help with stress?

If you need help to handle or prevent stress during your studies, there is fortunately lots of help at hand. 

What is relevant for you depends on your specific situation.

UCPH Study Guidance

Your study and career guidance counsellor can help you: 

  • talk through your situation and options 
  • plan your studies according to your situation 
  • apply for a dispensation or take sick leave, if necessary. 
Contact UCPH Study Guidance

UCPH Study Guidance also organises various events and courses that will provide you with the tools you need to handle your busy life as a student and prevent serious stress. See all events.

Student Counselling Service 

The Student Counselling Service offers free assistance to help you better understand the strain you are experiencing and handle and reduce stress. 

The Student Counselling Service offers: 

  • Individual counselling sessions 
  • Group sessions 
  • Workshops 

More information is available on the Student Counselling Service website.

Your doctor 

If you experience severe and persistent signs of stress, you should contact your doctor. 

Your doctor can: 

  • help you assess your situation and get the right treatment 
  • issue a medical certificate if you need to apply for a dispensation or take sick leave.
