Can I change supervisor along the way?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-change-supervisor-along-the-way-SCI.aspx

You cannot change your supervisor during your project except in the case of very extraordinary circumstances.

Talk to your head of studies if problems arise that you and your supervisor cannot resolve together.

Contact UCPH Study Guidance if you and your supervisor need help with how to move on with a new or revised thesis.
