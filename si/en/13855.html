How do I apply for SPS?

If you want to apply for support (SPS), you will need to book an interview with UCPH Study Guidance. 

The counsellors will then help you apply for support. If you are given support or aids, they will also help you get it up and running. 

It may take a couple of months before your support is fully in place.
