You have 3 different options for undertaking a project in collaboration with a company or organisation during your programme:

  • A project in practice
    Read about the rules for a project in Practice
  • A project outside the course scope
    Read about the rules for a project outside the course scope
  • Your thesis
    Read about the rules for your Master's thesis


You need to familiarize yourself with the rules for the specific type of project and assess which project format suits the project you want to undertake.

Questions to help you clarify what you want
  • Which type of project makes the most sense for you to use for a project collaboration?
  • When does it best fit into your study programme?
  • If you're considering an elective project, do you have room for the project in your education?

You can discuss your considerations and study plan with a study and career guidance counsellor in UCPH Study Guidance.
