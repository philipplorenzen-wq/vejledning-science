Can I take exams on special terms due to exam anxiety?

If you feel that your exam-related anxiety prevents you from taking exams on the same terms as your fellow students, you can apply for special exam conditions.


Examples of special exam conditions due to exam anxiety:

  • a non-public oral exam, because possibly having an audience increases your anxiety
  • extra time to take breaks during the exam because your anxiety often makes you feel unwell.
