If you wish to use your elective options to obtain a specific academic profile, I have made these recommended study tracks for you.

When you plan your studies, make sure you comply with the number of ECTS credits, which constitutes your programme structure.
See the structure of your programme and the recommended study plan.

Make sure to check up on all courses in the course database. Sometimes elective courses may be cancelled or moved.

We have grouped some courses that could be relevant, if you have an interest in any of these tracks below. Remember, that you are free to choose any combination of elective and restricted elective courses, as long as you follow the programme structure.

If a course is outside SCIENCE, as several of the courses particularly in the Health track are, remember to apply for pre-approval. A pre-approval is not required for those courses at the Faculty of Health that are listed as restricted elective courses within the programme.

Read more about your programme structure.

In addition to these courses, you always have the opportunity of doing a Project in Practice or a Project outside the course scope. These courses offer great opportunities to obtain experiences in the lab, to test an idea and not at least to work with environmental science topics in public and private companies.
