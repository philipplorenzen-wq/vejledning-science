If you are ill before your exam starts – up to a week before or on your exam day –  you must as soon as possible

  1. obtain documentation of your illness. Read the requirements for the documentation of illness on this page.
  2. submit a notification of illness by filling out an online form via the button below and uploading your documentation no later than 8 calendar days from and including the day of the exam.
    If your documentation isn’t ready by the deadline, you’ll need to upload a document instead, stating when you expect to be able to submit it. 
  3. oral exams: You also need to inform your course coordinator about your illness no later than 09:00 on the day of your exam.
Notify of illness

Answer to your notification of illness 

If your notification of illness is approved, you will avoid using an exam attempt. You will usually receive a reply within 6 weeks.

Register for the exam again

You must register for the exam again yourself, since this does not automatically happen. Find out how to do so under Exam registration
