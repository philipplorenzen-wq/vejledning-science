How do I register for courses?

You always register for 2 or 3 blocks at a time. Once you have registered for a course, you are automatically registered for the exam.

How to register for courses at Self Service:
    1. Find the course code of your courses at SCIENCE (e.g. NBIA04058U) in the course database on kurser.ku.dk or in your programme's structure. Check the course is in the correct volume.

    2. Go to Self Service and choose 'Enrolment, courses & exams'.

    3. Choose 'Courses' and 'Register'.

    4. Click on the course list in the drop-down menu that matches the block and the first letters of the course code.
    5. Tick the courses you would like to register for and confirm your registration.

You are responsible for ensuring that you are registered for the correct courses.

If cannot find your course in Self Service

Please contact UCPH Study Guidance if you 

  1. cannot find your course in any of the lists available in Self Service
  2. are sure that the course will be offered in the relevant block.

Courses at other faculties or universities are listed in separate lists

Please note that if you are doing compulsory or restricted elective courses at other faculties or universities, these are listed in a separate list in Self Service.
