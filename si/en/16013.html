  • Consider your study as full-time work and use the time it requires
  • Structure your time and plan ahead, so you will have time for both studies and leisure. Participate in SPOT events on study techniques.
  • Read your study messages and study events so you do not miss important messages and events.  
  • Beware of the rules regarding exam cheating and good scientific conduct. Do not cheat or plagiarize - neither intentional nor non-intentional. It can have very serious consequences for you personally. Learn the rules.  
  • Engage in the study environment – this education is situated in Kalundborg where you will be one of the pioneer for MA in Biosolutions, which started in 2024.
  • Familiarize yourself with the curriculum for the study. These are the official rules that regulate your study.  
  • Avoid negative stress. You must find strategies to get relaxed and refilled with energy. Read more about positive and negative stress.
  • As it is a new education – use your influence to improve your studies and study environment (Course evalutions)
