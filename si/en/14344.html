I have completed the inclusion survey. How was my data used?

The inclusion survey's responses have been processed by the University's external data processor, ConnectingCultures. No one at UCPH has had access to your response.

ConnectingCultures has analysed both the quantitative and qualitative data from the survey's free-text fields. The analytical focus has been on uncovering inclusion, including experiences of exclusion, difficulties with belonging, study/career barriers and accessibility challenges.

The results have subsequently been compiled in overall result reports at UCPH-level.

Data has not been analysed at a more detailed programme level, partly due to the confidential data processing of your response. There had to be at least 20 responses from a specific response group before their responses were analysed. If there were fewer than 20, the group was expanded and the analytical level thus made more general.
