What do I do if I lose my student ID card?

If you lose your student ID card, you must block it to avoid misuse.

When you block your card, a new card will be automatically ordered. When the card is ready, you will receive an e-mail on your KUmail with information on how to obtain your student ID card.

You can block your card in Self Service under ID-card > Block ID cards
