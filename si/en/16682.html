Each subject area at UCPH has a study board. The study board consists of an equal number of representatives of:

  • academic staff
  • students.

The board is responsible for organising and ensuring the quality and development of the degree programmes in the given subject area. This involves:

  • following up on programme and course evaluations
  • preparing proposals for curricula
  • organising exams
  • processing applications for credit transfers and exemptions.

Influence on your study board and study programme

You can stand for election to the board of studies. Students are elected for one year at a time.

You can also influence who is on the board of studies linked to your degree programme by voting at the UCPH elections.
