I am Jacob B. Hansen and I serve as your Head of Studies. I know the academic contents of your programme in detail, and have contributed to determining your curriculum. My research area is metabolic regulation and obesity.

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the content of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here are my advice and recommendations for successfully completing your Master's programme in Molecular Biomedicine. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are written on the basis of the latest programme structure, unless I have specifically written otherwise.
