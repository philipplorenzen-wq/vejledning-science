At Kalundborg there are several teaching buildings where courses are held, and you will find University College Absalon. The teaching buildings are shared facilities among other programmes across different educational institutions. 

You will also find Helix Lab Research and Education Center where collaboration between industry and research takes place.

Emergency preparedness, evacuation and first aid

Defibrillator

There is 1 defibrillator at University College Absalon. The defibrillator can be found on the outer wall at the main entrance.

First-aid case

There are first-aid cases in the laboratories and the studies administration room A1.11 at University College Absalon.

Evacuation plan

Get to know the evacuation plan (only in Danish) thoroughly. In an emergency, it can save human lives.

In the event of fire, fire alarm or other incidents that require buildings to be evacuated:

  1. If the evacuation alarm is activated (a voice asks you to leave the building and not use the lifts)
  2. use the nearest emergency exit and
  3. proceed at a calm but determined pace to the assembly point outside the bicycle parking area opposite the main entrance.

People in wheelchairs should head to the nearest emergency exit and make contact with staff.
