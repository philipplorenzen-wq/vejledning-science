When you are allowed to leave depends on the rules for the specific exam. The invigilators will tell you the rules before the exam starts.

In some exams you are not allowed to leave the room until the exam is over.

 Always stay in your seat for at least:

  • the first half hour of the exam
  • the last half hour of the exam.

You need to get permission from an invigilator before you leave the exam room.

If you leave the exam without permission

If you leave the exam room without getting permission from an examiner or invigilator, you will be considered to have stopped your exam and will not be allowed to return.
Your exam paper will not be graded, but you will still have used one exam attempt.

No smoking breaks allowed at exams on Frederiksberg

You cannot take smoking breaks with neither e-cigarettes nor tobacco, when you have exams in the halls on Frederiksberg.

The reason is that all the outdoor areas belonging to the municipality are smoke free.
