Requirements for your report:

  • It must include reflections in relation to the description of objectives for the project in practice. Among other things, it must show that you can apply theory and methods from your study programme and include reflections on the company.
    Find the full description of objectives in the shared section of your curriculum

  • You must write in English or Danish.
  • You must write an abstract (summary) in the same language as the project report. 
    Your abstract will be published at the study information site as inspiration for other students.
    Fill out your abstract
  • You must fill out and submit an AI declaration, regardless of whether you have used generative AI or not.
    How to fill out and submit the AI declaration
  • You must avoid plagiarism. You must be aware of this both if you use your own as well as others' academic work.
    Read more about good scientific practice and plagiarism


Talk to your supervisor about scope and content

SCIENCE has no fixed requirements regarding how your report is structured.
Talk to your supervisor about

  • what your report should contain
  • how much you need to write.
