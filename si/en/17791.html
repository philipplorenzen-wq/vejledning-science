The specialisation is set at 120 ECTS credits and consists of three elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 30 ECTS credits
  2. Elective subjects, 30 ECTS credits
  3. Thesis, 60 ECTS credits
The specialisation has changed its title from Physical Chemistry to Physical and Computational Chemistry (applicable from the academic year 2023/24).
