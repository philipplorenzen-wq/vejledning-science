There are several things you can do yourself to get better at handling your exam nerves.

Download the Exam Stress app

You can download the Student Counselling Service's 'Exam Stress' app and get digital exam support via your smartphone.

The app gives you tips and exercises that can help you

  • structure your time and preparation for the exam
  • stay calm and contain your nervousness
  • avoid negative, spiralling thoughts.

You can find the app in Google Play or App Store.

Improve your exam techniques

Good exam techniques can help you manage your exam-related nervousness.
You can improve your exam techniques by:

  • try the 5 tips and exercises for good exam techniques here at Study information 
  • join a SPOT ON event on exam techniques.

SPOT  ON events on exam techniques

Study and Career Guidance arranges several events a year on exam techniques. The events include presentations and exercises.

Join an event and get:

  • tips on tackling nervousness and being more confident in the exam situation
  • advice on structuring your preparation for exams
  • advice on how to present what you know at the exam.

Keep track of upcoming events on the SPOT  ON website
