When studying for an exam, it's a good idea to clarify what the exam subjects are and what is expected of you at the exam.


3 tips on how to gain an overview before you start studying for an exam:

  1. Read the course description. Focus on the section 'Learning Outcome'. That is where you can read what skills, knowledge and competencies you must acquire during the course and bring to the exam table.
    Find your course description
  2. Go through the syllabus and divide the subjects into what you are good at and what you are not so good at. Start by tackling the subjects that challenge you the most.
  3. Check out the exam form and where and when you are going to take the exam.
