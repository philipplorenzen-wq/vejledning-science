If you use personal data when writing a group assignment or for a report or master's thesis on which you collaborate with one or more other students, an agreement on joint data controlling must be entered into.

The agreement on joint data controlling means that all members of the group are responsible for the personal data collected for use in the assignment/project/master's thesis.
