Check in Digital Exam which format is allowed for uploading your assignment.


How to find the format for your assignment:

  • Log into Digital Exam and go to 'My Exams'.
  • Select the relevant exam.
  • Press 'Next' (bottom right) repeatedly until you get to the submission page itself. Here you can see which format is allowed for submitting your assignment.
