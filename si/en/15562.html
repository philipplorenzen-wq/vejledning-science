How do I order a student ID card?

Placering: IT and facilities on campus › Practical information › Access and student ID card

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Alle uddannelsesniveauer · Gæstestuderende HUM - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-to-do-before-IDcard-gets-produced_KU.aspx

  1. To order your student ID card, you need to go to the “Self Service" on KUnet. Select: ID  card > Upload photo for ID card.

  2. Upload a photo of yourself in Self Service. The photo must be exactly 200x200 pixels and in JPEG/JPG format.


    GO TO SELF SERVICE

  3. When your ID card is ready, you will receive an e-mail in your KUmail with more information on how to obtain it.
