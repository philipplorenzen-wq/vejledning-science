IT support over Christmas and New Year

From December 24 through January 2, all support centers will be closed. However, on December 29–30 and January 2 you can call +45 35 32 32 32 between 8:00 AM and 3:00 PM and get help.

If you did not find an answer to your IT question, please contact IT Support by using one of the following options.

IT Support 

  • Call +45 35 32 32 32
    Monday-Friday: 8:00-16:00 (find opening hours over Christmas and New Year above) 
  • Submit a request in Serviceportal
  • Find local support centres and opening hours
