There are plenty of places to find help, tools and inspiration for your job search.

You can, for example

  • call for a guidance session with a study and career guidance counsellor in UCPH Study Guidance
  • keep up to date on study events and find relevant SPOT ON events
  • join a trade union and/or an unemployment fund and look into their job search support
