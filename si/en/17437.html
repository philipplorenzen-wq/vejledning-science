The study programme for all specialisations (exept Cell Biology and Physiology) include a “mobility window”, making it possible to study one semester abroad, for summer starters in block 3+4 in the first year. Depending on the length of your stay and how you have used your ECTS you should find programs with equivalent compulsory or restricted elective courses abroad. You have to get a preapproval of the courses abroad. As it takes time to arrange a study abroad, you must start the planning as soon as possible - best during block 1. If you follow the Ecology specialization there is a 4UE+ alliance learning pathway in Biodiversity, Ecology and Evolution. You may of course also travel abroad during your Master's thesis work. Talk to your coming supervisor about your options. 

  • Start planning your stay abroad as soon as possible
  • Look for SPOT events (UCPH Study Guidance)
  • Consult the study and career advisors from UCPH Study Guidance
  • Contact the international coordinator at Department of Biology
  • Read reports from other students having studied abroad
  • Look for funding possibilities
