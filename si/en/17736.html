The specialisation in Ecology, double degree at a 4EU+ partner university, is set at 120 ECTS credits and consists of the following:

  • Compulsory courses:
    • 7.5 ECTS credits at UCPH
  • Restricted elective courses:
    • 22.5 ECTS credits at UCPH
  • Elective subjects: 
    • 15 ECTS at UCPH

You can apply for the double degree option in the first semester of your MSc programme when your specialisation is Ecology.

4EU+ partner universities

Current 4EU+ partner universities available:
  • University of Warsaw (UW), Poland
