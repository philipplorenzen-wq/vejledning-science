The University of Copenhagen would very much like to stay in touch with you after you have graduated. We hope you will register with the UCPH Alumni Network so that you may receive invitations and news from us in the future.

You can join the University of Copenhagen Alumni Network as a student or a graduate and thus become part of a large network of former and current students at the University of Copenhagen.
