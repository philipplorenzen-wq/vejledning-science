You can complain about unacceptable aspects of a course or problems which it has not been possible to resolve.


You can, for instance, complain about

  • the specific content and form of the course
  • the quality of the teaching
  • factors affecting the teaching situation (e.g. noise, teaching facilities).

You can complain both during and after completing the course.
