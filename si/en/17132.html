As the name suggests, you use your thesis preparation project to get ready for the thesis, before you begin your thesis period. Specifically, you must be able to continue working with the content, and based on your experiences from the project, in your thesis.

You can use the thesis preparation project to:

  • Fine tune the topic you are considering writing a thesis on. 
  • Gather specific empirical and theoretical knowledge and methodological experience you can use in the thesis.

Form of your project

A thesis preparation project is independent study with guidance. You must write a report on the thesis preparation work you complete during the project period.

Agree with your supervisor on how you will specifically complete the thesis preparation work.

Examples of thesis preparation work:

  • Problem analysis
  • Trial planning
  • Review of relevant literature
  • Prepare applications to relevant authorities
  • Pilot trial
  • Assess the strengths and weaknesses of experimental methods
  • Obtain empirical data
  • Field work
  • Prepare problem formulation and thesis contract

Project purpose and requirements

Generally speaking, you must: demonstrate your ability to

  • acquire knowledge within your subject area
  • work independently
  • improve your skills and readiness for doing a thesis.

See which specific purposes and requirements your thesis preparation project must meet in the shared section of your curriculum.
