How do I write a Master’s thesis in a group?

The first thing you must do is find up to three people with whom you would like to work (max. four in a group).


To write your thesis as a group you must

  • all be required to do the same size of Master's thesis (in ECTS credits)
  • choose the same supervisor
  • draw up a joint contract with the same start and submission dates (if you are from the same study programme) or draw up separate contracts (if you are from different study programmes).
  • if you come from different study programmes, you must have the topic approved by your respective heads of studies.
