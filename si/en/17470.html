What rules must I follow when taking courses at another university/faculty?

When you take courses at another faculty or university, you follow

  • the other university/faculty's specific rules and procedures regarding courses and exams – e.g. rules about registration, sick leave, re-exam etc. 
  • SCIENCE's general rules regarding your programme – e.g. rules about study activity requirements, distribution of your ECTS credits etc.


If you do not wish to complete the course

In specific situations, you can apply to annul your pre-approval as to not be bound to pass your course at the other faculty or university.

See when you are bound to pass a pre-approved course under Pre-approval.
