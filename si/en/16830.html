You are automatically registered for the ordinary exam. 
If you need to take the re-exam, you can decide whether you want to register for the re-exam in the ordinary exam periodthe re-exam period 
You can register for exams via email to studyguidance-jur@adm.ku.dk. Please contact UCPH Study Guidance if you have problems with your registration.
