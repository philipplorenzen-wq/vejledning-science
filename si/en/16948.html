The exam week is primarily for exams. You may have classes to attend during the exam week, for instance on courses with ongoing tests.  

Your course coordinator is not allowed to schedule extraordinarily work-intensive activities requiring your attendance during exam weeks. For instance, excursions or assignments to be handed in.
