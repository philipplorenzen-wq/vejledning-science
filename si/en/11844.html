All staff and students at UCPH have access to participate in remote teaching through Zoom and Microsoft Teams.

  • Teams is a part of Office365. To log in to Microsoft Office365 and Teams, use your UCPH-username@alumni.ku.dk - for example, ABC123@alumni.ku.dk + your password.
  • UCPH IT only supports participation in meetings or live events in Teams and Zoom, and not other features in Zoom, Teams or Microsoft Office365.
