Fill out the form “Get Study Administrative Help” if you have issues with

  • registering for or withdrawing from courses, projects or exams
  • getting your project or thesis contract approved
  • finding your timetable or accessing course rooms on Absalon
  • changing classes
  • submitting assignments
  • receiving a response to an application e.g. for dispensations, pre-approvals or credit transfers

Get Study Administrative Help

Note: Please use the forms available here on your Study Information page if, for example, you need to apply for an dispensation, report parental leave, apply for pre-approval etc.


Request a signed document

Fill out the form “Enrolment and Transcript Requests” if you need signed documentation — e.g. for when applying for student accommodation, scholarships or going abroad.

We can send the following documents, stamped and signed, to your KUmail within 24 hours:

  • Transcript, which shows the courses you have completed or confirms that you have graduated from your study programme.
  • Documentation of Enrolment, which verifies that you are currently enrolled at UCPH/your study programme and indicates when you are expected to complete your degree (e.g. for use in student accommodation applications).

Enrolment and Transcript Requests
