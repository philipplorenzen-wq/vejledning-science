As a Master's degree student, you can go abroad from your first year of study.


If you want to go abroad during the first year of your Master’s programme, you must remember to

  • apply for the Master's programme by 1 March. You can only go abroad if you are admitted on the programme.
  • start planning your stay as soon as possible. If you have a Bachelor's Degree from SCIENCE start before you are admitted on the Master's programme.
