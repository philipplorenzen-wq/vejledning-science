Before the deadline for withdrawing from courses, you can withdraw from the course on Self Service.

See the deadline for withdrawing from courses


If the deadline for withdrawing from courses has passed, you can only be withdrawn from courses if you apply for a dispensation based on your illness and the dispensation is approved.
