You can do a Project in Practice or a Project outside the course scope (PUK) as a replacement for an elective. You can also do a Thesis preparation project (PREP). In practice the main difference between a PUK and a PREP is that your PREP-advisor is obligated also to supervise your MSc thesis, but this is an option for the PUK advisor. 

All of these can be written in collaboration with an external partner.

Feel free to contact a relevant faculty member to inquire about supervision. This also applies to your final Master's thesis.

You may choose your supervisor anywhere from SCIENCE. For students in Mathematics-Economics you may also choose your supervisor from the Department of Economics

UCPH Study Guidance regularly post information about career events in the Study events on KUnet. You can also follow the Facebook group.
