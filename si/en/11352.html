The curriculum for the MSc programme in Biotechnology is governed by the Study Board for the Biological Area at the Faculty of Science, University of Copenhagen.

Curriculum 2012 (revised 2026): Applies to students enrolled at the start of the 2012/13 academic year or later. The curricula consists of
