How and when do I apply for leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances?

You can apply for leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances as soon as you need it.

Your leave of absence will start at the earliest on the date you submit the application
Your leave of absence must end at the same time as a block.

If you wish to take an exam at the end of the last block during your leave of absence, you can apply for an end date immediately before your exam date. You do so by entering your request in the comments field of the application form.


How to apply for leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances:

  1. Get documentation of the situation/condition that is the reason for your leave of absence (e.g. a medical certificate from a doctor or an opinion from a psychologist, maternity records, call-up for military service).
    See a guide to what your documentation should include
  2. Fill in the application and upload your documentation via 'Apply for leave of absence'.

Apply for leave of absence


Reply to your application

You will usually receive a reply within 2 weeks of submitting your application.


Starting your leave of absence

If your application for leave of absence is approved, your leave will apply retroactively from the start date applied for (at the earliest from the day on which you submitted your application). You will be withdrawn from courses, exams, etc. during your period of leave, and you therefore do not have to do anything else before starting your leave of absence.
