If you have any ideas that you want to explore in more detail, there are various things you can do to focus on and flesh them out:

  • Take part in career events and listen to companies and workplaces presenting what they do.
  • Collaborate on a project with a company. This could be a project in practice or a thesis collaboration. Even if you are not sure whether this is exactly what you want, it can give you experience that can help you decide what path is right for you. 
    Learn about the possible project types
  • Apply for a student job in one of the fields you are considering.
    Get tips for your job application
  • Contact people who work in the fields you are interested in. Be curious and ask about their working life. 
    Use LinkedIn to get in touch with interesting people
