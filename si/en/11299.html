The Study Board has the resonsibility for planning, implementing and developing their programmes and instruction, including aspects such as quality assurance, programme and instructor evaluations and the preparation of curriculum proposals.

In addition, the Study Board handles pre-approval, credit transfer and dispensation applications, expresses its opinion in all matters of importance for education and instruction, and discusses education and instruction issues as forwarded by the university leadership.

The board is composed of an equal number of representatives from the scientific staff and the student body.

When will my application be processed?

You will receive a reply to your application within 6 weeks. The 6 weeks are counted from the date where we have received all the information from you that we require to process your application.

If we require information from you in order to process your application, we will contact you on your UCPH e-mail.
