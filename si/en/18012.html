My recommendations:

  • Create a LinkedIn account as soon as possible. Keep it updated! Many recruiters will check your LinkedIn profile.
  • Get to know the companies - join a career event – for instance the science career horizons in week 40 every year.
  • Think about what kind of job you would like before you decide on your thesis.
  • Consider doing a project or your thesis in collaboration with a company.
  • Look at typical jobs for recent graduates for inspiration
  • Join the Bohr Alumni Association even before you graduate in order to participate in career inspiration events
