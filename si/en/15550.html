You need to withdraw your registration for an exam by 23:59 on the last day of the withdrawal period.


Other deadline for withdrawing from project exams

You can only be deregistered from exams in project in practice, project outside the course scope and thesis preparation project if you withdraw before the end of the late-registration period for the block. After this period, you must apply for a dispensation to withdraw. See the late-registration periods under Course registration.

You cannot withdraw from the Master's thesis exam.
