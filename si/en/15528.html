Can I study reduced hours if it means exceeding the framework of the study programme?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-study-reduced-hours-if-it-means-exceeding-the-framework-of-the-study-programme-SCI.aspx

You can only do so if you are granted a dispensation from the study activity requirements by your Study Board.

You may be able to apply for a dispensation if you can document that you need to study at reduced hours due to exceptional circumstances.


Exceptional circumstances include, for example:

  • a need to resume your studies slowly after illness – e.g. stress or a concussion
  • a physical or mental functional impairment that prevents full-time studies
  • serious illness or death in your immediate family


Examples of circumstances which do not qualify as exceptional circumstances:

  • Your student job has taken up too much time.
  • Your housing situation or financial circumstances.

You can mention such circumstances in your application, but they cannot stand alone when you apply for a dispensation.
