The Board is the supreme authority of UCPH. Its mandate is to safeguard the University's interests as a research and educational institution.

The Board has 11 members:

  • Six external members
  • Two academic staff members
  • Two student representatives
  • One technical/administrative staff member.

 The student representatives serve on the Board for a two-year period.

Board meetings are held approximately six times a year with attendance from the Rectorate.

You can run for the Board yourself and influence who its members are by voting at the UCPH elections.
