How am I bound by my course after the deadline for withdrawal?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-am-I-bound-by-my-course-after-the-deadline-for-withdrawal-SCI.aspx

Binding registration for courses means that you

  1. must pass the course in order to complete your study programme
  2. can only withdraw from the course – and no longer be bound by your course registration – by applying for a dispensation due to exceptional circumstances.

You will be bound by your course registration after the expiry of the deadline for withdrawal (the same as the deadline for late-registration).
