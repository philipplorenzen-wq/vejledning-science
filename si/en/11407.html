Keep up to date on Absalon when you are ill. This will often help you avoid problems on the course.

Illness can quickly affect your ability to pass if your course

  • has examination prerequisites that you must meet to take the exam
  • has ongoing tests.

Are you unsure about your course or exam form? Read more in the course description
