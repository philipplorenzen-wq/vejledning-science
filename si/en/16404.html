In the first year, "projects outside of course scope" can replace one or two elective courses. A "project in practice", which is done in collaboration with a company, can also replace elective courses. There is even the option of preparing for your thesis work with a "preparatory project", where you may learn an advanced method or technique.

The second year is for your thesis work. I recommend the full 60 ECTS master's thesis since it is likely to be your favorite part of the programme. But, there is also a 45 ECTS thesis option to ensure maximal flexibility in your choices.

Read more about the Master's thesis and other projects here.
