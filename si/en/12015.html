A self-arranged stay is for students who would like to study at a university with which UCPH does not have an agreement. In connection with a self-arranged stay, you pay the tuition fee yourself.

If you are considering arranging your study abroad period yourself, you should be aware of the following:

  • Where you can go
  • What criteria your stay abroad must meet
  • How you plan a self-arranged stay abroad.
