DIKU Padel is a student organization at the University of Copenhagen that brings together students from the Department of Computer Science (DIKU) for padel and social activities.

The club organizes padel events where the focus is as much on having fun with friends as it is on the sport itself. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, there's room for everyone, and we emphasize creating a relaxed and friendly atmosphere.

Our activities offer DIKU students the chance to meet, stay active, and build friendships through sport.

Contact: padel@di.ku.dk
