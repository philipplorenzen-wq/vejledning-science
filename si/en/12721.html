Computer

To get KUmail on your private computer, you can add KUmail to your personal email account – for example, Hotmail or Gmail – or install a UCPH license for Microsoft 365 for free. With the UCPH license you get Word, Excel and Outlook and 5 TB of storage among other things. Read more about the UCPH license for Microsoft 365.

Once you have installed Microsoft 365, you can set up KUmail in Outlook.

  1. Follow the instructions to install Microsoft 365:
    Microsoft 365 - install
  2. Set up your KUmail in Outlook by following the instructions that fits your computer:
    Windows Outlook 
    Mac instructions

Smartphone and tablet

You can read KUmail on your smartphone or tablet by adding your KUmail account to the email application you already use:

  • Add KUmail on Android device
  • Add KUmail on iPhone and iPad
Please note, that you need to use multi-factor authentication when accessing your KUmail from Outlook and other mail apps on your phone or tablet.
