If you detect acute faults and/or defects on or in the buildings of the University of Copenhagen, you must report it. Below, you will find information about how to report acute faults and defects.

Acute faults include:

  • Faults that may cause personal injury or property damage.
  • Water leaks.
  • Freezer malfunctions.
  • Power failure affecting multiple users.
  • Problems with lifts involving people.

In the event of acute faults, please call the emergency phone on your campus. You can find the emergency numbers for all campuses below.

Call the emergency phone on: 35 32 70 28

Frederiksberg Campus, Østervoldgade 10, Nødebo and Taastrup:
