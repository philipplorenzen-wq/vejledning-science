The SU rules on study activity requirements are not the same set of rules as the academic study activity requirements, set by the UCPH.
The SU requirements depend on whether you have started on your current programme before, or after 1. July 2016.

It will be considered as starting a new programme if you:         

  • Start a master's degree programme
  • Change your field of study
