You can become a part of Actory, which is the University's new space for sustainable involvement. Actory is a community and a workshop for those of you who want to promote sustainable change – large-scale and small-scale. In Actory, you can participate in innovation projects, events, case challenges, semester courses and talks and get input and feedback on your project whenever it suits you.

You can also join a green student organisation. See a list of green student organisations at UCPH.
