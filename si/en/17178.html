As long as your stay can be approved under your study programme, you can choose a company or an organisation based anywhere in the world. In the final analysis, you are responsible for finding and contacting the company/organisation in question.

Apply for an Erasmus+ Traineeship for a stay within the EU

You can apply for an Erasmus+ Traineeship grant for a stay within the EU if this falls under the EU's definition of work placement. This entails a working week of approximately 37 hours with the purpose being that you will acquire competences enabling you to hold a job after completion of your study programme.
