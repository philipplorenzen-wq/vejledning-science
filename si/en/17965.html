On City Campus you will find: 

  • Geobar. At Geocenter you can visit Geobar – run by students from Geography and Geology.
    Read more about Geobar on Facebook
  • Café Kommunen. On the ground floor of Building 12 at the Centre for Health and Society (CSS), you will find Café Kommunen, which is both a student function room and a student café for all students at CSS.
    On normal weekdays, Café Kommunen sells coffee and tea at affordable prices from early morning until late afternoon. Many students drop in during the day and pick up supplies. The café has a large seating area, where students can meet in their study groups, eat their lunch or hang out between lectures and classes. Café Kommunen is run by volunteers from all study programmes based at CSS. The volunteers do one shift a week, and take it in turns to brew coffee and tea – and you can lend a hand, too.
    Read more on Café Kommunen’s Facebook page
  • KØS. Friday after Friday, KØS (in Danish Kommunal Øl-Service, literally ‘municipal beer service’) serves a variety of special brews, for example from the illustrious microbreweries Mikkeller and Nørrebro Bryghus, at extremely student-friendly prices. KØS events are held at Café Kommunen, and in the summer beers are served outside, when the weather is pleasant. The atmosphere is friendly and informal, and it is a perfect chance to enjoy a delicious quality beer with your fellow students after a hard week of being bent over the books.
    Read more about KØS on the Facebook page
