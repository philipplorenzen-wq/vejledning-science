You decide the structure of your exam in a thesis preparation project together with your supervisor.


Your exam can be a

  • written report
  • written report followed by an oral exam.

Agree on the specific details about, for example, form, content, length and focus for what you submit and/or present with your supervisor.

Your exam must be placed in the exam period of the last last block of your project.

Find this year's exam and re-exam weeks
If you do an oral exam, you can choose to do it online, if you and your supervisor agree on it.
