Please report your foreign TIN and address in Mobility-Online when indicating whether you have a NEM account or not for the disbursement of your scholarship.

Please note: If you provide incorrect information about TIN or foreign address, International Education will contact you directly via email and the disbursement of your grant will be delayed.
