Applying for admission through an exchange programme is for students who would like to study abroad for one or more semesters at a foreign university with which UCPH has an agreement. If you go on an exchange stay to a partner university, you do not have to pay any tuition fee.

UCPH has a large number of exchange agreements worldwide – so there are ample opportunities for finding an exchange place that suits precisely your specific needs.
