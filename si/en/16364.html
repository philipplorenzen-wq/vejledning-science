When can I do my project?

Placering: Projects › Individual Study Project › Purpose, requirements, ECTS and timing

Gælder for målgrupperne: Gæstestuderende SCI - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/When-can-I-do-my-project-SCI-1.aspx

Depending on the size of your project, you can work with it over one or more blocks:

  • A project totalling 7.5 ECTS credits can only be done over one block.
  • A project totalling 15 ECTS credits can either be done in one block full-time or part-time over two blocks.

You must start your project at the start of the block and finish it by the end of the exam week in the last block of the project.
