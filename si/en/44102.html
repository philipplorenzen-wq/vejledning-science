The University seeks to meet the needs of you and your fellow students. Therefore, there is not a finite list of special conditions you can apply for.

Examples of special conditions in class:

  • Special lighting and other design features in the classroom.
  • Permission to use and room for special educational support aids.
  • Permission to bring assistance (interpreters etc.)
  • Rooms with special design/access.
  • Consideration for allergies in connection with experiments.

Whatever the circumstances, the special conditions cannot lower the academic level or give you an advantage over your fellow students.
