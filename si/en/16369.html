Your main supervisor is

  • your academic supporter and sounding board, from the ideas development phase and start-up to the actual project work
  • the examiner on your report and for your oral exam.

The supervisor's responsibility for the project

The main supervisor is responsible for ensuring that the scope of the project is such that it can be completed within the set timeframe, and that you receive regular feedback as to whether your work is progressing at a pace that will allow you to comply with the timeframe. 

The main supervisor must also administer the examination.
