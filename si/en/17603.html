It is possible to include a stay abroad in your studies. The ideal time to go is block 1 and 2 in your second year.

The programs that resemble ours the most are located in the capital cites of mainland Europe. In the US, UK or Australia it is typically easier to find relevant electives  in finance or statistics.

UCPH holds collaboration agreements with (for example) TU Wien, TU Berlin, Universität Zürich, ETH Zürich, LMU München, Université de Paris VII Denis Diderot, Université Pierre et Marie Curie (Paris VI).
