You can replace some of your courses on your study programme with courses at a foreign university during the summer.

You must obtain pre-approval of the summer course if it is to be included in your study programme at UCPH.

There are two options for taking summer courses:

  • Apply for a summer course at a university with which UCPH cooperates.
  • Arrange your summer course stay yourself.
