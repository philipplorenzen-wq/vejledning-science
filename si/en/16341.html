You submit the report in Digital Exam at the deadline written in your digital contract after it has been approved.

A couple of days before your deadline please ensure that it is possible for you to submit your report in Digital Exam.

You cannot edit your report or submit a new version of it, once you have submitted it the first time.
 

Go to Digital exam

How to submit as a group

If you have a shared contract and are enrolled at the same study programme only one of you should submit the report in Digital Exam on behalf of the group. You will all be able to see, that the report has been submitted if you log onto Digital Exam.

If you have different contracts and are enrolled at different study programmes, all group members need to submit a copy of your report.


If you are prevented from submitting by the deadline

If you e.g. fall ill in a way that prevents you from submitting the Master's thesis report by the deadline in your contract, you can apply for a dispensation.
