Enroll to the library's ABSALON module.

https://absalon.ku.dk/enroll/E76LA4

Here you can learn more about literature search, reference management and source criticism. If you are first enrolled in the module, you will have access to the material throughout your study time.

You will also meet the library in your professional teaching, but in addition you can always sign up for a course.

You are always welcome to contact us in person or via the Ask Library.
