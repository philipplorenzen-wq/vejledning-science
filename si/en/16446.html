The University of Copenhagen conducts a graduate survey for your programme every 2 years.

In the survey, graduates answer questions about their programme and their first experiences in the labour market.

In the graduate survey, you can find statistics on

  • which industries the graduates are seeking employment in
  • key elements (factors) for obtaining a job
  • demanded and utilised competencies in the graduates' jobs
  • distribution of working hours across different types of tasks
  • what graduates think about the study programme.
