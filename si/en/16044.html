When am I allowed to use generative AI?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/When-am-I-allowed-to-use-generative-AI-KU.aspx

You are allowed to use generative AI to a limited extent:

  • For your projects and Master’s thesis.
  • In your written take-home assignments, if the course description states ‘All aids allowed’ under ‘Exam’.

In all other exam types, you are not allowed to use generative AI.

Read about limited extent under ‘What am I allowed to use generative AI for?’.

As a rule of thumb, it is a good idea to disable generative AI features in any programs/software you use for your exams. Ask your supervisor or course coordinator if you are in doubt.
