If you need to participate in Teams remote teaching, your teacher will share a link to Teams.

  1. When you click on the link, you will be taken to the Teams-webpage.
  2. Either tap "Join on the Web instead", or use the Teams app if you have it installed. If you do not have the app installed - read more under "App"
Web
  • If you choose web, log in with KU-username@alumni.ku.dk and password
  • Press join now.
  • Remember to mute your sound -  unless you need to contribute to the class.
App
  • If you select the Windows app, you will have to install it on your computer.
  • Click "Download the Windows app" and follow the setup instructions.
  • When the installation is complete, press "Join now".
  • Remember to mute your sound - unless you need to contribute to the class.
