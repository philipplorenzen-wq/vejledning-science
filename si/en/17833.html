Can I study at reduced hours?

Placering: Special support, Illness and parental leave › Illness › Illness or death in the family

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-study-at-reduced-hours-SCI-3.aspx

It is possible to plan an alternative course of study which allows you to study less intensely for a period. You stand a good chance of being allowed to study at reduced hours if your reason for wanting to do so is serious illness or death in the family.
