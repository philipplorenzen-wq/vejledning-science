This is a unique period in your life, and an excellent opportunity to study abroad for a semester. The University and our Department has free exchange places at top-ranked universities in Europe and dozens of countries worldwide. Follow your academic interests, practice your language skills, and experience another society and culture. The ideal time is the 2nd year, B1+B2 (your elective courses).

To study abroad requires that you apply for a place about 1 year ahead. So you must start planning in the first months of your studies, in order to go on exchange in your second year.
