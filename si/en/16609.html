You must meet three requirements for your stay to be eligible for an Erasmus+ grant:

  1. Host country: You must move to the country, where the host university is located. You cannot receive an Erasmus grant to an exchange in Denmark or in the country where you live during your studies at UCPH (for example: it you live in Sweden, you cannot receive an Erasmus grant for Erasmus exchange in Sweden or Denmark).
    You must physically live and be in the host country during the entire exchange period, if you want to receive a full Erasmus grant. If you leave the host country, before the end of the semester, or if you follow online teaching in Denmark, this will affect the amount of your Erasmus grant.
    Your Erasmus exchange must take place in an EU Member country or a country participating in the Erasmus+ programme:
    Belgium, Bulgaria, Czech Republic, Denmark, Germany, Estonia, Ireland, Greece, Spain, France, Croatia, Italy, Cyprus, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Hungary, Malta, Netherlands, Austria, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovenia, Slovakia, Finland, Sweden, North Macedonia, Serbia, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway and Turkey.
    You cannot receive an Erasmus grant for an exchange taking place in Denmark, United Kingdom, Greenland or the Faroe Islands.
    For the academic year 2023-2024 an Erasmus grant is given for Erasmus exchange at the University of Edinburgh. The amount of this Erasmus grant is expected to be smaller than Erasmus grants given to Erasmus exchange in other countries.
  1. 30 ECTS per semester: You must have courses pre-approved at the host university corresponding to a full-time semester (i.e. 30 ECTS per semester).
    If you do not get a pre-approval from UCPH for 30 ECTS per semester, you must provide a valid reason for not receiving a 30 ECTS pre-approval from UCPH. A valid reason can for example be having less than 30 ECTS left in order to complete your degree, or if you must take compulsory courses at UCPH (for example translation courses). If you do not have a valid reason, it will affect the amount of your Erasmus grant.
    If you have a pre-approval for less than 30 ECTS per semester, you must always let us know (ucphabroad@ku.dk).
  1. Duration of the exchange: The exchange must last between 2-12 months. Your total Erasmus mobility must last maximum 12 months per BA  or MA education. For example: if you have had a 6 months Erasmus study exchange during your BA education, you can only do an extra Erasmus study exchange/Erasmus Traineeship for max 6 months during your BA education.
