The Geology-Geoscience education is organized in geological topics covered by four research groups and cross-disciplinary activities. The four research groups are responsible for designing and teaching courses related to their topics. The Geology-Geoscience education consists of one compulsory course (15 ECTS), 30 ECTS restricted elective courses, 15 ECTS elective courses and one MSc thesis project (60 ECTS). 
This means that you have a large degree of freedom to combine your education to reflect your ideas/interests for Geology-Geoscience.

You can look up the description and content of the courses on the course catalogue. Also check your programme and curriculum under Planning your studies. 

Depending on your interest, I'd like to draw your attention to restricted elective courses as suggested by the research groups. Please note that an interest in the field of a specific research group by no means forces you to select this groups suggested restrictive elective courses. You can choose among all restricted elective courses. 

Water Resources 

Find the research group on Water Resources. 

If you're interested in Water Resources, we'd like to draw your attention to the following restricted elective courses:

  • Processing of seismic and georadar data
  • Climate change and water resources
  • Geological evolution based on interpretation and Integration of reflection seismic and wireline log data
  • Aqueous geochemistry
  • Integrated water resources
  • Integrated sedimentary systems analysis
  • Mineral resources   
  • Introduction to geomicrobiology
  • Marine geoscience
  • Field and methods course depending on topic
