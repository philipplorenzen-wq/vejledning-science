  • There are plenty of career opportunities when graduating, and there is virtually no involuntary unemployment among our graduates. Which courses and project topics you choose during your study might affect your subsequent job opportunities. This is why I suggest you choose according to what interests you, as this might also direct you towards an interesting job later in your career.
  • While studying, you might consider taking a student job as this might also be a way to establish valuable networks that can lead to a job once graduated. While I encourage you to take a student job, you should only do so if the job is study relevant and if it does not prevent you from studying full time.
