It is a very good idea to seek guidance if you are concerned that you may have transgressed someone's boundaries, or if you have become aware that someone at UCPH has felt harassed by you.

People who can help you:

  • The study and career guidance counsellors on your study programme are subject to a duty of confidentiality, and can discuss the situation with you and explain your options.
  • UCPH's student ambassador can provide legal guidance on your rights and options in the situation.

Read more under Seek support and guidance at UCPH

If someone lodges a complaint about you

If someone lodges a complaint about you due to harassment or offensive behaviour, you will be informed about the complaint and be given an opportunity to respond.

If the complaint is deemed to be founded, you may be given an oral or written warning. If you are found to be guilty of seriously or repeatedly violating the rules of conduct, you may be expelled from the university in extreme cases.
