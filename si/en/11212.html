The MSc programme in Nature Management consists of four elements:

  • Compulsory courses, 15 ECTS credits
  • Restricted elective courses, 45 ECTS credits
  • Elective courses
    • 30 ECTS credits (with thesis 30 ECTS credits)
    • 15 ECTS credits (with thesis 45 ECTS credits)
  • Thesis, 30 or 45 ECTS credits
The programme is two years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title:
  • Master of Science in Nature Management (Landscape, Biodiversity and Planning) / Cand.scient. i naturforvaltning (landskab, biodiversitet og planlægning)
