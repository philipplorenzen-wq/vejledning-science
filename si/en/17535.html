You have the opportunity to do a project outside the course scope at Department of Science Education. You can focus specifically on the didactics or the communication of your subject area.


It is a relevant opportunity for you, curious about what is special about natural sciences compared to other academic disciplines, and also interested in

  • teaching
  • education
  • communication
  • learning.

Examples of projects


You can, for instance, do a project, where you

  • focus specifically on concrete course progressions
  • try out new types of teaching instructions
  • examine public school students' attitudes to natural science
  • find out how museums can communicate natural sciences.


See examples of projects

Develop skills you can use in various jobs

If you do a project outside course scope at Department of Science Education, you learn to understand and use concepts and theories within

  • communication
  • philosophy of science
  • didactics
  • dissemination.


You can, for instance, use the skills to communicate your subject area to other professional target groups or to teach – regardless of whether you are considering becoming an upper secondary school teacher, assistant professor, museums teacher, academic adviser or something completely different.
