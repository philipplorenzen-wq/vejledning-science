My name is Anne Tietjen and I am your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic content of your programme in detail and have contributed to defining your curriculum. 

As a student, you have many options and a flexible framework to influence the content of your studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours.

Here are my advice and recommendations for successfully completing your master's programme in Landscape Architecture successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your decisions - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are written based on the latest programme structure unless I have specifically written otherwise.
