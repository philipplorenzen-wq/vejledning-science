Can credits be transferred for a previously passed Master’s thesis?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-credits-be-transferred-for-a-previously-passed-Master%E2%80%99s-thesis-SCI.aspx

Credits cannot be transferred for a Master's thesis done as part of a Master's programme which you have already completed.


You can only receive credit transfer for a thesis from a previous non completed degree if the two programmes have the same title.

Read the rules for your Master's thesis under Master's thesis and other projects
