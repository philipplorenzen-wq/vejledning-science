Uddannelseszoom is a website created by the Ministry of Higher Education and Science. Uddannelseszoom uses statistics from Statistics Denmark and surveys with graduates from your programme.

At Uddannelseszoom, you can find statistics about

  • how many graduates have found employment
  • graduates' routes to their first job
  • graduates' typical salary
  • in which industries graduates work
  • graduates' average working hours and typical methods of working
  • how the graduates' programme has prepared them for their professional career.

You can also use Uddannelseszoom to compare different programmes
