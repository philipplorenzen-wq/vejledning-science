If you wish to use your elective options to obtain a specific academic profile, I have made these recommended study tracks for you. The study tracks are written from the point of view of the course offerings in 2025-26 only. If you are looking further ahead, please know that the offerings may change.

If you have any questions about which courses to follow, please get in touch with me.
