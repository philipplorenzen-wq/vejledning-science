Do I need to meet the SU activity requirement if I have received additional grant portions?

Placering: SU and grants › Your SU › SU study activity requirements

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: gxd · Københavns Universitet - Kandidat · Københavns Universitet - P-Bachelor

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Do-I-need-to-meet-the-SU-activity-requirement-if-I-have-received-additional-grant-portions.aspx

Yes. You must meet the SU activity requirement even though you have received additional grant portions.

With additional grant portions you will be compensated by receiving grant portions that you have used while you were delayed on your programme and did not pass the prescribed number of ECTS. In this way, you can receive SU grant portions to prolong your education corresponding to your delay.

Example:

If you have studied for two semesters and received 12 months' of SU (corresponding to 12 grant portions) but only passed 30 ECTS (corresponding to one semester) due to illness, you can apply for six additional SU grant portions, which correspond to your delay.

Then, your total use of SU grant portions will be reduced from 12 to six grant portions, which will balance the number of received grant portions and passed ECTS.

You can read more about additional grant portions here.
