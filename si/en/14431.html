All students at UCPH automatically have a print account. To print or copy, you must have money in your print account.

At print.ku.dk, you can add funds to your account with a credit card.

You get a small discount if you print on both sides of the paper. At the same time, you are helping to reduce paper consumption and increase sustainability at UCPH.

Conditions

The conditions of trade appear on print.ku.dk when you add funds to your printing account. Here are a few important extracts:

  • Payment can be made using Dankort, Visa-Dankort, MasterCard, Maestro, Visa, Visa Electron and MobilePay. Refunds can only be made to the card that was originally used to add funds to the account.
  • Funds in your print account may be refunded - except for a fee of DKK 50. 
    Funds transferred from the old print systems (before KUprint) cannot be refunded. 

    If you have money left on your print account when you complete your studies, you can transfer it to a fellow student via print.ku.dk.
    Alternatively, you can get a refund by contacting UCPH IT via serviceportal.ku.dk.

  • The prices listed here apply from 1 September 2022.
