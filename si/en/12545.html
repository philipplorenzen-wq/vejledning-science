Stat/Act IF is an association that promotes interest in sport and physical activity and strengthens social cohesion among students studying Statistics and Actuarial Mathematics. 

Stat/Act IF regularly organises a variety of activities, such as badminton, bouldering, ice skating trips, whist tournaments and pub crawls. In addition, Stat/Act IF takes part in social events such as the start-of-semester party, Christmas lunch, Shrovetide party and cabin trips, in collaboration with the Mathematics-Economics board. 

If you have an idea for an event, would like to help plan activities, or simply wish to take part, you are always welcome to get in touch via Stat/Act IF’s Facebook group. 

Membership costs 80 kroner per year.
