The MSc programme in Quantum Information Science consists of four elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 20 ECTS credits
  2. Restricted elective courses
    • 40 ECTS credits (thesis 30 ECTS credits)
    • 25 ECTS credits (with thesis 45 ECTS credits)
  3. Elective subjects, 30 ECTS credits
  4. Thesis, 30 or 45 ECTS credits

The programme is two years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Quantum Information Science / Cand.scient. i kvanteinformationsvidenskab.
