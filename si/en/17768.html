Bioinformatics can contribute to a greener future in multiple ways, for example via research in biodiversity or protein engineering for more efficient use of resources. While we do not have dedicated courses in these areas at the moment, research projects (including MSc theses) are within these areas are available. 

Another important aspect of stustainability is the mindful use of resources. In Biological Sequence Analysis, you learn how to estimate the runtime of algorithms, which can help you make a more sustainable choice in programming and algorithm design.
