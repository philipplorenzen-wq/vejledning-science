Support can be necessary if you have a functional impairment, diagnosis, disability or neuro-divergence. 

Such impairments may cause study difficulties for which you can get help.

Below, we focus on the functional impairments, diagnoses or disabilities that entitle you to special educational support – also called SPS. 

At the same time, you can learn more about what kind of support and aids you can apply for, and the kind of documentation you need to provide in order to be granted support.
