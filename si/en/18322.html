How and when do I apply?

You apply by filling out a form. In the form you must answer these 3 questions:

  1. Why do you wish to be an MSc student ambassador?
  2. What do you find important in order to give new students a good study start?
  3. What worked well and not so well for you in your own study start and first year?

Please also upload a CV.  

Note that the application deadline has been extended; fill out the form and upload a CV to apply no later than 25 May 2026.

 Apply to become an MSc student ambassador
After you have applied

Quickly after 25 May, we will get back to you and let you know if you are invited for an interview. 

Note: The form will be inactive after 10 minutes, so please write your answers in a separate document and when you are ready, you can copy/paste it into the form.
