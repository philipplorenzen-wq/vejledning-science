How and when should I apply for special conditions in class?

To apply for special conditions in class, you must submit an application for exemption to your study board. You can expect to receive an answerwithin six weeks.

How to apply:

  1. Fill in the application for exemption.
  2. Attach an account of why you are applying for the specific special conditions in class (the actual application).
  3. Attach documentation of the functional impairment or medical condition (i.e. doctor's note, test result or similar). If relevant to the application, please attach documentation of any special educational support you have received in the past.

go to the application form 

Unfortunately, we cannot tell you in advance whether or not you will be granted exemption. It depends on your application and situation vis-à-vis the specific course or study programme. The practical and financial possibilities may differ from case to case.
