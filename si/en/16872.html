When and how do I register for courses at DTU?

You register for your compulsory and restricted elective courses at DTU at UCPH Self Service in the same periods and in the same way as for your courses at SCIENCE.

Read more under 'When do I register for courses?' and 'How do I register for courses?'.


Courses in June and July at DTU
The registration period for DTU courses in June and July is the same as for block 4 courses at SCIENCE.
