Can I receive emails from UCPH in my private email account?

Placering: IT and facilities on campus › IT and support › KUmail

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Københavns Universitet - Alle uddannelsesniveauer

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Can-I-receive-emails-from-UCPH-in-my-private-email-account-IT-KU.aspx

Yes, in your private mailbox (Gmail, Hotmail, etc.) you can add KUmail as an extra email account to get easy access to emails from UCPH. Setup-guides are in the section 'How do I setup KUmail on my device?' above.

Attention! If you are employed as a student assistant, member of a study board or undertake other trusted activities, you are not allowed to forward emails received via KUmail to your private email account as your KUmail will then be used for your work or trusted activities.
In the role as student assistant etc. you may receive information that is not allowed to leave the servers of the UCPH. When handling personal data or confidential information, you must be aware of the rules on information security and personal data protection - see below.

It you receive research data to be used in your studies, you are not allowed to forward you KUmail to external mail clients.
