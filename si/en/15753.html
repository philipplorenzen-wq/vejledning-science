Why do I need to identify my competences?

Identifying your competences will help you figure out what you are good at and what makes you attractive to a potential employer.

If you work systematically on identifying your competences, you might find you are capable of more than you think.


Once you have an overview of your competences, it is easier for you to

  • show what you have to offer to a potential employer, a project partner or a new study buddy
  • identify the areas you would like to develop and improve.

evaluate whether a course, a workplace or a project/job suits you and your competences.
