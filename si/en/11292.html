 I'm Morten Ingerslev and I'm your Head of Studies. I know the academic contents of your programme in detail, and have contributed to the curriculum. My research area is ecology in various ecosystems ranging from forests to urban ecosystems.  

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here are my advice and my recommendations for completing your master's programme in Nature Management - landscape, biodiversity and planning successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are written on the basis of the latest programme structure, unless I have specifically written otherwise.
