The MSc programme in Agriculture with specialisation in Plant Science consists of four elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 15 ECTS credits
  2. Restricted elective courses, 
    • 37.5 ECTS credits (thesis 45 ECTS)
    • 22.5 ECTS credits (thesis 60 ECTS)
  3. Elective subjects, 22.5 ECTS credits
  4. Thesis, 45 or 60 ECTS credits
