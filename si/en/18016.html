Can I change my topic along the way?

Whether or not you can change the topic, that is, the problem formulation of your thesis contract here and now depends on the extent of the change.

Ask your supervisor if the change you want to make will still fall within the topic of your thesis. If this is the case, you can adjust your problem formulation right away.

If you want to change to an entirely new topic

If you want to work on an entirely new topic that does not fall within your existing contract, you must obtain a dispensation ind order to deregister from your thesis and start over with a new contract. You will be allowed to do so only in extraordinary circumstances.

Find more information on this page 'Can I withdraw from submitting my Master's thesis?'.
