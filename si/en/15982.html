How many credits can a thesis preparation project account for?

Placering: Master's thesis and other projects › Thesis preparation project › Purpose, requirements, ECTS and timing

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Environmental Science - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-many-credits-can-a-thesis-preparation-project-account-for-PREP-7-5-SCI-ENVSCI.aspx

You may do a thesis preparation project for a total of 15 ECTS credits.

Your thesis preparation project can have two sizes:

  • 7.5 ECTS-credits (workload: 206 hours)
  • 15 ECTS-credits (workload: 412 hours).

Note, if you have chosen the Soil, Water and Biodiversity specialisation, you may only do a thesis preparation project if your are a student on the EnvEuro, 2nd year at UCPH and only for a total of 7.5 ECTS credits during you course of study.


Check if there is a limit on how many ECTS credits you can use in total for different projects

How your project can count as a part of your programme

  • Chemistry, Toxicology & Health specialisation:
    If you have chosen this specialisation a thesis preparation project can count as both restricted elective and/or elective ECTS credits.
    If you are planning a project of 15 ECTS credits you can divide it between both types of electives, so that it may look like this:
    - 7.5 elective ECTS credits
    - 7.5 restricted elective ECTS credits.
  • Soil and Water specialisation and Soil, Water and Biodiversity specialisation:
    Your thesis preparation project can only count as elective ECTS credits.
