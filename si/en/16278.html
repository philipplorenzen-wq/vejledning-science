You can apply for the most commonly used dispensations by filling in and submitting the appropriate online form. Find the form and guidance on required documentation etc. by clicking on the appropriate situation below.


Apply for a dispensation for

  • course withdrawal after the deadline for withdrawal
  • exam withdrawal after the deadline for withdrawal
  • postponing the study activity requirements
  • being granted extra exam attempts
  • extra time or permission to use special aids and support materials for exams


Dispensations not covered by specific forms

To apply for a dispensation not covered by the standard forms, you must fill in the online form: "Apply for a dispensation"

Follow the 'Guide on how to apply for dispensations'.
