What kind of support can I get?

Placering: Neurological functional impairment, concussion, etc.

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-kind-of-support-can-I-get-with-neurological-functional-impairments.aspx

As a student, you can get support in the form of, for example:
  • Clarification and study support hours at the student counselling service
  • Student mentor
  • Academic support teacher
  • Dictaphone
  • Assessment at the Centre for Rehabilitation of Brain Injury
  • Assessment at the Institute for the Blind and Partially Sighted (IBOS).
