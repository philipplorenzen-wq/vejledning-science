Grants for exchange in the UK from the autumn semester 2023 and onwards

It is possible to go on exchange in the UK from the autumn 2023 and onwards.

You will not receive an Erasmus grant, because UK does no longer participate in the Erasmus+ programme due to Brexit.

When you apply for an exchange semester in UK you will automatically apply for an Internationalisation stipend (5000 DKK per semester).

The deadline for applying for an exchange semester and the Internationalisation stipend is November 1st.
Erasmus grant for The University of Edinburgh

For the academic year 2026-2027 an Erasmus grant is given for Erasmus exchange at the University of Edinburgh. The size of this Erasmus grant is expected to be smaller that Erasmus grants given to Erasmus exchange in other countries.
