My name is Benedikte and I am your Head of Studies in Human Nutrition. 

I know the academic contents of your programme well, and I am contributing to determining your curriculum. 

My research area is child nutrition with focus on malnutrition in low-income countries. I am course responsible for Project in Practice. I teach in other courses.  

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make. Below you find my advice and recommendations for completing your master’s programme in Human Nutrition successfully. 

Read my advice as inspiration for your choices – not as rules to follow. My recommendations are based on the latest programme structure unless I have specifically written otherwise.
