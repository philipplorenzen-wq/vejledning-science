The Student Council at MATH covers all four study programmes at The Department of Mathematical Science. Their purpose is to improve the study experience for the students in all of these programmes. 
The role of The Student Council: 
  • They represent all students at MATH as a part of different councils on campus. 
  • They host a series of educational events during the year, such as a career night or student colloquiums.
