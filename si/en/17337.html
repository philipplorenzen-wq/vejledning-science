Only in few and very specific situations is it possible to have your credit transfer from previous studies reassessed.

The 2 situations where credit transfer from previous studies can be changed

  1. If your credit transfer prevents you from having a specific specialisation, you can apply to have it reassessed.
  2. If your credit transfer prevents you from having a specific minor subject, you can apply to have it reassessed.


How to apply to have your credit transfer reassessed

You apply to have your credit transfer reassessed using the link below

Please attach this when you apply:

  1. A written description of why you need to have it reassessed.
  2. The letter you have received about credits transferred from previous studies
