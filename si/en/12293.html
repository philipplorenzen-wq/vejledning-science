Please notice that there is special information about your exams at LAW and SUND.

How can I see if my course is at LAW, SUND or SCIENCE?

  • Course codes with J are at LAW, e.g. JJUA55120U
  • Course codes with S are at SUND, e.g. SASA10141U
  • Course codes with N or L are at SCIENCE, e.g. NBIA04033U
