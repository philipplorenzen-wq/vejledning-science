You can get involved and find communities in many ways – and you are probably already doing a lot without thinking about it. 


You can find inspiration in UCPH’s involvement model – with 10 specific examples of how you can get involved in activities centred around your studies. 

The model was developed by a group of students, researchers, lecturers and study and career guidance counsellors at UCPH.


Reflect on your involvement and communities  

As you look at the model, think about: 

  • Are there things you are already doing? 
  • Are there new things that could be of interest to you? 
  • Are there things you are aware of, but need to practise using in your daily life? 
  • Should you prioritise and plan your daily routine differently, in order to leave room to cultivate a new community?
