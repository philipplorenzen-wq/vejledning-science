My name is Jens Emborg and I am your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies I know the academic contents of your programme in detail, and have helped develop and design your curriculum.

I followed the same education as you, have a PhD in forest ecology and a Master degree in Mediation and Confict Resolution, lately I have studied Peace Building at Chulalongkorn University in Bangkok and become a Rotary Peace Fellow. I teach courses and supervise projects related to environmental conflict, public policy, peace building, decision making, negotation and collaboration. My research focuses on various aspects of environmental conflict - assessment, analysis, collaboration, trust/distrust and how to engage citizens, private sector and public authorities in environmental issues.

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework to influence the content and profile of your education. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here I present my advice and recommendations for completing your master's programme in Forest and Nature Management successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are based on the latest programme structure.
