When do I risk not meeting the examination prerequisites?

Whether you risk not meeting the examination prerequisites depends on the requirements for your course. In some cases, even short periods of absence can affect your ability to qualify for the exam – for example if you are absent for a test or experiment during the course.
If a group assignment is part of the examination prerequisites, each student must account for their own individual contribution in the assignment in order to meet the examination prerequisites for the course.

Find the examination prerequisites for your course in the course description


Ask your course coordinator if you are in doubt

Your course course coordinator knows all about the examination prerequisites for your course. So always talk to her or him if you are unsure whether you can meet the requirements.
