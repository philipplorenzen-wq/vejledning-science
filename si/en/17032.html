What does an individual study project consist of?

An Individual Study Project can to a very large extent, be shaped according to your interests.

Your project must include this:

  1. Your independent work on a selected methodological/theoretical topic.  
  2. Your academic reflections on and communication of an academic problem/issue.
  3. A written or oral exam – or both.

You agree on the exam type with your supervisor.
