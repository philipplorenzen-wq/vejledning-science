When the study environment survey is running, you can take it by clicking on the link you will receive in your UCPH email.

The link is personal, and if you forward it to others they can overwrite your response. Completing the questionnaire on your computer is the easiest way.

It takes about 15 minutes to complete the study environment survey.

Your response will be treated confidentially, and your teachers cannot see it.
