Can I postpone submitting my thesis report?

You can apply for a dispensation to postpone the submission of your thesis, if you can document that extraordinary circumstances prevent you from submitting in time.

Extraordinary circumstances that can delay the submission of a thesis: 

  • You fall seriously ill.
  • Serious illness or death in your immediate family.
  • Problems at the company you are cooperating with.

 
Reasons that doesn't count as extraordinary circumstances:

  • Lack of results.
  • Technical problems.
  • Problems with your motivation or planning.

If you want to postpone submission due to extraordinary circumstances

Apply as soon as possible to have your submission postponed, if you are experiencing one of the extraordinary circumstances mentioned or similar circumstances.

You can only postpone submission if you have applied for – and been granted – the postponement.

See how to apply here on this page under 'How do I apply to postpone my submission?'.
