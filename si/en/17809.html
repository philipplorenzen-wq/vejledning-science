If you are interested in getting a job in Denmark after your graduation, I recommend non-Danish speaking to start learning and practicing the Danish language. You can easily study at the university and live in Denmark without speaking Danish. However, getting a job and having a career with Danish authorities or a Danish company is a different thing and often require that you speak Danish.

If you go for a PhD, I recommend that you try to get your thesis work published as a scientific paper.
