When you devote time to considering and working with your decisions: 

  • you improve your chances of making the right choice 
  • you can tell yourself that you have done everything you could – this is a comfort if the outcome is not what you hoped for. 

Use the form here to reflect on your decision and express in your own words the considerations and points that will ultimately lead to your decision.
