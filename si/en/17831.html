Can I annul or change my pre-approved course?

Placering: Courses and teaching › Registration and withdrawal › Pre-approval

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/can%20I%20annul%20or%20change%20my%20pre-approved%20course-SCI.aspx

You need to annul your pre-approval if you e.g. 

  • did not get a place on your course
  • have applied for more courses than you want to take.

Your pre-approved course will take up ECTS credits in your study programme and prevent you from using them for other courses or projects untill you annul it.

You must change your pre-approval if you need to 

  • add more courses to your pre-approval
  • replace a course applied for with another one during the same period.

Deadlines for annuling or changing your pre-approved courses

When it is possible to annul a pre-approved course or project, depends on where it is offered.


The place you want to take the course or project

Here you can apply for annulment/changes

A UCPH faculty

Untill the deadline for late-registration.

See the deadline for late-registration under Course registration

Other universities in Denmark

As long as you have not passed the course

Universities abroad As long as you have not passed the course

Apply to annul or change your pre-approval

If you have passed other or fewer courses outside UCPH than you got pre-approved

You should not annul or change your pre-approval if you have already taken other courses or fewer courses at another university than are covered by your pre-approval.

Instead, point out the change in your credit transfer application.
