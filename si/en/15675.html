Your project contract must be drawn up, signed and submitted at the latest at the end of the second week of the block in which you want to start working on the project.


The contract is important to ensure that, right from the outset, you have a

  1. clear agreement with your supervisor about your cooperation
  2. project definition and delimitation from which to work. 


How to draw up the agreement/contract

SCIENCE has a standardised contract for the MSc Research Project for Exchange or Guest Students that we kindly ask students and supervisors to use at all times.


The contract must be signed by

  • you
  • your primary supervisor
  • Head of Department.


You and your supervisor must read the rules for MSc Research Projects carefully before filling in the contract.

When the agreement/contract has been completed, your main supervisor should send it to incomingmobility@adm.ku.dk.  


Fill out the contract


What you need to know to fill out the contract:

  • Submission date for the project report.
  • Focus of the project.
  • Scope and structure of your supervision.
