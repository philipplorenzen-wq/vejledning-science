How do I print/copy/scan?

You can print, copy and scan on all KUprinters on UCPH from your computer, smartphone or tablet with UCPH Mobilityprint or UCPH WebPrint.

When at a KUprinter, you log on by swiping your student card. 

  • Print: Select Print All or Print release to print either all documents or individual documents.
  • Copy: Find the copy funktion under Device functions
  • Scan: Choose Scan in the printer's menu and scan the documents to your KUmail. 

Remember to log out after use.

To scan is free, but you can only print and copy when you have money in your print account. Read more below.

You can only use MobilityPrint when you are logged on to the wireless network eduroam or wired UCPH-network and have completed the installation (see how below). 

WebPrint works everywhere you have network conncetion. With MobilityPrint, you get more options when printing, eg number of pages.

MobilityPrint

UCPH guides on how to install MobilityPrint, choose your OS: 

  • Windows
  • macOS 
  • iOS iPhone/iPad
  • Android

WebPrint

If you do not have access to eduroam, wired UCPH network or another OS, print with KU WebPrint:

  1. Log on to https://webprint.ku.dk
  2. Select Web Print in the menu
  3. Send your document to the server

Pick it up from a KUprinter.
