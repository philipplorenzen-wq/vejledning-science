Can I apply for re-admission on the study programme later?

You may be re-admitted on the study programme and avoid having to apply for admission all over again if

  1. there are vacancies on the study programme
  2. you meet the current admission requirements.


You can only apply for re-admission

  1. for the summer study start on 1 September 
  2. when at least 5 months have passed from the date of your withdrawal – if you withdrew from your study programme yourself.

For you to be re-admitted, there must be vacancies on the study programme.


Conditions for re-admission

In order to be re-admitted, you must have passed all courses in the 1st year of study (60 ECTS credits) under the curriculum applicable when you wish to return (may be other courses).
Read how to be re-admitted on your study programme at:
www.science.ku.dk  > Degree programmes > Choose your study programme > Application procedure > Are you studying or have you studied a Master's degree programme? > Re-admission

If you do not meet the applicable admission requirements, you must apply for a dispensation when applying for re-admission.


If you cannot be re-admitted

If you have not passed the required 60 ECTS credits, you must apply for admission on ordinary terms.
