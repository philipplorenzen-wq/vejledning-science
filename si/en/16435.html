Enrolled in September 2020 or later

Integrated Food Studies (IFS) is structured in a way where you get all your compulsory courses in the first year of your programme. 

In the second year of your programme you can plan more freely what you are interested in - writing projects in collaboration with organizations or companies (Project in Practice), writing your project (Project outside the course scope ), taking elective courses at UCPH or elsewhere, or travelling abroad to study. You can also do a prolonged Master's thesis (45 ECTS) or a Thesis preparation project.
