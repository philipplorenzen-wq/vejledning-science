Be ready to use at least four months to find the right company or organisation. It could be any type of company, organisation or other professional setting that does not involve teaching or research at a university. 

You will often have to ask several companies before one of them agrees to work with you.

Get tips on finding a company or organisation to do your project with

If you do not find a company

If you are registered for the project outside the course scope but do not find a company, you can withdraw before the deadline for the block in which you intended to start the project.

If you miss that deadline, you may be able to apply for a dispensation to withdraw from the project.

Withdraw via the form ‘Get study administrative help’.

Check the deadlines for both registration and withdrawal under ‘What is the deadline for sending the contract for approval?’
