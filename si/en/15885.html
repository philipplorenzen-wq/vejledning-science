You must yourself apply for a place directly at the foreign university if you want to take a summer course at a university with which UCPH does not have cooperation.

Find information about the application process, deadline for applications, forms, language test requirements and prices either

  • on the university's website or
  • by contacting the university directly by email or telephone.

Five things to remember:

  • Check the foreign university's application process, the period of the course, price, etc.
  • Fill in the application form and send your application.
  • Apply for pre-approval if your stay is to be included in your study programme at UCPH.
  • Sort out insurance, travel safety, accommodation, etc.
  • Apply for relevant grants, study-abroad scholarships and to private foundations, etc.
