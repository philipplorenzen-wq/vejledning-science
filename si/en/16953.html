You can withdraw from exams yourself on Self Service if you do so before the deadline for withdrawing.

How to withdraw from exams


Your options after the deadline for withdrawing

If you fall ill for an exam, you must submit a notification of illness as quickly as possible

  • immediately before the day of the exam (about a week before)
  • on the actual day of the exam
  • during a written take-home assignment
  • during a course with ongoing tests, preventing you from passing the course
  • during a course with specific examination prerequisites, preventing you from being allowed to take the exam.

How to submit a notification of illness in connection with an exam

You must apply for a dispensation to be withdrawn from the exam if

  1. there is still some time before your exam (about a couple of weeks)
  2. your course concludes with an 'ordinary' exam (without specific examination prerequisites or ongoing tests).
