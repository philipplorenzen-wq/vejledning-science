When am I delayed?

As a general rule, you are delayed if you have passed less than the standard 30 ECTS in a semester.
Please note! You must have received SU in the period in which you have been delayed in order to be entitled to extra grant portions.
Example: You have been delayed a semester if you have not passed any exams and have received SU.
Read more about being delayed on www.su.dk.
