To study abroad is an option but it is more complicated on the Dairy specialisation, because you will have to find equivalent courses to your compulsory courses at the visiting University. I suggest that you investigate if you can do part of your thesis or your whole thesis abroad because that is one of the few possibilities within these specialisations.

On the other hand, there are several options if you want to go abroad on the general specialisation and Plant Based Foods. The suggested window is block 3+4 of the first year for the General specialisation, where you only have one compulsory course in Food Processing. So, either you have to find an equivalent course at the visiting university, or postpone this course to the second year, and follow this alongside your thesis. As mentioned above, the suggested moobility wondow is block 1+2 of the second year, if you follow the Plant Based Foods, as this semester does not have any compulsory courses.
