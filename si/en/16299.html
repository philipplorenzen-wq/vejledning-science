If your pre-approved course is at a different university that UCPH, you will still need to complete and submit an application for credit transfer when you failed your pre-approved course.

Check when you are obliged to pass a pre-approved course under Pre-approval


Notifying the university that you have failed a pre-approved course:

  1. Get documentation of your failed course.
  2. Fill in an application for credit transfer and upload your documentation via 'Notify university of failed course'. 
  3. In the comments box of the form, note that you failed the course.

Notify university of failed course

Deadlines for applying for credit transfer

You must submit your application for credit transfer within a certain deadline.

The deadline for submitting your application for credit transfer is:

  • For courses taken in blocks 1 + 2: by 1 March.
  • For courses taken in blocks 3 + 4 + 5: by 1 September.
