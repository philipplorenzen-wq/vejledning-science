Your oral exam must be placed in the exam period of the last block of your project. The specific date must be agreed with your supervisor.

See the exam and re-exam weeks for this academic year

Your supervisor will publish the time and place and find an internal examiner.


Length and format of your exam

  • Your supervisor determines the length of your exam. Project exams last 30-60 minutes.
  • You must give a 10 to 20-minute presentation at the start of the exam.
  • No preparation time is allowed for your oral exam.
  • Your exam is public. The doors may be closed for some of the exam if it involves confidential information from a company, for example.
  • You can choose to do the oral exam online, if you and your supervisor agree on it.
