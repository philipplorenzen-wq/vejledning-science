Depending on what passport you hold, it may be necessary to obtain a visa for living in certain countries. Be aware that visa rules and prices can vary from country to country. Expenses incurred during the visa process are not covered by UCPH and must be paid by you.
If relevant, you will get information visas and immigration rules when you receive your admission from the host university. Therefore, it is important that you read the admission letter thoroughly as soon as you receive it. You are also encouraged to research relevant immigration matters yourself – for example visa requirements, entry rules and costs – via the host country’s embassy, host university website, or other official sources.
