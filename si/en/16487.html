How to submit a notification of illness at a written exam

  1. Summon one of the proctors and explain that you are ill.
  2. Never hand in any unfinished written assignments when leaving the exam. It will count as an exam attempt If you hand in anything.
  3. Contact your doctor immediately to obtain documentation of your illness, which covers the day of the exam. Find the requirements for the documentation on this page.
  4. Submit a notification of illness by filling out an online form via the button below and uploading your documentation no later than 8 calendar days from and including the exam date.
    If your documentation isn’t ready by the deadline, you’ll need to upload a document instead, stating when you expect to be able to submit it. 
Notify of illness How to submit a notification of illness at an oral exam
  1. Tell the examiner that you are ill before the grading starts. Afterwards, it is too late.
  2. Contact your doctor immediately to obtain documentation of your illness which covers the day of the exam. Find the requirements for the documentation on this page.
  3. Submit a notification of illness as soon as possible by filling out an online form via the button below and uploading your documentation no later than 8 calendar days from and including the exam date.
    If your documentation isn’t ready by the deadline, you’ll need to upload a document instead, stating when you expect to be able to submit it. 
Notify of illness
Answer to your notification of illness 
If your notification of illness is approved, you will avoid using an exam attempt. You will usually receive a reply within 6 weeks.
Register for the exam again
You must register for the exam again yourself, since this does not automatically happen. Find out how to do so under Exam registration
