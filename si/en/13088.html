The university chaplains are available for personal conversations, which can be about different aspects of life. You can, for example, contact the student chaplains about: grief, anxiety, burnout, feelings of inadequacy, anger, loneliness, suicide, belief, identity, relationships etc. 

The university chaplains are under an obligation to maintain confidentiality. No records are kept, it is possible to contact the chaplains anonymously without providing a name, and all enquiries are free of charge.
