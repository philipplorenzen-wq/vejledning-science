Procedure for Special Educational Support (SPS) 

  1. SPS interview at UCPH
    Start by booking an interview with a SPS-counsellor at the University of Copenhagen. You can do this as soon you are offered admission at UCPH We will help you get started with support.
    Remember to prepare for the interview by reading everything on the page about psychological functional impairments. Then we will talk about what is important for you and your needs.
  2. Message in your e-Boks after 3-6 weeks
    Your SPS-counsellor at UCPH applies for the support and/or aids that you have discussed during the interview.
    3-6 weeks later, you will be notified via e-Boks when your application has been processed.
    The SPS-counsellor at UCPH sends the application to the Student Counselling Service (‘Studenterrådgivningen’ in Danish).
  3. The Student Counselling Service contacts you.The Student Counselling Service will contact you within 10 days to arrange for an interview, which is known as a Assessment interview, ‘afklaringssamtale’ in Danish.
  4. Assessment interview at the Student Counselling ServiceYou will talk to a counsellor in the Student Counselling Service, and together you will identify what is the right support for you.
  5. UCPH applies for the recommended support for youThe Student Counselling Service makes a report with recommendations for your support.
    The SPS-counsellor at UCPH applies for the recommended support.
  6. Notification in your e-Boks3-6 weeks later, you will be notified in your e-Boks when your application has been processed.
