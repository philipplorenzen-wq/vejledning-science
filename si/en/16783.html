How do I apply for exchange?

You apply for an exchange under one of UCPH's exchange agreements through the Mobilty Online application system.

You should preferably select up to five universities that you are interested in when applying for an exchange place. With several options, you increase your chances of getting a place.

Please note that the application process takes place in two steps – first in a registration form and then in an application portal where you prepare the actual application.

  1. Click 'Apply here' under this guide.
  2. Complete the registration form you arrive at in one go and submit it. You cannot save your details while filling in the form, but you may subsequently edit it in the application portal.
  3. Follow the link you receive in your KUmail when you have submitted the registration form. You will now arrive in the application portal.
  4. Complete your application in the application portal.  Here, you must, among other information, fill in a 'personal statement' (see below), personal details (address etc.) and wishes for courses at all your prioritised universities. You can complete your application in several stages until you submit it.
  5. Upload a transcript of your grades to your application in the application portal. You can download the transcript of your grades as a PDF on 
    SelfService > Enrolment, Courses and Exams > Transcripts.
  6. Send all information via the application portal before the deadline.

You may write the application in Danish.

How to prepare a personal statement

In your personal statement, you must provide your academic grounds for your choice of university and courses in relation to your overall study programme and profile.

You are not bound to take the courses you state in your application.
You only need to prepare a personal statement for your first priority. You do not have to prepare any for your other priorities.
