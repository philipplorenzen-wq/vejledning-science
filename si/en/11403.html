While pregnant, you may experience discomfort or illness that may affect your ability to study to varying degrees. There are several ways in which your studies can be planned to take account of your situation.


How to plan your studies to accommodate your condition:

  • Study at reduced hours within the framework of your study programme (with SU)
  • Apply for leave of absence due to illness (without SU)

Apply for exemptions for e.g. special exam conditions, withdrawal after the deadline.
