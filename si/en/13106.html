What is a neurological functional impairment?

Placering: Neurological functional impairment, concussion, etc.

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: gxd · Københavns Universitet - Kandidat · Københavns Universitet - P-Bachelor · kff

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-is-a-neurological-functional-impairment.aspx

A neurological impairment is a functional impairment that relates to the central nervous system and may be caused by disturbances in the structure and function of the nervous system.
These include:
  • Concussion (post-concussive syndrome)
  • Brain hemorrhage and stroke
  • Migraine and other forms of headache
  • Neuritis
  • Cerebral palsy
  • Parkinson’s disease
  • Epilepsy
  • Brain tumors
  • Sclerosis
  • Certain pains and disorders of the musculoskeletal system.

ASF /ADHD:  If you wish to apply for support because of neurological functional impairments such as autisme or ADHD please read under the section Mental impairments, AFS, ADHD.
