Do I need to bring ID to the exam?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Do-I-need-to-bring-ID-to-the-exam-SCI1.aspx

You must bring the following to all your written exams:

  1. A valid form of photo ID, i.e. your UCPH student identity card with photo, the ID app AltID, driver's licence, passport, national ID card (issued by Borger.dk) or a residence card issued on basis of your residence permit.
  2. Your exam number, which must be stated on all your written exam papers.

Find your exam number on Self Service > Exam schedule, time and place
Without a valid ID, you will not be allowed to take the exam. You will be registered for 'non-attendance' and use up 1 exam attempt.
