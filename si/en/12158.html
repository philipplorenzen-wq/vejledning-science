The MSc programme in Computer Science with a minor subject consists of three elements:

  1. Major subjects, 45 ECTS credits
  2. Minor subjects,
    45 ECTS credits inside SCIENCE* or 75 ECTS credits outside SCIENCE
  3. Thesis, 30 ECTS credits

*Sport Science is 75 ETCS credits

The programme is two or two and a half years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 or 150 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Computer Science with a minor subject in [the minor subject] / Cand. scient. i datalogi med sidefag i [sidefagets navn]
