When you have passed a pre-approved course at another university than UCPH, you must apply for a credit transfer to make it an official part of your study programme.

Make sure you meet the application deadline to avoid problems in relation to your study activity requirements and your SU grants.

If you have passed a pre-approved course at UCPH it will automatically be transferred to your programme at SCIENCE.
