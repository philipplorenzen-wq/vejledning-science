You are allowed to use generative AI to a limited extent for your

  • projects
  • written take-home assignments, if the course description under ‘Exam’ states ‘All aids allowed’.
Limited extent means there are defined guidelines on how you may use generative AI.

When use of generative AI is allowed to a limited extent, you must always include an AI declaration – even if you have not used generative AI.
If you forget to attach an AI declaration, there is a risk that your exam will not be assessed.
