I am Hans Henrik and I am the Head of Studies for the MSc programme in Biology. In that capacity, I coordinate the academic contents of the studies and, together with the teachers, I make sure your get the best possible learning outcomes and competencies. My own research area is community ecology and biodiversity conservation. At the MSc level, I teach in the courses Advanced Ecology,  Macroecology and Community Ecology and Advanced Plant Identification. I also supervise MSc thesis students working on biodiversity, conservation and restoration.

Below you find advice and recommendations for completing your MSc programme in Biology successfully. Read the advice as an inspiration for your own choices.
