The re-exam may take a different form from the ordinary exam which takes place just after the course.


The re-exam may take a different form if

  • you take your exam in the re-exam period
  • you do not take your exam until a later academic year after your first attempt, and the exam form has changed in the meantime
  • there are few registrations for the exam – in that case, you will be notified directly via KUmail no later than 7 days after the registration deadline.

In the course description, you can read about what applies to your specific exam.
