As a new student, you must attend at least one exam in your first exam period.

If you do not, you will not be considered an active student, and your SU will be discontinued in either May or November, depending on when the SU activity check takes place.
Your SU can only be resumed once you have participated in an exam – and the study start test (studiestartsprøven) does not count as exam participation.Remember to contact the SU Office  as soon as you have participated in an exam.

Documentation if you have received a consultation letter

If you receive a consultation letter regarding lack of study activity, you must submit documentation showing that you are actively studying. This could be:
  • Documentation of exam participation
  • Pre-approval and admission letter for a study abroad stay
  • Transcript from the host university + pre-approval if you have taken courses at another university during your 1st semester
  • Documentation of a completed academic internship from your 1st semester
  • Completed “Schein” if you were on exchange in Germany during your 1st semester
  • Documentation of an application for credit transfer if you were on exchange during your 1st semester
