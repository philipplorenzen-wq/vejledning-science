At UCPH Lighthouse you can:

  • Be part of an innovation environment with other students, researchers and entrepreneurs.
  • Enjoy shared office space and meeting facilities at two UCPH locations.
  • Get advice from business developers, industry experts and other relevant advisors.
  • Network with other students.
  • Meet with an innovation consultant from your faculty. Learn how to earn ECTS credits for participating in innovation processes.
  • Attend courses, workshops and events about innovation models, entrepreneurship and idea generation.
  • Apply your academic expertise in a commercial context.
  • Be creative and innovative in an environment where you can grow your network and skills.
  • Acquire entrepreneurial and innovation skills and methods that can prepare you for the future labour market – regardless of whether you start your own business.

UCPH Lighthouse also hosts events that can be useful for you. You can see the events here.
