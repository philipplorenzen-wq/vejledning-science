You can get help from KU-IT if you need technical support.

If you have questions or problems during the exam concerning:

  1. Entering the exam at the scheduled time:
    • Contact Support on 3532 3232 from 7:30 to 16:00 every weekday.
    • State which platform you are trying to access (Zoom, Adobe Connect, MS Teams) and describe the issue in as much detail as possible, including any error messages and what you did just before the error occurred.
       
  2. If you are not moved from the waiting room within a reasonable time:
    You will be placed in a waiting room (main lobby) until the examiner is ready to admit you to the exam. If you are still in the lobby after a reasonable time, contact the exam administration.
     
  3. You are thrown off during the exam or the meeting suddenly shuts down and the platform can't be reopened:
    • Start by checking Driftsinfo on the front page of KUnet or www.it.ku.dk.
    • If there are no disruptions associated with your issue, please contact Support on 3532 3232 between 7:30 and 16:00 every weekday.
    • Please remember to report to the exam administration in accordance with the guidelines.
    • Describe the issue in as much detail as possible, including any error messages and what happened just before you were thrown off and what you have done to get back in.
  4. Poor sound or image quality during the exam:
    You should immediately alert the examiner to the problem, including whether you know what has caused it, if it is your device or internet connection that cause the poor sound or image quality. Do not shut down your device or connection without prior agreement with the examiner.

Check for operational issues at KUnet

You may want to check if there are any operational issues listed on ‘IT driftsinfo’ (in Danish) on the front page of KUnet or on www.it.ku.dk.

Support can help you with general IT functionality on the platforms, general help with installation and setup and with error messages. When you are using your own PC, IT-support will help you the best they can, but cannot answer everything. Please help the support by having as much information as possible ready, especially the exact error message you have received.
