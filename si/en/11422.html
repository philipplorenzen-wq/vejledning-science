As most students know, it's only natural to feel a bit nervous about your exams. In fact, moderate nervousness can help improve your performance.

However, if your exam-related nervousness starts to affect your general well-being, it may tip over into more serious stress and anxiety.

Fortunately, help and tools are available to help you deal with both nervousness and anxiety in connection with exams.
