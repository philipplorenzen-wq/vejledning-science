Gælder for målgrupperne: Agricultural Economics - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Your-options-after-graduating-SCI-AGECON.aspx

Graduates from the Agricultural Economics programme are in high demand, with basically no unemployment. You may, as an example, be employed at:

  • Banks and financial institutions or international organisations such as the EU and OECD
  • Ministries (e.g. the Danish Ministry of Food, Agriculture and Fisheries and the Ministry of the Environment) and government agencies (e.g. the Danish Environmental Protection Agency), where you can contribute to establishing the basis for the policy in the food and environmental area
  • Central agricultural organisations and companies
  • Economic consultancies and advisory services

Or what about becoming a researcher or a lecturer in (agricultural) economics?
