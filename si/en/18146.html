Your Tax Identification Number (TIN) is personal. You can find it by contacting the tax authorities in the country where you have your official address. TIN can also typically be found on official documents such as tax statements, ID cards, or letters from tax authorities/agencies in the country where you have your official address.

You can find more information on how to find your TIN on the following pages:

If you live in an EU/EEA country: https://ec.europa.eu/taxation_customs/tin/#/tin-country

If you live in a non-EU/EEA country:

https://www.oecd.org/tax/automatic-exchange/crs-implementation-and-assistance/tax-identification-numbers/
