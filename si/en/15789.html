The MSc programme in Biochemistry with a specialisation in Molecular Cell Biology and Immunology consists of four elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 22.5 ECTS credits
  2. Restricted elective courses, 22.5 ECTS credits
  3. Elective subjects, 15 ECTS credits
  4. Thesis, 60 ECTS credits
