  1. Book an appointment

    Start by making an appointment with UCPH Study Guidance.
    Book an appointment via this page.
    Remember to prepare for the interview by obtaining documentation from your doctor for your hearing or visual impairment. 
  2. Message in your e-Boks after 3-6 weeks

    Your student counsellor at UCPH applies for an assessment and the support and/or aids that you have discussed during the interview. 
    3-6 weeks later, you will be notified via e-Boks when your application has been processed.
    Your application will be send to Tonax or IBOS - the Institute for the Blind and Partially Sighted. 
  3. Then (perhaps) assessment

    Tonax or IBOS will contact you within 10 days to arrange for an interview and assessment, if relevant. We might recommend more assessments.
  4. You receive a report
    Tonax and/or IBOS prepare a report with descriptions of recommended aids and support.
    The report can be used as documentation for applications for dispensation and/or additional support. 
  5. UCPH applies for support
    UCPH applies for the recommended support. 
  6. Notification in your e-Boks

    You will be notified in your e-Boks when your application has been processed. This typically takes 3-6 weeks. 
  7. Now your aids are ready
    You must pick up the aids yourself at Njalsgade 72, 7. sal (vest), 2300 København K.
    Please note that when using a dictaphone, you must obtain permission from the lecturer and your fellow students before you can use it in classes.
