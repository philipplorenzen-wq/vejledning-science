In case of serious illness or death in the family, it is possible to plan your studies to accommodate your particular needs.


Depending on your situation, you can

  • withdraw from exams
  • withdraw from courses
  • study at reduced hours for a period
  • take leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances (without SU).
