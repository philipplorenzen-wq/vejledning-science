Typically, the CV is the first impression of you as a potential candidate.

Your CV should provide a potential employer/project collaboration partner with a quick overview of your experience and skills relevant to the current job.

Therefore, always make sure to

  • tailor and target your CV to the specific job/project from the very first line
  • hand-pick what is relevant to highlight for each position
  • emphasise the skills you have gained from previous positions and education, rather than just listing tasks and courses.
