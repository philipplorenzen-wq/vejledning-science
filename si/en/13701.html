The event is divided into three activities:

1. Meet your future fellow students and tutors

The day will start with meeting your tutors and future fellow students. 
See when and where your programme will be meeting (In Danish)

2. Matriculation Ceremony at 15:00 at Frue Plads (the gate opens at 14:00)

The Rector will officially welcome you to the University of Copenhagen at Frue Plads. In addition, the chairman of the Student Council will be giving a speech, and, according to tradition, you can shake hands with the Rector.

If your class don’t meet up before the event, you are welcome to come directly to Frue Plads. Your tutors will meet you there after the matriculation ceremony and guide you to the welcome party at North Campus.

3. Welcome party at North Campus (16:00 to 22:00)

When you have shaken hands with the Rector, seen the University’s ceremonial hall and heard the speeches at Frue Plads, you, your fellow students and tutors will go to North Campus. 

Here, you can hear musical performances by the artists Brimheim and Kalaset, visit stalls with representatives from different associations and enjoy delicious food and drinks at student-friendly prices. The party ends at 22:00.

We look forward to welcoming you.
