If I’m ill at the time of the exam, will I use one exam attempt?

If you fall ill in connection with your exam, you can in some cases avoid using an exam attempt by obtaining a medical certificate as soon as possible and by either

  • submitting a notification of illness
  • applying for dispensation to withdraw from the exam.

What you need to do depends on whether you are ill on the day of the exam or whether you are prevented from taking the exam due to illness during the course.
