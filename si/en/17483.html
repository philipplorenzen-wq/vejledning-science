If your anxiety affects your exam performance and general well-being, you may need help to deal with it.

The Student Counselling Service

The Student Counselling Service has several offers for students who suffer from exam-related anxiety or who are facing other study-related challenges.


The Student Counselling Service offers

  • telephone counselling
  • individual appointments
  • group sessions
  • workshops.

Read more about the Student Counselling Service's various offers

Study and Career Guidance at ÚCPH Study Guidance

A study and career guidance counsellor from UCPH Study Guidance can give you:

  • advice on how you can handle your nervousness
  • advice on how to get help if your nervousness about exams has developed into exam anxiety
  • help with applying for special exam conditions if your anxiety affects your ability to take exams
  • help with planning your time when preparing for the exam so you feel calmer and more confident.

Contact a study and career guidance counsellor from UCPH Study Guidance


Your own doctor

It may be relevant to talk to your own doctor if you

  • need more help than Study and Career Guidance and/or the Student Counselling Service can offer
  • also experience anxiety outside the exam situation and it's a problem both in relation to your studies and in your personal life
  • need a medical certificate to document your anxiety – for example if you need to apply for special exam conditions.
