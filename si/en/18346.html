European Horizons at UCPH is part of the international student-led European Horizons umbrella organisation, and works to give young people a stronger voice in shaping Europe's future. 

The association is not affiliated with any political parties, but is a meeting place for diverse opinions.

European Horizons:

  • hosts events
  • does research
  • writes articles.

As a student at UCPH, you can become a member of the association.
The only requirements are that you:

  • are interested in Europe
  • are willing to contribute to the association's activities.
