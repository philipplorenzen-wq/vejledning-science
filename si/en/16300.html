How the Study Board will process your application for pre-approval or credit transfer

Placering: Planning your studies › Rules and dispensations › Processing of your application

Sprog: English · Elementtype: Accordion (narrow format)

Gælder for målgrupperne: Det Natur- og Biovidenskabelige Fakultet - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-the-Study-Board-will-process-your-application-for-pre-approval-or-credit-transfer-SCI.aspx

  1. You apply for pre-approval or credit transfer
    Fill in the form and press ‘Send’.
  2. We check if anything is missing
    We will check if you have completed and attached what we need. If anything is missing, we will contact you.
  3. We will check if there is room for the courses in your study programme
    We check for any available ECTS credits in your course of study and if the courses you have applied for are on an academic level (Bachelor/Master), which can enter your study programme. The points will be converted into ECTS credits if you have taken courses abroad.
  4. If you have applied for elective courses
    If everything is as it should be, we register your application for pre-approval or credit transfer. You can go directly to step 6.
  5. If you have applied for compulsory or elective courses
    We forward your application to your Head of studies or to the Study Board’s chairmanship, which will assess whether the courses you have applied for can replace the courses that are usually a part of your study programme. If so, we register your pre-approval or credit transfer.
  6. You’ll get a mail
    You will receive an answer to your application for pre-approval or credit transfer on your KUmail.
