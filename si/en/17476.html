What does a credit transfer mean to my course of study?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/What-does-a-credit-transfer-mean-to-my-course-of-study-SCI.aspx

Once you have been granted a credit transfer, your course of study will always be different from the recommended course of study because there are courses you will no longer be taking.


Prepare your own study plan

Get an overview of your course of study by preparing your own personal study plan.

You could base your study plan on the digital study plan template in which the recommended structure of your study programme has been entered, which you will find under Planning your studies and your year of admission.


If you have been granted more than 15 ECTS credits as credits transferred from previous studies, your Study Board will have enclosed an alternative study plan in the email you received about credits transferred from previous studies.
