A written assignment may only be assessed once. You may not submit the same assignment – or parts thereof – several times.

You are allowed to cite from or reproduce your own previous assignments if you:

  1. cite yourself as you would any other source (for example title and author)
  2. clearly mark quotations. 

If in doubt, your teacher or head of studies can advise you on when and how to make citations.
