DIKU's Academic Counsil represents all students at the department. The student council supports student life, conducts student politics on behalf of the students, and serves as a forum where all DIKU students can easily engage both academically and politically.

DIKU's Academic Counsil acts as a link between the students and the administration. We typically have representatives in various bodies such as the Study Board, the Teaching Committee, and SCIENCErådet.

We regularly hold student council meetings, which are announced in our DI'Kalender and on Facebook. All DIKU students are very welcome to attend these meetings, where there is always free food and a no-obligation opportunity to help improve the programmes and the study environment at DIKU.

Do not hesitate to contact us if you have suggestions or wishes regarding the study environment at DIKU. You can find our contact information as well as an easily accessible overview of all events at DIKU in our DI'Kalender on our website.
