I'm Yongluan Zhou and I'm your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic content of your programme in detail and have contributed to determining your curriculum. My research area is Data Management and Distributed Systems.

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make.

Here are my advice and my recommendations for completing your master's programme in Computer Science successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.

My recommendations are written based on the latest programme structure unless I have specifically written otherwise.
