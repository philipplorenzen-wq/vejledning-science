If you detect faults and/or defects on or in the buildings of the University of Copenhagen, you must report it. Below, you will find information about how to report faults and defects.
Faults and defects include:
  • Dripping faucets
  • Missing light
  • Loose doorsteps.
If you have discovered a fault and/or a defect in buildings belonging to the University of Copenhagen, you must report it using the link below. 
Describe the problem in as much detail as possible and, if possible, attach a photo – it saves time and money.
