For the Department to support an activity, the following must always be met:

  • General rules and guidelines for financial transactions at UCPH must be observed.
  • The activity must comply with the Code of Conduct at the Department of Mathematical Sciences.
  • The activity must not be of a religious or party-political nature.
  • Catering must comply with the Catering policy at the Department of Mathematical Sciences.
  • Support cannot be given for alcohol, tobacco, and gifts (including gift cards/vouchers). However, wine gifts for presenters are accepted.
  • Support cannot be given for deposits or coverage of reduced repayment of deposits.
  • Funding cannot be given to cover broken equipment, alarm activation or additional cleaning costs.
