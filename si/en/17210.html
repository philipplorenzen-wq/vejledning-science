How do I document that I need special conditions?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/How-do-I-document-that-I-need-special-conditions-SCI.aspx

If you need to show documentation to the course responsible or the institute, you can, e.g., 

  • show the reply to your dispensation application if you have already applied for special exam conditions
  • ask the course responsible to contact the Exam Team in SCIENCE Study Administration to get confirmation of your need for special conditions.
