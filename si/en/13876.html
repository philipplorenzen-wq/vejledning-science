The reports conclude that there is room for improvement in several areas when it comes to inclusion. Why is that the case at UCPH?

In its analysis of the inclusion survey’s responses, the external data processor, ConnectingCultures, has had a deliberate focus on inclusion challenges. Many other educational institutions and study environments deal with the same challenges as presented in the result reports, and these challenges are thus not solely present at nor specific to UCPH.
The reports identify tendencies and possible measures in relation to inclusion efforts at UCPH. Many initiatives and measures in this area are already in place, for example in counselling services and special educational support (SPS). New initiatives will be launched based on insights from the inclusion survey and in connection with the University’s strategic framework for diversity, equity and inclusion.
