The MSc programme in Actuarial Mathematics consists of four elements:

  1. Compulsory courses, 45 ECTS credits
  2. Resticted elective courses, 15 ECTS credits
  3. Elective subjects, 30 ECTS credits
  4. Thesis, 30 ECTS credits

The programme is two years if you study full-time (60 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Actuarial Mathematics/ Cand.act. i forsikringsmatematik
