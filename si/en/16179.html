Before you can start filling in your project contract, you need to have the following in place:

  • Choose your topic
    Begin 2–3 months in advance. Decide on the title as well. You must be able to describe, as precisely and in as much detail as possible, what the project will be about.
  • Do you want to collaborate with a company?
    Start roughly 4 months beforehand. You need a signed agreement and the name of a contact person ready for the project contract (attach the agreement when completing the form).
  • Find a supervisor
    Start 2–3 months in advance. Align your expectations: What expectations do you have of each other? What will each of you contribute? How many hours do you expect to spend on supervision? How many hours do you expect to spend on preparation and writing?
  • Do you want to do the project with others?
    Consider whether you want to write in a group or alone. If you are doing a group project, you must have agreed on the topic, title, supervisor, etc. before you complete the project contract together.
  • Complete and submit your project contract
    You fill in the form together with your supervisor. The deadline is before the start of the block.
