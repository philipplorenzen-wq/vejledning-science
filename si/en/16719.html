Gælder for målgrupperne: Biosolutions - Kandidat

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Your-way-through-the-programme-SCI-BIOS.aspx

Your MSc-programme in Biosolutions is composed of compulsory, elective  and restricted elective courses as well as your Master's thesis project. Consider your study as full-time work and use the time it requires.

The first three blocks starts with mandatory courses where you will be familiar with the setting in Kalundborg and learn about the production from rawmaterial to down stream processing. In the fourth block you will be able to chose restricted elective courses or chose courses of your free choice. Instead of courses you will be able to chose between different projects e.g. in collaboration with an industrial partner.

The courses from a list of restricted elective courses, have already been pre-approved for you to take. 

In general, I advise you to choose courses that fit your interests and are relevant for complementing your skills. Either you want to follow a line of courses where you are involved in finding solutions for cases given by the industry or you want to take a direction to aquire competences to run projects.

MSc thesis project

During the second year, you do a 45 ECTS Master's thesis project e.g in collaboration with a company.
