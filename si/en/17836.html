What happens with my courses and exams while on parental leave with SU?

If you give notice of parental leave after having started a block, you can choose to be withdrawn from the exams and courses in that block.

If you withdraw from courses and exams:

  1. you can be sure of a place on the courses again next time
  2. you do not use an exam attempt on the courses.

If you still want to take your courses and exams, you can also choose this option on the childbirth notification form.


Withdraw from courses in forthcoming blocks on Self Service

You must withdraw from the courses and exams you are registered for in forthcoming blocks on Self Service – if you need to.
