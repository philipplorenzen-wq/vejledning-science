You can apply for a dispensation if, due to exceptional circumstances, you need to arrange your studies in ways which fall outside the rules and framework of your study programme.

You may, for example, apply for a dispensation to

  • withdraw from courses and/or exams after expiry of the deadlines
  • postpone the study activity requirements
  • be granted extra exam attempts.
