If you don't get a place on a course during the normal registration period, you will receive a KUmail from UCPH Study Guidance explaining:

  1. Why you did not get a place (no vacancies or to do with your status)
  2. What your options are.

Follow the instructions in the email carefully.


Your options depend on why you did not get a place

If the course was fully booked:
You will be allowed to select another course within a limited period before the late-registration period.


If you were not allowed to take the course:

Your registration will be rejected if you register for courses

  • which your curriculum does not allow you to take
  • for which you do not meet the formal requirements
  • for which you do not have enough ECTS credits.
