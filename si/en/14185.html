The purpose of the survey is to get insights into what it's like to be a student at UCPH. The survey has questions about your well-being and your experience of administrative services at UCPH. Which means your views on:

  • the social and psychological environment on your programme
  • physical surroundings such as buildings and the interior
  • the administrative support of your programme; for example student counselling, information and IT support.
