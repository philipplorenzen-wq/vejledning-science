You can attend an open house event in the exam rooms at South Campus before your exam in order to be better prepared for the practicalities.

At open house, you can:

  • see the exam room
  • do a test exam
  • ask questions to the IT supporters

Sign up

Open house will be held twice during the semester. When the exam house announces the dates, they will be visible on this page and in a study message.

The next open house will be held on 19 May at 3 PM - 5 PM.

In the registration form, you can select a time slot that suits you. 

Registration for the Open House on 19 May in the exam rooms is over.

Registration is closed


You will also receive a study message with dates and registration links at the latest 2 weeks before the event takes place.  You can find the study message here.

Open house takes place in building 5A, Njalsgade 76. 

Check Digital Exam to see if you have received your exam room number. Then you can see the room when you are at the Open House.
