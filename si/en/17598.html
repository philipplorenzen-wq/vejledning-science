You can withdraw from the project if you do so before the deadline for the block in which you were due to start the project. This is the same deadline as for submitting the project contract.

After that deadline, you will need a dispensation to withdraw.

You can find the deadlines on this page under ‘What is the deadline for sending the contract for approval?’

Withdraw via the form ‘Get study administrative help’.

Select ‘Projects: Registration, withdrawal [etc.]’ under ‘What is your inquiry about?’
