It is expected of you that you have read and understood the structure of the program and the choices you have to make. You should know about deadlines that you have to meet, e.g., for signing up for courses or choosing supervisors for projects and for your thesis. We will support you in making these choices. 

Besides me as head of studies there will be a vice head of studies at the technical university, and you will also be assigned a faculty member as mentor. We hope and expect that you reach out to us for help when needed. Each course in the program has its own rules for how to pass or obtain a grade. You should understand those rules and follow them. 

We hope and expect that students will help and support each other and help us in creating a friendly and welcoming study environment. We encourage all of you to participate in the social and other extracurricular events that we organize. These will include evening lectures, career events, and internship matchmaking, etc. 

We strongly encourage students to form study groups. We also hope that you as students will organize yourself and interact with faculty to improve the study program. As part of improving the study programme all students are strongly encouraged to participate in the course evaluations conducted as part of every course in the programme.
