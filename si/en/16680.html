KSI (KU Studenteridræt) offers a wide range of sports activities at student-friendly prices. 


For example, you can sign up for: 

  • dance 
  • ball games 
  • martial arts 
  • yoga 
  • water sports 


KSI is a great place to: 

  • be involved in fun activities with your fellow students 
  • meet new fellow students across UCPH’s degree programmes.
