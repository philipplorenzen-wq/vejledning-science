The Master's thesis (30 ECTS credits) completes your study programme. As a general rule, you are in charge of finding your own thesis supervisor. Often it's best to ask a teacher personally on a course that you like or is related to your favorite topics. Have a look at the research and projects of the research groups and contact a group member for advice and recommendations. Denmark is known for its casual style, so feel free to talk to your teachers. If you cannot find any supervisor, I may help you to find one. My advice: start 1-2 blocks before your planned thesis to find your supervisor. Popular supervisors and areas are quickly "booked".

Consider doing a Project outside the course scope and/or a Thesis preparation project before starting on your Master's thesis. This is a good way to explore an interesting topic and to practice your research and writing skills before you start your Master's thesis. You may also work in a small group on a project, which is often more fun. Who knows, maybe you get inspiration for an exciting research topics to work on in your thesis?

Companies who joined the DIKU Business Club also propose topics for Master's Thesis, Project in Practice or Project outside the course scope. In this case you will have a main supervisor at our department and a co-supervisor at a company.
