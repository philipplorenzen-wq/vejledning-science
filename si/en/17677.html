The programme in Quantum Information Science is part of an International

DigiQ Consortium. Within this framework, it will be possible to do a physical exchange and study abroad at one of the partner universities. There is also an option to follow selected courses from the DigiQ partner universities online and in this sense do a “virtual” exchange. If you follow at least 30 ECTS of DigiQ designated courses as part of your full 120 ECTS programme you will receive a DQ-master transcript on top of your regular Master’s diploma. It will also be possible to study abroad through other Erasmus agreements. The best mobility window for going abroad is in the first semester of the second year. You should however be aware that this is not really an option if you do a 45 ECTS thesis.
