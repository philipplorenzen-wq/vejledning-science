Studenterhuset is the student centre for activities, parties and ideas, where you can meet with other students from UCPH's many faculties and study programmes. 


There are many ways to be involved as a volunteer at Studenterhuset. You can:

  • work in the bar
  • be part of the language café
  • help do repairs in the Repair Café
  • help refugees with their university studies
  • take part in and help launch sustainability activities in Actory
  • launch new projects in the project centre.


Read more about the possibilities on the Studenterhuset website
