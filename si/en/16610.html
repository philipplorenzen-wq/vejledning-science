To obtain approval of your stay, you must meet these three requirements:

  • The stay must not prolong your period of study.
  • You must meet the host university's admission and grade requirements.
  • The courses that you want to take must have been pre-approved.

Please also note that the individual university may have minimum requirements for your language skills, or that courses provided in English may be limited.
