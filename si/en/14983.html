You are free to use the canteen at University College Absalon in Kalundborg. The canteen is open Monday to Friday from 8:00-14:15. The lunch buffet is open from 11:15 to 12:45.

Selection and prices

The canteen offers healthy and tasty meals prepared using fresh seasonal ingredients. Choose between filling a plate or mixing a salad at the lunch buffet. You can also buy a quick sandwich or choose one of the easy-to-go dishes. The canteen also offers breakfast and snacks.

See today’s menu on Facebook

You will always find a selection of hot and cold drinks and you can fill your water bottle.

The prices vary depending on what you choose at the buffet. It is possible to buy discount vouchers for the lunch buffet and coffee/tea.

The canteen has a self-service checkout. This means, you take the items you want and then pay for them by typing them into the screen. Save your receipt until you have eaten.

The canteen is closed:

  • Saturdays, Sundays and holidays
  • Between Christmas and New Years
  • The whole of July
