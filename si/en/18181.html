Can I extend my Erasmus exchange?

 You can only apply for an Erasmus exchange in the application rounds in October (deadline 1 November), January-February (deadline 15 February) and May (deadline 15 May).

If you are on Erasmus exchange in the autumn semester, you cannot extend your Erasmus exchange to the spring semester, because there are no application round in the autumn semester for the following spring semester.

If you are on exchange in the spring semester and want to extend your exchange to the autumn semester, you must apply for this in the application round that is open in January-February (deadline 15 February).
