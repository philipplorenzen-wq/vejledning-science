Do I need to do anything during my stay abroad in order to get SU?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Do-I-need-to-do-anything-during-my-stay-abroad-in-order-to-get-SU-SU-KU.aspx

If your studies abroad stretch over 2 semesters or more, you need to submit documentation for your studies at the end of each semester.
The documentation may be an exam printout, a statement from a teacher or similar.
Send all documentation to the SU at the UCPH: su@adm.ku.dk.
