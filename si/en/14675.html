Do the survey participants reflect the general gender, age and nationality distribution at the University?

Original side på KUnet: https://kunet.ku.dk/study/study_content/Pages/Do-the-survey-participants-reflect-the-general-gender-age-and-nationality-distribution-at-the-University-KU.aspx

6,623 students and employees answered all the questions in the survey and in addition, 1,745 contributed with partial answers to the questionnaire. The total number of participants in the survey is thus 8,368, which corresponds to approximately 17% of the University's students and staff.
The responses are evenly distributed across the faculties and the university administration. The gender, age and national backgrounds of those who have participated are also similar to the overall distribution of genders, ages and nationalities at UCPH.
