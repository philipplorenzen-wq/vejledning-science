The MSc programme in Computer Science (part-time) consists of four elements:

  • Restricted elective courses, 60 ECTS credits
  • Elective courses
    • 30 ECTS credits (with thesis 30 ECTS)
    • 15 ECTS credits (with thesis 45 ECTS)
  • Thesis, 30 or 45 ECTS credits

The programme is four years if you study part-time (30 ECTS credits per year). If you complete all of the 120 ECTS credits in the programme, it will lead to the title

  • Master of Science in Computer Science / Cand. scient. i datalogi
