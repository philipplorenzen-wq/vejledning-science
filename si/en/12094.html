My name is Jan Philip Solovej and I am your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic contents of your program in detail and have contributed to determining your curriculum. My research area is Mathematical Physics – in particular the study of quantum matter. I am not teaching a course in your program.
As a student, you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your course of studies. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make. Here are my advice and my recommendations for completing your master's program in Quantum Information Science successfully. Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.
