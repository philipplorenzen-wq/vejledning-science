If you need to change your civil registration number (CPR number) or the name with which you are registered at the University of Copenhagen, you can learn how to on this page.

The 2 situations where you need to take action yourself in regards to change of name and CPR number: 

  • if you wish to change your name while you await a legal name change
  • When you get your official CPR number as an exchange student and now need to replace your placeholder CPR (pseudo CPR number).
