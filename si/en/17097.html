Can I do a project outside the course scope outside SCIENCE or SUND?

A project outside the course scope must be anchored at SCIENCE or SUND. This means that you must

  • have a supervisor at SCIENCE or SUND
  • hand in the project and have it assessed at SCIENCE or SUND.

You are welcome to collect empirical data outside SCIENCE or SUND, for example at a laboratory, at another educational institution or at a company.

Discuss with your supervisor whether it can be used for your project.
