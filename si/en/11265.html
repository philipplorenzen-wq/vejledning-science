I'm Sinne Smed and I'm your Head of Studies. As your Head of Studies, I know the academic contents of your program in detail and have contributed to determining your curriculum. I graduated as an Environmental Economist for many years ago. Currently my research area is within sustainable and healthy food demand. I teach on the courses Applied Econometrics (compulsory) and Applied Economics of Consumption (restricted elective).

As a student you have many options and a flexible framework for influencing the contents of your study path. The choices within your curriculum are yours to make and your interests should be your main guide.

Here are my advice and recommendations for completing your master's programme in Enviromental and Natural Resource Economics (ENRE for short) successfully. 

Read my advice as inspiration for your choices - not as rules to follow.
