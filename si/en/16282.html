What counts towards my maximum duration of study?

Your completion time runs from the date on which you start the first block of your study programme (e.g. 1 September or 1 February).

You must be enrolled in the study programme for it to count towards your maximum duration of study.


Periods that do not count towards your maximum duration of study

  • Parental leave
  • Leave of absence due to extraordinary circumstances
  • Periods during which you have been disenrolled from your study programme


If you have been disenrolled for a period of time

If you have been disenrolled from your study programme for a period of time, your maximum duration of study will run from when you were first enrolled. The period during which you were disenrolled does not count.
