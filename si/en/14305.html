If you are studying at reduced hours and have an individual study plan, you should be aware of your SU situation. The study activity requirements on your programme differ from the activity requirements for receiving SU.

Read more about your programme's activity requirements.

Read more about the SU activity requirements.

The SU office at UCPH can check your study plan to see if you comply with the SU activity requirements. Send your study plan to su@adm.ku.dk, and we will check it for you. If you want our dedicated athlete counsellor to call you, please state it in your email.
