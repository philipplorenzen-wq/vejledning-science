A great way to organise your bibliography and avoid plagiarism is to use a citation tool. 

A citation tool helps you organise your references, cite correctly and make a proper bibliography.

The library offers courses in three citations tools

  • Endnote (UCPH license): description in Danish only
  • Zotero (Free)
