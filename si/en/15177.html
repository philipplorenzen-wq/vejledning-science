When deciding whether you qualify for the “living at home" or “living away from home" SU rate, what matters is where you are officially registered as living on the first day of the month.

The SU Office at the University of Copenhagen receives address updates directly from the CPR register. Therefore, it is your responsibility to ensure that your address is correctly registered in the national registry.

Any adjustment to your SU will be made within 14 days from the date you officially change your address.
