We offer guidance on

  • how to plan and make choices during your studies
  • what to do if you e.g. become ill or go on parental leave 
  • your well-being, motivation and doubts about your studies 
  • how you can handle abusive behaviour 
  • your future plans and career 
  • special educational support (SPS) and special conditions

BOOK a guidance session 

You cannot book guidance sessions at the moment. We will offer sessions again from 1 September. The booking system will open here on the site two weeks beforehand.
You can book a session with a study and career guidance counsellors to discuss anything affecting your student life. Sessions last up to 25 minutes. 

If you have a functional impairment and are in a complex situation, you can book a special support session. If you need to apply for SPS, choose the session with SPS in our booking system. Sessions last up to 35 minutes.
Appointments take place either at Dyrlægevej 10, 1870 Frederiksberg or online via Zoom.
