The internationalisation scholarship is awarded according to the following criteria:
  • Grade-point average: The scholarships are awarded based on the applicants’ grade-point average. 
  • Academic and disciplinary diversity: All faculties at the University are considered in the distribution of scholarships to ensure that students across UCPH have access to the scholarships regardless of grade differences between the faculties.
  • Your stay abroad must provide full credit. This means that you must have courses equal to 30 ECTS credits per semester pre-approved by your study board before your stay abroad, and you must transfer 30 ECTS to your study programme at UCPH when your exchange stay ends.
In addition, the following rules apply:
  • Changes for your stay abroad (e.g. destination or period) may result in a different amount being awarded to you or in the scholarship being cancelled.
  • You cannot be awarded the scholarship for a stay abroad which automatically includes other grants or scholarships administered by UCPH (Erasmus+ or Nordplus).
  • You cannot receive payment of the scholarship after your stay abroad has been completed.
  • If you have previously received the scholarship, you cannot receive it again.
