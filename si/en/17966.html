The Student Union represents all students at the University of Copenhagen. The Student Union looks after the interests of students – both in relation to the university and society in general, including students':

  • social interests
  • educational interests
  • political interests.

The Student Union is an association of the various study councils at UCPH.

The study councils can voice their opinions at the Student Union's annual general meetings.

Each study council decides how they want to be organised, within the basic organisational principles of the Student Union.
